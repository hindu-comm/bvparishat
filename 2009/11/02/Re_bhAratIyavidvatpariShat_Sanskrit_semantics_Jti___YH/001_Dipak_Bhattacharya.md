What struck me most is the occurrence oftwo contradictory sentences

1.\<The other Jati, Akriti, Vyakti or the three are more or less the same in the function of denoting the meaning.>

2.\<Jātih eva vācyāh padānām vyaktayastu lakşyāh iti Bhaţţāh.>

In the first Jāti has an instrumentalfunctionin denoting while in the second it is itself denoted. The second view is Shastric. The first one contradicts it and is beyond my comprehension.
