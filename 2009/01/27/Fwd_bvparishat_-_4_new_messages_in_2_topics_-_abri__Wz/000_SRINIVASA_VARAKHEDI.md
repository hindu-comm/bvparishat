Dear All,

This year another good news for Sanskrit Research Community after  
getting JnanaPitha Award is that Shri Bannanje Govindacharya one of  
the great scholar of Vedas, Upanishat, Mahabharata, Ramayana,  
Bhagavata and Dvaita Vedanta, has bagged Padmasree Award 2009.

Sri Govindacharya is from South Canara District in Coastal Karnataka  
State. He has edited whole works of Acharya Madhva for the first time  
from an original Mss available from Acharya's direct disciple. He has  
authored several commentaries on various works. His Kannada  
Translation of many Sanskrit plays are considered great literature in  
Kannada. His recent publications - Mahabharatatatparyanirnaya,  
Madhvavijaya and Upanishad-bhashya are notable. BVP community  
expresses its happiness and congratulates the award winner.

regards,  
shrivara
