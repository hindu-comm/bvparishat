Respected Sirs,

It is very difficult to accept that the sutras are lecture notes taken hurriedly. It is stated by Patanjali "Pramanabhuta AcharyaH Suchaavavakaashe DarbhapavitrapaniH Praanmukha Upavishya Mahataa Yatnena Sutraani Pranayati Sma. Tatraashakyam Varnenaapyanarthekena Bhavitum."

Even Bloomfield once told Astadhyayi "the highest monument of human intelligence." The way the sutras are written by Panini is not a simple thing as taking notes in class. It must take a deep insight and potential.
