1\. Niititattvaavirbhaava of chidandanda pandita first published by Travancore Sanskrit series Trivandrum. This is very important work on Bhatta system.

Miimaamsaabhaasyaparisista of Shalikanatha first published by Madras University Sanskrit series rs.300

2\. Vibhramaviveka of Mandana mishra and other manuals on Khyativaada rs 200

3\. Nyaayaadhvadiipikaa and Upasamhaaravijaya of Vijayiindra tiirtha along with Upakramaparaakrama of Appayyadiiksita rs 100

4\. Shaabdataraginii of V. Subrahmanya Shastri first published by Sanskrit Education society Madras
