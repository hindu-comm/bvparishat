Sub : Call for Articles for 4^(th) Issue of peer reviewed Research Journal “प्राची प्रज्ञा” (ISSN 2348 – 8417).

महोदयाः !

 “प्राची प्रज्ञा” (ISSN 2348 – 8417) इति अन्ताराष्ट्रियान्तर्जालपत्रिकायां “शास्त्रमञ्जूषा” इति षाण्मासिकखण्डस्य चतुर्थाङ्कनिमित्तं प्रकशनाय मौलिकाः शोधलेखा आविश्वं विद्यमानेभ्यो विद्वद्भ्य आमन्त्र्यन्ते। लेखाः न्यूनातिन्यूनं पञ्चपृष्ठात्मका निम्नप्रारूपेणैव टङ्कयित्वा [prachip...@gmail.com]() इति अन्तर्जालसंकेतेन मृदुप्रतिकृतिरूपेणैव 31 मार्च 2017 पर्यन्तं मुख्यसम्पादकं प्रति प्रेषयितुं शक्यन्ते।

Ø **शोधलेखाः संस्कृतभाषया/ English / हिन्दीभाषया वा १२/ १४ अक्षरमानेन A4 पृष्ठे १.५ पङ्क्ति-अन्तरालेन न्यायसङ्गतपार्श्वसीमसहिताः (Justified aligned) Microsoft Word आधारितं सञ्चिकाप्रारूपमेवाश्रित्य टङ्किताः स्युः। संस्कृतभाषया हिन्दीभाषया च लेखाः Unicode आधारितदेवनागरीलिप्या / रोमनलिप्या वा टङ्किताः स्युः।**

Ø पादटिप्पणी ग्रन्थकार-ग्रन्थ-प्रकशक-वर्ष-पृष्ठसंख्याक्रमेण / ग्रन्थ-ग्रन्थकार-प्रकशक-वर्ष-पृष्ठसंख्याक्रमेण वा सज्जिता स्यात्।

Ø शोधे प्रयुक्तानां ग्रन्थानां पूर्णा सन्दर्भसूची स्यात्।

Ø शोधलेखस्य संक्षिप्तिकाऽपि प्रायः २००शब्दैः प्रेषणीया।

Ø शोधपत्रेण सह स्वीयमेकम् सद्यः गृहीतं 3.5 x 4.5 cm परिमितं स्वीयं
भावचित्रं (JPEG प्रारूपेण) चेत्यादिकमवश्यं प्रेषणीयम्।

Ø शोधलेखस्य अन्ते लेखकस्य नाम, वृत्तिः, संकेतः, देशकूटसहिता दूरभाषसंख्या , अन्तर्जालसंकेतः (Email ID) चेत्यादिकमवश्यं लेखनीयम्।

Ø शोधलेखेन सह घोषणापत्रमवश्यं स्याद् यद् – अयं शोधलेखः मदीयः मौलिकः तथा न अन्यत्र कुत्रचिदपि प्रकाशितः प्रकशयितुं वा चितोऽस्ति।





 विशेषज्ञमण्डल्या पुनरीक्षणोपरान्तं (Peer Reviewing) प्रकाशनयोग्याः शोधलेखाः प्रकाशयिष्यन्ते। प्रकाशनयोग्यतायाः मानदण्डाः निम्नवत् निर्धारिताः सन्ति –

**1. शोधलेखः संस्कृतवाङ्मयस्य केनाप्येकेनाङ्गेन सह सम्बद्धः स्यात्। यथा- वेदः, वेदाङ्गानि, उपनिषद्, दर्शनानि, पुराणानि, स्मृतिः, योगः, आगमः, साहित्यं, कव्यशास्त्रं, व्याकरणं, तन्त्रं, ज्योतिषं तथा संगणकीयभाषाविज्ञानाद्याधुनिकविषयाः।**

2. शोधलेखः स्वीयः मौलिकश्च स्यात्।

3. शोधलेखे विवेचनप्रक्रिया समस्याचयनत → आरभ्य क्रमेण प्राक्कल्पना → ततः समालोचनात्मकं मूल्याङ्कनं → तथा अन्ते शोधस्य मौलिकनिष्कर्षेण सहोपसंहारः इत्येवं क्रमवद्धतापुरःसरं विकसिता स्यात्।

4. शोधलेखे काचिन्नवीना अवधारणा, किमपि नवीनं तथ्यं, नवीनं मूल्याङ्कनं, नवीनः सिद्धान्तः, नवीना शैली, नवीनः संकेतो वा अवश्यमाविष्कृतः स्यात्।

5. शोधलेखस्य भाषा परिष्कृता, प्राञ्जला, स्तरानुगुणा च स्यात्।

6. शोधलेखे समसामयिकी सम्बद्धता, तार्किकम् अनुशीलनं, तथा सन्दर्भाणामौचित्यमवश्यं स्युः।

7. शोधलेखे अन्यस्य प्रकाशनस्यांशोद्धरणे तदनुमतिः अवश्यं स्वीकर्तव्या यदि तादृशमुद्धरणं ५०० शब्देभ्योऽधिकमस्ति।

8. प्रकाशनात्परं शोधलेखस्य सर्वाधिकारः “प्राची प्रज्ञा” इत्यस्याः एव सुरक्षितः भविष्यति। शोधलेखस्य पुनः प्रकाशनस्य स्थितौ “प्राची प्रज्ञा” इत्यस्याः लिखितानुमतिः अवश्यं स्वीकर्तव्या।

9. प्रकाशनसम्बन्धे पत्राचाराय शोधलेखेन सह प्रयुक्तान्तर्जालसंकेतः (Email ID) एव उपयोक्तव्यः।

10. सर्वापि प्रक्रिया अन्तर्जालमाध्यमेनैव सम्पादयिष्यते। मुद्रितरूपेण न काचित् प्रक्रिया आश्रियते। शोधलेखस्य प्रकाशनयोग्यताऽयोग्यतानिर्धारणदायित्वं “प्राची प्रज्ञा” इत्यस्यामेव न्यस्तं भविष्यति।

Respected Scholars !

 Research Papers in Sanskrit/ English/ Hindi are invited from the scholars from the entire glove for the 4^(th) Issue of peer reviewed International Research Journal **“****प्राची प्रज्ञा”** having ISSN no. 2348 – 8417, for its half yearly section **“****शास्त्रमञ्जूषा”. The Last date of receipt of articles is 31^(st)
March 2017. **A **submission** to “प्राची प्रज्ञा” must be the original work of the author(s) and must have not been published elsewhere or be under consideration to be published. The paper in not less than five pages, should be emailed to the Editor in Chief on [**prachip...@gmail.com**]() in soft copy only. A submission must fulfill the following criterion”

1. Format:

Ø All the fonts of Devanagari / Roman and Roman with diacritical marks, must be Unicode compatible. For Devanagari, Arial Unicode MS and for Roman, Times New Roman is preferable. The size of fonts should be 12 in case of English and 14 in case of Sanskrit / Hindi

Ø Format of file must be Microsoft Word only. File Formats in Adobe Page Maker / pdf / Corel Draw / Adobe Indesign etc. are not acceptable.

Ø Page size must be of A4 typed on 1.5 line spacing and justified aligned and 1" margined format.

Ø References should be embedded in the text in a consistent style and order, e.g Author, Title, Details of Publication (Place, Publisher, date if book; name of journal in bold, volume no. and date if article), p. or pp or Title, Author, Details of Publication (Place, Publisher, date if book; name of journal in bold, volume no. and date if article), p. or pp

Ø All research papers should be accompanied by an abstract of 200 words.

Ø All contribution should also include (i) Name(s) of the author (ii) His / Her professional affiliation (iii) postal address (iv) phone/mobile numbers and (v) email address.

Ø There should be a proper Bibliographic reference at the end of the Research Paper.

Ø A passport size photograph in JPEG format only of 3.5 x 4.5 cm, must be attached along with the Research Paper.

Ø Each Research paper should be accompanied with a declaration by all the authors that **“****The paper is original and has neither been published earlier nor been submitted for publication elsewhere.” **



 All the research papers will undergo a review process. The papers (without the name of the contributor) will be sent to the reviewers having expertise on the field, and will be considered for publication only on the recommendations of the reviewers on following parameters:

**1. **The Research Paper must have a link to any branch of Sanskrit Studies, i.e. **Vedas, Vedāṅgas, Upaniṣads, Philosophies, Purāṇas, Smṛtis, Literature, Poetics, Grammar, Tantras, Astrology, Astronomy** **and Modern subjects like Computational Linguistics Environmental Science and allied fields.**

**2. **The Research Paper must be an original contribution of the author.****

**3. **Development of discussion should be from Statement of Problem to→ assumption or hypotheses to→ critical evaluations → to a conclusion with the findings of research. ****

**4. **The Research Paper must present a discovery / invention of a new - idea / thought /design / plan / awareness / inkling/ indication / appreciation / conception / understanding / opinion / view/ concept / notion / theory / principle or even a new style of interpretation. ****

5. The language must be lucid and must meet the standard of presentation.

6. The Research Paper must have contemporary relevance, contributions to knowledge, clarity and logic in analysis, methodology and appropriateness of references.

7. If the author has quoted more than 500 words / a table / a figure from a published work, in the article, a copy of permission obtained from the respective copyright holder needs to be enclosed.

8. The copyright of the article after it gets published is reserved by the “प्राची प्रज्ञा”. It should not get re-produced elsewhere without the written permission of the “Editor in Chief” of “प्राची प्रज्ञा”.

9. All the correspondence should be made through the same email ID only by which the paper has been submitted.

10. All the process will be made on line only and no printing media will be entertained. The selection of the Research paper is entirely entrusted to “प्राची प्रज्ञा”.



 If the paper meets the preliminary requirements, the article will be processed for review, which could take up to 2 months. The accepted articles are scheduled for publication in the chronological order in which they are accepted.
