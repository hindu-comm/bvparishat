This also reminds me of another beautiful verse taught to me by Late Pt. Bhide. This is a conversation between Rama and Lakshmana:

  

Rama: सौमित्रे ननु सेव्यतां तरुतलं चण्डांशुरुज्जृम्भते

Lakshmana: चण्डांशोर्निशि का कथा रघुपते चन्द्रोऽयमुन्मीलति

Rama: वत्सैतद्विदितं कथं नु भवता

Lakshmana: धत्ते कुरङ्गं यत:

Rama: क्वासि प्रेयसि हा कुरङ्गनयने चन्द्रानने जानकि

  

If my memory serves me, this verse occurs as an illustration of Smaraṇa Alaṅkāra in Jagannātha's Rasagaṅgādhara. Don't have the book at hand for reference.
