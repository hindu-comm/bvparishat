The object of the workshop will be to study the texts and learn editing of them on the basis of manuscripts available in Grantha script. In this connection, instructions on the principles of transcription, collation, editing, etc. of Sanskrit texts written in Grantha script, features of the Grantha script etc, demonstration of their practical application in the area of Indological study and research will also be a part of the programme. The purpose is to make a team of young scholars with expertise in Grantha script and encourage them to take up text-editing for MPhil, PhD and Post-Doctoral researches.

  

Candidates who are willing to pursue MPhil, PhD and Post-Doctoral research on textual criticism on the basis of manuscripts may apply for participation in the workshop to Dr. Bhaskarnath Bhattacharya (Director, School of Vedic Studies, Rabindra Bharati University, email: [jaym...@rediffmail.com](), phone: [+91 8336965536](tel:+91%2083369%2065536)) with complete bio-data by 5th January, 2017.
