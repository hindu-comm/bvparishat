Dear friends and colleagues,



Revised and elaborated versions of most of the talks presented at the thematic panel on Adaptive Reuse at the Deutsche Orientalistentag in Münster in 2013 have just bee published in the volume “Adaptive Reuse – Aspects of Creativity in South Asian Cultural History”. The volume is published in open access, and a PDF-file can be downloaded free of charge [here](https://www.academia.edu/31005848/Adaptive_Reuse_Aspects_of_Creativity_in_South_Asian_Cultural_History). The print version is available from the Harrassowitz Publishing House for the price of 64€ [here]().
