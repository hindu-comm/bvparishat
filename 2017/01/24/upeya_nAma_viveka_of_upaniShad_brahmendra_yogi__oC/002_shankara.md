Sriramji,

  

Upeyanamaviveka edited by Dr. V Raghavan was published in Adyar Library Bulletin no. 29,1965 pp. 203-58. This issue of Adyar Library Bulletin is not available for download.  

  

3 more works of Upanishad Brahmendra were published serially in Adyar Library Bulletin, as mentioned below.

  

1. Brahmasutra Bhashya Siddhanta Sangraha

2. Tattvampadarthaikya Satakam

3. Karmakarmaviveka in Adyar Library Bulletin no. 25, 1961 pp. 436-46.

  

The first two are available at <https://archive.org/details/Works_of_Upanishad_Brahmendra>
