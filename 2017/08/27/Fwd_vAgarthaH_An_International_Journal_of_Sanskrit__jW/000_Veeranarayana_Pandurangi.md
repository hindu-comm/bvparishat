**First Issue** of **वागर्थः** **(An International Journal of Sanskrit Research)** has come out and it is available online freely on the following website-



<http://cphfs.in/research.php>



We are quarterly publishing an Online **वागर्थः** **(An International Journal of Sanskrit Research)** to promote publication of qualitative research papers and review articles world-wide. Our vision for authors and scholars is to provide an international standard platform to share their knowledge and innovation and to grow, and for others to provide qualitative research publication to everyone and help to improve the quality of education.



**Salient Features**:



-   This journal follows standard format of research papers. -   Research papers/review articles written in **Unicode Font** or
    **Times New Roman Font** can be uploaded for review. -   This journal follows international standard of **TWO-LAYER**
    **Double-blind peer review** to determine the originality of
    research and the viability of publication. In this method, the
    research paper is forwarded to two reviewers without mentioning name
    of the Author(s), affiliated institution and email ID. Paper
    evaluated on different aspects and if both reviewers give their
    positive remark then only paper will be accepted for publication. -   Any paper may be published only after the approval of the Review
    Board and Editorial Board and in consultation with the Advisory
    Board. -   This journal follows international norms and is to be indexed in
    different National/International database of journals. -   Papers published in this journal will be available on the Google
    search engine and we are facilitating the citation count of each
    paper cited worlds-wide. -   This Journal is supervised by eminent scholars having excellent
    knowledge of the subject in addition to traditional as well as
    modern approaches.



Any qualitative research paper/review paper in the areas of *Veda, Purāṇa, Brāhmaṇa, Araṇyaka, Upaniṣad, Vyākaraṇa, Sāhitya, Bhāratiya Darśana, Nirukta, Chanda, Kalpa, Kāvya, Mahākāvya, Nāṭaka, Śikṣā* etc. may be uploaded for review purposes. All papers/articles published in our journals are under open access policy.
