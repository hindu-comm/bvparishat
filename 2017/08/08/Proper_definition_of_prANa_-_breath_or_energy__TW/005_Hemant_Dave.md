P. Olivelle in the introduction to his translation of the Upanishads (p. 23) discusses the meaning of*prana* with literature. The two references he cites are:

  

Bodewitz, H. W. 1986. Prana, Apana and other prana-s in Vedic literature. *Adyar Library Bulletin* 50: 326-348.

  

Zysk, K. G. 1993. The science of respirationand the doctrine of the bodily winds in ancient India. *Journal of the American Oriental Society* 113: 198-213.
