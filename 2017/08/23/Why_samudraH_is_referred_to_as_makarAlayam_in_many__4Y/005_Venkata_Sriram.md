Namaste,

  

Also, inलक्ष्मीनृसिंह  करावलम्ब स्तोत्रं,शन्करभगवत्पादः compares the samsAra as Ocean and the Kala (time) as Makara:

  

संसारसागरविशालकरालकाल\_  
नक्रग्रहग्रसननिग्रहविग्रहस्य।  
व्यग्रस्यरागरसनोर्मिनिपीडितस्य  
लक्ष्मीनृसिंहममदेहिकरावलम्बम्॥५॥

  

In thisHuge OceanofSamsara(Worldly Existence), whereKala(Time)cleaves asundereverything like aCrocodile; my life is restrained and being eaten away likeRahu restrainsanddevoursthePlanet(i.e. Moon), ... and my senses beingengrossed intheWavesof theRasa(Juice) ofRaga(Passion) issqueezingaway my life, ...OLakshmi Nrisimha, Pleasegive meYourRefugeby holding me with YourDivine Hands.

  

regs,

sriram
