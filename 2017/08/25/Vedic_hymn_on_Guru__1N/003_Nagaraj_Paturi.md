[[Nagaraj Paturi	2017-08-25, 18:30:55 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/1NUhR_mvevQ)]]



अग्ने नय सुपथा राये अस्मान्विश्वानि देव वयुनानि विद्वान्।  
युयोध्यस्मज्जुहुराणमेनो भूयिष्ठां ते नमउक्तिं विधेम ॥ ऋ. 1/189/

  

is a mantra on Agni in which Agni is viewed as Guru. i.e., this is taken as an indirect hymn on Guru.
