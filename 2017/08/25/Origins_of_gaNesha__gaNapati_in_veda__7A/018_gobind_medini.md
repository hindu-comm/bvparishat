"गणानां त्वा गणपतिम्" इति मन्त्रेण वेदत्रयेऽपि स्तूयमानस्य देवस्य गणपतेःपूजाराधनस्य युक्तत्वात्। ...........................(शंकर भगवत्पादः)

  

It is also not clear if Shankara is talking about Gajanana (Gajamukha.. who has an elephant face) or "devasya ganapati" (god of group of devas). I believe we are tracing the evolution of elephant face Ganapati of today. (Incidentally, I participate in Ganesha Puja and love this god like many others in Maharashtra).
