Dear Pandurangiji,

H. Oldenberg and after him M. Winternitz regarded the repetitions as meant to enhance the magical power of the charm. They declined to accept them as having poetical value.

Please see Oldenberg's The Religion des Veda and Winternitz's History of Indian literature Volume 1. I do not have the books with me so I cannot give the page numbers.

Best
