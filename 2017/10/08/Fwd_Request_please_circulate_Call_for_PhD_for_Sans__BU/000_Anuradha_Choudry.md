I am resending the mail regarding the PhD as i was informed that the previous link was not working.

  

I am happy to share that the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, IIT Kharagpur, is inviting applications for PhD candidates for **Sanskrit** and **Indian Psychology**this year.

  

Furthermore, we are also seeking applications for prospective scholars to apply for PhD at the **Rekhi Centre of Excellence for the Science of Happiness**through ATDC**.**

**  
**

The last date for applications for all Ph.Ds is **10th October.**

  

I would be grateful if you could please share the information in your circles despite the short notice.Further details can be found on the following link for more details. <http://www.iitkgp.ac.in/phd>

  

Kindly note that candidates need to have a minimum of 55% throughout till their Masters included. There will be an entrance exam between the 29th Nov- 5th Dec (details in the link). Those having a Masters with JRF or equivalent, or a 2 year MPhil will be short-listed.

  

The next call for PhD will take place next semester for intake in the Autumn Semester.
