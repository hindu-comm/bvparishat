Dear Sir,

The Stotra is from Skanda Puran only. I recite it regularly. At the end of the Stotra, it is said that "इती श्री स्कन्द पुराणे कार्तिकेय महात्मे प्रज्ञाविवर्धन स्तोत्र संपुर्णम्" . I have checked it in Date Panchang also, the famous Panchang of Maharashtra.

  

So, it is sure that the stotra is from Skanda Puran only.

  

Regards,

Nishikant
