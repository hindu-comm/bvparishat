In the Prapanchasāratantra of Shankaracharya there is a verse that asks the aspirant optionally to meditate on Rudra in the form of a babe:

  

Chapter 26 \[Dakṣiṇāmūrti upāsana\]

  

![Inline image 1](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/4572424ca8113/image.png?part=0.1&view=1)  

अथ वामलकमलपुटा-

न्तरितं शिशुवेषभूषणं रुद्रम् ।

ध्यात्वा जपेद्यथाव-

द्धुतक्लृप्त्या मृत्युनाशनं दृष्टम् ॥

  

Is there any reference to this form of Rudra in any Vedic or smṛti texts or even in the kāvya-s?
