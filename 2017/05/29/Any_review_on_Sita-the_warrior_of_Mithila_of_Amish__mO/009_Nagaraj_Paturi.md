[[Nagaraj Paturi	2017-05-30, 15:31:25 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/mOfNzesMFJ0)]]



The krauncha mithuna incident, the narrative of Uttara Ramayana have this KaruNa born out ofone of the partners of a love couple getting separated from the other, in common with the main narrative of Ramayana. This is pathos (s'oka) linked to love (s'ringAra-rati).

  

If it were to be s'oka born out of fear (bhaya) of Ravana, then it would have been weakness. Sita was fearless in front of Ravana.

  

Abalaa, durbalaa etc. are mentioned by the poet, to highlight the dushTatva of the pratinAyaka.

  

It is also mentioned to highlight the need for the nAyaka to show his valor (veera).
