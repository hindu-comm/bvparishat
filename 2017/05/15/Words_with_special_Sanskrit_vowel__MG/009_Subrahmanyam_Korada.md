नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

Under ऋलृक् - Katyayana raises the questions as to whether the letter लृ is required due to its limited applicability etc - by taking लौकिक examples only - Patanjali made प्रत्याख्यानम् of these वार्तिकs towads the end and also remarks this exercise as cumbersome .

  

In fact, for Vedic usages like - क्लुप्तं च मे क्लुप्तिश्च मे ( शतरुद्रीयम्, चमकम् ) etc we want लृ ।

  

तवल्कारः --- ’ शीतार्तश्च तवल्कारः ’ (your nose is suffeing from common cold)
