If the intended sense is "leaves are being moved by the wind", then the usage should be

  

पत्राणि वायुना कम्प्यन्ते

  

and not

  

पत्राणि वायुना कम्पन्ते

  

The latter would mean "the leaves are trembling along with the wind" which does not make much sense.
