The highest *pramāṇa* being *śruti*, here is a *śruti* in support of *tasya vācakaḥ praṇavaḥ*.

  

*om khaṃ brahma*

(OM is the sky, the Brahman)

(Śukla-Yajurveda’s Vājasaneyi-mādhyandina-saṃhitā, 40.17)

  

*omiti nāma vā pratimā vā brahmaṇaḥ*

(OM is the name or image/symbol of Brahman)

Uvaṭa’s commentary on Vājasaneyi-mādhyandina-saṃhitā, 40.15

  

*omiti nāmanirdeśaḥ*

(OM is the indicative name)

Uvaṭa’s commentary on Vājasaneyi-mādhyandina-saṃhitā, 40.17

  

*omiti nāmanirdeśo brahmaṇaḥ*

(OM is the indicative name of Brahman)

Mahīdhara’s commentary on Vājasaneyi-mādhyandina-saṃhitā, 40.17
