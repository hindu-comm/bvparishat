Would anyone kindly clarify whether the term 'svāhā' uttered in the context of an offering of āhuti' is with dīrghasvarita? One instance that is commonly known is the Taittiriya śikṣāvallī:

  

लोमशां पशुभिः सहस्वाहा। आमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। विमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। प्रमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। दमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। शमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा॥ २ ॥

  

The symbol for dīrghasvarita is not printed here, but while chanting this the sound is clearly with dīrghasvarita.
