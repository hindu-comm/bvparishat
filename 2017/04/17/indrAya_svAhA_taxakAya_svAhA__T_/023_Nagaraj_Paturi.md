[[Nagaraj Paturi	2017-04-20, 19:21:07 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/T_Xc0C3M3ns)]]



Not connected to svara aspect or vibhakti going with svaahaa , the following commentator's opinion shared by venerable Sri V. Subrahmanian-ji shows that svaahaa need not necessarily make part of the sentence preceding it. It can just be an independent recitation made after recitation of a sentence indicating the end of the previous sentence (and that an aahuti is simultaneous with its recitation. );

  

*स्वाहा,स्वाहाकारोहोमार्थमन्त्रान्तज्ञापनार्थः।*आमायन्त्विति।आयन्तु,मामितिव्यवहितेनसम्बन्धः,ब्रह्मचारिणः।विमायन्तुप्रमायन्तुदमायन्तुशमायन्तुइत्यादि।यशोजनेयशस्विजनेषुअसानिभवानि।श्रेयान्प्रशस्यतरः,वस्यसःवसीयसःवसुतराद्वसुमत्तराद्वाधनवज्जातीयपुरुषाद्विशेषवानहमसानीत्यर्थः।किंच,तंब्रह्मणःकोशभूतंत्वात्वांहेभगभगवन्पूजार्ह,प्रविशानि।प्रविश्यचानन्यस्त्वदात्मैवभवानीत्यर्थः।सःत्वमपिमामांभगभगवन्,प्रविश;आवयोरेकात्मत्वमेवास्तु।

![](https://ci6.googleusercontent.com/proxy/x7Az90uC0FlqG1NQNJvbQl7YtYRlnWzoqu2O_ZLb9xIKhYpJarh3aZrZ_qlU6_VKMQpRwI5_nDi9FsJOqf0XSQHw_6yv1VK7rhIBScGS2g=s0-d-e1-ft#https://ssl.gstatic.com/ui/v1/icons/mail/images/cleardot.gif)

sahendraaya takshakaaya or indraaya takshakaaya can be such sentences independent of the svaahaa succeeding them.

  

Generally when the the devataa whose name ends in chaturthee is the intended receiver, a sentence beginning with the chaturthee ending name of the same devataa and with a structure of ."........... (chaturthee ending name of the devataa) idam na mama" is also recited.

  

लोमशां पशुभिः सहस्वाहा। आमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। विमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। प्रमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। दमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा। शमायन्तु ब्रह्मचारिणःस्वाहा॥ २ ॥

  

does not have idam na mama sentences following svaahaa ending ones.

  

Probably that is why the commentator says,स्वाहा,स्वाहाकारोहोमार्थमन्त्रान्तज्ञापनार्थः।

  

Whether there was a recitation of takshakaaya idam na mama was done after takshakaaya svaahaa or not is not mentioned in the narratives from where that sentence reaches us.
