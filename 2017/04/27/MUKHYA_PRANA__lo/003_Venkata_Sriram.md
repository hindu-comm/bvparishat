Namaste,

  

Further to the trailing mail, you may go thru the commentary ofप्राणाग्निसूक्तम् with elaborate notes by Bannaje Govindacharya Ji. 

  

As mentioned earlier, you may approach the Satyapramoda Vidya Peetha in Kachiguda for further guidance. For madhvas, प्राणाग्नि, मन्यु, पुरुष, श्री suktAs and हरिवायु स्तुति are very important and these are first taught here.
