Derivation of तुष्टुवांसः fromतुष्टुवस् is on the same lines as derivingविद्वांसः from विद्वस्. You may please refer any of the Kaumudi works for this part.

  

तुष्टाव इति तुष्टुवान्. \[He\] who has praised =तुष्टुवान्.  

तुष्टुवुः इति तुष्टुवांसः. \[They\] who have praised = तुष्टुवांसः.
