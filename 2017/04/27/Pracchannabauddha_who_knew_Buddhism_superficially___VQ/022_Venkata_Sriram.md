Namaste,

  

There is a beautiful commentary on Dakshinamurti Stotram in Sanskrit byमहामहोपाध्याय अनन्तकृष्ण शास्त्री published by Dakshinamurti Mutt, Varanasi calledमानसोल्लासवर्धिनी.

  

Though, I have not completely read the book, but a cursory glance at the commentaries of couple of shlokas viz.,

  

1)देहंप्राणमपि....शून्यं विदुः 
(2)राहूग्रस्तदिवाकरेन्दु........मायासमाच्छादनात्

  

The prevalent baudha concepts (existent during Sankara's time) as well the opinion of Anantakrishna Sastry Ji has been elaborately discussed and mahAyAna concept has been refuted.

  

I am yet to read them carefully. Hope this would be of use to the scholars here.

  

The link is below:

  

<http://dakshinamurtimathaprakashan.org/view.php?id=Manasollas%202400.pdf>

  

The discussions from 70th page are interesting !

  

regs,

sriram
