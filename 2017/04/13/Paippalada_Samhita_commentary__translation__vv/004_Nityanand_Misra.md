Thanks to Dr. Dhaval Patel, Vishal Agarwal Ji, and Prof. Dipak Bhattacharya Ji.

  

Please treat this as a follow-up question about AVP mantra 13.8.5. As per the online version on[https://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/पैप्पलादसंहिता/काण्डम्\_१३](https://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/%E0%A4%AA%E0%A5%88%E0%A4%AA%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%B2%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%B8%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%B9%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BE/%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%A3%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A1%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8D_%E0%A5%A7%E0%A5%A9), the reading is

  

इत्थमेके प्र व्रजन्तीत्थमेके दक्षिणा: प्रत्यञ्च उदञ्चः प्राञ्चोऽभि व्रजन्त्येके।

तेषां सर्वेषामिह सङ्गतिः साकं स एको भूतश्चरति प्रजानन्॥ ५ ॥

  

As per Jibananda Vidyasagara's edition of Atharvanopanishadah with the Dipika commentary by Narayana Bhatta (Calcutta: 1892), the mantra as cited in the Atharva-shira-upanishad is

  

तीर्थमेके व्रजन्ति तीर्थमेके दक्षिणा: प्रत्यञ्च उदञ्चः प्राञ्चोऽभि व्रजन्त्येके।

तेषां सर्वेषामिह सङ्गतिः साकं स एको भूतश्चरति प्रजानाम्॥



Even the Dipika commentary explains तीर्थम्, rendering it as उपायः.

  

Are both readings इत्थमेके प्र व्रजन्तीत्थमेकेandतीर्थमेके व्रजन्ति तीर्थमेके available in manuscripts?Any edition or translation which notes these two readings? Would also be interested to see how various translators have treated the mantra, since the meanings of the two readings are slightly different.
