1\. Present participle or past participle are verbal adjectives: given book = a book that is given; talking doll = a doll that talks or a doll that is talking,

  

2\. jeetaa jaagtaa is a Hindi example of such an adjective. 'Jeetaa jagtaa raashTr' can be a usage.

  

3\. It is possible to treat जाता हूं ,जाती हूं, जाता है, जाती है etc. as present participle + copula / be form . Such structures are called periphrastic constructions.

  

4\. गच्छामि is a verb formed through inflection. गच्छन् अस्मि is a periphrastic construction.

  

5\. To my knowledge, गच्छन्is a non-finite verbal form. भ्रमत् in भ्रमन्निलिंपनिर्झरी is a participle i.e., verbal adjective.
