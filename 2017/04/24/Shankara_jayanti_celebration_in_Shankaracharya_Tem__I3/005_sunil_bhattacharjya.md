At the fag end of the 19th century there also arose the controversy on the date of Ad Shankara, when Pathak published a paper about one Shankara who was born in 788 CE in Chidambaram and Max Muller came to the conclusion that Adi Shankara was born in 788 BCE, whereas according to the Kanchi Kamakoti Math, this Shankara was the Abhinava Shankara, who was born in 788 CE and became the pontiff of that math (Kanchi Kamakoti Math) in 801 CE and remained so till 841 CE..
