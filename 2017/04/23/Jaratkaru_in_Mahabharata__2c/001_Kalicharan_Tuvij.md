नमस्ते,

  

जरत् = decay, in the sense of:

**RV** **01.092.10** \[devatā uṣas, ṛṣi gotama rāhūgaṇa\]

śvaghnī-iva ǀ kṛtnuḥ ǀ vijaḥ ǀ ā-minānā ǀ martasya ǀ devī ǀ jarayantī ǀ āyuḥ ǁ

  

कारू = doer (in an intrinsic manner, e.g. artist etc)

  

The जरत्कारू pair \~ उषा-संध्या/रात्रि

उषा-संध्या relate with transitions; ऋषि जरत्कारू assumed significance because of the युगक्षयः (and his guiding/control on it).

  

P.S.

जरत्कारू the (पौराणिक) word in itself is not sufficient: in my view one must take into account the whole प्रकरण (including वासुकि etc).
