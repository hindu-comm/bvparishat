Regarding गौ शास्त्र

1- गवायुर्वेद manuscript was partially available to Scholar Late Krishnaji Vinayaka Vaze of Nashik of which Shloka mentioned in his शिल्प शिक्षणाचे महत्व (संस्कृत-मराठी) book published in 1920's. Searching for this.

  

2- गो शास्त्र in Odia language is published in Odisha. Also the same exists in the form of proverbs (folk system of knowledge), stills in the villages of Odisha. Also well documented & published as small booklets & also research journal.

  

Translation from Odia/Sanskrit can be carried out. Please contact- [ubija...@gmail.com]()
