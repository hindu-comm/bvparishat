Is it okay to think that "the rules in सपादसप्ताध्यायी there are no options. But त्रिपादी provides options." ?

  

For example एचोsयवायावः is the rule as far as only सपादसप्ताध्यायी is concerned. But लोपः शाकल्यस्य provides an option. Simple example of both the options having been employed isअवाप्य भूमावसपत्नमृद्धम् (गीता 2'8) hasएचोsयवायावः inभूमावसपत्नम्. However न योत्स्य इति (गीता 2'9) hasलोपः शाकल्यस्य. If we had onlyसपादसप्ताध्यायी, validity ofन योत्स्य इति cannot be explained.त्रिपादी provides that.

  

Since त्रिपादी provides options, one may not need sequential application of the aphorisms therein. So the परिभाषा - विप्रतिषेधे परं कार्यम् is the परिभाषा primarily ofसपादसप्ताध्यायी and not necessarily to be extended toत्रिपादी.

  

Maybe, which aphorism ofत्रिपादी is optional for which aphorism(s) ofसपादसप्ताध्यायी becomes a subject of study by itself.

  

Generally speaking,त्रिपादी may be seen as an annex toसपादसप्ताध्यायी, an annex on options. Since they are options, each aphorism ofत्रिपादी isof course पूर्वत्रासिद्धम्. Soपूर्वत्रासिद्धम् becomes the अधिकारसूत्रम् for the entire त्रिपादी, rather, the title ofत्रिपादी.
