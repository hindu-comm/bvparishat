Namaste

पृषोदरादीनि यथोपदिष्टम्। Under this the Mahabhashya has said the Sistas are Brahmanas कुंभीधान्याः and अलोलुपाःwith one pot of grains without greed and so onliving in Aryavarta. But they are not found today or if found they may be very small in number. We cannot take Sanskrit language to have basis in them today. Probably in ancient times they were available in Aryavarta but not now. If Panini's language = language of Sistas where are the Sistas today?
