तरप् is comparative and तमप् superlative. It can be used as per the विवक्षा। Sometimes, whom the comparison is with may not be stated explicitly. It could be anyone such as Rama gets more victory \[compared to any other person\], or in cases such as "may you grow stronger", with oneself.
