haTha (हठ ) as a word is currently in use in Oriya with the meaning of "moodiness". It is a noun.

The origin is not known to me.

A similar word is maTha (मठ), meaning "delay". It is also a noun.

maTha is used in Sanskrit.

Another word is s'aTha (शठ) meaning "crooked". It is an adjective.

s'aTha is used in Sanskrit.

  

Oriya has a good collection of two letter words ending in Tha. They are mostly nouns.
