दुरितं क्षयमायातु शान्तिर्भवतु शाश्वती ।

सर्वो लोक: सुखी भूयात् नवे संवत्सरे शुभे ।।

“May the evil go away and may there be permanent peace. May the entire world be happy in this shining New Year.”

  

Madhav Deshpande
