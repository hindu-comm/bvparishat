Though it is popularly believed that Satyavati, Vayasa's mother was a Sudrakanya, Mahabharata itself says that Satyavati was the daughter of a Kshatritya king Uparicharavasu born to an Apsara named Adrika.

  

You may find the following article by Acharya Sitaramdas Naiyayik useful.
