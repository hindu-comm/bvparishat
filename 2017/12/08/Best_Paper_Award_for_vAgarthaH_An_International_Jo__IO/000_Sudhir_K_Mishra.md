Any qualitative research paper/review paper in the areas of Veda, Purāṇa, Brāhmaṇa, Araṇyaka, Upaniṣad, Vyākaraṇa, Sāhitya, Bhāratiya Darśana, Nirukta, Chanda, Kalpa, Kāvya, Mahākāvya, Nāṭaka, Śikṣā etc. may be uploaded in prescribed format for review and publication purposes. The citation is counted from the respective website of journal available on the Google Scholar. The website link of Google Scholar of respective journal is available under Board Members heading.



You are requested to forward this information to your students and colleagues also. Research papers are invited for the forthcoming issue of the वागर्थः (An International Journal of Sanskrit Research) (ISSN: 2456-9186). You can upload the research papers on the following website-
