I have this entire set and strongly recommend anyone with serious interest in the taittiriiya corpus get this set. It's a fantastic reference set.

  

Since it has the bhAShya-s as well, it's a little difficult to use just for the pada pATha. The svara and some other markings also follow the Maharashtra style and not the Tamil style, which the kumbhaghoNam texts use.

  

Rama
