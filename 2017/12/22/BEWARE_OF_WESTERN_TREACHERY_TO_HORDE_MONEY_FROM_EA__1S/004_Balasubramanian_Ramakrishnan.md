I agree. Have we become so suspicious of everything that sweeping statements indicting "Western culture" are being bandied about? [archive.org](http://archive.org) is not taking anyone's rights away. The whole project began as an effort to make out-of-copyright books as widely available as possible. With the DLI initiative, I see some books with copyrights being uploaded, but I am assuming the readership for these texts are so low + the possibility of reprinting is near zero, that most authors are not going to see any monetary set-backs.
