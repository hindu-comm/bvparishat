(The details provided below existed prior to 1954 in Samskruth resources for over millennia, in the lexicons, Koshas and practices. How the author of publication miss this wealth of information is beyond my comprehension.) 



1. The term ‘ Chakra’ is a technical term from ‘Artha Shaastra’. One who is guarded by a team of people mentioned in Artha shaastra ( The Upaveda). ( Chakre varatate, chakram pravartayati vaa) . Contrast the definition ‘ Saamraat’. ; One who guards the Team of people and values for which Artha shaastra is aiming for, namely the ‘Raja –Dharma paripaalna as derived from Vedas /Sanatana Dharma’.



 What then is ‘chakra’ ? Multiple meanings ! From the simple concept of a wheel, circle to the exotic ‘ seven chakras’ to the technical definition in Artha-Shaastra.

 

2. The confusion seems to arise from taking ‘ Chakra-vartin’ to be the hyperbole expression for ‘ Emperor of the Universe, a glorified King of Kings’. A case to highlight the errors in understanding
Samskrutham !



 This again is inaccurate hyperbolic understanding of ‘Saarvabhauma’. The kings presence is felt in the total kingdom ( sarva –bhoomishu bhavaH) ; and here ‘ bhoomi’ is limited to the ‘ localized kingdom’; and not the entire Earth !



3. Responsibilities of a Chakravartin : Balanced implementation and guarding of ‘Santana Dharma in Desha /Rashtra’ – Prajaa sukhe sukham rajnah.

चक्रवर्त्ती¦, \[न्\] पुं, (चक्रं पद्माकारशुभचिह्नं करे वर्त्तते यस्य । वृत् + णिनिः । यद्वा चक्रं पृथ्वीचक्रं तेन वर्त्तते इति । वृत + णिनिः ।)

वास्तूकम् । इति राजनिर्घण्टः ॥ (श्रेष्ठः । यथा, गीतगोविन्दे । १ । २ ।

“चारणचक्रवर्त्ती नर्त्तकश्रेष्ठः ।” इति तट्टी- कायां चैतन्यदासः । यद्वा पद्मावती महालक्ष्मीः राधा तस्याश्चरणचारर्णे परिचर्य्यायां यच्चक्रं मण्डलं तत्र वर्त्तते इति व्युत्पत्त्या वैष्णवसम्प्रदाचिविशेषः ॥)
