dolo is in fact written as daloদলো and pronounced as dolo. Meaning of daloদলো is given as 'pull up' by Bengali to English google translator.

  

roktomaakhaa is written as raktamaakhaa রক্তমাখা . Meaning is given as 'bloodshed' by Bengali to English google translator.  

  

bajraana (vajraana?) বজ্রান is thunder.  

  

moner kothaa is written as manEra kathAমনের কথা . Meaning of manEra kathAমনের কথা is given as mind by Bengali to English google translator. Probably manEr is 'of mind' and kathA is content. Thus manEra kathAমনের কথা may mean 'content of mind'.
