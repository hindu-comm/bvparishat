Sri Vedanta Desikan in कामासिकाष्टकम् (२) refers to this upanishat:

  

तपनेन्द्वग्निनयन: तापानपचिनोतु न: ।

तापनीय रहस्यानां सार: कामासिकाहरि: ॥  

  

Sri Rangaramanuja Muni "Upanishad Bhashyakarar" also refers to the Narasimha-Tapaniya-Upanishat, but I will be able to check the precise details later.
