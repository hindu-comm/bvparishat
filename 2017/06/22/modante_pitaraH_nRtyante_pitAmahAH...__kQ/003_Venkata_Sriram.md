Namaste,  
  
A similar verse can be found in 71st sutra from Narada Bhakti Sutra :  
  
मोदन्ते पितरो नृत्यन्ति देवताः सनाथा चेयं भूर्भवति ॥७१॥  
  
With the presence of devotee, his ancestors rejoice, deities dance and the earth is endowed with a protector.
