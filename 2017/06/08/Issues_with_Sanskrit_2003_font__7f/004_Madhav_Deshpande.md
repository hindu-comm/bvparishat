Dear Colleagues,

  

  I had communicated issues with the font Sanskrit 2003 to Swami Satchidananda of Omkararashram, and to my dilight, he immediately responded and sent me an updated version of this font. I installed this font on my MacBook this morning, and, yes, it has taken care of the issue of the anusvāra merging with the sign for short "i" as you can see in this sample:

  

![Inline image 1](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/112920534c320/image.png?part=0.0.1&view=1)  

This can be compared to the previous sample with the older version of the font:

  

![Inline image 2](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/112920534c320/image.png?part=0.0.2&view=1)  

  

I am grateful to Swamiji for helping me out. For the benefit of those who are using this font, I have attached the updated font file. With best wishes,
