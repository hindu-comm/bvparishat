What is the earliest source like inscription/text using the name Sanskrit as a language? From which century the name Sanskrit came into use.

This is an inscription of Dharasena II of Maitraka dynasty of Vallabhiand is dated 5-6th century.It says the king Guhasena excelled in works in the three Languages of Sanskrit, Prakrit and Apabhramsha.

The information is taken from The Indian Antiquary ,Vol 10.
