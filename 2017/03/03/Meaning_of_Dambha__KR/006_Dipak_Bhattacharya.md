Dear Sir,

*ḍambha* is a loanword, and hence apparently, of the avyutpanna category. PIE had no retroflex. Burrow (95ff) extensively deals with the retroflexes under the heading ‘THE CEREBRALS’. They are original to the Dravidic languages. Both OIA and Austric developed them under Dravidic influence.

Best

DB
