'what are the reasons for the two recensions 'कृष्ण Kṛṣṇa' and 'शुक्ल Śhukla' in Yajurveda?'  

The traditional interpretation is ;sukla means unmixed, pure, while krsna means mixed. The Vajasaneya Samhita is metrical ie composed in unmixed poetry while prose and poetry are both found in the Taittiriya S, etc.
