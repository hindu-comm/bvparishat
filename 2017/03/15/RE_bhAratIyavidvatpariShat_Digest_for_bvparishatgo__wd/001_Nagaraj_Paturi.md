                    ========================= Moderation Note =======================

  

To all members,

  

This post in this thread was sent as a reply to a digest type mail delivered to the member. Pressing the 'reply' button from a digest type mail delivery creates this kind of a post which is to be avoided

  

1\. to avoid an unnecessarily huge post and save space

  

2\. to avoid a thread without a subject line detailing the purpose and content of the post.

  

This could have happened because some members receive such digest or abridged type mail delivery and do not know how to reply to a specific post. We thought that it would be easy for such members to do the same, if they receive each mail separately.

  

As such, we changed the mail delivery settings for all the members to "all mails".

  

Some members seem to have got inconvenienced by this change.

  

We request those members who do not want mails to be delivered to them post by post, can change these settings from their side by going to <https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/bvparishat>,
