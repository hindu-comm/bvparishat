A beautiful verse on the occasion of women's day...  
पादसंवाहने वज्री  
केशसंमार्जने फणिः ।  
अहो भाग्यं पुरन्ध्रीणां  
दधिसंमथने रविः ।।  
(from Subhashitaratnabhandagara)  
  
Harshada Sawarkar  
University of Mumbai.
