**Sanātana Dharma Parichayam**  
*Talks in English by Swami Sharadananda Sarasvati, Acharya CIF*

  

The Series introduces the scheme of life that forms Sanātana-dharma. With a background of Sanskrit, Advaita-vedānta, Śaivism, and Yoga,Swami Sharadananda, spans the traditions of Bhārata in the true sense. Through his upbringing in Kerala, his study of Yoga in Bihar, Advaita through the Gurukula in Sandeepany Sadhanalaya, Swamiji breathes life into the tradition with meaning, symbolism, the underlying philosophy and historical perspective. These talks would be of great value to the contemporary youth, as well as those who seek the deeper understanding of current practices.

**  
**

**Topics**

Scriptures; Sanskrit Language; Concept of God; Concept of Avatāra;Ṛṣis and Guru-paramparā; Theories of Creation; Social Setup; Five Basic Duties;Ṣoḍaśa-saṁskāra(16 Sacraments); The Cycle of Birth and Death.

**  
**

**About the Speaker:  
**Swami Sharadananda Sarasvati, an Acharya at CIF, is a polyglot with expertise in Sanskrit, Indian Philosophy (Yoga, Advaita) with in-depth understanding of Ritual and Cultural Traditions. Swamiji combines the best of Gurukula learning as well as academic excellence with post graduate degree in Advaita Vedānta.
