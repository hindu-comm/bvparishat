The Shloka is uttered by the groom generally after Panigrahana or Saptapadi.

अमूहमस्मि सा त्वं द्यौरहं पृथिवी त्वं सामाहमृक्त्वं तावेहि संभवाव सह रेतो दधावहै पुंसे पुत्राय वेत्तवै रायस्पोषाय सुप्रजास्त्वाय सुवीर्याय ॥

 --- Hiranyakeshi Grihya Sutra 1.20.2

(Other versions in Taittariya Brahmana 3.7.19

Apastamba Mantra Patha 1.3.14)



*I am this(God) You are that (Goddess)*

*I am the heaven You are the earth*

*I am the Song You are the words*

*Let us get together. Let us share the sperm to beget a male child for wealth, offspring and strength.*
