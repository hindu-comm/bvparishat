1\. In the Taittiriya Uppanishat, there is a vakya स्वाध्यायप्रवचने एवेति नाको मौद्गल्यः.

2\. In the Baudhayana brahmayajna(tarpana), Baudhayana is designated as प्रवचनकार( in contrast with सूत्रकार). This seems to be based on the प्रवर section of श्रौतसूत्र.
