The only scholarly essays of the type that Mishraji is looking for (AFAIK) are by Prof.KV Sharma.

See his:

1.Critical Text editing in ancient India ( J. Sanskrit Academy, Usmania Univ 1979)

2.Canons of textual criticism in Medieval India (J.Dayananda vidyapeetha 1988)

  

These are available in his collected works brought out by SSES, Chennai.
