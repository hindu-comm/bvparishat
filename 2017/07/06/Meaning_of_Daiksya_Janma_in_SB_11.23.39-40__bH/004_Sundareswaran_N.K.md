Manusmrti has this verse:

मातुरग्रेधिजननं द्वितीयं मौञ्जिबन्धने।

तृतीयं यज्ञदीक्षायां द्बिजस्य श्रुतिचोदनात्।।

  

The Aitareyabrahmana has this statement:

पुनर्वा एतमृत्विजो गर्भं कुर्वन्ति यं दीक्षयन्ति।। (1.1.3.1)

  

  

The Brahmana texts explicitly state that the यज्ञदीक्षा is the गर्भवास for the दीक्षित.

 

  

For further details one may see the concluding portion of my paper '***The Upanayana ritual in the Satapathabrahmana***'    
<https://www.academia.edu/13697394/The_upanayana_ritual_in_the_Satapathabrahmana>
