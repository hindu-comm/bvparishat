Hare Krishna.  
  
Respected Vidvajjanas,  
I also wanted to know the status of Bharadvaja Samhita. I am not able  
to understand the position of Narada Pancaratra. Many samhitas are  
considered its part -- jnanamrtasara, Bharadvaja Samhita, etc. I am  
not able to understand are these different parts of one Narada  
Pancaratra or different set of instructions given to different sages  
under the same name. Is there something Narada Pancaratra original  
without any sub name as jnanamrtasara, etc.?  
  
In different samhitas considered part of Narada Pancaratra, similar  
instructions are repeated, like diksa procedure.  
  
Thankyou,  
damodara das
