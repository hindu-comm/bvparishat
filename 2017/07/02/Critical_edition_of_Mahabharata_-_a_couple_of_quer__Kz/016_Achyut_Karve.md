[[Achyut Karve	2017-07-04, 12:16:23 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/Kziie9QogX4)]]



Can an oral tradition carry the name of the composer unless it is a part of the composition itself?

I am a student of the tabla whose compositions have come down by an oral tradition which are not more than two hundred years old and yet one cannot find the name of the composer. The same is true of compositions of classical music. Is it then not possible that the names of the rishis merely show the classification of the hyms and not the name of the author?
