Thought I should share a happy experience.

  

Once I had visited Raghavendra Swami temple in Bangalore near my house. After the evening aarati and prasad I was about to leave when I saw a priest bring out a pair of tablas. Soon another priest appeared with a flute. Everyone was treated to a lovely recital of a raga (hindustani) for about half hour. On the way back home I felt delight - and a tinge of pride - to belong to this our Vedic civilization.

  

  

regards   



Shrikant Jamadagni  
Bengaluru
