ऋत has several meanings. One of them is truth.

  

अनृत means lie, falsehood etc.

  

ऋत in its origin refers to one of the pivotal, highest, vital, central ideas of Vedic thought.  

  

The meaning truth forऋत is secondary and is a derivative of the above mentioned deeper meaning in the Vedic texts.
