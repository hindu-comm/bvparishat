In the medieval period, Bhedābheda Vedānta became closely associated with theism in general, and the movement of*bhakti*devotionalism in particular. There is a reason that*bhaktas*such as Vallabha (1479-1531 CE) and Caitanya (1485-1533) built the foundations of their theological systems on centuries-old Bhedābheda concepts.
