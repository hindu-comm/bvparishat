The book was written to show that other teachers besides Shankara have equal status as teachers of Vedanta. This book is a presentation of the lives and philosophies of Ramanuja Nimbarka Madhva Vallabha and Caitanya.  

  

The book does not have any chapter on Bhedaabheda.

  

It does have a chapter on Achintya Bhedaabheda.
