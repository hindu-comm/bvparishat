[[Sivasenani Nori	2017-07-26, 18:17:30 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/2iZx4r6ZsW8)]]



In Advaita, entire Veda is an upAya to attain Brahman. cf. यत्र वेदा अवेदाः - बृहदारण्यकोपनिषत्. By extension all sorts of Brahmavidyas are upAyas.

  

Similarly in Vyakarana-darsana, Bhartrhari says the same thing, after describing Brahman's lakshanas: प्राप्त्युपायोऽनुकारश्च तस्य वेदः (Brahmakanda)
