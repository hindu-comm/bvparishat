There is a whole area with many bookstores near Appa Balvant Chowk, and some of them carry Sanskrit books. TryVasudev Mahadev Godbole Book Sellers. They used to carry a lot of old Nirnaya Sagara Publications.

Near the Appa Balvant Chowk is also the famous Anandashrama, where you can buy books published in the Anandashrama Sanskrit Series.

The other places in Pune where one can buy Sanskrit books are the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute and the Vaidika Sanshodhan Mandal. They sell their own publications. The Deccan College sells its own publications.
