Respected Sirs,

Request your permission to ask this question:

  

There is this shloka in गुरुपादुका स्तॊत्रम्

  

नृपालिमौलि व्रजरत्नकांति

सरिद्विरज झ्षकन्यकाभ्याम् \|

नृपत्वदाभ्याम् नतलॊकपंक्तॆः

नमॊ नमः श्री गुरुपादुकाभ्याम् \|\|

  

My question is, what exactly is the sense in which the author compares गुरुपादुका with सरिद्विराज झ्षकन्यका ?

I understand that सरिद्विराज झ्षकन्यका means "mermaids who are glowing in a river".

So, how does गुरुपादुका compare with सरिद्विराज झ्षकन्यका ?

  

As far as I can see, नृपालिमौलि व्रजरत्नकांति is an entirely different उपमा and it can not, I think, have any influence on the next उपमा

So, if सरिद्विराज झ्षकन्यका is a stand alone उपमा, how does it really compare with गुरुपादुका ?

  

Many thanks for your time and patience.

  

\- Srinivas
