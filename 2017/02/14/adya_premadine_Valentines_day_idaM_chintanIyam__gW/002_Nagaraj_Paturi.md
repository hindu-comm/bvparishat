Valentine's day is a celebration of *their own mutual love* (celebrated) by the *lovers*.

  

In contrast, Vasantotsava / Kaamana hunnavi / Kaamuni Punnami / Holi is a worship /celebration of (resurrection of burnt down) *love in general* by *all humans*.
