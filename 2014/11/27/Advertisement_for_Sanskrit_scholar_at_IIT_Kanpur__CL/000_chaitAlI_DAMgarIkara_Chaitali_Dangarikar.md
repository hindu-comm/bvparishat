Advertisement for Sanskrit scholar  
  
Applications are invited for the post of Senior Project Scientist (one) for a period of one year (extendable up to four years), in the institute sponsored project entitled “Scientific study of Indian knowledge systems” (A SandHI project,[*http://iitk.ac.in/sandhi/*](http://iitk.ac.in/sandhi/)). The details of the position are given below.  
  
The interested candidates are requested submit their curriculum vitae containing informationregarding their degree, statement of purpose, short-term research proposals and work experience on plain paper or by email. The applications should reach Dr. Anurag Gupta, Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Kanpur – 208016 by 15^(th) December 2014. Applicants who do not meet the minimum qualification and the work experience will not be called for the interview. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview. The post is purely temporary and will be offered for a period of one year and renewal on satisfactory progress.  
  
Position:  
Senior Project Scientist  
Salary: 27000-2700-54000  
  
Eligibility:  
M.A. (Sanskrit) + 2 years of experience OR PhD (Sanskrit).  
  
Job Profile:  
The candidate must be able to take up translation and interpretation of indigenous scientific texts in Sanskrit and develop modular courses based on these texts. The candidate is expected to interact with the expert faculties of science and technology in IIT Kanpur to develop research projects related to scientific traditions and sustainable technology in ancient India.  
  
Desired:  
Strong Sanskrit background and knowledge of scientific works in Sanskrit. Candidates having working knowledge in (at least) one of the following areas will be preferred: Astronomy, Mathematics, Geometry, Architecture, Physical sciences, Material Science, Music, Archeology.  
  
Last date for applying: 15^(th) December 2014
