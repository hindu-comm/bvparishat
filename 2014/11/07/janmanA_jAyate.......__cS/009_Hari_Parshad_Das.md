śāṇīprāyāṇi vastrāṇi  
śamīprāyā mahīruhāḥ  
śūdraprāyās tathā varṇā  
bhaviṣyanti kalau yuge (Viṣṇupurāṇa 6.1.54)  
  

ity ādi vākyena kali-yuga-prasaṅge 'janmanā jāyate śūdraḥ' iti yuktaḥ bhavati \|

  
  
sādhu-caraṇa-rajo 'bhilāṣī,  
  
hari pārṣada dāsa
