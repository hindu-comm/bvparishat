While welcoming the samasyaa, may I be permitted to say that the present samasyaa has a metrical flaw as the saMyuktaakShara "vya" in the word vyathitaM changes letter "ta" in the word "hanta' in to a guru which is not desirable in the given metre vasanta-tilaka. Hence may it be changed to ruditaM or dalitaM or galitaM?....
