Namaste

  

Has Bhatta Bhaskara Misra commented on the following Mantra (it is numbered 4.42.9 in the commentary by Sayana on Krsna Yajurveda Taittiriya Aranyaka) - चतुर्थे प्रपाठके द्विचत्वारिंशे अनुवाके नवमो मन्त्रः –

इन्द्र॑स्य गृ॒हो॑सि॒ तं त्वा॒ प्रप॑द्ये॒ सगु॒ साश्व॑ स॒ह यन्मे॒ अस्ति॒ तेन॑

इन्द्रस्य गृहोसि तं त्वा प्रपद्ये सगुः साश्वः सह यन्मे अस्ति तेन

  

I looked at the first volume of publication (1900: Government of Mahraja of Mysore, edited by A Mahadeva Sastri and K Rangacharya), there the commentary on the fourth Prasna ends with the twelfth Anuvaka.
