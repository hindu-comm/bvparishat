“करः” इति प्रथमान्तपाठो मया नोपलब्धम्। “करम्” इति द्वितीयान्तपाठः सार्वत्रिकः।

Is there an edition or manuscript with the reading “करः”?

(1) hitpadesa01 – Dual commentary by Nyayacarya Srikrsnavallabhacarya. Chaukhambha Sanskrit Series Office. Varanasi. 1991. ISBN 81-7080-032-3

(2) hitopadesa02 – Edited with notes by Friedrich Max Müller. Longman, Green, Longman, Roberts, & Green. London. 1864.

(3) hitopadesa03 – Edited by Vasudevacharya Ainapure. Gopal Narayen & Company. Bombay. 1908.

(4) hitopadesa04 – Edited by Sitarama Sastri. Dual commentary by Guruprasada Sastri. Bhargava Pustakalaya. Varanasai. 1955. Prefers the reading कृष्णयते and an alternate reading in footnote as कृष्णायते.

कृष्णयते can be explained as कृष्णं कुरुते (commentator says मलिनीकुरुते). णिच् by तत्करोति तदाचष्टे. आत्मनेपद by णिचश्च, but the कर्तृगामित्व of फल still seems doubtful here.
