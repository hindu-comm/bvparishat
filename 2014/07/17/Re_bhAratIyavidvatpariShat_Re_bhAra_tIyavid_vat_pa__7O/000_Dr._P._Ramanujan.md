The mantra विष्णो त्वं नो अन्तमश्शर्म यच्छ .. occurs in TS 3.1.10 and is commented by both

Sayana and Bhattabhaskara. The last shanti mantra आभिर्गीर्भिः ... also is commented upon by both.

It occurs in 4.42 in Sayana and 7.42 last mantra in Bhattabhaskara.



Ramanujan
