The Nori Narasimha Sastry Charitable Trust is pleased to announce the felicitation to Br. Ve. Prof. Korada Subrahmanyam garu with the Vedavyasa Puraskaramu on 13th July 2014, Sunday at Tyagaraya Ganasabha, Chikkadpally, Hyderabad starting at 6:00 pm on the occasion of Gurupurnima. Sri Abhinavoddanda Srividyanrisimha Bharati Swami, head of the Pushpagiri Pitham would make the anugrahabhashanam. All are welcome.
