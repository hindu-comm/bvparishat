**The Sanskrit Academy is inviting research articles for the JOURNAL of SANSKRIT ACADEMY - vol-24' 2014, from eminent scholars. Please send your articles on or before 30th of September.**



One of the objectives of Sanskrit Academy is to encourage research scholars to publish the results of their research in the form of a paper, whether in Sanskrit or English or Hindi. Accordingly, contributions from scholars working in different fields like literature, grammar, philosophy, various sciences. Veda, Vedanta and so on could be highly welcome. However, good and original small Sanskrit creative writings in the form of poetry, prose, stories etc., equally are welcome.

Our Journal has three parts

1. Articles from Eminent scholars.

2. Kriti Parichaya

3. Kriti Sameeksha (Book Review)

****

3. Papers must be submitted in the digital form. Digital text can be sent through mail[jsae...@gmail.com](http://mc/compose?to=jsaeditor@gmail.com)or in CD.
