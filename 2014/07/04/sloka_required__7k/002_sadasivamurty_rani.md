There is a context in the Hitopadesa where the following sloka is found which emphasizes the superiority of Buddhi bala over Dehabala. Of course in this sloka no where any reference to Atma bala is made.

बुद्ध्र्यस्य बलं तस्य निर्बुद्धेस्तु कुतो बलम्।

पश्य सिंहो मदोन्मत्तश्शशकेन निपातित:॥

Warm regards,

**Dr. Rani Sadasiva Murty**
