नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

Marriages between trees - tree and bride -- प्रदक्षिणम् of tree -- marriage of frogs ...Vidvan Raghavendrabhattah --

  

In Andhra the custom is there --

  

1\. for children , marriage between अश्वत्थ and निम्ब - sometimes both the trees are seen intertwined and if not the case bring a निम्बवृक्षक (अल्पार्थे कन्) to an अश्वत्थ ।

  

2\. for children - प्रदक्षिणम् of अश्वत्थ

  

3.Second husband died - get the bride married with a कदळीवृक्ष and then with a bridegroom - I witnessed this 50years ago . My father , a घनपाठी and वैयाकरण with good knowledge in धर्मशास्त्रम् was supervising this . There may be sanction in निर्णयसिन्धु / धर्मसिन्धु etc.

  

4.For rains marriage of Frogs is performed and the couple is taken around the village. I witnessed this - there was good rain.
