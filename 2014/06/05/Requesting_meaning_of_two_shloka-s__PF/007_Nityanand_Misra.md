प्रामाणिकवचनं गृहाण।

By the सूत्र –

न ङिसम्बुद्ध्योः (पा॰सू॰ ८.२.८)

नलोप does not happen in case of सम्बुद्धि. However, by the वार्त्तिक –

वा नपुंसकानाम् (वा॰ ८.२.८)

नलोप can happen optionally in vocative case for neuter gender. Therefore –

हे चर्म हे चर्मन् हे वर्म हे वर्मन्

(examples cited by पतञ्जलि).

See page 99 of the eighth book of the महाभाष्य (volume sixth of six, Chaukhambha Sanskrit Pratisthan, 1988 reprint).

[*https://archive.org/stream/TheMahabhashyaOrGreatCommentaryOnPaniniVolume6/Mahabhashya_VI#page/n371/mode/1up*](https://archive.org/stream/TheMahabhashyaOrGreatCommentaryOnPaniniVolume6/Mahabhashya_VI#page/n371/mode/1up)

The LSK gives the वार्त्तिक as

सम्बुद्धौ नपुंसकानां नलोपो वा वाच्यः।

This is not present in some editions of the LSK, but the LSK with the भैमी commentary, page 566 of the first edition (1950) includes the वार्त्तिक followed by the example –

हे दण्डि हे दण्डिन्

So अहन्धनि as vocative is valid only if the base अहन्धनिन् is taken in the neuter gender, which is highly unlikely in this context (though not impossible).
