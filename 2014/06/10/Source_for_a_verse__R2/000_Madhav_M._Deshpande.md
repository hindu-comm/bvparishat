Dear BVP members,

  

  Can someone point to me the source of the following verse:

  

दृप्यद्द्राविडदुर्ग्रहग्रहवशान्म्लिष्टं गुरुद्रोहिणा

यन्म्लेच्छेति वचोऽ विचिन्त्य सदसि प्रौढेऽपि भट्टोजिना ।

तत्सत्यापितमेव धैर्यनिधिना यत्स व्यमृद्नात्कुचं

निर्बध्यास्य मनोरमामवशयन्नप्यप्पयाद्यान् स्थितान् ।।

There is one book that says that this verse occurs in the commentary Uddyota by Nāgeśabhaṭṭa on the Pradīpa of Govindaṭhakkura on the Kāvyaprakāśa. I went through the Anandashrama edition of this work, but could not locate this verse. I would appreciate if anyone knows the specific source (with bibliographic details, please). Thanks.
