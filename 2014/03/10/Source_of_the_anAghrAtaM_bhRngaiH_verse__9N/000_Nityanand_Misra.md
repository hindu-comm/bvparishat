Namaste  
  
I am tracing the source of a verse, likely from the Ānandavṛndāvanacampū of Mahākavi Karṇapūra (or some other Gaudiya Vaishnava work). I could not find a Sanskrit original copy of the Ānandavṛndāvanacampū. Does anybody know where this verse is from?  
  
  

अनाघ्रातं भृङ्गैरनपहृतसौरभ्यमनिलैरनुत्पन्नं नीरेष्वनुपहतमूर्मीभरकणैः

अदृष्टं केनापि क्वचन च चिदानन्दसरसो यशोदायाः क्रोडे कुवलयमिवौजस्तदभवत्

anāghrātaṁ bhṛṅgairanapahṛtasaurabhyamanilairanutpannaṁ nīreṣvanupahatamūrmībharakaṇaiḥ

adṛṣṭaṁ kenāpi kvacana ca cidānandasaraso yaśodāyāḥ kroḍe kuvalayamivaujastadabhavat

  
Thanks, Nityanand
