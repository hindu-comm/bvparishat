Namaste

I have this reading of a verse from Bālarāmāyaṇam in a work I am currently editing –



अत्रासीत्फणिपाशबन्धनविधिः शक्त्या भवद्देवरे

गाढं वक्षसि ताडिते हनुमता द्रोणाद्रिरत्राहृतः।

दिव्यैरिन्द्रजिदत्र लक्ष्मणशरैर्लोकान्तरं प्रापितः

केनाप्यत्र मृगाक्षि राक्षसपतेः कृत्ता च कण्ठाटवी॥



atrāsītphaṇipāśabandhanavidhiḥ śaktyā bhavaddevare

gāḍhaṁ vakṣasi tāḍite hanumatā droṇādriratrāhṛtaḥ

divyairindrajidatra lakṣmaṇaśarairlokāntaraṁ prāpitaḥ

kenāpyatra mṛgākṣi rākṣasapateḥ kṛttā ca kaṇṭhāṭavī



I checked this against a published version. The book is available on DLI (Barcode 1999990073488), edited by Paṇḍita Govindadeva Śāstrī amd published by Medical Hall Press, Benares in 1869. On page 285 (PDF page 289) of the work, I found this reading (attached image)



The published reading has three variations – (1) द्रोणाद्रिरत्राहूतः instead of द्रोणाद्रिरत्राहृतः (2) लम्भितः instead of प्रापितः and (3) कोनाप्यत्र in place of केनाप्यत्र.  

  

Variation (1) violates the metre and variation (3) does not make sense – they are almost certainly mistakes in the book. Variation (2) does not make any difference metrically or in the meaning.



Can somebody confirm readings from any other publications?
