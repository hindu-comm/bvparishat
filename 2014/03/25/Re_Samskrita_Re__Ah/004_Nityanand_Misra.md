As per Amarakosa याम and प्रहर are synonyms, both used to refer to the eighth part of a day (3 hours).  
  
Some sources give the names of eight यामs as -  
पूर्वाह्ण, मध्याह्न, अपरा ह्ण, सायम्, प्रदोष, निशीथ, त्रियामा, उषा,  
Some others give the names as -  
निशान्त, प्रातः, पूर्वार्ध/पूर्वाह्ण, मध्याह्न, अपराह्ण, सायम्, प्रदोष, रात्रि/नक्तम्  
  
These names are found in the अष्टयाम works in Braja and Awadhi from the middle ages. Maybe some work on Jyautisa will have these names listed.
