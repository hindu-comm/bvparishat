A two week summer school will be organised in Chinmaya International Foundation Shodh Sansthan, Ernakulam Kerala from July 15th to 31st 2014, under the joint collaboration of Manipal University and CIFSS. Apart from philosophy, different facets of Indian art forms will be demonstrated. Prof. R. Ganesh, Prof. K.G Paulos, Prince Rama Varma and many other eminent experts are invited.
