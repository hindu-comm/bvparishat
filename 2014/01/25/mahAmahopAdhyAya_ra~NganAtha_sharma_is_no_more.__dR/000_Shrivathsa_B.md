Eminent Sanskrit and Kannada Scholar Maha mahopadhyaaya Nadahalli Ranganatha Sharma is no more !

  

Sri Sharma is a brilliant grammarian, true vedantin and wonderful human being.

  
Poojaneeya Ranganatha Sharma’s Profile: Born to an agricultural family at Nadahalli village in Soraba (taluk) of Shimoga (district) on 7.4.1916,  
He began his career at Chamarajendra Sanskrit College in Bangalore, where he served for nearly 40 years.  
A prolific writer, Dr. Ranganatha Sharma was credited with over80 works, including Kannada translation of Sanskrit version of Valmiki Ramayana and other Sanskrit works on Vishnu Purana, Srimad Bhagavatha, Amarakosha, Bhartruhari etc.  
  
Among the many awards and prizes he has won include Best Teacher Award both at the State and National level, DVG Memorial Award, Tirupati Sanskrit University Award and Hon. Doctorate from Karnataka Sanskrit University, Bangalore.

  

Deepest condolences to his family members and salutations to his noble soul.
