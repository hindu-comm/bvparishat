Just as there is in most Indian languages the phenomenon of using words inherited from Skt, there is the phenomenon of coining a Skt term for a new concept followed by its gradual spread into other Indian languages. Bengali, especially since the time of Rabindranath Tagore, has been especially influential in this area. Now the transmission probably takes place more through Hindi than directly from Bengali.  
  
;sobhaa is more likely to have been added to yaatra/jaatraa to convey the sense of 'beauty' or ''auspiciousness' that is an element of marriage processions, celebratory communal walks etc.
