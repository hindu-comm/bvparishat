[[sunil bhattacharjya	2014-08-21, 22:35:53 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/Br3WMtXsK0g)]]



This, to my knowledge, was composed by Madhusudana Saraswati about 500 years ago, in which he had incorporated the following verse from the Bhishma Parva, where Vaishampayana says as follows:

  
  
गीता सु‍गीता कर्तव्या किम् अन्यैः शास्त्रविस्तरैः।  
या स्वयं पद्मनाभस्य मुखपद्माद् विनिःशृता॥
