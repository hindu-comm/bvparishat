क्रतुशब्दस्य श्रौतयज्ञ इत्यर्थ उक्तः,यज्ञशब्दस्य स्मार्तयज्ञ इतिच



This is from S'ankara bhaashya.



हिंसाविशिष्टो यज्ञः क्रतुरिति क्वापि श्रुतम्



Obviously हिंसाविशिष्टो यज्ञः क्रतुः is not श्रुतम् from S'ankara.



Thus to combine क्रतुशब्दस्य श्रौतयज्ञ इत्यर्थ उक्तः,यज्ञशब्दस्य स्मार्तयज्ञ इतिचand हिंसाविशिष्टो यज्ञः क्रतुरिति is paradigm-mixing.



The doubt raised here is pertinent because quite often क्रतुःand यज्ञः are used synonymously. 



हिंसाविशिष्टो यज्ञः क्रतुरिति makesक्रतुः a subcategory under the broader category यज्ञः.



But S'ankara'ssentences indicate two different words with the same form यज्ञः. : 1. The broad category 2. Subcategory The uttarapada in his श्रौतयज्ञ and स्मार्तयज्ञ is the broad category word. The यज्ञः in the ninth chapter of The Giitaa is being taken as thesubcategory word.



क्रतुः is a subcategory under the broad category यज्ञः. and is qualified by being श्रौत. The subcategory यज्ञः differs from क्रतुः inbeing स्मार्त that is स्मृतिचोदित . स्मृति is also शृतिचोदित only. But the details ofस्मार्तयज्ञ are found in स्मृति.The details of श्रौतयज्ञ come from the शृति and/or from the memory of the श्रौति.
