It is not even Panganad Ganapati Sastri, but *PAINGANADU* Ganapati Sastri.  
If I am correct, Painganad Ganapati Sastri served also as the president of the Advaita Sabha. Painganad is a village near Mannargudi, in Thanjavur district.  
Many great Vedic scholars lived in that village even upto the mid 20th century.
