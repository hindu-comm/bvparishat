Obviously suśrutasahitā should be the source for mādhavanidānasamhitā for this verse.



I found this word anātmavān for ajitēndriyah a wonderful and interesting term.



I was curious to see where else does suśruta use this word. At least in chikitsāsthāna itselfI found it in two more places:



**सुश्रु४.३.३/ अल्पाशिनो+अनात्मवतो जन्तोर्वातात्मकस्य च/
