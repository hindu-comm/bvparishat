Giving below list of a few Sanskrit works related to freedom struggle and biographies of freedom fighters.

  

Bharata Swatantrya Sangrametihaasah - RS Shukla 1981  
  
Biographies of Freedom fighters  
Tilaka Yasornava in 3 parts by M S Aney 1969  
Lokamanya Tilaka Charitam - KW Chitale 1956  
Lokamanya Charitam - Vasa Suryanarayana Sastri 1970  
Vinayaka Viragatha (biography of Vira Savarkar) - G B Palsule 1966  
Dhanyoham Dhanyoham (Sanskrit play on Savarkar) - GB Palsule 1972  
  
Sanskrit plays of Jatindra Bimal Chaudhuri on freedom fighters  
Bharata Hridayaravindam (on Aurobindo Ghosh)  
Bhaskarodayam (on Tagore)  
  
There are several Sanskrit works on Gandhiji & Nehru.
