Actually सलील is a valid Samskrita word different from सलिल.

Please check Monier-Williams and other dictionaries. Here is the entry from M-W.

This is famously used as an adverb in उल्लङ्घ्य सिन्धोः सलिलं सलीलं .... in the आनन्दरामायण’s हनुमत्कवच. The verse has been sung in his incomparable divine voice by Hindustani musician Pandit Jasraj Ji, a version of which can be heard here –
