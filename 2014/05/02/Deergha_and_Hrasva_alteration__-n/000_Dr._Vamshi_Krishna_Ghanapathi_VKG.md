As I observing the distortions/ alterations of Samskruta words in Marathi, I also want to put this submission in the domain of scholars.

  

In Marathi, Deerghas are shortened and vice versa Hrasvas are lengthened.

  

For example: The word Salila becomes Saleela.

  

Any anecdotes from Marathi speaking scholars is appreciated.

  
With Warm Regards
