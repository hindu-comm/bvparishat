An update:

  

The PhD dissertation of Sri R. Muralikrishna Srowtigal of Chennai has a very elaborate discussion on

"ऊहरहस्यगानयोः पौरुषेयापोरुषेयत्वविमर्शः". He has stated the position of the Meemamsakas as quoted in

my previous post. In addition he says "अपोरुषेय इति निदानकाराः। ...... निदानकाराश्च पतञ्जलिमहर्षयः। यैश्च सामवेदीयेषु

दशसु सूत्रग्रन्थेषु अन्यतमं तृतीयं निदानसूत्रं विरचितम्। तत्र द्वितीये प्रपाठकेऊहापौरुषेयत्वविषयः गूढं विमृष्टः। "

  

Sri Muralikrishna Srowtigal cites this relevant text from theनिदानसूत्रंand then appends a 14 page extract on this

topic by Sri M. Ramanatha Deekshitar, a former Head of the Theology, Sanskrit Mahavidyalaya, Benares Hindu University and a renowned Sama Veda scholar of the 20th century).

**

**Their conclusion that** **the उहम् and रह्सयम् are अपौरुषेयम्**
