It is a theory for the satisfation of logic and mental inquires to consider the comparison of macro and micro cosmos from the level of Vyavahara, But in paramarthatha none of these is valid and exist. As the Bhashya says it is a Abutha Abhinivesham. Please refer to M. Karika quoted below:

  

## प्रपञ्चो यदि विद्येत निवर्तेत न संशयः \| मायामात्रमिदं द्वैतमद्वैतं परमार्थतः \|\|१७\|\| विकल्पो विनिवर्तेत कल्पितो यदि केनचित् \| उपदेशादयं वादो ज्ञाते द्वैतं न विद्यते \|\|१८\|\|
