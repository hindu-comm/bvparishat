First of all thread initiator's context needs to be known. That Samyoga is a kind of Sannikarsha holds good for all the contexts.



Ayurvedik Padartha Vijnana literature particularly the Pratyaksha PramaaNa section of Dravya Roga Vinis'chaya draws mostly from Nyaya-Vais'Eshika .



The following is a compilation of various indriyaartha sannikarsha types in a various schools:



**Types of Indriyaarthasannikarsha in various Vaidika Darshanas:**

1. NYAAYA-VAISHESHHIKA:  
  
a) samyoga

b) samyukta-samavAya

.c) samyukta-samaveta-samavAya

d) samavAya

e) samaveta-samavAya

 f) visheShaNatAbhAva



2. SAANKHYA-YOGA:  
  
a) samyoga

b) samyukta-tAdAtmya

c) samyukta-tAdAtmya-tAdAtmya

d) tAdAtmya

e) tAdAtmya-tAdAtmya

3. THE PRABHAAKARA SCHOOL OF MIIMAAMSAA:  
a) samyoga

b) samyukta-samavAya

c) samavAya

4. THE KUMAARILA SCHOOL OF MIIMAAMSAA (smarta):  
a) samyoga

b) samyukta-samavAya

c) samyukta-samaveta-samavAya

5. SHANKARA (ADVAITA) VEDAANTA:  
  
a) samyoga  
b) samyukta-tAdAtmya  
c) samyuktAbinna-tAdAtmya  
d) tAdAtmya  
e) tAdAtmyavadabinnatva  
f) visheShya-visheShaNa bhAva

6. RAAMAANUJA (VISHISHHT.AADVAITA) VEDAANTA:  
a) samyoga  
b) samyuktAshrayaNa:

7. MADHVA (DVAITA) VEDAANTA:  
 samyoga alone



It may be noted that Samyoga is included in all the systems as a type of Sannikarsha.
