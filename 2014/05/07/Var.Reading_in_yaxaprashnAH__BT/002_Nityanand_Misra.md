You may refer Upasargārthacandrikā by Cārudeva Śāstrī for possible meanings of आ√वप् and नि√वप् and also आ√पत् and नि√पत्.

“Offering to manes” does not make sense here.

Gita Press edition takes the आ√वप् नि√वप् reading and Rāmanārāyaṇa Datta Śāstrī Pāṇḍeya gives the meaning –

"Rain is best for those who farm, seed is best for those who sow, …”
