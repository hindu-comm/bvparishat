The problem thus is with the rule of composition, and the order is important.  

स्वर - अनुनासिक - स्वरचिह्न is the correct order.  
  

We had put the स्वरचिह्न on the स्वर first and then we added the अनुनासिक.  

But अनुनासिक-अच् is one unit, and hence first we should write these two together and then the स्वरचिह्न follows.  

This makes sense.
