नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

’सार्वत्रिकाः’ - can be a term for Universals -- सर्वत्र भवाः - ठञ् - ’ अध्यात्मादेष्ठञिष्यते (वार्तिकम्) - अध्यात्मादिः आकृतिगणः।

  

The तद्धितविभक्ति , त्रल् is in अधिकरणार्थ - सर्वत्र is an अव्ययम् - it can be भाषा also .

So these norms are applicable to language in general .

  

My effort is for telling the students that पाणिनीयम्  deals with Universals first and then the structure of Sanskrit.

This will be encouraging to students of other languages to learn Sanskrit thru पाणिनीयम् ।

Second thing is to have a clear idea of Philosophy of Language one should study पाणिनीयम् first and the other Systems next. One cannot separate this Philosophy from Language.

As a वेदाङ्ग and दर्शन , पाणिनीयम् leads one to मोक्ष - this is also Philosophy.

  

Patanjali under चतुर्थी संप्रदाने discusses ' तादर्थ्ये चतुर्थी वाच्या’ and also the necessity of चतुर्थी संप्रदाने । ’ मुक्तये हरिं भजति’ ।
