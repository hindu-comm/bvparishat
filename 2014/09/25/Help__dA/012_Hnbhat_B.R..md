Yes indded.

  

कृष्ण त्वदीयपदपङ्कजपञ्जरान्त-

*रद्यैव* मे विशतु मानसराजहंसः ।

  

would be better reading.

  

त्वदीयपदपङ्कजपञ्जरान्ते  

is another reading.

  

The reading :

कृष्ण त्वदीयपदपङ्कजपञ्जरा*न्तं*

*अ*द्यैव मे विशतु मानसराजहंसः ।

  

is grammatically wrong, as अनुस्वार follows अ. Strange splitting of modern Sanskrit.

  

andत्वदीयपदपङ्कजपञ्जरान्ते अद्यैव also the same type. Because it will be पूर्वरूप. Even though would make some sense.

  

*अध्यैव* मे विशतु मानसराजहंसः  

  

is meaningless reading for अद्यैव, even without Sandhi.

  

only in one web page I found the correct reading:

  

कृष्ण त्वदीय पदपङ्कजपञ्जरान्त-

रद्यैव मे विशतु मानसराजहंसः ।  

  

with correct splitting, but the space between the त्वदीय and the remaining part of the line makes it uneasy for a reader of Sanskrit texts. Such errors are common in web pages and typed pdf versions from printed version.

  

The word अन्त may mean some meaning, of many the word has.

  

"अन्तः स्वरूपे निकटे प्रान्ते निश्चयनाशयोः।अवयवेऽपि" इति हेमकोशात्।

  

The word अन्तर् as अव्यय, means "inside of the cage" in this case. And not the word अन्त.
