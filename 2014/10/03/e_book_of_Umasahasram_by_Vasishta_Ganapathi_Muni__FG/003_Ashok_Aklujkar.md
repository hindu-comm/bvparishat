\>  
\> OmkAravadanaM vandyaM  
\> kavInAM AdimakaviM  
\> vandE gaNapatiM dEvaM  
\> naravESadharaM guruM  
\>  
\> The above dhyAna sloka on gaNapati muni was composed by Shri.Vishwanatha Swami before commencing  
\> gaNapati muni ashtOttara nAma.  
  

Metrically, I would expect the second line/quarter of the verse to read kavInAM AdimaM kavim.  
  
Using "e" where you have used "E" and "o" where you have used "O" will all be better, since "E" and "O" are needed for the diphthongs "ai/ऐ" and "au"
