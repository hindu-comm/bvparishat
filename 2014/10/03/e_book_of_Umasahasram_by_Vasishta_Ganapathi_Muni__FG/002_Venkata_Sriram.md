Namaste madam,  
  
Congratulations. As mahOpAdhyAya kAsi kriSNAchArya used to say that gaNapati muni was education himself. Shri Muni's kapAla bhEda siddhi is quite unique experience. Only another gaNapati can understand that.  
  
OmkAravadanaM vandyaM  
kavInAM AdimakaviM  
vandE gaNapatiM dEvaM  
naravESadharaM guruM  
  
The above dhyAna sloka on gaNapati muni was composed by Shri.Vishwanatha Swami before commencing  
gaNapati muni ashtOttara nAma.
