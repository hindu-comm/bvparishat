नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

Yes , it is परितः सीदन्ति अस्याम् - and the प्रक्रिया is offered in पदमञ्जरी and is correct .

  

I generally do not refer to वाचस्पत्यम् or शब्दकल्पद्रुम । You said the following and the same prompted me . You may stick to either gun --

  

Though Monier Williams gives "associate" as the first meaning, a better translation is "attendant" \[in a council\] or "councillor", since as per शब्दकल्पद्रुम and वाचस्पत्य, the word is derived from परिषद् (council) by counting it in पृशोदरादि. -- 4 Oct 2014 By Vidvan Nityananda Misra
