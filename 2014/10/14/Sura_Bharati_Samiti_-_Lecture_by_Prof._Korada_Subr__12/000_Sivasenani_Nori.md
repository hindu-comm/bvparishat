As part of the monthly lecture series (organized every third Friday of the month) of Sura Bharati Samiti, on 17th October 2014, Firday, Prof. Korada Subrahmanyam has kindly agreed to deliver a lecture, in Sanskrit, on "Certain Aspects of Paspasahnika in light of Nyaya, Mimamsa, Vedanta and Vyakarana" from 5:00 pm to 6:00 pm. All are welcome.

  

Address: Sura Bharati Samiti, O. U. Campus, Hyderabad - 500007.

  

Directions: When you enter Osmania University on the Vidyanagar side, there is an entrance arch. Sura Bharati Samiti is on the right side of the entrance arch. There is space for parking vehicles (cars and two-wheelers) inside the Samiti premises.

  

Bus routes: Those travelling by bus may taken any bus from Koti / Nallakunta / Dilsukhnagar etc. which goes towards Osmania University and get down at the Andhra Mahila Sabha Women's College bus stop. Sura Bharati Samiti is opposite the bus stop. Those coming from Secunderabad / Tarnaka etc. may take any bus that crosses Osmania University and goes towards Vidyanagar and alight at the O. U. Engineering College bus stop. Sura Bharati Samiti is next to the Engineering College.

  

Local train: Those travelling by local train may walk to Sura Bharati Samiti from Vidyanagar station (nearer) or Jamia Osmania station (slightly farther).
