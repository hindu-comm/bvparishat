नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

Sri Vidyarthi

  

note that there is no any branch (save the Tantra and its offshoots) which discusses लिपि ।

व्याकरणभेदः लिपिभेदवत् - is a sentence from लघुमञ्जूषा । लिपि may change but not शब्दस्वरूपम् ।

  

ओम् is the Sabda - if followed by a हल् it will get अनुस्वार and becomes ओं (मो’नुस्वारः - पा सू)।
