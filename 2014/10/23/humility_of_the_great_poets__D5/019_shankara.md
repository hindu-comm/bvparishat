Namaste,

  

Madhusudana Sarasvati's concluding verse in Siddhantabindu is often quoted as an example of humility.  

  

यदत्र सौष्ठवं किञ्चित् तद्गुरोरेव मे न हि।  

यदत्रासौष्ठवं किञ्चित् तन्ममैव गुरोर्न हि॥



regards  
shankara
