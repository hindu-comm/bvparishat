\~ śrī-śrī-rādhā-dāmodarāya namaḥ \~  
  
tṛṇād api sunīcena — by \[a person\] humbler than a blade of grass.  
  
taror iva sahiṣṇunā — by \[a person\] as tolerant as a tree.  
  
amāninā — by a person who does not exhibit pride  
  
mānadena — by a person who is mānada (who gives all respect to others)  
  
kīrtanīyaḥ — glorifiable  
  
sadā — always  
  
hariḥ — Lord Hari  
  
Translation: Lord Hari is glorifiable \[only by\] a person who is humbler than a blade of grass, tolerant as a tree, who does not exhibit pride and gives all respect to others.  
  
sādhu-caraṇa-rajo 'bhilāṣī,  
  
hari pārṣada dāsa.
