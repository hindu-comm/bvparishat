Dear Prof N Paturi Thanks for information on Academy of Sanskrit Research



Speech recognition and Speech synthesis are different fron Spectral Analysis of Sanskrit words or sentences



Softwares are available for producibg spectrograms of any word. One gets 3 D display- X axis time, Y axis Frequency and Z axis (perpendicular to paper) Intensity or amplitude. Frequencies of humanspeech are normally below 3000 cycles per sec. Mouth acts like a filter-Source Filter theory.. Svaras dominates spectrograma. Consonanta occupy very small time duration so they carry higher information. Vowels occuoy larger time duration and hence they carry less information. One see formant frequencies due to resonances in the mouth.More study is needed.Thanks.NRJoshi
