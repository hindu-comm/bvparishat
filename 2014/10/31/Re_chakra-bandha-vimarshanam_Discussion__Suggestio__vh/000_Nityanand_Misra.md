The formation follows the pattern of शिशुपालवधम् १९.१२०, which is a Chakrabandha with a hidden signature.

  

जयिता (nominative singular of जयितृ) is masculine. The deity being feminine, it should be जयित्री. But that would break the metre. You may want to rephrase that.
