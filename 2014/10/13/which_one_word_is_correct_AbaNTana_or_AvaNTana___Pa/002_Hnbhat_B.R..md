***(H1) वण्ट् \[p= 915,2\] \[L=185343\] ***

***(also written बण्ट्) cl.1.10. P.*** वण्टति or वण्टयति (accord. to some also वण्टापयति), to partition, apportion, share, divide Dhātup.

xxxii, 48 (only वण्ट्यते Cāṇ. , and वण्ट्यमान Hcar. , v.l.)

  

Here is the explanation of Monier Williams. Now it is up to you to decide the correctness of the verb वंण्ट् or बण्ट्.

  

वबयोरभेदः , रलयोरभेदः, श-स are interchanged in certain words. But not in all cases.

  

रसना, रशना, सिञ्जिनी, शिञ्जिनी etc. are some of them. बटुः, वटुः, विरुदः बिरुदः are cases of व-ब. but not विबेक, बिवेकः etc. are found.
