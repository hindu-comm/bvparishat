नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

This is an error of Mallinatha - because it is clearly stated in Mahabhasyam and Kasika (Mallinatha refers to Kasika often) - प्रमादो धीमतामपि ।

  

In a great ocean like Mahabhasyam it is difficult , sometimes , to trace the word .

आनुशतिकादीनां च - no doubt , it is an आकृतिगण - but when Patanjali himself read the word as साधुशब्द , there is no room for further discussion.

  

Other instances of Mallinatha ,where the commentary is not as per शास्त्रम् -

  

1\. शिशुपालवध - पुरीमवस्कन्द लुनीहि नन्दनम् (सर्गः 1) - ’ पुनः पुनः चस्कन्द ’ इत्यर्थः।

This is not correct - discussed in Kaumudi - a usage of the other लोट् - ’ लोटो हिस्वौ ...".

  

2\. नैषधम् - कविना बुधेन च ( 1) - here it is related to ज्योतिषम् ।

  

3\. शिशुपालवध - ’ नारद इत्यबोधि सः ’(1) - here there is difference of opinion between Diksita and Nagesa.
