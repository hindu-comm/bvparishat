[[nagarajpaturi	2014-04-23, 00:04:04 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/5Y30St9RGJk)]]



That the three fields of Indian tradition that aresensationallyshaking the world : Yoga, Ayurveda andPaniniyan linguistics were considered to be a triad of sciences of TrikaraNa S'uddhi /sciences of depollution of Mind, Body and Speech.



The famous prayer to Patanjali reads as:

योगेन चित्तस्य पदेन वाचम्

मलम् शरीरस्य च वैद्यकेन

योपाकरोत्तम् प्रवरम् मुनीनाम्

पतंजलिम् प्रांजलि रानतोस्मि



Vakyapadiyam 1-147 reads as



कायवाग्बुद्धिविषया



ये मला समवस्थिताः



चिकित्सालक्षणाध्यात्म



शास्त्रै स्तेषां विशुद्धयः



This grouping of these three sciences needs to be paid more attention.



धन्योस्मि



नागराजः
