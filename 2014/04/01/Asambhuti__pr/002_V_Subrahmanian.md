According to the commentary of Shankaracharya the terms 'asambhUtiH' and 'sambhUtiH' refer to kAraNam, prakRti, and kAryam, the manifest world. Those who contemplate on these attain to fruits as 'prakRtilayaH' and 'aNimAdisiddhiH'. You can look into the original bhAShyam for these three mantras and/or the English translation of the commentary by Sw.Gambhirananda published by the Advaita Ashrama in their 'Eight Upanishads' series, Vol.1 where the very first upanishad taken up is the IshAvAsya.
