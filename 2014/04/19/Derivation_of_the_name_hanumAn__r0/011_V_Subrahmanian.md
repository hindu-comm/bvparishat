25. tataH= then;gandha vahaH= scent, carrier;pra bhanjanaH= in their entirety, breaker - of trees etc., by his wind-storms, tempestuous Air-god;vaayuH= Air-god;tvaam nihatam dR^iSTvaa= you, mutilated, on observing;svayam= personally - physically;bhR^isha sankruddhaH= highly, infuriated;trailokyam= in triad of worlds;na vavau vai= not, circulate, really.

Is there any explanation, by the revered Acharya, for Hanuman trying to catch the blazing sun thinking it to be a fruit? 

  

  

Here is one from the website:

  

<http://valmiki.iitk.ac.in/index.php?id=tilaka>  

  

फलमिति । जिघृक्षुरुत्पत्योत्प्लुत्य दिवमभ्युत्पतः । "उत्प्लुत्याभ्युत्थितो दिवम्" इति पाठान्तरे उत्प्लुत्य दिवमभ्युत्थित इति योजना । उदयकालिकभास्करस्याम्रफलवद्रक्तवृत्तत्वात्फलबुद्धिविषयत्वम् । यथा जातमात्राजादिबालानां प्राचीनवासनया स्तनपानादौ प्रवृत्तिः तथैवास्य जातमात्रस्य वानरोचितफलादानप्रवृत्तिरिति बोध्यम् ।। 4.66.21 ।।  

  

Of course, from the commentaries we do get a firm impression that Hanuman was not in any way pained by the weapon-striking.
