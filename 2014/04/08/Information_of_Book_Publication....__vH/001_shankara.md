[worldcat.org](http://worldcat.org) is the best place to look for such information. It is very easy to find the details of publications of books by searching with the title of the book, author or publisher. It is easier to find if you type in IAST.  
  
The following online tool can be used to convert devanagari script into IAST.  
<http://epandit.shrish.in/tools/IAST-DV-IAST/>  
  

Giving below link to worldcat search result for Sarvadarsanakaumudi.

<http://www.worldcat.org/search?q=ti%3ASarvadar%C5%9Banakaumud%C4%AB&qt=advanced&dblist=638>
