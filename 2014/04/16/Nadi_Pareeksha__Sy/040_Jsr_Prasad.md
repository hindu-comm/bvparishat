A great scholar in Vedas, Vyakarana and Ayurveda, Pandit Satyadeva Vasishtha authored a book titled 'नाडीतत्त्वदर्शनम्' published by Ramlal Kapoor Trust, Bahalgadh, Sonipat, Haryana in 1950. Scholars may remember distributing copies of his scholarly सत्यभाष्यम् on विष्णुसहस्रनामस्त्रोत्रम् during the 1997 World Sanskrit Conference at Taralabalu Kendra, Bangalore.

  

The नाडीतत्त्वदर्शनम् has about 330 pages with the following contents:

  

Chapter 1 - त्रिदोषवेदमूलीयम्

Chapter 2 - नाडीपदविज्ञानीयम्

Chapter 3 - पञ्चमहाभूतत्रित्त्वज्ञापकम्

Chapter 4 - त्रिदोषसमक्षेपीयम्

Chapter 5 - दूतनाडीविज्ञानीयम् (It is believed that, still, two or three scholars are available who can use this technique)

Chapter 6 - रोगगणना

Chapter 7 - त्रिदोषसङ्गणनीयम्

Chapter 8 - रावण-नाडीविकृतिः

  

The coveted book is impregnated with references culled out from the Vedas and Samhitas.
