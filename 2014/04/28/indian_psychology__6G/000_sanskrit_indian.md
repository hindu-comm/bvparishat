**अन्तर्वैषयिक संगति Interdisciplinary relevance**

संस्कृत एवम् मनोविज्ञान दोनों विषयों में उपयोगितापूर्ण सम्वाद की सम्भवनाओं पर विचार करना ।

**शोध का महत्त्व Significance of the study**

This study is important for various reasons and they are listed below-

1\. To open up a dialogue between east and west regarding the understanding of various aspects and dimensions of human psyche.

2\. To experiment with the new ideas and issues regarding human behavior.

3\. To find that is Indian tradition is capable of solving problems of human mind and behavior in its own terms.

4\. To provide a rightful place to the Sanskrit texts in the field of psychology which has been deliberately denied by the psychologist who have worked only in western framework.

**उद्देश्य Objectives-**

1\. To develop- **शिक्षण (Teaching), शोध(research) तथा उपयोग(application)**

2\. To experiment with Indian terms with Indian people in Indian situations and to provide or suggest an alternative theories and therapies if possible.

3\. To restructure and reorient psychology.****

**कार्यपद्धति Methodology- **

1. Theorizing the major concepts and therapies of Indian psychology.

2. Experiments will be conducted on different subjects in the laboratory to verify the theories and concepts.****
