Speechless thanks, Vidvadvara Sivasenani Soni Mahaabhaaga, for your useful explanation. Thus, for the word 'Paraardha'-(sankhyaa), we can say : Parashcha (largest/greatest) asau Ardhah (enhancement)---the largest or maximum available enhancement (of the number). Now, the word 'Ardha' from the root 'Ridh Vriddhau' may be formed through the efforts of Vidvadvara Nityananda Mahaabhaaga, Vidvadvadvara Korada Mahaabhaaga,
and others-- a galaxy of scholarly people. With best regard,
