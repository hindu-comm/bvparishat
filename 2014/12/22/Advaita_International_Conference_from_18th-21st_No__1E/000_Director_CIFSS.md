Dear Scholar Friends

Hari Om

On behalf of Chinmaya International Foundation I feel immense pleasure to announce **the International conference on ‘The Contribution of Advaita Vedanta to Humanity’** to be held at Chinmaya Vibhooti, Kolwan Pune, Pune from 18th – 21st November,2015.

  

The organising committee is working towards making the conference most memorable for all the participants by

  

providing a vibrant platform for discussions and exchange of view. We are doing our best to make this conference a

  

gathering of highly learned and senior scholars, endowed with wisdom and oration, from all across the globe. The

  

audience will be composed of academicians, research scholars, Acharyas of Chinmaya Mission and members of the

  

Chinmaya Mission. Please do follow our website **www.chinfo.org.advaitaconference** for information and regular

  

The conference will enable research scholars to have an opportunity to meet and interact with experienced philosophers, scholars, and senior acharyas of the Chinmaya Mission and take home the wisdom of Advaita.

  

A copy of the conference schedule/brochure is enclosed for your kind information. It is also proposed to bring out a commemorative volume which will contain articles written by scholars in English or Sanskrit.

  

I would request to scholars to send their papers before 31st March and also request to participate in the International Conference.

  

With regards and thanks
