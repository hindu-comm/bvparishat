*आचारात्प्राप्यते स्वर्ग आचारात्प्राप्यते सुखम् ।।*

In fact, there no topic about आचार, at all in the text of दक्षस्मृति. But the half verse is correctly quoted in नाददीयपुराण, with similar verses, but here it has got a different second half. The edition of आनन्दाश्रम series, in the collection of 27 स्मृति-s also do not contain the verse pp.72-84. The content list do not show any mention of आचार at all.
