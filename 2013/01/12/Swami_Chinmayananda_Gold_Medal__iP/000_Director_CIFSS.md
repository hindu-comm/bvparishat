**I am directed to inform you that Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Sansthan has instituted a prestigious award in the name of Swami Chinmayananda for young scholars (Swami Chinmayananda Gold Medal) below the age of forty to recognize their intellectual achievements and encourage them in their academic pursuits.**

**Heads of institutions/departments, eminent citizens and applicants themselves can make the nominations in the prescribed form so as to reach the Director, CIFSS on or before 31-01-2013. **You are requested to make nominations of deserving candidates and also to circulate this among your colleagues/Research scholars and to encourage them to send the nominations.**
