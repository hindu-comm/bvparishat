With deep respect for the scholars who have posted on the topic, this alpamati may be permitted to point out that Rigveda 10.164 as per the Mandala division corresponds to Rigveda 2.3.22 as per the Ashtaka division.



While the Mandala division is now assumed to be *the* division used, if nothing on the method of reference isstated, many books do carry both the references - usually alternating thetwo on even and odd pages (for instance inMax Muller's editions).
