Dear all,

I am glad to inform that the book entitled THE MARRIAGE-KNOT (Vivaha Bandhan in Bengali, authored by Dr. Bishnupada Goswami and translated into English by Dr. Ruma Bandyopadhyay) has recently been published from Sanskrit Pustak Bhandar, 38, Bidhan Sarani, Kolkata 700 006, price Rs. 250/-, pp i-xx +145. This manual deals with the details of marriage ceremony including Nandimukha Shraddha, according to the Kauthumi School of the Samaveda, along with introduction, original mantras in Devanagari Script, list of materials to be used, glossary, photographs, etc. Scanned copy of the cover pages (front and back) are attached herewith for kind perusal. Interested persons/scholars may go through the book and any suggestion for further improvement will be greatly appreciated.

Best wishes,

Nabanarayan Bandyopadhyay
