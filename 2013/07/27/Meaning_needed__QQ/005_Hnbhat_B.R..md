Dear Kashyap,

  

Why don't you look into any dictionary of Sanskrit Words before placing a flat request for the meaning of a word like this? Many are available online. Here is the entry in Monier Williams Dictionary:\\

  

मितद्रु

  

(H3) मित--द्रु \[p= 815,3\] \[L=164090\] mfn. (मित्/अ) strong-legged , running well ib.

(H3B) मित--द्रु \[L=164090.1\] m. (perhaps fr. 2. मित , " having a measured course ") the sea , ocean L.
