गीताप्रेस गोरखपुर यु.पि. २७३००५ दूरभाष ०५५१२३३४७२१. You will get various versions that is each kaanda separately or in one volume, paper back, hard cover, pocket edition and all other sizes. Gita press has sales counters all over India and a big office at गोविन्दभवन, १५१ महात्मा गान्धी रोड, कलकत्ता ७००००७ दू.भा.०३३ २२६८६८९४. They also sell the biography of saints, bhakti granthas, and the traditional versions ie not critical editions of the epics. If you live at Varanasi or in a metropolitan city go to the main railways terminus. You get various versions.
