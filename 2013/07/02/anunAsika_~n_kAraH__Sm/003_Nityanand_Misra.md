> पञ्चाननः/ काञ्चनः/ पतञ्जलिः एतेषुपदेषु 'ञ्' कारः 'न्' कारवत् > उच्चार्यते \|

  
This is wrong. ञ् should be pronounced the same, whether followed by a स्वर or a व्यञ्जन or विराम.  
  
As per International Phonetic Alphabet, pronunciation of ञ् is ɲ, while that of न् is n̪. Both are different. Refer <http://www.sanskritweb.net/deutsch/ipa_sans.pdf> for IPA pronunciations.
