The mantra is (without accents RV Samhita 8-69-12)  
सुदेवो असि वरुण यस्य ते सप्त सिन्धवः । अनुक्षरन्ति काकुदं सूर्म्यं सुषिरामिव ॥  
  
What is the accent on "su" of "sudevo"? Is it  
  
सु॒दे॒वो  

  
or

सुदे॒वो

  
I refer the edition R L Kashyap and S Sadagopan from <http://www.sanskritweb.net/rigveda/>. But for this Mantra, the PDFs are inconsistent. The hymn file (page 2 of <http://www.sanskritweb.net/rigveda/rv08-068.pdf>) gives the second form with उदात्त, while the combined file (page 456 of <http://www.sanskritweb.net/rigveda/rv.pdf>) gives the first form with अनुदात्त.  
  
I believe if 6.1.158 अनुदात्तं पदमेकवर्जम् applies, then the first form (अनुदात्त) makes sense.  
  
Thanks, Nityanand
