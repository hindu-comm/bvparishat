Respected Scholars,  

Hari Om. In Mallinatha's commentary on the following Shloka:  
  
अनुचरेण धनाधिपतेरथो नगविलोकनविस्मितमानसः ।  
स जगदे वचनं प्रियमादरान्मुखरताऽवसरे हि विराजते ॥ ५.१६  
  

He comments गदतेर्गत्यर्थस्य दुहादित्वात्प्रधाने कर्मणि लिट्। According to the famous Karika, for Duh Yaac etc गौणे कर्मणि लिट्. So, it should be स नाम अर्जुनो जगदे। and not वचनं जगदे। Am I right? Is it a printing mistake there? As in two other cases in the same Kavya he stipulates correctly. Both in 1895 Tukarama Javaji's Nirnaya Sagar press edition and in 2009 Chowkhamba Krishnadas Academy Dr. Sudhakar Malaviya's edition we find this.  
  

  
  
नारायणस्मृतिः
