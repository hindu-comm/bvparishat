In the Yoga Sutra VRtti of Sri Sadashivendra Saraswati, for the sutra 15 of the 2nd pAda occurs this phrase:

सर्वमेव भोगसाधनं जगत् विवेकिनो दु:खमेव , विवेकिनमक्षिपात्रमूर्णातन्तुरिवात्यन्तमुद्वेजयति । अत: सर्वं दु:खं हेयमिति भाव: ॥१५॥

I am unable to understand the meaning of the highlighted portion: I presume the split words will be: विवेकिनम् अक्षिपात्रम् ऊर्णातन्तुरिव अत्यन्तमुद्वेजयति. I request scholars to help me with the meaning for the word: अक्षिपात्रम्. I think ऊर्णातन्तुः means the fine thread that the spider brings out of itself for weaving its nest/net or a woolen thread. Another meaning given for UrNA is 'the circle of hair between the eyebrows.'
