The Hindi translations of both नृसिंहपूर्वतापनीय and नृसिंहोत्तरतापनीय upanishads form chapter 66 and 67 of the Upanishad Ank (उपनिषदङ्क), the 23rd special edition of Kalyan magazine, brought out by Gita Press, Gorakhpur.  
  
You can search for the book on Gita Press site, by book code 659.  
  
<http://www.gitapress.org/Search_main.asp>  
  
The book is still in print.
