Nidana Sutra of Patanjali (The complete text in 10 Prapathakas) was published by Meharchand Lachhmandas, Otirnysl & Foreign Book Sellers, 2736, Kucha Chailan, Daryaganj, Delhi - 6 (india) in the year 1971 with the commentary up to the Chandoviciti part of it (Tattvasubhodhini of Tataprasada) edited by KNBhatnagar.  
2. Exclusively the Chandoviciti Part of Patanjali's Nidanasutra was published by RASHTRIYA SANSKRIT VIDYAPEETHA TIRUPATI in the year 2000 with the title : CHANDOVICITIH of PATANJALI with two commentaries:  
1. Tattvasubodhini Vritti of Sri Tataparasada and 2. Chandoviciti vritti of Sri Petta Sastri critically edited by Prof. BR Sharma and Prof. LN Bhatta, Cost Rs. 279/-
