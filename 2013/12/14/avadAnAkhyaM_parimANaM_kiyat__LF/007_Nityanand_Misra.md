There is a commentary on theĀpastamba Yajñaparibhāṣā Sūtra published by Satyavrata Sāmaśramī Bhaṭṭācārya. In the commentary on the 100th Sutra, Avadāna is explained as "a thumb's breadth".

  

I do not have the commentary by Satyavrata Sāmaśramī Bhaṭṭācārya, but I have access to its translation of the commentary, edited by Max Mueller.
