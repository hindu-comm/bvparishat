कविर्मनीषी परिभूः स्वयंभूर्याथातथ्यतोऽर्थान् व्यदधाच्छाश्वतीभ्यः समाभ्यः ॥८॥  
  

is a part of a mantra in the IshAvAsyopaniShat. Here the word 'kaviH' denotes the Atman/Brehman who is the All-knower: कविः क्रान्तदर्शी .
Shri Pawan Tiwari ji has correctly indicated the reference number which is referring to this upanishadic section of the shuklayajurvedeeya samhitA.
