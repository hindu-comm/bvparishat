You are correct, as per Kashika and SK प्लुतप्रगृह्या अचि नित्यम् (6-1-125) blocks इकोऽसवर्णे शाकल्यस्य ह्रस्वश्च (6-1-127) so ह्रस्व cannot happen. I thought विप्रतिषेधे परं कार्यम् (1-4-2) would apply here, but is does not. Without ह्रस्व the metre would break, so it is better put as  
  
सदा लसन्तु प्रथिताश्वलायनाः  
  
This also makes Dr. Narayanan an illustrious and celebrated आश्वलायन which all of us agree with. :)
