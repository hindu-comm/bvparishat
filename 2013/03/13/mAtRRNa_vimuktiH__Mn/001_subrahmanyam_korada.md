नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

The quotation is taken from 'A History of Dharmasastra', P 580, Part-2, of MM Kale .

  

Kale attributes this to Sankha-Likhita Smriti , that is not available.

  

He , in the footnotes quotes --

  

न मातापित्रोन्तरं (sic) गच्छेत्पुत्रः। कामं मातुरेवासुब्रूयात्सा(sic) हि धारिणी पोषणी च। न पुत्रः प्रतिमुच्येतान्यत्र सौत्रामणियागज्जीवन्नृणान्मातुः(sic)।

It can be मातापित्रोरन्तरं , मातुरेवानुब्रूयात् , सौत्रामणियागात् ।

In Santiparva , Mahabharata , there is story involving two brothers - Sankha and Likhita .

  

Sankhalikhitasmriti is quoted in Tantravartikam also . Yajnyavalkya and Baudhayana take Sankhalikhitasmriti as authotitative . It is followed by Suklayajussakha - शंखलिखितोक्तं तु वाजसनेयिनाम्।

Sankhasmriti is different .

  

However , in case, no clear authority is available it may be considered as an अर्थवाद।

न गायत्र्या समं जप्यं न मातुः परदैवतम् - मनुस्मृतिः - is also an अर्थवाद ।

धन्यो’स्मि
