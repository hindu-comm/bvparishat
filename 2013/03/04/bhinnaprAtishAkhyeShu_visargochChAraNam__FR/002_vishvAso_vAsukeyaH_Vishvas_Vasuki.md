अस्मिन् मम मित्रात् एकस्मात् शास्त्रीयपण्डितात् एतत् ज्ञातम् एतावत् -

  

अ\]"ऊष्मविसर्जनीयप्रथमद्वितीया अघोषाः, न हकारः,व्यञ्जनशेषो घोषवान्" इति उक्त्या हकारविसर्जनीययोः घोषे भेदः प्रतिपादितः। \[मम वाक्षु उक्तम्।\]

  

आ\] \[तद्वाक्षु\]...कु does not have the same sthana as हकारविसर्गौ.

(The matter of अ should be treated separately since it is a svara.)  
Because भट्टोजिदीक्षित included the statement अकुहविसर्जनीयानां कण्ठः  
(quoted from some older sutra texts) people think अ कु ह विसर्ग are  
all from कण्ठः. It would have been better if he had quoted the  
पाणिनीयशिक्षा instead, which very clearly says:  
  
कण्ठ्यौ अहौ ... जिह्वामूले तु कुः प्रोक्तः ...  
  
which places the हकार at the कण्ठ, but says कु is at जिह्वामूल. I am  
sure we will agree that the same कु and ह is being described, and  
there are no two different कवर्गs, one at जिह्वामूल and one at कण्ठ!  
  
Since the various सूत्रs and शिक्षाग्रन्थs employ slightly differring  
terminology to describe the same sounds, we have to be careful while  
interpreting them. Some texts are clearer than others, and we have to  
interpret the less clear texts in light of the more clear ones.  
  
Even the Taittiriya Pratishakhya says (and again, I'm sure the  
Taittiriya कवर्ग is not different from the पाणिनीय कवर्ग!) कण्ठस्थानौ  
हकारविसर्जनीयौ, हनूमूले जिह्वामूलेन कवर्गे स्पर्शयति । (Again I'm sure  
the Pratishakhyas of the RV or SYV could also be consulted.) This  
clarifies the situation very much, since it says the pronunciation of  
कवर्ग is \*by\* the जिह्वामूल \*at\* the हनूमूल, which is nothing but the  
velum in modern terminology. This does not conflict with जिह्वामूले तु  
कुः प्रोक्तः because that statement only gives one articulator but in  
general there are two: one active (करण) one passive (स्थान) and the TP  
gives both. The कवर्ग is pronounced by the जिह्वामूल contacting the  
velum. This is the सर्वसम्मत (including all ancient आचार्यs and modern  
researchers) pronunciation of the कवर्ग.  
  
....  
  

So how to reconcile the statement अकुहविसर्जनीयानां कण्ठः? I have had  
this discussion with one of the most eminent वैयाकरणs of our time,  
मान्यश्री रङ्गनाथशर्मा of Bangalore, and he said that we have to  
understand कवर्गस्य कण्ठो नाम न हकारविसर्गयोः कण्ठः प्रत्युत  
तत्समीपवर्तिप्रदेश एव.
