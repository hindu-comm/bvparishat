नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

It is a not an easy task - consumes a lot of time and taxing on mind .

  

For each item there will be more than one reference followed by
पूर्वपक्ष and सिद्धान्त , in many cases .

  

Better to entrust the task to a Reasearch Scholar with adequate knowledge in Sanskrit .

  

Knowledge in व्याकरणम् , ज्योतिषम् and धर्मशास्त्रम् would make the job easier जोब्

  

धर्मसिन्धु and निर्णयसिन्धु are considered as authoritative .
