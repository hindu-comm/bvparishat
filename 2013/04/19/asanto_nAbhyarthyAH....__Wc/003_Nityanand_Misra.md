Please read तुर्य in place of तूर्य and पदमनुविधेयं पदनुविधेयं. हुलसी is correct, being the name of the mother of तुलसीदास.  
  
Also अन्वय is missing the word विषमम् which should come before असिधाराव्रतम्. Meaning is clear.
