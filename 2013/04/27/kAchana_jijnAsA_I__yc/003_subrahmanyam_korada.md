’श्रीमद्भ्यःधन्यवादाःIभवतां परिष्कारःमहान्तं प्रमोदंजनयतिI
परन्तु वाणशब्दस्यवीणार्थेनैघन्टुकः अथवा यत्रकुत्रापिग्रन्थेप्रयोगःउपलभ्यतेवा?’(Dr Ayyangar)

  

Dear Dr Ayyangar

  

Please permit me to put this question on BVP and answer in English --

  

The meaning of वाणशब्द is provided by Madhava in धातुवृत्ति । The rest is from my mind .

  

One has to search for प्रयोग - as well as entry in a निघण्टु ।

  

There are six kinds of Sadbdas and eighteen kinds of Meanng . The Mimamsanyayas , 12 / 24 are useful in वाक्यर्थ ।

  

By and large , we cannot expect the Lexicographer to register all 'possible' Sabdas - महान् शब्दस्य विषयः ( पस्पशा - महाभाष्यम् ।

  

धन्यो’स्मि
