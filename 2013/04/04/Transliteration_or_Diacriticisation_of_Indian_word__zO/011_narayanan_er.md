Tamil: வட்டெழுத்து *vaṭṭeḻuttu*; Malayalam: വട്ടെഴുത്ത് *Vaṭṭeḻuttŭ  
*

*Here we see *ழு as *ḻu.*

*But your transliteration for *ழ as ĺa is not popular.

Moreover கொற்றவை is written as Koṛṛavai, but not as Koṟṟavai.

So ற is to be transliterated as ṛa but not as ṟa

These things are clear from the characters recommended to the Unicode and the charts will tell the story.

Grantha script is only used to write Sanskrit etc. other than Tamil. வட்டெழுத்து is the round scripts like Malayalam, Simhalese, Tulu, Kannada etc. as the characters appear and written in round shape; whereas Tamil is கோலெழுத்து because the characters appear and written stick-like figures. கோல்=stick.

Anyway the transliteration of ழ – ĺa is a self made one and does not appear to match with the popular version of ழ=ḻa.
