However, the query is about the Anglicised words. Whether the Anglicised Indian particularly Sanskrit words needs diacritical marks or not.

  

Brahman is referred to Upanishadic/Aupanishadic – Paraṁ Brahmā, Brahmā is referred to Caturmukhabrahmā, Brāhmaṇa refers to the person of that community.

  

If the word Brahmanical is used like Upanishadic/Aupanishadic, do they need to be diacriticised? Many of Indian words are now available in English lexicons like Veda/Vedic, Yoga/Yogic, Dharna, Bandh and Dalit without diacritical marks.

  

Do the Anglicised words such as Brahmanical (Brāhmaṇical) and Upanishadic (Upaniṣadic) needs to be diacriticised in publication series like *popular series* or *general readers*?
