Nagappa Sastri, Hosakere was a former professor of Sanskrit and Kannada in Khalsa College, Mumbai.

Works are:  

1. The Story of My Experiments with Truth of Mohandas K. Gandhi is titled as Satya Shodhanam in Sanskrit by Nagappa Sastri.  
2. Srimat Buddha Bhagawatam by Hosakere Nagappa Sastri ( Book ) Bombay : Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, 1990, 3 editions published in 1990 in English and held by 28 libraries worldwide.  
3. Tennāla-Rāmakr̥ṣṇa-kathā by Hosakere Nagappa Sastri ( Book ) 1 edition published in 1996 in Sanskrit and held by 3 libraries worldwide.
