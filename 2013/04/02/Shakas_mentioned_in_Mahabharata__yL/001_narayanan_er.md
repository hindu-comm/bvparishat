Respected Dr. Joshi,  
There are couple of instances noticed in the Mahabharata which mentions the term शक, once in the Udyogaparvan and the other in the Dronaparvan. I have with me the printed versions of the Pune Editions of the Vanaparvan and the Virataparvan only. The following lines are copied from the net. So the correctness of the following is to be checked:  
शकानां पह्लवानां च दरदानां च ये नृपाः। (उद्योगपर्वन्-५.४०.१५)  
काम्बोजानां सहस्रैस्तु शकानां च विशां पते। (द्रोणपर्वन्-७.९५.३८)  
On the contrary, there are some inscription verses mentioned as:  
त्रिंशत्सु त्रिसहस्रेषु भारतादाहवादितः।  
सप्ताब्दशतयुक्तेषु गतेष्वब्देषु - - -।  
पंञ्चाशत्सु कलौ काले षट्सु पञ्चशतासु च।  
समासु समतीतासु शकानामपि भूभुजाम्।।  
which are been referred to EPIGRAPHIA INDICA Vol.4 .Page.No. 7. (Vol. IV (1896-97), ed., E. Hultzsch (reprint 1979). I have not at all seen these works either. I reply to this mail on the basis of the internet informations only.  
Regards,  
Narayanan
