Respected Dr. Joshi,  
According to the work: "Rethinking the Mahabharata: A Reader's Guide to the Education of the Dharma King" by Alf Hiltebeitel refers the following Mahabharata verse:

  

अन्ताखीं चैव रोमां च यवनानां पुरं तथा। दूतैरेव वशे चक्रे करं चैनानदापयत्।।४९।। (२.२८.४९)

  

He suggests (Introduction page No.31) that अन्ताखी means the city of Antakya, which is in Turkey.  
Regards,  
Narayanan
