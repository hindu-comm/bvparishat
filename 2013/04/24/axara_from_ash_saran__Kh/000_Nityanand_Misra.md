Dear scholars

  

The commonly shown derivation of अक्षर is given as a नञ् तत्पुरुष - न क्षरति इति अक्षरम्

  

A rarer derivation is from the root अश् (अश्नोति व्याप्नोति इति अक्षरम्) by adding the उणादि प्रत्यय सरन्. This is given in several Hindi dictionaries and also in Vachaspatyam under the entry अक्षरी.

  

My question in the situation अश् + सर, which Sutras cause the कत्व and षत्व to result in अक्षर.

  

Thanks, Nityanand
