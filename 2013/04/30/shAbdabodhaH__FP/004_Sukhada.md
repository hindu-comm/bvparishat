Though not sure but the impression I had about it was the following:  
\[पठ व्यक्तायां\] वाचि = वाच्+ङि \<- वाच्(वाक्) = वचन  
It might be read as: वाचि is equal to वाच्+ङि, which is derived from वाच्(वाक्), which is equal to वचन;
