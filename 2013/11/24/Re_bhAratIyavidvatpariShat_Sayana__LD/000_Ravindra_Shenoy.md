Dear Anil Kumar  
Sayana was a great Advaitin scholar and was instrumental during the Vijaynagar empire formation. He was also called madhavacharya . He wrote bhashyas or commentaries on Vedas and Puranas.  
Madvacharya also called as Srimad Ananda tirtha or Poornaprajna was the greatest exponent of Realism or Dualism and was born in Udupi in 13th century. He wrote 37 works and termed as Sarvamula. Sarvamula includes works on brahmasutras, bhagavatha, mahabharata and tantra ,achara.
