Check your source. It is improbable that the two halvesbelong to the same verse, it does not make sense.

  

वेदाश्च should be वेदांश्च -> च is used twice, the accusative is expected withवेद.संस्कृते makes no sense here. Some member pointed out aboutमस्थंalso.

  

The two halves independently are found in different Parvans of the Mahabharatam.

  

  

[http://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/महाभारतम्-06-भीष्मपर्व-067](http://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/महाभारतम्-06-भीष्मपर्व-067)

See verse 6-67-7

मुखतः सोऽग्निमसृजत्प्राणाद्वायुमथापि च।

सरस्वतीं च वेदांश्च मनसः ससृजेऽच्युत।।

This is Bhishma speaking to Duryodhana.

  

[http://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/महाभारतम्-12-शांतिपर्व-347](http://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/महाभारतम्-12-शांतिपर्व-347)

See verse 12-347-57

वेदानां मातरं पश्य मत्स्थां देवीं सरस्वतीम्।

ध्रुवं च ज्योतिषां श्रेष्ठं पश्य नारद खेचरम्।।

This is Bhishma speaking to Narada.
