दृष्टसारेण (दृष्टः सारो बलं यस्य तेन दृष्टसारेण - बहुव्रीहिसमासः - Apte's Sanskrit-Hindi dictionary lists दृष्टसार as षष्ठीतत्पुरुष but gives the बहुव्रीहि meaning) and वध्यते (वध् कर्मणि प्र.पु. ए.व.).  
  
"An elephant-king (i.e. mighty elephant) is killed only by another mighty elephant, whose prowess has been seen".  
  
This is the second half of verse 69 of chapter 8 of कामन्दक's नीतिसारः. For the full verse and the context, see
