( १. १. ८१) व्योमकेशो भवो भीमः स्थाणू रुद्र उमापतिः ।।१-१-३४।।

synonym of शिव. or one of the 11 Rudra-s.

also called "ahibradhna"

  

\[L=3142\] ahibradhnaḥ, puṁ, (ahiḥ bradhne yasya .) śivaḥ .

(“ajaikapādahibradhnaḥ pinākī cāparājitaḥ” .

iti harivaṁśe .) **rudraviśeṣaḥ** .. iti jaṭādharaḥ ..

(yathā harivaṁśe .

“surabhī kasyapādrudrānekādaśa vinirmmame .

mahādevaprasādena tapasā bhāvitā satī ..
