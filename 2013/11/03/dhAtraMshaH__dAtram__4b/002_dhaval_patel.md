Here my precise question is that the धात्त्रंशः can not be derived from those vArttikas.

यणो मयो द्वे वाच्ये

1\. yaN in paJcamI and may in SaSThI doesn't apply here. because there is no yaN - may combination.

2\. may in paJcamI and yaN in SaSThI. This can possibly happen, but that would duplicate र्‌ and not त्‌ . In the example धात्त्रंशः, it is त्‌ which get duplicated.

Therefore, in my opinion, this vArtika can not apply here.

In my opinion, the sUtra causing the dvitva here can be only अनचि च.

Just a clarification. This is not my answer. This is answer given by my friend. But from what I understand, this is not to be taken asइको यण् द्वित्वाभावपक्षे स्थानिवद्भावभाक्... but इको यण्‌ द्वित्वनिषेधपक्षे स्थानिवद्भावभाक्‌.

  

There is no prakriyA / niSedha which will apply in the case of
सुध्य्युपास्य. Whereas in case of धात्त्रंश - there is possibility of a niSedha applying.

  

The sUtra न पदान्तद्विर्वचनवरेयलोपस्वरसवर्णानुस्वारदीर्घजश्चर्विधिषु has explicitly prohibited the application of स्थानिवद्भाव in case of द्वित्व (Therefore the duplication of य in सुध्य्युपास्य can go on). It has not prohibited application of द्वित्वनिषेध by दीर्घादाचार्याणाम्‌ anywhere explicitly. This was the crux of the answer as I understand it.
