भारतीयविद्वत्परिषदः सदस्यान् सादरं प्रणम्य  

I am glad to inform that Sanskrit Academy, Hyderabad has published a book entitled**Instrumental case in Sanskrit Literature** with an ISBN -978-93-80171-23-4 by Sri Dr. Swarna Samba Shiva Rao in English. price Rs. 100/-, pp xiv +120. (20% of Discount is available)



This work, as I observe, in the first of its kind giving a clear picture of the instrumental case with its style and scope in Sanskrit literature. It is studied in connection with both the morphology and the semantics, which is useful to the students of Sanskrit Grammar and Linguistics. It is with Critical and Analytical approach with several examples in Sanskrit (Vedic and Laukik) and Prakrit. It bears the stamp of authenticity as the author explained vividly about the mechanism of instrumental case with various examples taken from the Vedas, Upanishads, epics and Kavyas. This book not only narrates the contextual usage of Instrumental case but also gives a good explanation.
