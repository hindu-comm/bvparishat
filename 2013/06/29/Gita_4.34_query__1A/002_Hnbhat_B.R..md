यद्वाचाऽनभ्युदितं येन वागभ्युद्यते । तदेव ब्रह्म त्वं विद्धि नेदं यदिदमुपासते\|\|  

  

Whatever taught through words, can be anything other than ब्रह्म।

  

So there is nothing wrong, if he finds whatever taught tattvadarshin through words can be nothing else than ajnana.

  

Since only सत्यं ज्ञानम अनन्तं ब्रह्म

ब्रह्म is true knowledge and others taught orally isअज्ञानम only.
