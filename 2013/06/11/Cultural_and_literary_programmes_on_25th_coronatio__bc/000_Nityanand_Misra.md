Namaste  
  
For your information.  
  
Ganga Dussehra VS 2070 (June 19 2013) marks the 25th anniversary of coronation (Padābhiṣeka) of Svāmī Rāmabhadrācārya on the Siṃhāsana of Jagadguru Rāmānandācārya. Starting today, several cultural and literary programmes are planned in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, to honour the life and works of the Guru. The program details are attached. All are invited.  
  
Summary of programme  

Śobhā Yātrā - Starts 6 AM today (June 11) at Har Ki Pauri, Haridwar

Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa Kathā by the Guru

-   9:30 AM to 1 PM daily from June 11 to June 19. Place: Shivnagar,
    Rani Street, Bhupatwala, Haridwar. Live on Sanskar TV.

Jñāna Satra sessions between Dharmacharyas, philosophers, grammarians, poets and Samskrita scholars on the works of the Guru.  

-   6 PM to 9 PM daily from June 11 to June 19. Place: Vasisthayanam
    Ashram, Rani Street, Bhupatwala, Haridwar

Book release function on June 19  

-   Jagadguru Rāmānandācārya Svāmī Rāmabhadrācārya (2013).
    Śrīhanumānacālīsā Mahāvīrī Vyākhyā (2nd edition). Publisher: JRHU,
    Chitrakoot. Editors: Dr. Rāmādhāra Śarmā and Nityānanda Miśra. ISBN
    978-93-82253-03-7. 66 pages.
