Thanks Sharma Ji for getting the exact source.

  

In the Audichya manuscripts, the below is verse 3-131-10

  

धर्मं यो बाधते धर्मो न स धर्मः कुवर्त्म तत्

अविरोधात्तु यो धर्मः स धर्मः सत्यविक्रम

  

As seen under <http://asi.nic.in/asi_books/8996.pdf>, page 113

  

In the Nirnaya Sagara Press edition 1908 (based on Dakshinatya manuscripts), the below is verse 3-133-11

  

बहून्यो बाधते धर्मो न स धर्मः कुवर्त्म तत्

अविरोधी तु यो धर्मः स धर्मः सत्यविक्रम

  

As seen under <http://asi.nic.in/asi_books/9004.pdf>, page 219
