I think there is no need of other testimony to show Purushottama is Vishnu and Rama himself and Lakshmi, is Sita. to apply to this verse.

  

In any case, the purpose of the verse is clear: even Lord Vishnu himself and Lakshmi, being the bride and bride groom, as Sita and Rama, could not avoid the destiny. in spite of other things auspicious things for the marriage. I don't think moving away from the purpose of verse, does make in any way the meaning clear.
