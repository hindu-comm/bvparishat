What is Conference, Symposium, Colloquium, Research Colloquium, Seminar and Workshop called in Sanskrit?

  

**Discussion Forums**

Conference (परिषत्?) - A discussion or exchange of information among participants who have an agreed (serious) topic

  

Symposium - A meeting or conference for the discussion of some topic especially one in which the scholarly participants form an audience and express opinions, contribute articles, make presentations

  

Colloquium - An academic meeting or seminar usually led by scholars or experts present paper, analyse and discuss on a specific topic

  

Research Colloquium - A colloquium at which the results of (scientific) research are reported

  

Seminar - Any formal meeting for an exchange of ideas and hold discussions with a small group engaged in advanced study and research

  

Workshop (कार्यशाला) - A brief intensive course of instruction and demonstration of applications for a small group; emphasizes problem solving

  

**Research**

Research – (scientific and systematic investigation) careful, diligent and systematic inquiry or investigation into a subject in order to discover or revise facts, theories, applications, etc.; to establish facts or principles or to collect information on a subject
