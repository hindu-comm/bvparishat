धर्म एव हतो हन्ति धर्मो रक्षति रक्षितः।

तस्माद्धर्मो न हन्तव्यो मा नो धर्मो हतो’वधीत्॥  
  

This slokam is from Mahabharatam. While replying to Yaksha for the question 'why Dharmaja is asking for the life of Nakula over Bhima and Arjuna, Dharmaja started his reply with this observation and expressed his reason for the option of Nakula.
