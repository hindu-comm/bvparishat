I shall come to my doubt directly here. In Kiratarjuniya canto 10 shloka 27 starting with मुकुलितमतिशय्य for the word पलाश he quotes Amara - ’पलाशे किंशुकः पर्णः’ where as he should have quoted \`पत्रं पलाशं छदनं दलं पर्णं छदः पुमान् ।' or \`कर्चूरोऽपि पलाशोऽथ कारवेल्लः कटिल्लकः ।'. In the first quoting, it means \`words किंशुकः पर्णः have the meaning of पलाश . In some cases it may or may not be vice versa. THAT IS WHY I HAD THIS DOUBT WHETHER KOSHA CAN BE USED REVERSE WAY TOO? Because, in the first verse of that same canto beginning with अथ परिमलजामवाप्य for the word meaning परिमल he quotes सम्भोगः स्यात्परिमले instead of विमर्दोत्थे परिमलो गन्धे जनमनोहरे । or विमर्दनो परिमलोभ्युपपत्तिरनुग्रहः ।.  
  

Please clarify
