Dear All,  
  
This is inform you all that the Government of Uttar Pradesh, India, has  
announced that the prestigious Viswabharati Award for the year 2013 will  
be given to Prof. N.S.R. Tatacharya for his scholarly work in 4 volumes  
titled "Sabdabodhamimamsa", published by the French Institute of  
Pondicherry and the Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan, New Delhi.  
  
The award will be given to him by the Honourable Chief Minister of Uttar  
Pradesh, Shri Abhishek Yadav, on September 14, 2013, in Lucknow.  
  
For more information on Prof. N.S.R. Tatacharya:  
<http://www.ifpindia.org/Prof-N-S-Ramanuja-Tatacharya.html>
