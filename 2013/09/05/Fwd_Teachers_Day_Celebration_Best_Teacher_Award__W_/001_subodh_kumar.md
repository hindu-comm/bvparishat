Develop talents to develop failsafe solutions to problems, and ability to balance the allocation of resources for (R&D) knowledge development and execution of the projects.

To follow well meditated, fair and just strategies in all situations in short term and long term.

युवोरप्तूर्य्यं हितम्‌ \|\| ऋ3.12.8

To cultivate both the staff functions and line functions- the planners and executors should perform in tandem to implement all projects efficiently.

योजना आयोग और कृयान्वन पक्ष राजा और शासन एक दूसरे के पूरक बन के समस्त योजनाओं को शीघ्रता से सफल बनाते हैं .

Staff & Line Function
