> Dear Respected Scholars  
>   
> I am collecting Stotras and other remedies when husband and wife > quarrel. In दुर्गासहस्रनामस्तोत्र फलश्रुति it is said दम्पत्योः कलहे > प्राप्ते मिथः प्रेमाभिवर्धकम्. This Durga Sahasra Nama is very > powerful to  
> cure any quarrel between husband and wife and make them to love each > other. If they say it regularly the marriage problems will be > solved.  
>   
> Like this are there any other Stotras and remedies like Japa, Homa for > quarreling couple? I would like to know from respected scholars > here.  

  

In Madhva tradition this shloka is recited -

उमा वै वाक् समुद्दिष्टा मनो रुद्र उदाहृतः ।

तयोस्तन्मिथुनं ज्ञात्वा न दाम्पत्यात् विहीयते ॥ (quoted in bra.sU.bhA. 4-2-1 or 2)

  

I have also heard स्वयंवरपार्वती होम. I don't know much about it. Purohits from Udupi region may give more info.
