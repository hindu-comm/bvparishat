Sir, this reminds me of the famous verse of Kalidasa:  
  
वागर्थाविव संपृक्तौ वागर्थप्रतिपत्तये। जगत: पितरौ वन्दे पार्वतीपरमेश्वरौ ।।  
  
In response to the original question, maybe one can take out verses from well-known kAvya-s and other literature where the sAhacharyam of pati and patni is described with several upamAnams. The couple could be naLa and damayantI, Rama and sItA, Indra and sachI, vasiShTha and arundhatI, mAlatI and mAdhava, etc.
