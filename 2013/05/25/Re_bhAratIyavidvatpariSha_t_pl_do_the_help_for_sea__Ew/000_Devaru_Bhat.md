> > According to the information given by Shri Āryasvāmī in the Upodghāta > to the Bhāṭṭacintāmaṇi, a commentary on Khaṇḍadeva’s Bhāṭṭadīpikā by > Vāñcheśvarayajvan(1780-1860), Shri Venkaṭasubrahmaṇyaśāstrī started > publishing the Bhāṭṭacintāmaṇi, in 1927. After his death in 1928 his > son Shri Veṅkaṭarāmaśāstrī and Shri Āryasvāmīśāstrī took up the task. > The book was finally published in 1934 from the Madras Law Journal > Press. The text of the Bhāṭṭadīpikā was taken mainly from a printed > version as it had appeared in Mysore. For the Bhāṭṭacintāmaṇi > Venkaṭasubrahmaṇyaśāstrī had a manuscript. The printed text of the > Bhāṭṭadīpikā often varied from that of the –cintāmaṇi where the > latter’s reading was accepted in the new edition. >
