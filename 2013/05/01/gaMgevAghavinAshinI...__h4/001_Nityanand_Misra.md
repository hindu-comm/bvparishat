I see several problems -

1.  छायेवाखिलतापनाशनकारी breaks the metre - it should करी and not कारी  
2.  तीक्ष्णांशोरपि is strange and I cannot make out the purpose of अपि  
3.  The whole foot तीक्ष्णांशोरपि सत्प्रभेव जगदज्ञानान्धकारापहा is
    strange - the सत्प्रभा of the तीक्ष्णांशु takes away the darkness of
    the world, but not the darkness of ignorance. Satsangati can take
    away darkness of ignorance, but not of the world. What is then the
    samasa in जगदज्ञान which forms part of जगदज्ञानान्धकारापहा?  
4.  छायेवाखिलतापनाशनकरी->छाया can destroy only आधिभौतिक ताप but not
    आधिदैविक or आध्यात्मिक, so how canअखिलतापनाशनकरी property be
    compared to छाया?
