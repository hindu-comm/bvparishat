The verses had been orally learnt in childhood as Cātakasubhāṣitas. Later I found a book in my family collection where they occur on pages 236-237. The book must have belonged to the third generation upward from me. It is dilapidated beyond use but the title page is still legible. It informs Subhāṣitaratnabhāṇḍāgāra, Nirṇaya Sagar Press, Mumbai, 1911. Collected by Kashinath P.Parab and ed. Vasudev L. Panashikar.
