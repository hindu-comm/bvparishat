The Collection of परिभाषा-s called परिभाषासंग्रहः has got a collection ascribed to व्याडि, who predates पतञ्जलि according to the commentators, and the author of संग्रह mentioned by पतञ्जलि:

  

परिभाषासंग्रहः : व्याडिशाकटायनादिप्रोक्तपरिभाषापाठानां तत्प्रणीतवृत्तीनां च संग्रहः

अभ्यंकरोपाह्व-वासुदेवशास्त्रिसुत-काशिनाथेन संकलितः संशोधितश्च

（Post-graduate and Research Department series, क्रमाङ्कः 7）

Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute, 1967

タイトル別名  
Paribhāṣāsaṁgraha (a collection of original works on Vyākaraṇa Paribhāṣās)

परिभाषा संग्रहः : व्याडि शाकटायन आदि प्रोक्त परिभाषा पाठानाम् तत् प्रणीत वृत्तीनाम् च संग्रहः

Paribhāṣāsaṅgraha

&nbsp;

タイトル読み  
परिभाषा संग्रहः : व्याडि शाकटायन आदि प्रोक्त परिभाषा पाठानाम् तत् प्रणीत वृत्तीनाम् च संग्रहः

Paribhāṣāsaṅgrahaḥ : Vyāḍiśākaṭāyanādiproktaparibhāṣāpāṭhānāṃ tatpraṇīta

  

The संग्रह of व्याडि though now not available, should be the original source of the परिभाषा-s. Whether the author/compiler of the collection व्याडिपरिभाषा can be identified with the grammarian mentioned by पतञ्जलि may be open for debate on valid grounds and unless otherwise, we can take the identification granted and could take the work of व्याडि as the source, even if the identity could not be established, which is a similar but larer work than महाभाष्य. But for confirmation, the work lost should be available, otherwise not.
