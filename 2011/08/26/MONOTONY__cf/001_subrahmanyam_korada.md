(6-4-174) Patanjali discusses Ekas'ruti --  
**  
'ekas'rutinirdes'At siddham' (**VArtikam**) - **ekas'ruti.h svarasarvanAma , yathA napumsakam lingasarvanAma .  
**  
Pradipa -- **svarabhedapratyastamayena** , nipAtanam sarvasvaralaukikaprayogasamgrahArthamityartha.h ,yathA napumsakamiti , sAmAnyasthitau AvirbhAvatirobhAvasthitivis'[es.An.Am](http://es.An.Am) antarbhAvAt  
  
**Pratyastamaya= tyAga.h .**  
  
If thee is no any difference in terms of **UdAtta , AnudAtta and Svarita then that UccAra.na is called Ekas'ruti .**  
  
Just like **Napumsakam is neither Pumlinga nor StrIlinga -- refer to
sarUpAn.Am ekas'es.a ekavibhaktau ( 1-2-64) or 'striyAm' (4-1-3) for Linga with help of Gunas from SAmkhya .  
**  
**S'rautasUtram -- tAnena yaj\~nakarman.i -- during Yajn\~nakarma the Mantras are to be pronounced in Ekas'ruti (=tAna).  
**  
**Pracaya = UdAtta (PrAtis'Akhya)
