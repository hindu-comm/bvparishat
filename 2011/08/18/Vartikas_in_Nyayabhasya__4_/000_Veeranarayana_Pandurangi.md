dear all,

there occur many passages in Nyayabhasya which seem to be authored by some other person. the very first sentenceप्रवृत्तिसामर्थ्यादर्थवत्प्रमाणम्seems so because Bhasyakara comments upon it. he would not comment if it is his sentence. likewise there are many passages in तदत्यन्तविमोक्षोपवर्गः also. I doubt they are Vartikas by some unknown person.

scholars may clarify.
