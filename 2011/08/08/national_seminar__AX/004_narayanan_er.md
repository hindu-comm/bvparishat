There is a popular story regarding health.  
The Asvinidevas disguised in the form of two birds chirped : कोऽरुक् ? कोऽरुक् ? (who is not ill?)  
Aalathiyoor Nampi, a great physician replied in an aaryaa verse :  
  
काले हितमितभोजी  
कृतचङ्क्रमणः क्रमेण वामशयः ।  
युक्ताहारविहारः  
स्त्रीषु च यतात्मा सोऽरुगरुक् ।।  
  
(He is not ill, one who eats that he likes in limited quantity, having habit of roaming around, tentatively sleeping left side down, consuming suitable food and having regular patient walk and one who controls self while activities with women).  
  
The Asvinidevas pleased with Nampi's answer and blessed him. The story belongs to the Aitihyamala of K. Sankunni.
