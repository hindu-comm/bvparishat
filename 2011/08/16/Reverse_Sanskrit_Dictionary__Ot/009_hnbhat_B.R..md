The order is followed by ending or beginning in the case of नानार्थवर्ग after the synonyms listed under different headings semantically classified in general lexicons like Amara etc. or otherwise specialized एकाक्षरकोश, द्व्यक्षरकोश or two, three or more अक्षर-s in the order in the polysemy or homonym sections. Here is the system followed by Yadavaprakasha in his वैजयन्तीकोश, which after he finished listing synonyms and genders differently ordered in previous sections:

  

अथ काण्डैरनेकार्थाः प्रोच्यन्तेऽत्र परैस्त्रिभिः।

द्व्यक्षरास्त्र्यक्षराः शेषा इति काण्डेषु ते क्रमात्॥ वैज.६.१.१॥

अकारादिहकारान्तं नाम्नामाद्यक्षरक्रमात्।

संग्रहो द्व्यक्षरादीनां प्रोक्तान् प्रायो विना पुरा॥॥ वैज.६.१.२॥

  

(Vaijayantikosha of Yadavaprakashacharya, Ed.Haragovinda Shastri,Chowkhambha Sanskrit Series Office, Varanasi, 1971)

  

Amarasimha follows a different order of classification of homonyms
ordered by the in the consonants the word ends without taking into the ending of the nominal stem, in general in the inflected form:

  

नानार्थाः केऽपि कान्तादिवर्गेष्वेवात्र कीर्तिताः।

भूरिप्रयोगा ये येषु पर्यायेष्वपि तेषु ते॥ Amara 3.3.1)

  

Even though many of them are listed in the classified synonyms group in their popular group, some of them are listed here also under कान्तवर्ग, खान्तवर्ग etc. Thus in जान्तवर्ग-

  

केकितार्क्षयावहिभुजौ दन्तविप्राण्डजा द्विजाः॥ (अमर ३.३.३०a-b)

  

अहिभुज् is listed with द्विज both ending in ज. The first end with ज in the inflected form listed here, without considering the the case ending inflections.

  

(Amarakosha, with Ramashrami, Chowkhambha Sanskrit Series Office, Varanasi, 1970)

  

It is the same order in मेदिनी, विश्वप्रकाश etc. specially designed for नानार्थ, including the section in त्रिकाण्डशेष of Purushottamadeva.

  

Otherwise, they follow generally a semantic classified group on the model of Amarakosha. But in these, no ordering by their ending vowels is seen. Even within the kaanta varga, no alphabetical order of the final vowel is seen followed. Thus,

  

बृहती क्षुद्रवार्ताकी-छन्दोभेदे महत्यपि।

वासिता स्त्रीकरिण्योश्च वार्ता वृत्तौ जनश्रुतौ॥

  

बृहती is followed by वासिता and वार्ता, the common ending being the consonant "t"and not त or ति under तान्तवर्ग. It is the same with the section of नानार्थवर्ग in त्रिकाण्डशेष also.

  

सूर्यावर्तेऽपि जामाता ना वाते विष्टपे जगत्।

जातिश्छन्दसि मालत्यां चुल्ल्यां जातीफले कुले॥३.२/१६७\|\|

व्याप्तविस्तृतवाद्येषु ततं ..............(त्रिकाण्डशेष ३. १५८a

  

ending "a" followed with ending in "i" again followed by तत but common ending "t" for the three. The Vishvaprakasha also follows the same system under कान्त, खान्त etc. ordered by the number of अक्षर-s within the group.

(त्रिकाण्डशेष of पुरुषोत्तमदेव, Venkateshwar Steam Press, Bombay. 1919)

  

एक-द्वि-त्रि-चतुः-पञ्च-षड्वर्णानुक्रमोज्ज्वलैः।

कान्तादिवर्गैर्नानार्थसङ्ग्रहोऽयं वितन्यते॥विश्वप्रकाश १.१.१॥

  

Vishvaprakasha of Maheshwara Suri,

Chowkhambha Amarabharati Prakashan,

Chowkhambha Sanskrit Series 37, 2nd Edn1983.

  

  

This is the classification by the ending consonants, in the above lexicons and not sorting by vowels is seen in the above as I have seen.

  

This sorting by the ending syllables is seen in the listing of धातु-s also in the धातुपाठ within the group.
