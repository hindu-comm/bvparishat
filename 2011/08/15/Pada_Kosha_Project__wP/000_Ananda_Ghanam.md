Pranaam.

A humble effort to put an online dictionary on scalable, extendable and collaborative, non-profit, free-sharing platform (with Unicode fonts) is on.

Researchers, students are welcome to join the initiative. Needless to say, this is a project by academics for academics, similar to BVP forum.

  

Any sort of participation is welcome. Profound scholars to starters can join. Proof Reading is the most important job on hand.

Full-timers ( 6- 8 hours)/ part timers (minimum an hour/ 5 hours a week). Apart from volunteering scholars, unemployed persons in need of remunerative support are also welcome to join.

  

(Since, this is internet based platform, physical relocation/ proximity are not an issue at all.)

  

Gestation period for First Phase is 2 - 3 months, to bring the first Kosha to the domain of Parishat.
