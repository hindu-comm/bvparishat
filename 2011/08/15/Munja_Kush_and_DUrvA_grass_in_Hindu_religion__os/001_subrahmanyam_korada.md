**Kalpa -- Upanayanam - Mau\~njI mekhalA and Ajinam --  
**  
as'mani sthApito yena vAso'pi paridhAya ca I  
mekhalAmajinam caiva yAvadas'mAnamAcaret II  
  
mau\~njyajinAvasthApanaproks.an.Ani tu I  
samantrametAnAcArya.h pUrvam kr.tvAtha vAcayet II  
  
iyam duruktAditi dvAbhyam paridIya vAcayet --  
  
**Mau\~njI(Mantra.h)** -- iyam duruktAtparibAdhamAnA s'armavarUdham punatIva AgAt I prAn.ApAnAbhyAm balamAbharanti priyA devAnAg'm subhagA mekhaleyam IIr.tasya goptrI tapasa.h parasvI ghnatI raks.assahamAnA arAtI II  
  
Meaning -- BrahmacArI , while putting on the girdle of three strands and made of munja grass says --  
this girdle destroys the defects in our conversation , while purifying our house it has come to us , it provides strength to PrA.na and ApAna , it is liked by gods , beautiful , protects Satyam and Tapas completely , kills RAks.asas'aktis , insults enemies . O! MekhalA ! by you are auspicious and by wearing you ,we will be in a position to run this As'rama with more caliber and as per the S'Astra .
