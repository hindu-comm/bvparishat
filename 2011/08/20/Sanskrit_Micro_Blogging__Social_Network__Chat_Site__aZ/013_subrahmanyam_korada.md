**Linga and Gun.as  
**  
This aspect is widely discussed in **Mahabhasyam under 'sarUpAn.Am ekas'es.a ekavibhaktau' (1-2-64) --  
  
dArA.h (pumlinga), bhAryA(strIlinga) , kalatram(napumsakalinga , this word is mostly seen in Jyotis.am) - means wife .  
**  
**padArtha.h (pum) , vyakti.h (strI) , dravyam(napumsaka) - all the words mean the same .  
**  
In **vyakaranam ' lingam as'is.yam lokAs'rayatvAt lingasya' - is the Niyama .** One should depend on Kos'as etc. for Linga .  
  
There is one verse to decide Linga --  
  
**stanakes'avatI nArI lomas'a.h purus.a.h smr.ta.h ...(here kes'a means bhagam)  
**  
Patanjali offers the following --  
  
tasmAnna vaiyAkaran.ai.h s'akyam laukikam lingam AsthAtum , avas'yam kas'cit svakr.tAnta Astheya.h . ko'sau svakr.tAnta.h ?  
**samstyAnaprasavau lingam** . samstyAnaprasavau lingamAstheyau . kimidam samstyAnaprasavAviti ? samstyAne styAyate.h drat.  
strI sUte.h sap prasave pumAn . ... kasya puna.h styAnam strI , praver.ttirvA pumAn ? gun.AnAm , kes.Am ? **s'abdaspars'arUparasagandhAnAm** . sarvAs'ca punarmUrtaya.h evamAtmikA.h samstyAnaprasavagun.A.h s'abdaspars'arUparasagandhavatya.h . ..... kuto vyavasthA ? ' vivaks.Ata.h ' samstyAnavivaks.AyAm strI , prasavavivaks.AyAm pumAn , ubhayorapyavivaks.AyAm napumsakam .  
  
**PradIpa of Kaiyata --**  
  
**gun.AnAm [sattvarajastama.hparin.AmarUpAn.Am](http://sattvarajastama.hparin.AmarUpAn.Am) s'abdAdInAm vr.ddhihrAsamadhyamAvasthA.h s'abdaikagocarA.h lingatvena parigr.hyante**  
  
sarvAs'ca punarmUrtaya evamAtmakA.h -- of **Patanjali is following
VedAnta - all things fall under teja or ap or anna . Refer to S'AnkarabhAs.yam for details .**  
  
So finally **Vaiyakaranas depend upon** **SAmkhyasiddhAnta of Trigun.as for deciding Linga to a S'abda .**
