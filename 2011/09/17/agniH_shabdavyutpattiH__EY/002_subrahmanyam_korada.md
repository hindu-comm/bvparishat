namo vidvadbhya.h  
  
Vyakaranam is S'abdAnusasanam and not Arthanusasanam . Niruktam is Arthanusasanam .  
  
Therefore , the Bhasyam is quite right as well as Panini/ Unadi .  
  
The problem is by mixing both .  
  
Can't there be some meaning thru Prakrtipratyayavibhaga ? it is , but Niruktam is final .  
  
Patanjali under 'samarthah padavidhih' (2-1-1) asserts - nahyarthA
Adis'yante , cArthe dvandva.h ityAdInAm cArthe vartamAnasya ityAdirartha.h .  
  
dhanyo'smi
