namo vidvadbhya.h  
  
asandehArtham vYAkaran.am .  
  
Panini hints at VyaktAnukaran.a and AvyaktAnukaran.a -- 'avyaktAnukaran.asyAta itau'(6-1-98)- pararUpam , 'avyaktAnukaran.Ad dvyajavarArdhAdanitau d.Ac'(5-4-57) .  
  
In the case of Abhijnanasakuntalam etc it is possible to have the understanding as it is popular and the TaddhitArtha ( adhikr.tya kr.te granthe 4-3-87) is evident .  
  
!.In the case of 'Anyacca' it is difficult to understand that it is the name of a book .  
  
2. Also , even if it became popular (abhyupetyavAda) the last letter , being an Avyaya does not take a sup (Dr Bhat is right) .  
  
3.Since it is an Anukaran.am it is better to append 'iti' as it is the practice across works like Manjusa of Nagesa , i.e. they were careful while quoting from any work . Generally we do not have such names .  
  
So it should be - ' anyacceti granthe' following 'asandehArtham' or still 'anyacca' iti granthe / 'anyaccanAmake granthe' .  
  
dhanyo'smi
