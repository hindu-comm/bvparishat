dear all,

it is an interesting question put forth by our Peethadhishji Prof. Dayananda bhargava that there is a novel by prof. Radhavallabha tripathiji named "अन्यच्च".

  

can we say that "आचार्य त्रिपाठिभिः अन्यच्चे (i.e. सप्तमी विभक्ति) स्वकीयकवितासामर्थ्यं सम्यगभिव्यक्तम्"?

that means is it possible that we can add saptami after the name of novel अन्यच्च? like "अभिज्ञानशाकुन्तलेस्वकीयकवितासामर्थ्यं सम्यगभिव्यक्तम्"

in support of adding the vibhakti there our VC Prof. Devanathan has given an example \<"वो कौन थी" में कथानक बहुत अच्छा है> here saptami is added after the name of novel.

  

Prof. Bhargava posed a more difficult question as to if the name of novel is "रचनाः" what will be the saptami rupa? is it रचनासु? रचनाःसु?
रचनाःयाम्?
