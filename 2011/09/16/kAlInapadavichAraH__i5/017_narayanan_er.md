Respected Professor Iyengar,  
M.M. Williams in his dictionary puts the term केन्द्रम् as the centre of a circle etc. He says it is a borrowed word from Greek term κέντρον (kentron) and it is discussed in the Sabdakalpadruma as an astronomical term लग्नम् and interestingly no etymology has been provided. The Siddhantashiromani says:  
वृत्तस्य मध्यं किल केन्द्रमुक्तं केन्द्रं ग्रहोच्चान्तरमुच्यतेऽतः।  
यतोऽन्तरे तावति तुङ्गदेशान्नीचोच्चवृत्तस्य सदैव केन्द्रम्॥ (सि.शि. गोलाध्यायः)  

It is natural that there are Indian astronomical terms are heard in resemblance with Greek terms like हृद्रोग etc.  

Regards,  
Narayanan
