Though there is no difference in meaning, the first highlighted in red are not acceptable if you follow Paninian Grammar. In the second is acceptable forms. The verb being "ग्रह् उपादाने" which is covered under the purview of the rule:

  
6-1-16 ग्रहिज्यावयिव्यधिवष्टिविचतिवृश्चतिपृच्छतिभृज्जतीनां ङिति च

  

will take the form गृह् in the first क्त suffix, and other where "क्" or "ङ्" are dropped. so गृहीतः, अनुगृहीतः, गृहीतवान्, जगृहे, etc.

  

But in other suffixes, it will retain its original form ग्रह् - as in the cases:

  

ग्रहीता, ग्राहकः, ग्रहः, ग्रहणम्, जग्राह etc. This is the lucid explanation of Paninian Grammar applicable in this case.
