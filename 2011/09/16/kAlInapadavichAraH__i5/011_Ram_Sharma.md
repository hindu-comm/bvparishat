Respected Scholars,

I am not sure about what I am attempting for something wrong or right. I request the grammarians to correct me.

कालात् (५.१.७८) is an adhikara sutra till व्युपदिष्टात् (५.१.९७). We have another rule मासाद्वयसि यत्खञौ (५.१.८१) and we take खञ् suffix from this rule to apply with काल to have कालीन?? If possible, on what meaning we apply खञ्?  

The Tattvabodhini says: स्वरूपग्रहणमिह न भवति,.....मासादीनां कालेन विशेषणात् etc.  

I did not consult neither Nyasa or Padamanjari in this regard, as the Tattvabodhini hints to those works.

S ūta (अजेर्व्यघञपोः २.४.५६) says: प्राप्तिज्ञो देवानां प्रियः, न तु इष्टिज्ञः, इष्यत एतद्रूपम्। Similarly, is कालीन an इष्टरूप?  

निवृत्तः, अधीष्टः, भृतः, भूतः, भावी, वयः are the meanings recommended in the sutras for the suffixes ठञ्, यत् and खञ् under कालात् by Panini. We hear the form मासीनः where मास is a type of काल। Does it means that कालात् = not कालशब्दात् but कालार्थकशब्दात् apart from the term काल ?

It is clear that the suffix खञ् is not applied to the form काल but to the forms of different periods of time. If खञ् is possible to apply with काल then आयनेयीनीयियः फढखछघां प्रत्ययादीनाम्‌ (७.१.२) substitution इन for ख is possible. I could not follow Dr. Patel's comment- "It might be possible that the suffix is not allowed in isolation." I request the grammarians to correct me. Thanks.

Regards,  
Narayanan
