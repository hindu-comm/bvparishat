namo vidvadbhyah  
  
avidhipUrvakam yajanti (GItA 9-23)  
  
  
**avidhipUrvakam iti kriyAvis'[es.an.am](http://es.an.am) .  
**  
**na vidhi.h - avidhi.h -- UttarapadArthapradhAna.h Na\~ntatpurus.ah - na\~n (Pani 2-2-6)  
  
avidhi.h pUrva.h yasmin karman.i tat avidhipUrvakam -- BahuvrIhi.h - anekam anyapadArthe (Pan 2-2-24) , kap by 's'es.AdvibhAs.A (Pan5-4-154) .  
**  
**kriyAvis'es.n.AnAm karmatvam nityanapumsakatA ca -- yathA mr.du
pacati -- avidhipUrvakam yajanti .  
  
Why not AvyayIbhAva by 'avyayam vibhakti... (Pan2-1-6) in ' arthAbhAva' ?  
  
AvyayIbhAva is PUrvapadArthapradhAna -- [maks.ikAn.Am](http://maks.ikAn.Am) abhAva.h nirmaks.ikam - here 'atyantAbhAva.h vivaks.ita.h' by Avyayam(nir)  
  
(Na\~n) Tatpurus.a is UttarapadArthapradhAna -- na brAhmana.h abrAhman.a.h -- Aropito brAhma.na.h - arthAt brAhman.abhinna.h .  
Here the Vis'es.ya , brAhman.a is important . Aropitatva is dyotya by na\~n . If you take VAcakatvam to na\~n then 'AropamAtram" .  
Na\~nvAcyam is Vis'[es.na](http://es.na) into uttarapadArtha .  
  
These discussions are there in BhAsyam and other commentaries - Praudhamanorama, S'abdaratna, S'ekhara etc.  
  
Here is Praudhamanorama under 'na\~n' - uttarapadArthapradhAno'yam samAsa.h . tathA hi - Aropitatvam na\~nA dyotyate . AropamAtram vA . vis.ayatvam tu samsarga.h . tathA ca abrAhman.as'abdAdAropito brAhman.a iti bodhe arthAt brAhman.abhinna iti paryavasyati .  
  
S'abdaratna -- [spas.t.am](http://spas.t.am) cedam bhAs.ye . .... evamanyatrApi bodhyam . etadeva bhAsyasammatam ....  
**  
Bonus Info ---  
  
**Meaning according to 12 commentaries on GitA --  
  
1.SAnkarabhAs.yam , Anandagiri, HanUmatpais'AcabhAs.yam , VenkatanAthabrahmAnandagiri, VallbhatattvadIpikA, Purus.ottama -Amr.tataran'gin.I -- aj\~nAnapUrvakam bhajanti .  
  
2.RAmAnujabhAs.yam says - indrAdInAm . 'caturhotAro yatra sampadam gacchanti devai.h' (Taitti Aran 4) ityAdIni vidadhati . na tatpUrvakam yajanti .  
  
3.TAtparycandrikA of Des'ka - vidhi.h svaj\~nAnadvArA yasya pUrvam kAran.am tadvidhipUrvam , tadanyadavidhipUrvam .  
  
4. MAdhvabhAs.yam and Jayateertha-prameya-deepikA -- tarhi 'aham kratu.h'(9-16) ityAdi asatyamityAha - ye'pIti .  
  
5. Neelakan.thabhAs.yadeepikA -- vidhi.h abhedabuddhi.h tadrAhityAt avidhipUrvakam  
  
6.Abhinavagupta and Guru -- na hi brahmavyatireki kimcidupAsyam asti , kintvavidhinA iti vis'es.a.h . avidhi.h anyo vidhi.h , na tu avidhinA = dus.t.avidhinA , asmadgurava.h -- avidhinA dus.t.ena vidhinA .  
  
In TaittirIya , while commenting on ' asadbrahmeti veda cet' , S'amkarAcArya says - asat is also Brahman only . So it is due to Aj\~nAna that people worship Indra etc. still no problem it finally belongs to me only .  
  
Give up all other Karmas and try to have J\~nAna thru BrahmasAks.AtkAra - is the gospel .  
  
In TarkapAda (PUrvamImAmsA) - the term 'dars'anam means 'uccAran.am'. So here also 'vidhi' means 'j\~nAnam' .
