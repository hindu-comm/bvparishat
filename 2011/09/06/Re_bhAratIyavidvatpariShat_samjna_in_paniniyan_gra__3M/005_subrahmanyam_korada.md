What is the exact meaning of the word 'SamjnyA'?



There is a chapter "SamjnyA PrakaraNam" in the book "VyAkaraNa-Candrodaya" of late Pundit Shree CArudeva Shastri. It
explains samjnyAs of PAniNi grammar.



Now I have a simple question- Could samjnyA have a specific meaning?Examples 'Svara' is samjnyA. Vyanjana is samjnyA, Upasarga is samjnyA. But tree (SAmAnya nAma), NArAyaNa (Vishes'a nAma), he, she (SarvanAma) are not samjnyAs.



Please help me to resolve my thoughts. Thanks. N.R.Joshi



In **Astadhyayi the term 'samj\~nA' is employed in several meanings** --  
  
1.**sam + j\~nA (kryAdi - avabodhane) (bhvAdi and curadi are also there) - bhAve an' by ' Atas'copasarge' (3-3-106) --samj\~nAnam samj\~nA = understanding (of Linga, SamkhyA etc) .**  
  
-- **tadas'is.yam samj\~nApramAn.atvAt 1-2-53)** -- yuktavadbhAva **(lupi yuktavadvyaktivacane 1-2-51)** is not to be ruled ( na s'Asitavya.h)  
because there is **samj\~nA = avagama.h/sampratyaya.h** and the same is an authority .  
just like in the case of dArA.h (wife , pumlinga, nityabahuvacanam) , vars.A.h (rainy season , strIlinga, nityabahuvacanam) etc. it is  
possible that people will have the 'samj\~nA' = understanding and therefore in the case of **'pancAlA.h,' , 'varan.A.h' etc.also 'yuktavadbhAva' need not be ruled .  
**  
**yuktavadbhAva** - means both **vis'es.an.a and vis'es.ya** having the same **Linga and SamkhyA** - s'ukla.h pat.a.h , nIlAni utpalAni etc.  
  
**BhAs.yam -- (samjnAs'[abdArthanirn.ayAdhikaran.am](http://abdArthanirn.ayAdhikaran.am)) - kim yA etA.h kr.trimA.h t.i - ghu - bha - Adisamj\~nA.h
tatprAmAn.yAdas'is.yam ?  
netyAha , samj\~nAnam samj\~nA tadas'yam**  
-- iti na Aha pAn.ini.h , by the word samj\~nA , which is in bhAva , one should not take **artificial Samj\~nas of PAn.ini** like t.i **(aco'ntyAdi t.i 1-1-63) , ghu (dAdAghvadAp 1-1-19)** etc. but yaugika - samj\~nAnam , i.e. understanding (see Kaiyata and Nagesa) .  
  
2. .**Samj\~nAsUtrAn.i -- samyak j\~nAyate anayA iti samj\~nA -- karan.e an'** ----  
  
**Laghvartham smj\~nAkaran.am** - declares Patanjali . Panini compiled six types of SUtras . **Samj\~nAsUtras are like 'vr.ddhi.h Adaic'(1-1-1) etc. A, ai and au - are called Vr.ddhi in my S'Astra .**  
Such Sutras will get **ekavakyatA** with VidhisUtra following two Paribhasas - **yathoddes'am samj\~nAparibhAs.am and kAryakAlam samj\~naparibhAs.am . Vr.ddhireci (6-1-85) , mr.jervr.ddhi.h (7-2-114) etc are VidhisUtras** where the above Samj\~nA is applicable .  
  
3. Sometimes there may be confusion - **PAn.ini had 'samkhyA' as a Samj\~nA - bahugan.avatud.ati samkhyA (1-1-23) and we have SamkhyA
outsie the S'Astra . Which one to take ?**  
  
The general norm is - **kr.trimAkr.trimayo.h kr.trime kAryasampratyaya.h ( kr.trima prevails** ) -- but there is a **ParibhAs.A** implied by PAn.ini thru the SUtra - **samkhyAyA atis'adantAyA.h kan (5-1-22) --  
**  
**ubhayagatiriha bhavati (ParibhAs.endus'ekhara - 10) - either can be taken - commentary is the resort .  
**  
4.**samj\~nAyAm
samaja-nis.ada-nipata-mana-vida-s.u\~n-s'In'-bhr.\~n-i.n.h (3-3-99) - kyap-pratyaya -- vidyA , s'ayyA etc.are considered as Samj\~nas .**  
  
5. **samj\~nAyAm (3-3-109)** n.vul - uddAlakapus.pabha\~njikA -- name of a game .  
  
6**.aiun. - r.l.k etc. , svaujasaumaut.chas... ( 4-1-2**) - these are called **Samj\~nAsUtras - thru Laks.n.Avr.tti** , i.e. are useful in generating Samj\~nas like ac , hal , sup etc.(see **Laghus'abdendus'ekhara - Samj\~nAprakaran.am) .  
**  
7. **under 'r.l.k' , Patanjali and other commentators discuss the Samj\~nas (catus.t.atI s'abdAnAm pravr.tti.h - the behavior of s'abdas is four-fold - jAti , gun.a, kriyA and yadr.cchA (pravr.ttinimittam**) . Under yadr.cchAs'abdas , Samj\~nA is discussed . **The SiddhAnta is that any name should conform to VyAkaran.am . So 'kUcI' ,'mancI' (kUcimanci - is the sirname of some Brahmins in Andhra and first quoted by Haradattamis'ra in Padama\~njarI ) etc. are not acceptable .  
**  
**Kr.dantam nAma kuryAt -- is KalpasUtram** (Gr.hya).  
  
8**.sarvAdIni sarvanAmAni (1-1-26**) - PAn.ini borrowed from his predecessors - **sarvanAma is a Samj\~nA** . Here it is Tadgun.asamvij\~nAna.h bahuvrIhi.h , i.e. 'sarva' is also to be counted (Atadgun.asamvij\~nAnabahuvrIhi is - citragum Anaya -- fetch the person with cows of assorted colors -- the cows are not to be fetched -- **citrA.h gAva.h yasya sa.h citragu.h )  
**  
9. **MImAmsA -- Samj\~nA is called Namadheya (-vAkyAni) -- udbhidA yajeta pas'ukAma.h (TAnd.ya - SAma) , jyotis.t.omena svargakAmo yajeta (SAma) - Udbhid and Jyotis.t.oma are names of YAgas .  
**
