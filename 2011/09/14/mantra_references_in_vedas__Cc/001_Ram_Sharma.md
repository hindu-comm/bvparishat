For Dr Gururaja Ji.Vide CarakasaMhita ,Cikitsasthana Ch 3 verses 310-15

SomaM saanucaraM devaM samaatRgaNam IIshvaram//

puujayan prayataH shiighraM mucyate visHamajvaraat/

VisHNuM sahasramuurdhaanaM caraacarapatiM vibhum//

stuvan naamasahasreNa jvaraan sarvaan apohati/

BrahmaaNam ashvinaav IndraM HutabhaksHaM Himaacalam//

GaNGaaM MarudgaNaaMsh ce'sHTyaa puujayaN jayati jvaraan/

bhaktyaa maatuH pitush cai'va GuruuNaaM puujanena ca//

 brahmacaryeNa tapasaa satyena niyamena ca/

japahomapradaanena vedaanaaM shravaNena ca//

jvaraad vimucyate shiighraM saadhuunaaM darshanena ca/
