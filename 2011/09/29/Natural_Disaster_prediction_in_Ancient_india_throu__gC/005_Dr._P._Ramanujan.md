Dear Sir!

Animal behaviour, even normal, as observed will serve a good purpose  
towards your end. In Sabara Bhashya there is a mention of dogs fasting on  
krishna caturdasi nights while discussing adhikara for Vedic rituals in  
codanAlakshana.
