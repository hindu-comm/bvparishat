What is meant by Maatrikaa and Maatrikaa vijnyaana?



Dear Dr. V. Pandurangi, Your idea of bringing out a book on Shodhapravidhi and Maatrikavijnyan in SAnskrit is good idea. However please also prepare English version with diacritical marks for Sanskrit words along with (devanagari spelling in parathesis). The suggestion of Dr. Korada is worth following. I read his book ,"Theory of Language" andunderstood a lot of Sanskrit terms in new contexts. He briefly talked about Tantayukti in Aayurveda in his book. In the same way the book of Dr. Kapil Kapoor, "Dimensions of Panini Grammar" explains many SAnskrit words with appropriate English terminology.



Now I have question for you. What is meant bu Maatrikaa and Maatrikaa vijnyaana? Apte disctionary-Characters used to construct certain diagrams supposed to have magical powers. Do Maatrikaa simply mean letters of Sanskrit alphabet? Thanks. NRJOSHI
