Dear All

I announce the following new publications.

**  
**

**प्रमाणपद्धतिः**

with kannada translation and critical notes in Kannada script. edited and translated by Dr. Pandurangavithala Pandurangi and Dr. Madhava Pandurangi. This is the first full translation of Pramanapaddhati to appear in Kannada. Pramanapaddhati of Shri Jayatirtha which is a primary manual of dviata epistemology is divided as प्रत्यक्ष अनुमान and शब्द paricchedas. pp X+189. Published by poornaprajna samshodhana Mandiram Banglore 2011. Rs.120.

**  
**

**कृष्णामृतमहार्णवः एवं सदाचारस्मृतिः of Madhvacharya**with Hindi Translation byVeeranarayana N.K. Pandurangi. published by माध्वतत्त्वज्ञानप्रसादप्रतिष्ठान प्रयाग (श्रीपेजावरस्वामी जी अभिवन्दन समिति)**कृष्णामृतमहार्णवः and सदाचारस्मृतिः**are the works of Madhvacharya dealing with vaishnava achaara. Most of the shlokas are drawn from different puranas to explain the importance of Bhakti towards god his worship. Etext of this work can be shared with interested people. pp.X+140. Rs75.

  

**भारतीयदर्श की मौलिक अवधारणाएं** Dr. Sohan raj Tated and Dr. Vidyasagar simha. Literary circle Jaipur 2011. pp. 255, Rs 795.
