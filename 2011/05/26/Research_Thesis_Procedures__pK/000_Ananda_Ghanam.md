Dear Scholars!

  

After reading the comments of Shri Varakhedi and Jagannatha, I have a couple of points about some research aspects, which I have come across recently.

  

1.  Regarding Procedures: The research submission is atedious process,
    especially collecting the no dues certificate from Library and
    Hostel. Rules in some of the Universities mandate the two
    certificates, irrespective of the fact that the research fellow has
    applied for a membership in the library or residential facility in
    the hostel at first place; and ever used the facility?. These
    unnecessary procedural flaws can well be removed, to protect the
    students from callous officers/ low level comments of lower
    employees. 2.  Every researcher has to include a section called: **Benefits of this
    thesis and likely beneficiaries. (In line with traditional Shastra
    Format of Prayojanam, Adhikaari.** Normally, Vishaya and Sangati
    are discussed, but the above mentioned in detail. Any such inclusion
    and further publicising the same could endear the subject to reach
    its logical conclusion of reaching the intended sections; instead of
    the work being confined to the dusting almiras of offices.

  
With Warm Regards  
Vamshi K Ghanapathi
