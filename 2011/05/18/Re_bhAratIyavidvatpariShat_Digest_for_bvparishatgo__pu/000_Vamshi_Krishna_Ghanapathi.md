It is interesting ton read the dialogue between young students and seasoned scholars. Here the evident fact remains that there is a requirement and perception gap. Those scholars with links with the mighty in the HRD sector and Government: Can you visualize creation of series of jobs for linguists with simpler skills, along with the skill honing preparatory courses, to empower our students looking bleak in their outlook and prospective?

  

One set which could be very useful is to train our Archakas and Purohits apart from village folks, in many of our villages. Here also,as ably suggested, basic requirements of administrative language, letter writing for all purposes, courses on ethics, culture, morality, history, geography, civic sense, traffic, health points and required aspects for the society can be included in the courses.

  

Sanskrit scholars, researchers should be able to meet most of these requirements of the society as a resource person of the area, attached to a Panchayat, Block Development Office, school etc.

  

Please think about this seriously, if you are in a position to influence the Governance, even n micro scale.

  

With warm regards

Vamshi Krishna Ghanapathi
