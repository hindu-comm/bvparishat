Neither अष्टौ nor अष्ट are dual but plural, because eight can hardly be a dual. The rule अष्टाभ्य औश् (7-1-21) provides an affix औश् in place of जस् and शस्, the substituted plural affixes for Nominative and Accusative Cases respectively. अष्ट cannot be a singular like कर्म or अष्टौ cannot be a dual like वृक्षौ; though they appear to be so. Endings, genders and numbers of any noun is depended upon its suffix nature.
