[as.t.an](http://as.t.an) - ghr.tAdi - antodAttasvara.h  
  
PAn.ini etc are Smartas and not [Sras.t.as](http://Sras.t.as) - i.e. prayogas'[aran.am](http://aran.am) vyAkaran.am .  
  
sidhe s'abdArthasambandhe lokato'rthaprayukte s'abdaprayoge s'Astren.a dharmaniyama.h (VArtikam) .  
  
nityAs's'abdArthasambandhAstatrAmnAtA mahars.ibhi.h I  
[sUtrAn.AmanutantrAn.Am](http://sUtrAn.AmanutantrAn.Am) [bhAs.yAn.Am](http://bhAs.yAn.Am) ca pran.etr.bhi.h II (VAkyapadIyam - 1-23)  
  
indriyAn.Am svavis.ayes.vanAdiryogyatA yathA I  
anAdirarthais's'bdAnAm sambandho yogyatA tath/A II ( VAkya - Pada - Sambandha)  
  
There is , no doubt , VaicitrI in the form of S'abdas in Sanskrit --  
  
1. ciram , ciren.a , cirAya etc - what about other forms ?  
  
2. Kr.t is directly added on a DhAtu . But s'atr. and s'Anac are kr.ts and come in the place of lat and lr.t . For PrakriyAlAghava PAn.ini follows different methods --  
  
upAyAs's'iks.yamAn.AnAm bAlAnAm upalAlana.h I  
asatye vartmani sthitvA tatassatya samIhate II (VAkyapadIyam - Brahma)  
  
3. There is a different lot. -- with a single vacana and purus.a ( kriyAsamabhihAre lot. lot.o hisvau... 3-4-2) --  
  
purImavaskanda lunIhi nandanam mus.An.a ratnAni harAmarAnganA.h I  
vigr.hya cakre namucidvis.A balI ya itthamasvAsthyamahardivam diva.h II( S'is'upAlavadha - I )  
  
4. the term 'libi' might have been in use in the sense of Arabic script - check the names of ' al Queda' ( = The Base) group .  
  
Just like VedAntins , who accepted six AnAdis , VaiyAkaran.as also resort to AnAdi as far as S'abdaprayoga is concerned .  
  
So the word '[as.t.au](http://as.t.au)' looks like a dvivacana form .
