**'Is'vara' - in Upani.sad** --  
  
Here is **S'vetAs'vataropani.sad** (6-7)-  
  
tam**Is'varA.nAm** paramam **mahes'varam** tam devatAnAm paramam ca daivatam I  
patim patInAm paramam parastAd vidAma devam bhuvanIs'amI.dyam II  
  
**Is'a = ais'varye** , 'varac' by ' **sthes'abhAsapisakaso varac '** ( PA.n 3-2-175 ) - **Is'vara.h , 'cita.h** ' (PA.n 6-1-160) - antodAttasvara.h . ( **sthAvara.h , bhAsvar.h , pisvara.h , kasvara.h ) .  
**  
Is'vara.h = **niyantA (AbhidhA**) , **prabhu.h ([Lak.sa.nA](http://Lak.sa.nA)**) - why/how ? generally a person with Ais'varya (wealth -secondary meaning - **[Lak.s.nA](http://Lak.s.nA)**) only controlls the SamAja .
