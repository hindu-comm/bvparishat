KAlidAsa in Raghuvams'a (16-41)** compares ' ayodhyApurI' with a lady havidng Abharan.as on all limbs of her body - **sarvAn'ganaddhAbharan.eva narI** . So , it is possible that there used to be Abharan.as that can adorn the entire body , i.e. it is **UpamA (siddha) rather than Utpreks.a** .  
  
**Patanjali in Paspas'A discusses - svastikA , rucaka and kun.d.ala .
