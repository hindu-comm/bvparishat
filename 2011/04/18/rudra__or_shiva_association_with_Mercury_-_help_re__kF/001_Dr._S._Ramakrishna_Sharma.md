Namaste.  
  
Solid Rasa-Linga, like the Spatika-Linga is worshiped  
in Maharashtra(said to be a huge one). I have seen  
one in Sringeri with a devotee which was given to him  
by the earlier Jagadguru, Sri Abhinava Vidyatirtha Swaminah.  
  
Rasahrudayatantram, a text by Govida Bhikshu( considered to be the  
Guru of Adi Sankara, that is Sri Govinda Bhagavatpada) describes Mercury  
as Rudra-Veerya. Killing and rejuvenating Mercury, as described therein,  
is a long process which takes 7 years. The end product is solid linga with  
the innate shine. One such sample is available with the Principal of Karnataka  
Ayurveda College, Mangalore.
