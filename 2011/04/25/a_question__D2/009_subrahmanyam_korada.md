namo vidvadbhyah  
  
In the quoted text Jaimini wants to assert that the meanings of S'abdas in the MImAmsAsutras are not different from the ones that are in Loka . The discussion is about the meaning of the words that are there in JaiminisUtras - S'abara says that they are non-different from Loka (sati sambhave).  
  
why to say so ? in Panini 'vr.ddhi' , 'gun.a' 'sat' , 'nis.t.hA' etc have got a different meaning than Loka .  
also there will be 'vAkyAdhyAhAra' (see end of Paspas'A) etc in VyAkaran.am .  
  
About Laukikakas'abdas and Vaidikadas'abdas he discusses in [Akr.tyadhikaran.am](http://Akr.tyadhikaran.am) .  
  
So the waterline is clear . No room for discussion .
