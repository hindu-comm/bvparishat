ye loke utAnye ? iti sams'aya.h I......ya eva laukikAs's'abdA.h ta eva vaidikA.h ta eva es.Am artha.h' says **S'abarasvAmy under [Akr.tyadhikaran.am](http://Akr.tyadhikaran.am) ( tadantargata - navamam lokavedas'abda- tadarthaikyAdhikaran.am) JaiminisUtram 1-3-9-30.  
**  
Unless specifically said by **Panini, i.e. 'chandasi / chandasi vA / bahulam chandasi** etc. , the form of any S'bda is the same in both **Veda and Loka** .  
  
**sarve vidhaya.h chandasi vikalpyante** - is the general SUtra. or details see MahAbhAs.yam under - **vyatyayo bahulam (PaninisUtram) .  
**  
As far as **Svara** is concerned Panini did not make any **AdhikArasUtra , such as 'chandasi'** before **SvarasUtras** and therefore Svara is common to both **Loka and Veda** . Rather , as was remarked by **MammatAcArya** , there is no **'Adaran.a' of Svara in Loka** , except '**kAkusvara**' . This will become clear if one reads **SvaraprakriyA** .  
Also read **Kaiyat.a and Udyota under - kes.Am s'abdanAm ? laukikAnAm vaidikAnAm ca - Paspas'A of MahAbhAs.yam .**  
  
**Devabodha** at the outset of his commentary on **SrImanmahAbhAratam** remarks --  
  
**vAgujjahAra mAhendrAt vyAso vyAkaran.Arn.avAt I  
padaratnAni kim tAni santi pAn.inigos.pade II  
**  
In fact , **PAn.ini** cannot be ridiculed like this by **Devabodha** as the former took shelter under **'pr.s.odarAdIni [yathopadis.t.am](http://yathopadis.t.am)' (6-3-109)** . **Panini only deals with both Vaidika and Laukika and as such it is the only VyAkaran.am fit to be called 'VedAn'gavyAkaran.am' .
