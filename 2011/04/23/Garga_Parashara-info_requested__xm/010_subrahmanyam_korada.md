namo vidvadbhyah  
  
Garga , gArgya , gArgI , gArgikA, gArgyAyan.a etc. are from the same PrAtipadika - garga .  
  
No doubt , there were many with the same name -- mine , my father's and my grandfather's name is the same .  
  
**TaittirIyAran.yakam** -- gArgya.h prAn.atrAta.h .  
  
**Panini -**- gargAdighyo yan\~ , oto gArgyasya etc.  
  
**Patanjali** -- kUpe gargakulam , gargA.h s'atam dan.d.yantAm
artinas'ca rajAno hiran.yena bhavanti , asti no gargai.h sambandha.h  
  
**KaumudI** -- gArgikayA s'lAghate etc. ( Taddhites.u ApatyAdhikAra etc.)  
  
**As'valAyanasUtram** -- jAnanti bahavigArgyagautama -- .  
  
In **Panini** , one should not take any modern **Samjn\~A (here modern GArgya) for S'AstrAtrtha - says Hari --  
**  
**abhivyaktapadArthA ye svatantrA lokavis'rutA.h I  
s'AstrArtha.h tes.u kartavya.h s'abdes.u na taduktis.u II  
  
**abhivyaktapadArthA.h = S'bdas with clear meaning , i.e not anarthaka ; svatantrA.h = asandigdhArthaka.h , lokavis'rutA.h = not to be 'aprayukta , taduktis.u = tatsadr.s'es.u .  
  
Different meanings in which the word 'GArgya' is used --  
  
1. **apatyam pautraprabhr.ti gotram (Pan 4-1-162**) (apatyAdhikArAt anyatra pravarAdhyAyaprasiddham laukikam gotram - iti **BhAs.yam**)  
apatyatvena vivaks.tam pautrAdi gotrasaqmjn\~akam .  
  
2.**jIvati tu [vams.ye](http://vams.ye) yuvA (4-1-163)** - vams'ye pitrAdau jIvati pautrAde.h yat apatyam caturthAdi.h yuvasamjn\~am syAt .  
  
3.**bhrAtari ca jyAyasi (4-1-164)** - jyes.t.he bhrAtari jIvati
kanIyAn caturthAdi.h yuvasamjnam syAt  
  
4.**vAnyasmin sapin.d.e sthaviratare jIvati (4-1-165)** - bhrAtu.h anyasmin sapin.d.e sthaviratare jIvati pautraprabhr.te.h apatyam jIvadeva yuvasamjnam syAt  
  
5. **eko gotre (4-1-93)** - gotre eka eva pratyaya.h syAt  
  
-- gargasya (gotrA-) apatyam -- gArgya.h (gargAdibhyo yan\~ 4-1-105)  
  
gArgyasya apatyam gArgyAyan.a.h / gArgyo vA ( vA sthaviratare jIvati) -- gArgyAyan.a.h - phak by yan\~in\~osca(4-1-101)- AyanAdes'a.  
  
6. **vr.ddhasya ca pUjAyAm**(**Vartikam)** - gotra becomes yuvA in pUjA - tatrabhavAn gArgyAyan.a.h , without pUjA - gArgya.h .  
  
7. **yUnas'ca kutsAyAm gotrasamjneti vAcyam**(**VArtikam)** -- kutsA = jIvatsu api pitrAdis.u mAnyes.u tadanAdr.tya svatantratvam eva -- gArgoyo jAlma.h .  
  
8. **gotrAdyUnyastriyAm (4-1-94)** - yUni pratyaye gotrAdeva , striyAm na yuvasamjnA -- gargasya yuvApatyam gargyAyan.a.h  
  
  
**ParAs'ara --  
  
TaittirIyAran.yakam --** sa hovAca vyAsa.h pArAs'arya.h , vidyudvadhamevAham mr.tyumaicchamiti (**[arun.am](http://arun.am)**)  
  
**Panini -** pArAs'aryas'ilAlibham bhiks.unat.asUtrayo.h (4-3-110)
