namo vidvadbhyah  
  
**[proks.an.am](http://proks.an.am) - abhyuks.nam - avoks.ana.m**  
  
**Kalpa (Gr.hya) , VyAkaran.am  
**  
uks.a = secane (BhvAdi)  
  
In '**Agnimukham' , both Catus.pAtraprayoga and S.atpAtraprayoga** , these words are employed ---  
  
1. **'adbhi.h proks.ya ' , 'uttAnAni proks.ya**' -- **[proks.n.am](http://proks.n.am)** is sprinkling water with **'palm upside**' .  
  
uttAnA.h x nyan\~ci - the later means 'upside down' ( this word is discussed by **Deeks.ita and Nagesa** in Dvandva - Ref same context -  
**dvndvam nyan\~ci pAtrAn.i prayunakti devasamyuktAni . It is plural - ni + an\~cu (gatipUjanayo.h)  
**  
2. **'avAkkaro'bhyks.ya nidhAya vahnim ' - [abhuks.an.am](http://abhuks.an.am)** is sprinkling water **'palm downside**'(silently) **.'abhirAbhimukhye - Niruktam .**  
  
3.**''avAkkaro'bhyuks.ya nidhAya vahnim utsicyate'voks.an.atoyas'[es.am](http://es.am)' - avoks.ana.m** is sprinkling water with **'palm moving across'**  
  
**Panini under ' acaturasucaturavicatura ...." read three AcpratyayAnta - KarmadhArayas.as NipAtas -- mahoks.a , jAtoks.a, vr.ddhoks.a .**  
  
uks.ati = simcati veeryam strIgavi iti uks.a.h .  
  
dhanyo'smi
