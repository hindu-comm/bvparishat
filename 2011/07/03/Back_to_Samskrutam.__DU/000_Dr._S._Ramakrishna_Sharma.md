Kindly provide the text in Samskrutam if possible.  
This dhyana shloka on Vaaraahi is found quoted/posted  
on the pages of a Group by a disciple whose guru  
does not seem to know Samskrutam.  
  

###### Padhoruha pithagatam(standing on clouds), padhoruha mechakam(grey like clouds), kutila damshtram(curved canine teeth), kapilakshi tritayam( black eyes 3), Ghana kucha kumbham ( heavy breasted), pranata vanchita vadanyam (gracefully fulfilling desires of devotees), daksha (on right) urdhvato (from bottom up) ari(=discus), khadgam, musalam, abhitim ( 4 weapons), tad anyas tadvat (similarly on left from bottom up), sankham, khetam, hala, varaan ( 4 weapons), karair dadhaanaam (holding with hands), smarami Vartalim (I see Vartali.)

Pranams.
