namo vidvadbhya.h



**rAmAya.nam , pArAyan.am , turAyan.am , cAndrAyan.am**



**rAmAyan.am** -- sItA ca rAmas'ca rAmau - ekas'es.a by ' pumAn striyA' (Panini 1-2-67)

rAmayo.h ayanam gati.h ( in. = gatau , lyut.) rAmAyan.am , laks.an.ayA tadadhikr.tya kr.ta.h grantha.h rAmAyan.am



**pArAyan.am** -- yogarUd.ha.h -- pAram gacchati iti , the non-stop adhyayanam which starts fron the beginning and runs till the end ispArAyan.am= VedAdhyayanam .

In Paspas'A of Mahabhasya 's'abdapArAyan.am' is the name of a Grantha , [sundarakAn.d.apArAyan.am](http://sundarakAn.d.apArAyan.am) etc. - upacArAt -- pArAyan.am iva pArAyan.am .



Here is Panini (5-1-72) - KaumudI - t.han\~adhikAre kAlAdhikAraprakaran.am --



**[pArAyan.aturAyan.acAndrAyan.am](http://pArAyan.aturAyan.acAndrAyan.am) vartayati**



pArAyan.am vartayati ( nirvartayati) iti pArAyan.ika.h chAtra.h( na tu guru.h) .



In fact, explains Patanjali , PArAyan.am involves both . But as per the usage it is chAtra only .



**TurAyan.am** is a Yajn\~a that runs for one year . Both r.tvik and YajamAna have to run the same.

but the usage is apllicable to YajamAna only -- taurAyan.ika.h . r.tvik prepares Caru , Purod.As'a etc .



**CAndrAyan.am**--the vratI takes food according to the gati , i.e. ascent and descent ,of Candra . One cup on S'ukla Pratipat - fifteen on PUrn.imA etc . It is the name of a Vrata . turAyan.am vartayati taurAyan.ika.h . CAndrAyan.agut.t.a - is a colony in old city of Hyderabad .
