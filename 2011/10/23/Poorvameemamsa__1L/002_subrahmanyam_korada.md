**VartamAnakAle pUrvamImAmsAyA.h prayojanam -- paramesvara.h  
  
Today this is the most neglected but very useful S'Astra . It is the base of Panini's VyAkaran.a and many other disciplines as far as the devices are concerned .  
  
The S'Astras are ' trikAlAbAdhyA.h' - are useful in bhUta - bhavis.yad - vartamAnakAla .  
  
It is also called VAkyas'Astra / PUrvatantra / PUrvakAn.d.a / DharmamImAmsA / MImAmsA etc.  
MImAmsA chiefly deals with VAkya .  
  
According to Jaimini there is no any difference between Loka and Veda (S'Astra falls under Loka)as far as the VAkyArtha is concerned - avis'is.t.astu vAkyArtha.h (JaiminisUtram)  
  
Under Verse 83 - 87 , VAkyakAn.d.a , VAkyapadIyam , Bhartr.hari and Pun.yarAja quuoted the 24 NyAyas of MImAmsA and explained with examples from Veda , VyAkaran.a and Loka . Here , just sample --  
  
1.Krama.h - it is of seven types
