**Samkaracarya and Nyayadarsanam  
  
There are more such instances across samkarabhasyam . Similar sutra is there in Yoganusasanam .  
  
Under ' etena yoga.h pratyukta.h ' (Brahamasutram ) - samkaracarya says - why a separate sutra ( from samkhya) for yoga ? because yoga cannot be completely refuted , it is accepted by upanis.ads - s'rotavyo
mantavyo nidhidhyasitavya.h etc . so the yoga which is useful in Brahmajnana should be embraced .  
  
Vaisesika is called ' ardhavainAs'ika' (Adhyaya 2 ) - the only reason is - asatkAryavAda of Nyaya and Vaisesika .
