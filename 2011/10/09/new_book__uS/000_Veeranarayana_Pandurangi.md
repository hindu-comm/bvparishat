Sūryaśataka of Mayūra with the commentaries of Madhusūdana and Vaidyanātha:  
The present work is an attempt to bring out a critical edition and study of the commentaries of Madhusūdana and Vaidyanātha Pāyaguṇḍa on the Sūryaśataka of Mayūra. This book comprises two parts. Part one, the critical study, consists of five chapters that contain Sun Worship in Vedas, Purāṇas, Epics and Classical Literature; the life history of Mayūra, his other works, the commentaries on the Sūryaśataka; the authorship of the two commentaries, the authors’ dates, parentage, nativity, works authored by them; the study of these two commentaries, their similarities, differences and their contribution to understanding the real significance of the verses of Sūryaśataka. Part two is a critical edition of the commentaries of Madhusūdana and Vaidyanātha Pāyaguṇḍa on the  
Sūryaśataka.  
  
An appendix provides the list of all the manuscripts of Sūryaśataka and the different commentaries, published and unpublished, catalogued and uncatalogued deposited in the manuscript libraries in different parts of the world. Ably edited by Dr. Deviprasad Mishra, who is working as researcher in the french Insitutue of Pondicherry.  
  
Published by Literary Circle, Jaipur with the ISBN No. 9788192171128.
