**I would congratulate my highly esteemed friend Dr Jagannatha ji on his new creation which I should like to read to enjoy the feast of his felicity of idiomatic Sanskrit and emotive appeal that mark his writing.Let me pen a verse in his honor :**

***  
***

***astavyasta-samasta-hasta-suk.rtin  vidvan   jagannaatha !  
te***

***heramba-stuti-muktayaa'rcitamudaa mugdha.m madiiya.m mana.h /   ( heramba = R Ganesha ji )***

***kvaa'stavyastam ada.h suh.rdh.rdaya-sannirv.rtyupaaya.m bhaje***

***netrabhyaa.m navaniitam am.rtam .rta.m mitraah.rtaa.m saahitiim ! //***

**  
**

**Best regards,**

**smmishra**
