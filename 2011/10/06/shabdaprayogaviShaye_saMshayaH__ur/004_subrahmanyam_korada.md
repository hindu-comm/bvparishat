namo vidvadbhyah  
  
'yadarthavij\~nAnam sA pramA' (VAtsyAyana 1-1-1, PrastAvanA) .  
  
PramA itself means 'yathArthAnubhava' / 'yathArthaj\~nAnam ' . So there cannot be 'pramAj\~nAnam' .  
  
The only problem may be that there are usages like 'bhramaj\~nAnam' , so people may think it should be 'pramAj\~nAnam' rather than  
'pramA' .  
  
Different Darsanas offered different definitions .  
  
dhanyo'smi
