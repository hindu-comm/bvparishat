Mṛgendraṭīkā

Vidyāpāda, 10, 29.3, 1.0

tatra tāvadvainayikeṣu rūpeṣu krameṇārthāḥ sampadyante sphītim upayānti //

  

तत्र तावद्वैनयिकेषु रूपेषु क्रमेणार्थाः सम्पद्यन्ते स्फीतिम् उपयान्ति //

  

For vedic usage, Panini records frequently the deviations immediately in many cases. Hence they are not considerred as aberrations of Paninian Grammar. For the word कर्णेभिः,

  

immediately after:अतो भिस ऐस्॥ ७।१।९  
बहुलं छन्दसि॥७।१।१०

  

अतः इत्युक्तम्, अनतो ऽपि भवति नद्यैः इति। अतो न भवति , देवेभिः सर्वेभिः प्रोक्तम् इति।

  

Etc. All such rules are compiled in a separateप्रकरणin सिद्धान्तकौमुदी as वैदिकप्रकरण. There may be many more such usages not recorded by Panini.

  

बहुलग्रहणं सर्वविधिव्यभिचारार्थम्। सुप्तिङुपग्रलिङ्गनरणां कालहलच्स्वरकर्तृयङां च। व्यत्ययमिच्छति शास्त्रकृदेषां सो ऽपि च सिध्यति बहुलकेन।

  

The above is the implication of बहुल in respect of forms common classical and vedic usages, in addition to specific rules related to exclusively Vedic usage which are recorded separately at different places in अष्टाध्यायी. This is the position of Panini in respect of Vedic usages.
