*Ato bhisa ais* is immediately followed by *bahulaṃ chandasi* which allows us to invoke *kavcit pravṛttiḥ* etc. For applying *kvacid anyad eva* one goes to *bahuvacane jhaly et*. Thus we get *karṇebhiḥ* as a Pāṇinian Vedic form.
