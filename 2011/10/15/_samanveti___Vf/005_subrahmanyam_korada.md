**'tattu samanvayAt' (BrahmasUtram 1-1-4)  
  
sam + anvaya.h -- anu + i.n + ac ( erac, Panini 3-3-56 , gun.a.h , apavAda of gha\~n ) , 'gha\~najabantA.h' - LingAnus'Asanam - Pumlin'ga.h , Hetau pa\~ncamI .  
  
S'AmkarabhAs.yam --" ... tad brahma sarvaj\~nam sarvas'akti jagadutpattisthitilayakAran.am vedAntas'AstrAdeva avagamyate . katham ? samanvayAt . srves.u hi vedAntes.u vAkyAni tAtparyen.a etasya arthasya pratipAdakatvena samanugatAni ".  
  
samyak amvya.h - smanvaya.h . samyaktvam ca tAtparyavattvam . Sarves.Am api vedAntavAkyAnAm yathA brahman.i samanvaya.h yathA syat tathA ptatipAdanIyam .  
  
TAtparyanirn.AyakAni  [s.at](http://s.at). lin'gAni Br.hatsamhitAyAm
pat.hitAni --  
  
upakramopasamhArau abhyAso'pUrvatA phalAm I  
arthavadopapattI ca lin'gam tAtparyanirn.aye II  
  
The first three , viz. upakramopasamhArau , abhyAsa.h (puna.h puna.h Avr.tti.h - chAndogye - 'tattvamasi' ityasya navadhA Avr.tti.h) ,  
arthavAda - are s'abdagatA.h , itarAn.i arthagatAni iti viveka.h .  
  
Therefore , ' since there is samanvaya in / among VedAdntavAkyas it is Brahma that is being proposed ( see PUrvapaks.a) --  
  
sadeva somya idamagra asIt (SAma) , AtmA vA idameka evAgra AsIt ( R.k) , tadetad brahmApUrvam.. (Yajur) , brahmaivedam amr.tam purastAt (Atharva) - are examples .  
  
Tu = pUrvapaks.a.h vyAvartyate , tat = brahma , samanvayAt = vedAntavAkyasamanvayAt j\~nAyate .  
  
Here is a beautiful sentence of S'amkarAcArya quoted by my first VedAntaguru Sree Ran.I Narasimhas'AstrI ( kIrtis'es.A.h) , when I quoted this SUtra , in connection with my Thesis ' MahAvAkyavicAra.h ' (1982) --  
  
vedAntavAkyakusumagrathanArthatvAt sUtrAn.Am (1-1-4) . That is Samanvaya .
