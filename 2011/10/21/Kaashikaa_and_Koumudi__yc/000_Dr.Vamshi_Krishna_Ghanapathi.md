Dear scholars

Continuing the references of Sri NARAYANAN, I cannot resist explaining my admiration of Kaashika Vrutti. Though, we had studied Koumudi, the comparative analysis of Kaashika had drawn my mind towards the explanation and authoritative quotations and definitions, along with extensive examples.

Though not common in South India, I had started the classes in Kaashika. Our students could easily catch up the process.

  

Nyaasa and Pada Manjari commentaries enrich our learning experience respectively, paving the way to study Maha Bhaashya easily, my experience proved.  
  

With warm regards

Vamshi Krishna Ghanapathi

--------------------------------------

Sarvam Brahma
