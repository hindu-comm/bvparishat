As far as my knowledge goes, Prof. Ratna Basu's dissertation of Dr. Phil. (Bonn) was published in the Research publication of the University of Bonn in 1989 ( Length 534 pages in 3 volumes):

  

 Vol I: Eine litetature-kritse Studie zur Aaryasuura's Jaatakamaalaa. (A Literary Critical Study of Aaryasuura's Jaatakamaalaa).

 Vol II: Jaatakamaalaa.tiikaa. The Text-critical Edition of an anonymous Commentary on Aaryasuura's Jaatakamaalaa.

 Vol III. Jaatakamaalaapa\~njikaa. The Text-critical Edition of a Commentary on Aaryasuura's Jaatakamaala by Viiryasi.mha available only in Tibetan translation, the original Sanskrit version being lost, from Tibetan Tanjur.

  

There are some articles /papers also on the said topic written by Prof. Basu and published in different journals, periodicals, special vols., etc..
