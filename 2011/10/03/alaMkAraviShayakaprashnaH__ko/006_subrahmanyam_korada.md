**dhyAyatIva baka.h , dhyAyadntIva parvatA.h  
  
manye s'an'ke dhruvam prAya.h nUnamityevamadibhi.h ( KavyAdars'a.h)-- Dr Bhat  
  
Upanis.at , S'AmkarabhAs.yam , BrahmAmr.tavars.in.I of Sreeramakimkara ,YogAnus'Asanam , MahAbhAs.yam Pradeepa.h , Mr.cchakat.ikam , S'is'upAlavadha.h , CArudattam  
**  
  
The origin lies in **Upanis.ats --**  
  
**Taking up the question as to - in what position should one perform UpAsanA - BAdarAyan.a offers two SUtras - acalatve cApeks.ya , smaranti ca (BrahamsUtra 4-1-9 , 10) .  
Here Smr.ti means YogAnus'Asanam (SAmkhya also is called Smr.ti ) - sthirasukhamAsanam ( YogasU)  
  
-BrahmAmr.tavars.in.I says - AsIna eva upAsIta - why ? because Upanis.at is there - dhyAyatIva pr.thivI ( ChAndogya 7-6-1) .  
Elsewhere also - dhyAyatIva lelAyatIva (Upanis.ad ) - lelAyati = calati .  
  
One should perform UpAsanA in Asana , i.e. it can be Padmaka etc ([Vis.s.n.upurAn.am](http://Vis.s.n.upurAn.am)) .  
**  
**Here the term ' iva' is used in Utpreks.A and not UpamA . Why ? Because KriyA is 'sAdhya' and as such it cannot be referred to as - idam tat .  
  
Under - dhAto.h karman.a.h samAnakartr.kAdicchAyAm vA (Pan 3-1-7) , Patanjali , while discussing - pipatis.atIva pipatis.ati - asserts  
- na vai tin'antena upamAnam asti ( upamAnam = sAdr.s.yam ) .  
  
Here Kaiyat.a comments -- tin.antArthena , ivas'abdaprayoge tu adhyAropastu vidyate , roditIva gAyati , nr.tyatIva gacchati devadatta.h ( AdhyAropa - is Utpreks.A ).  
  
Taking the clue from MahAbhAs.yam Dand.I said - manye ... ivas'abdo'pi tAdr.s'a.h .  
  
In S'is'upAlavadha (1) - patatpatan'gapratima.h taponidhi.h puro'sya yAvanna bhuvi vyalIyata .. Here ' pratima.h ' is in Utpreks.A . This '[patatpatan.ga](http://patatpatan.ga)' is borrowed from SundarakAn.d.a of RAmAyan.am .  
**  
The rule is that - if the UpamAna is ' siddha ' (candra , padma etc ) it is UpamA , if it is 'sAdhya ' then Utpreks.A . The Sun never falls - ' patan'ga.h paks.isUryau ca ' ( Vis'va ?)  
  
So - **limpatIva tamo'n'gAni vars.atIvAnjanam nabha.h ( CArudattam - 1 ) is Utpreks.A .
