To add to what Srimaan Madhavachar said,it is said for any mantra-siddhi, japa of akshara-laksha is needed. If we take Gayatri upaasana, the mantra works for the person who chants it for 24 lakh times (because it has 24 aksharas)as prescribed. In the case of weapons like paaSupataastra, the holder is supposed to do the japa of the astra for as many lakh times as are syllables in the mantra, for the weapon to fire once.
