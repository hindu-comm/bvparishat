Q. of Sri Ashutosh Gupta and Sri Kalyanaraman  
  
**Abortion - AtmA - Garbha.h** (formation)  
  
**Yajurveda.h , ** **VAkyapadIyam, BhAgavatam , AdhyAtmaramAyan.am .  
**  

**'BhrUn.ahatyAm vA ete ghnanti'** - (these **Mantras** would destroy the sin of 'abortion')says **Kr.s.n.ayajurveda**  
  
Note - Abortion , that is done/caused deliberately is called - **garbha-/bhrUn.a- hatyA** . If it happened spontaneously , i.e. due to health problems or weakness then it is ' **garbhanirlun.t.hanam'** . The former causes **PApam** , for which one has to suffer ( no remedy) .  
Any remedy offered in **Veda etc. is an ArthavAda** , because '**avas'yam anubhoktavyam kr.tam karma s'ubhAs'ubham' .  
**  
**AtmA** is a term used in many senses ( hence untranslatable)--  
  
**AtmA buddhyA sametyArthAn - Pan.inIyas'iks.A** , it means '**[anta.hkaran.am](http://anta.hkaran.am)'** .  
  
**ayamAtmA brahma - Veda , ApnotIti AtmA** = all pervading **Parabrahman** (**Advaitam)**  
  
It means **'Dravyam'** ( a thing) -- **VAkyapadIyam Pada-Dravya**) --  
  
**AtmA vastu svabhAvas'ca s'arIram tattvamityapi I dravyamityasya paryAyA.h tacca niotyamiti smr.tam II  
**  
It means 'vastu' ( a thing ) - i.e. sarvam [svalaks.an.am](http://svalaks.an.am) , **Bauddhas** .  
  
SvabhAva (sattA) - **SattAdvaita**) . Tattvam = the conglomeration of four BhUtas(no AkAs'a)- body -- **CArvAkas .**  
  
**S'arIram** = the body that is going to perish - **s'Iryate iti s'arIram** . In that, there is **'purus.a'** , who is **'cetana'** ,also called '**s'arIrI' .**  
  
Here is Hari (**VAkyapadIyam (1-126,127)** -  
  
**sais.A samsArin.Am samjn\~A bahirantas'ca vartate I tanmAtrAmanatikrAntam caitanyam sarvajantus.u II  
arthakriyAsu vAk sarvAn samIhayati dehina.h I tadutkrAntau visamjn\~o'yam dr.s'yate kAs.t.hakud.yavat II  
**  
VAk itself is **Caitanyam** and the same **Caitanyam** urges the body to do the activities .  
  
The main question - when the **'caitanyam' enters the Garbha** ?  
  
**BhAgavatam (3-31**) --  
  
**karman.A daivanetren.a janturdehopapattaye I striyA.h pravis.t.a udaram pumso [reta.hkan.As](http://reta.hkan.As)'raya.h II (1)  
**  
Such a person(see the earlier text) , due to his karma, for which
Vidhi is an eye , resorts the sperm of a man and enters the womb of a woman to get a body .  
  
**ArabhyasaptamAn mAsAt labdhabodho'pi vepita.h (10)  
**  
At the beginning of seventh month he gets **'jn\~Anam'** .  
  
**AdhyAtmarAmAyan.am (Kis.kindhAkAn.d.a - Sarga 8 , Munees'vara
CandramA to SAmpAti ) --  
**  
**bhUtvA caturvidham bhojyam purus.airbhujyate tata.h I reto bhUtvA punastena r.tau strIyonisin\~cita.h II (21)  
  
jat.hare vardhate garbha.h striyA evam vihangama I pancame mAsi caitanyam jIva.h prApnoti sarvas'a.h II (31)  
**  
In the fifth month of pregnancy the **JIva** gets '**caitanyam'** .  
  
By and large, it can be concluded that **'caitanyam' will be there from the fifth month onwards .**  
  
Till then the body is considered as **'jad.a'** and once the '**caitanyam'** enters , it becomes '**cara' .**  
  
Please note , forceful abortion of even '**Jad.a' would cause 'PApam'** .
