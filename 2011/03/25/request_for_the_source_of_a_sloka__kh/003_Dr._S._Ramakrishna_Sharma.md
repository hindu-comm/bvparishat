Respected Sri Kannan Ji,

  
  
\>And were someone indeed to trace an ancient source,  
I would only be happy to discard my conjecture.  
  

Undoubtedly, even IF an ancient source is traced, still  
it would belong to "पुराणमित्येव न साधु सर्वं " category!  
Hence the latter part of your statement be discarded(with all humility  
by all those who know that humility is not just bending by popular convention;  
indeed, all that is popular need not be really/truly great. Be it from the walls of  
Parliament house or elsewhere.)  
धन्यो'स्मि
