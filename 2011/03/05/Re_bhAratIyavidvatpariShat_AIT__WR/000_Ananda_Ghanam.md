Pranaam.  
Commending the response of Sri Rajaram NS and Sri Ra Ganesha Avadhani; about the Aryan Invasion theory; including the basic premise with wrong derivation of Nakaaraanta Arya Shabda; I request this forum to take this to the next step.  
  
Can we not simply suggest the compilation of series of articles, written by eminent easterns and western historians and theorists; negating the false premise of invasion. If this is made available.  
  
The current day scenario is that staunch protagonists and learned people, constituting about 1 -2 % or even lessser fraction of our society understand the truth. Rest of them are swayed by the British Induced Concoction about the invasion.  
  
At least, I would like some baby steps to reverse this propaganda to divide the society. If this collection of articles in simple terms is compiled, translated into other languages and distributed among the decision makers, some positive sense may prevail - I hope. (First awareness about the original fact, followed by corrective measures can be a possibility).  
  
We have a number of nationalist organisations, thru which we can try to get this done. For this, a series of simple articles of 3 - 4 pages, with references to a exhaustive narrations in Books (With URLs - internet links); will be really needed. We would like to kickstart this process.  
  
This would serve as a logical conclusion of the brainstorming sessions/ debates of BVP. Can BVP' intelligentsia contribute its might for a common cause??  
  
If it does, it would be gratifying for its tagline  
अथ चेत्त्वमिमं धर्म्यं संग्रामं न करिष्यसि।  ततः स्वधर्मं कीर्तिं च हित्वा पापमवाप्स्यसि ।  
तस्मादुत्तिष्ठ कौन्तेय युद्धाय कृतनिश्चयः।  
  
  
With Warm Regards  
Vamshi K Ghanapathi
