namo vidvadbhyah  
  
**MAyA** is the **S'akti** /capacity of **Brahman .** The **Jagat** , which is a false form **(mithyAsvarUpam**) of **Brahman** is the **'KAryam' of MAyA** .  
  
**MAyA** conceals the real form of **Brahman** and exhibits **Brahman** in a different form (**jagat)** .  
  
**Pras'nopanis.adbhAs.yam (1-16,S'an'karAcArya**) - MAyA nAma
bahiranyathA AtmAnam prakAs'ya anyathaiva kAryam karoti sA mAyA .  
**  
BrahmasUtrabhAs.yam (1-4-3)** - na hi tayA vinA parames'varasya
srastr.tvam siddhyati , s'aktirahitasya tasya pravr.tyanupapatte.h .  
**  
[Br.hadAran.yakopanis.ad](http://Br.hadAran.yakopanis.ad)** (2-5-19) - indro (Brahman) mAyAbhi.h pururUpa Iyase  
  
**S'vetAs'[vataropanis.ad](http://vataropanis.ad)** (4-10) -mAyAm tu prakr.tim vidyAt mAyinam tu mahes'varam (**Is'vara=Brahman**).  
  
**BhagavadgItA** (7-14) - daivI hyes.A gun.amayI mama mAyA .  
  
**Synonyms of MAyA** -- Prakr.ti.h , Vais.navamAyA , MUlaprakr.ti.h , AkAs'a.h , Aks.aram , Avyaktam etc.  
  
**JIva** is under the control of **MAyA** and **MAyA** is under the control of **Is'vara(Brahman) .
