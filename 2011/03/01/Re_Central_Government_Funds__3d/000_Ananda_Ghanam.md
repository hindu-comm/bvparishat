Pranaam  
Bemoaning the lack of grants for Sanskrit/ Vedic Education in Central Budget fora like this, amounts to pure Aranya Rodanam.  
  
What required is, centre of excellence in Shastra and Veda Education. Currently the institutions' funding, is taking care of the meagre section. What lacks is the excellence, in the funded schools.  
  
People, who can be in touch with MPs, can bring this to their notice. Their voice alone matters.  
Seek more funds and autonomy for:  
1. Maharshi Sandeepani Rashtreeya Veda Vidya Pratishthan, Ujjain.  
2. Rashtreeya Sanskrit Sansthan, New Delhi  
  
Both are funding agencies of the Government for Veda and Samskruta respectively.  
  
उद्योगिनं पुरुषसिंह मुपैति लक्ष्मीः, दैवेन देयमिति कापुरुषा वदन्ति।  
दैवं निहत्य कुरु पौरुष मात्मशक्त्या यत्ने कृते यदि न सिद्ध्यति कोत्र दोषः?  
  
विश्वसिम्यहं, यत् श्रीमन्तः सर्वे परिषत्सदस्याः, एकां विज्ञापनां स्वीय हस्ताङ्कनेन सज्जीकर्तु मुत्सहन्त इति। सेयं विज्ञापना अचिरं यदि सिद्धा स्यात्, प्रणव मुखोपाध्यायं (प्रणब मुखर्जी) देहली प्रान्त निवासिनः, मिलेयुः। अथवा, अहं तत्प्रेषणे, तत्सन्निधापने यत इति निवेदयामि।  
  
With Warm Regards  
Vamshi K Ghanapathi
