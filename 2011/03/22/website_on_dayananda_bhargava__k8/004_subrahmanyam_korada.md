Q1.**Prakr.ti and Purus.a**  
according to **SAmkhyadars'anam** the Creation takes place due to the combination of **Prakr.ti** and **Purus.a** --  
**purus.asya dars'anArtham kaivalYrtham tathA purus.asya I  
pan'vandhavadubhayorapi samyogastatkr.ta.h sarga.h II (SAmkhyakArikA 21)  
**  
**MAyA and Brahma**  
According to **VedAntadars'anam MAyA** is a false form of **Brahman** itself and the latter is the very cause **(upAdAna/samavAya and nimitta - karan.a)** of the universe . **MAyA** is required in order to see that the 'beings ' face the **'karma'** ( both good and bad ) --  
  
**mAyAm tu prakr.tim vidyAt mAyinamtu mahesvaram (SvetAs'vatara - 4-10)  
**  
**MAyA hyes.A mayA sr.s.t.A yanmAm pasyasi nArada I  
sarvabhUtagun.airyuktam maivam mAm jn\~Atumarhati II (GItA)  
**  
**StrIpurus.a , candrasUrya , carAcara , dr.s'yAdr.s'ya , pin.d.abrahman.d.a  
**  
All the above are just names of different things that are there in the Universe**(MAyA) - nAmarUpe vyAkaravAn.i (ChAndogya) .**
