नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

गंगायां मत्स्यघोषौ स्तः - abhidhA or laks.n.A or both etc.

According to Vaiyakaranas there are two vr.ttis - abhidhA and vyanjanA  
Naiyayikas accept abhidhA and laks.an.A  
What is the problem here ?  
Panini says ' cArthe dvandva.h ' - 'matsyaghos.au ' - is a single  
thing and it should have connection into only one --  
dvandvAdyarthasya ekadharmAvacchinne anvaya.h -  
otherwise - since mInasya nIratvAvacchinne anvaya.h , ghos.asya  
tIratvAvacchinne anvaya.h , dvandva is asAdhu .  
yadi gangAtvenaiva tIrabodha.h tadA ekadharmAvacchine anvaya.h syAt  
iti na dos.a.h .  
yugapadvr.ttidvayavirodha is not for any dos.a , because there is no  
dUs.akatAbIjam .  
anvayAnupapattyA gangAtvenaiva nIratIrayorupasthiti.h . kAkebhyo dadhi  
raks.yatAm etc also the same way - dadhyupaghAtake bid.AlAdau kAkatvam  
Aropyate .  
If youdo not take this line then - ajAdhano devadatta.h , avidhano  
yajnadatta.h ityarthe  
ajAvidhanau devadattayajnadattau ityAdau dvandvavAran.Aya  
yathAsamkhyam cannot be done .  
So it is Tantra - two things by a single effort - this is from mImAmsA  
Loka - a single lamp is useful for reading for many .  
VyAkaran.a,m - 'taparastatkAlasya' - tAt para.h , ta.h para.h yasmAt  
sa.h = tapara.h  
Veda - there are six darsapUrn.amAsas , AgneyA etc. , one should  
perform PrayAjAnuyAjas either before or after the six Yagas so that  
separately they need not be performed .  
Avr.tti and Bheda go against Tantra .  
Under 'pumyogAdAkhyAyAm' Patanjali explains Laksana and Aksapada in  
second Adhyaya --sahacaran.asthAnatAdarthya....
