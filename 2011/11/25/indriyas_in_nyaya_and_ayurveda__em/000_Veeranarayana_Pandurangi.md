dear all, it is a nyaya vs science subject.

Nyaya usually says that इन्द्रियं गन्धग्राहकं घ्राणं नासाग्रवर्ति,**इन्द्रियं रसग्राहकं रसनम् तच्च जिह्वाग्रवर्ति,इन्द्रियं रूपग्राहकं चक्षुः, तच्चकृष्णताराग्रवर्ति,इन्द्रियं स्पर्शग्राहकं त्वक् सर्वशरीरवृत्तिः, (तर्कसंग्रहे)इन्द्रियं तु भवेच्छ्रोत्रमेकः सन्नप्युपाधितः भिन्नः (मुक्तावली)**

what we find in biology is quite different.घ्राणं is positioned in center point of two eyebrows i.e. नासामूलवर्ति.

  

but more difficult point is about ear (श्रोत्र) which is akasha itself.कर्णशष्कुल्यवाच्छिन्नं नभः श्रोत्रं. it is very difficult to understand even by the nyaya system.becauseearcan not be a mere hole (akasha) there must be something sensor that stimulates the nerves leading to mind. simple space can not grasp anything.

  

hence I request all of you to let us know what is the standpoint of Ayurveda on indriyas?
