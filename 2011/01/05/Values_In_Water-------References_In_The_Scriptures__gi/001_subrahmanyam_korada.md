Namo vidvadbhyah

The term ‘क्षेत्रिय’ is registered as **a NipAta** ( the readymade word is pronounced and one has to deduce the Prak.rti and Pratyaya ) -- ‘क्षेत्रियच् परक्षेत्रे चिकित्स्यः’ **(PA.n 5-2-92**) . The ‘c’ is **for AntodAtta** by ‘**cita.h’ ( PA.n 6-1-160**) . This is called ‘**vAkyArthe padavacanam’** .

A disease that has to be treated in another body , i.e. incurable , is called ‘ k.setriya.h’. There is a chapter clled **‘
k.setriyaroganAs’anam ‘ in Atharvaveda** ( 2-8-44) .

In fact , such a disease cannot be cured - **‘asAdhya’ in Ayurvedic terminology** . Consumption of water may reduce the intensity (**s’Anti
not nivAra.na** ) just like **‘grahajapa’** (**avas’yamanubhoktavyam k.rtam karma s’ubhAs’ubham**) .

**CarakasamhitA** says that there will be eight types of diseases due to (polluted) water .

**Jalam stambhanIyAnAm (CarakasUtram**) - water is good in controlling oozing blood .

Making **‘[gan.dU.sa](http://gan.dU.sa)’** ( rinsing water ) is very important . Here is Vaidyakam –

गण्डूषमथ कुर्वीत शीतेन पयसा मुहुः ।

कफतृष्णामलहरं मुखान्तः शुद्धिकारणम् ।।

कुर्याद्द्वादशगण्डूषान् पुरीषोत्सर्जने ततः।

मूत्रोत्सर्गे तु चतुरो भोजनान्ते तु षोडशम् ।।

**Rinsing with cool water - four times after urinals , twelve times after purI.sa , sixteen times after dinner ( and thirteen times after brushing the teeth) should be done before washing the feet** .

**How can there be any bad smell from the mouth ?**

**Mouth washing agents are carcinogenic** ( cause cancer) .

**S’ankarAcArya in his BhA.sya on 6-16 of BhagavadgItA** , quotes the following verse which prescribes that ¼ th of the stomach should be left for VAyu by a **Yogi –**

**ardham savyanjanAnnasya t.rtIyamudakasya ca I**

**vAyossancAra.nArtham tu caturthamavas’e.sayet II**

**Water Therapy** is dangerous - says **Caraka** --

**atyambupAnAt atisamgamAcca**

**divA ca supternis’i jAgarAcca I**

**nirodhanAnmUtrapurI.sayos’ca**

**.sa.dbhirnidAnai.h prabhavanti rogA.h II**

MUtrapurI.sa fall under **14 Vegas** , which should not be held back –

**vegasandhAra.nam anArogyakarA.nAm ( CarakasUtram** – others are
thirst, hunger, sleep etc.)

NidAnam tu AdikAra.nam (**Amara**) .

dhanyo’smi
