Namo vidvadbhyah

It seems many scholars who speak about **Aryas** (Aryans) did not
try to find out as to whether anything is said by our sages in this regard .

So I think that those who wish to counter the **Aryan – Vadins (Invasion** etc) should be well equipped with irrefutable source and this is for them.

Also , I personally feel, that every student should be trained on these lines about our culture and the false propaganda of ‘others’ shold be tackled effectively .

The entry of the word ‘aryan’ as Sanskrit in Oxford Dictionary is wrong.

There is / was no Indo Aryan Prototype etc. .

One can see as to how difficult it is to be called an ‘Arya’ .

**PA.nini** offered three ways to know the gamut of S’abdas –

1.**Prak.rti and Prtyaya** are provided and one has to combine them

- k.r + tavya = kartavya.h

2.**NipAta** (uccAra.nam of a readymade word ) – one has to deduce the Prak.rti and Pratyaya

- s’rotriyan chando’dhIte (5-2-84) – s’ruti + ghan (‘n’ is for nitsvara)

k.setriyac parak.setre cikitsya.h (5-2-92) – k.setriya + ghac(‘c’ is for svara )

3. **P.r.sodarAdIni yathopadi.s.tam** (6-3-109) – p.r.sodara , jImUta, manI.sA etc. are not analysed in A.s.tAdhyAyI but are there in currency . Such S’bdas have to be taken as authoritative if employed by S’i.s.tas .

**NyAsa and Padamanjary** count the first two only .

The third category has a lot to explain . Here is **Patanjali** –

**p.r.sodarAdIni = [p.r.sodaraprakArA.ni](http://p.r.sodaraprakArA.ni) , kAni [p.r.sodaraprakArA.ni](http://p.r.sodaraprakArA.ni) ? [ye.su](http://ye.su) lopAgamavar.navikArA s’rUyante na cocyante** .

atha kimidamupadi.s.tAnIti ? uccAritAni , kuta etat ? dis’iruccAra.nakriya.h , uccArya hi var.nAnAha – upadi.s.tA ime var.nA iti .

**kai.h punarupadi.s.tAni ? s’i.s.tA.h ? s’.s.tai.h .**

**ke punas’s’i.s.tA.h ? vaiyAkara.nA.h . kuta etat ? s’AsrapUrvikA hi s’s.t.i.h , vaiyAkara.nAs’ca s’AstrajnA.h .**

**(evam tarhi) nivAsatas’cAcAratas’ca .**

**sa cAcAra.h AryAvarte eva **

**ka.h punarAryAvarta.h ? **

**prAgAdars’At - pratyak kAlakavanAt – dak.si.nena himavantam – [uttare.na](http://uttare.na) pAriyAtram . etasmin AryAvarte AryanivAse ye brAhma.nA.h kumbhIdhAnyA.h alolupA.h ag.rhyamA.nakAra.nA.h [kincidantare.na](http://kincidantare.na) kasyAs’cidvidyAyA.h pAram gatA.h s’i.s.tA.h** .

here the **boundries of AryAvarta** are clearly defined .

**Manu** says the following –

**AsamudrAcca pUrvasmAt AsamudrAcca pas’cimAt I**

**tayorevAntarA giryo.h AryAvartam pracak.sate II**

**Amarakos’a** –

**AryAvarta.h pu.nyabhUmi.h madhyam vindhyahimAgayo.h **.

As the time went on the residents of AryAvarta migrated to other places of BhAratam and this can be justified thru their customs and other AcAra .

**AcAra of S’i.s.tas of AryAvrta** –

**kumbhIdhAnyA.h** -

this term is explained by **S’rIdharabha.t.ta of NyAyakandalI , on Pras’astapAdabhA.syam on Vais’e.sikasUtras (p 670)** –

those who store cereals that are required for a short period , i.e. three days or so . Kaiyata says – **kumbhyAmeva ye.sAm dhAnyam** . KumbhI is the name of a PAtra / container .

By and large they do not go for hoarding .

**AlolupA.h** –

One may be skeptical – may be , they are **kumbhIdhanyA.h** as their minds are full of ‘**dambha**’ , feeing that one is superior to all others –

so the answer is alolupA.h – no lust for anything .

**ag.rhyamA.nakAra.nA.h** –

d.r.s.tam kAra.nam antare.naiva sadAcArAnuvartina iti bhAva.h (Kaiyata) – they follow ‘sadAcAra’ described in YogAnus’Asanam and Dharmas’Astras for no reason visible ( invisible is Mok.sa) . d.r.s.tam kAra.nam = **lAbhapUjAdi** ( NAgesa)

[kincidantare.na](http://kincidantare.na) kasyAs’cid vidyAyA.h pAramgatA.h –

without guRUpades’a, AbhyAsa etc who learnt all Vidyas (Kaiyata – **vinaiv abhiyogAdinA sarvavidyApAragA.h , Nagesa – tapobalAdeva s’abdato’rthatas’ca pratibhAtasarvavidyA.h iti tAtparyam** ).

Bhart.rhari in **VAkyapadIyam** clearly says (BrahmakA.n.da) –

**AvirbhUtaprakAs’AnAm anupaplutacetasAm I**

**atItAnAgatajnAnam pratyak.sAnna vis’i.syate II**

**atIndriyAnasamvedyAn pas’yantyAr.se.n [cak.su.sA](http://cak.su.sA) I**

**ye bhAvAn vcanam te.sAm nAnumAnena bAdhyate** II

**Nagesa** elaborates (under above SUtra) –

**AvirbhUteti** – avidyApagamena [yogAbhyAsashak.rtapratyak.se.na](http://yogAbhyAsashak.rtapratyak.se.na) avirbhUtasarvavi.sayajnAnAm ityartha.h .

**Tatra kAra.nam** – anupapluteti – vihitakarmAnu.s.thAnAt s’uddhAnta.hkara.nAnAm ityartha.h .

**Pratyak.sAt** - vidyamAnavastuvi.sayAsmadAdipratyak.sAt ityartha.h.

**AtIndriyAn** – bAhyendriyAgrAhyAn

asamvedyAn – **anyai.h manasA api anupalabhyamAnAn**
