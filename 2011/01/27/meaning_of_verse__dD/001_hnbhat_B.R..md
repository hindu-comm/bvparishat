"अश्वरत्नस्त्रीः दत्वा" मथ्यमानः अब्धिः, विषमसृजद् would be natural sequence for the warning included in the first half. In concordance with this, the first half need be taken. In the second I added (even after) giving to make it clear the meaning of "atyartham" applicable in this case also to bring the analogy closer. The object is in the plural, and hence all the three should be taken individually related with the verbal form दत्त्वा.
