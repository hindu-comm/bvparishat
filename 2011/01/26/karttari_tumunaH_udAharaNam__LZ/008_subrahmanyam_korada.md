Kartari k.rt - is considered as an **AdhikarasUtra** (not Utsarga) - see the commentaries of Kaiyata and Nagesa .  
  
The analysis is good .  
  
For clarity -- whereever some meaning or the other is mentioned there
**the term 'k.rt' only will go as AdhikAra** -- kara.nAdhikara.nayos'ca , Adikarma.ni..., bhImAdayo'pAdAne etc .  
  
Please notice one thing - 'kartari k.rt etc are read by PA.nini at the end of 'K.rtpratyayas' in fourth leg of Third AdhyAya .  
  
Generally AdhikAra is read before the Sutras that receive .  
  
A lengthy discussion is there under 'Kartari k.rt' in **MahAbhA.syam** .  
  
Tumun is in BhAva following Avyayak.rto bhAve . Otherwise it would have been in SvArtha (Prak.rtyartha) .  
  
What is BhAva ? -- **BhAva eva hi dhAtvartha.h ityavicchinna Agama.h ( VAkyapadIyam - PadakA.n.da - KriyApariccheda.**h) .
