namo vidvadbhyah  
  
It is not possible to suggest a single book wherein one would get the gamut of things related to 'yajna' .  
The Researcher may note the following fundamentals and refer to MImamsA books, preferably with the help of a **guru who knows Veda , VyAkara.nam and MImAmsA .  
**  
Yajnah -- **yaja = [devapUjAsangatikara.nadAne.su](http://devapUjAsangatikara.nadAne.su) (bhvAdi) .  
**  
nan(ka) - pratyaya by - **yaja-yAca-yata-viccha-praccha-rak.so nan** (Pan 3-3-90), followed by s'cutvam -- yajna.h .  
  
Under - **patyurno yajnasamyoge (Pan 4-1-33) , Patanjali** explains that the 'yajna' here refers to the **'panca mahAyajnA.h' that should be performed ragularly by a 'g.rhastha'--  
**  
patis'abdo'yam ais'varyavAcI , [sarve.na](http://sarve.na) ca g.rhasthena panca mahAyajnA.h nirvartyA.h , yaccAda.h sAyamprAtarhomacarupuro.dAs'Annirvpati tasyAsAvI.s.te . evamapi tujakasya patnI na siddhyati ? upamAnAt siddham - patnIva patnI .  
  
Panca mahAyajnA.h are enumerated in **TaittirIyAra.nakam** (2-10-14) --  
  
panca vA ete mahAyajnA.h satati pratAyante satati samti.s.thante **devyajna.h pit.ryajno bhUtayajno manu.syayajno brahmayajna iti**  
yadagnau juhotyapi samidham taddevayajnassamti.s.thate yatpit.rbhya.h svadhAkarotyapyapa.h tatpit.ryajnassamti.s.thate yadbhUtebhyo balig.m harati tadbhUtayajnassamti.s.thate yadbrAhma.nebhyo'nnam dadAti tanmanu.syayajnassamti.s.thate yatsvAdhAyamadhIyeetaikAmapy.rcam yajussAma vA tadbrahmayajnassamti.s.thate ...  
  
Depending on various factors the meaning of the term 'yajna' has to be decided elsewhere .
