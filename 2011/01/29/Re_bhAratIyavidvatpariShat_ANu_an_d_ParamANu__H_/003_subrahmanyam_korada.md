Namo vidvadbhyah

**A.nu and ParamA.nu –**

**VyAkara.nam (Santanu and PA.nini) , NyAya , Vais’e.sika , Bauddham, SAmkhyam, S’AnkarabhA.syam , BhAgavatam (3 & 5 Skan) , GAru.dam, Ka.thopani.sat and Jaina **

1**.VyAkara.nam** – [a.na](http://a.na) = s’abdArthau (bhvAdi) – Au.nAdika.h ‘u’ pratyaya.h by – a.nas’ca (**U.nAdi1**-8) - a.nu.h = svalpa.h – lavales’aka.nA.nava.h (**Amara**).

a.nu.h = dhAnyam , ‘dhAnye nit’ (**U.nAdi** 1-9) – nittvAt AdyudAtta.h –

priyangavas’ca me’.navas’ca me (**Camakam – K.r Yajurveda.h) , ‘vrIhibhedastva.nu.h pumAn’ – Amara.h .**

a.nuprakAraka.h a.nuka.h – ‘sthUladibhya.h prakaravacane kan’ (**PA.nini** 5-4-3) , prakAra.h = bheda.h sAd.rs’yam ca .

a.nuka.h = sUk.smad.rk , [a.nu](http://a.nu) [nipu.ne](http://nipu.ne) iti yAvAdipA.thAt kan – yAvAdibhya.h kan **(PA.n** 5-4-29) .

a.nUnAm dhanyAnAm bhavanam k.setram – a.navyam / A.navInam –

‘vibhA.sA tilamA.somAbhangA.nubhya.h’ **(PA.n 5-**2-4) yat / khan(ca).

2.**NyAya. and Vais’e.sika** - janyadravyavayava.h kriyAvAn atIndriya.h niravayava.h nityas’ceti naiyayikavais’e.sikA.nam siddhAnta.h –

a.nutvam parima.navis’e.sa.h , sa ca parama.nau dvya.nuke ca ti.s.thati .

bhoktum as’kyA a.nava.h (Sarvdars’ana – Arhata)

na pralayo’.nusadbhAva.h (Gauta 4-2-14)

paramA.nus’ca param vA ru.te.h (Gaut 4-2-15) – tru.te.h param yad atisUk.smam tat paramA.nu.h.

a.normahatas’copalabdhyanupalabdhI nitye vyAkhyAte (Vais’7-8)

ato viparItama.nu , nityam parima.n.dalam(Vais’7-10,20)

**Pras’astapAdabhA.syam** (Dravye p.rthivIprakara.nam) – devar.sI.nAm s’arIram dharmavis’e.sasahitebhyo a.nubhyo jAyate ( na tu s’ukras’o.nitAbhyAm etc.) .

**NyAyakandalI **– ayamabhisandhi.h , s’arIrArambhe paramA.nava eva kAra.nam , yata.h param a.nurnAsti sa paramA.nu.h .

dvAbhyAmeva paramA.nubhyAm Arabhyate tad dvya.nukam iti siddham .

3.**BauddhA.h** – Parama.nUnAm utpattivinAs’au sta.h

4.**SAmkhyA.h** – bhUtatvAdi paramA.no.h lak.sa.nam .

5.**S’AnkarabhA.syam** – 2-2-11 , MahaddIrghAdhikara.nam and 2-2-12 to17 , paramA.nujagadakAra.natvAdhikara.nam –

paramA.nava.h kila kamcitkAlamanArabdhakAryA.h yathayogam rUpAdimanta.h pArimA.n.dlyaparimA.nAs’ca ti.st.hanti… paramA.nugu.navis’e.sastu pArimA.n.dalyam na dvya.nuke pArimA.n.dalyam aparam Arabhate …

sarvamidam jagad a.nubhya.h sambhavati , a.nugatebhyas’ca rUpAdibhyo dvya.nukAdigatAni rUpAdIni sambhavanti tantupa.tanyAyena iti kA.nAdA manyante .

tasmAdanupapano’yam paramA.nukAra.navAda.h .

6.**BhAgavatam (3&5 Skandhas**) –

caramassadvis’e.sA.nAm aneko samyutastu ya.h

paramA.nussa vijneyo **n.r.nAmaikyabhramo** yata.h

sa kAla.h paramA.nurvai yo bhunkte paramA.nutAm

sato vis’e.sabhugyastu sa kAla.h paramo mahan

**a.nurdvau paramA.nU syAt**trasare.nustrayo’.nava.h (3-11)

evam niruktam k.sitis’abdav.rttam asannidhAnAt **paramA.navo ye**

**avidyayA manasA kalpitAste ye.sAm samUhena k.rto vis’e.sa.h** (5-12-9) .

7.**GAru.dam** –

artho’yam brahmasUtrA.nAm bhAratArthavinir.naya.h

….s’rImadbhAgavatAbhidha.h

8**.Ka.thopani.sad** –

a.nora.nIyAn (2-20) – S’AnkarabhA.syam – kathampunarAtmAnam jAnAti iti , ucyate – a.no.h sUk.smAt a.nIyAn s’yAmAkAde.h a.nutara.h. … tasmAdasAvevAtmA a.nora.nIyAn .

9**.Jaina – Hari in BrahmakA.a.da of VAkyapadIyam** summarizes the concept of **Jainas**, who think that **‘a.nus’ are the cause of
S’abda** –

**vAyora.nUnAm jnAnasya s’abdatvApattiri.syate**

kais’ciddars’anabhedo’tra pravAde.svanavasthita.h (107)

**a.navas**sarvas’aktitvAd bhedasamsargav.rttaya.h

chAyAtapatamas’s’abdabhAvena pari.nAmina.h (110)

svas’aktau vyajyamAnAyAm prayatnena samIritA.h

abhrA.nIva pracIyante **s’abdAkhyA.h paramA.nava.h** (111)

In these verses ‘[a.nu](http://a.nu)’ is not that of Vais’e.sikas , but the **TanmAtras** of SAmkhyas , which are minute and the cause of MahAbhUtas .

Since there is no ‘**BhUtatva’** they are called ‘**TanmAtras**’ .

The **AkAs’a is filled with S’abdatanmAtras** .

**S’ankara’s** description of Vais’e.sikamatam is not in toto with the one available to us today .

dhanyo’smi
