Sri Achyut-ji,

  

1\. If you coined a term, you should have said that. You can not make statements like 'they are Nasikas' without making that it is your coinage clear to the audience.

  

2\. Anusvaara is a script issue and Anunaasika is a pronunciation issue. They can not be mixed up.
