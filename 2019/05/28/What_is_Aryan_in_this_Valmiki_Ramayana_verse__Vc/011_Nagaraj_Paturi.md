[[Nagaraj Paturi	2019-05-28, 18:44:13 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/Vc5RnnYtw9Y)]]



Prabhuh = king

  

prabhavah = kings

  

mlecchaprabhavah = mleccha kings

  

prabhavah is the commentators' substitute for bhoomipaah in the verse.

  

the verse has प्राच्यभूमिपाः, उदीच्यभूमिपाः , प्रतीच्यभूमिपाः, दाक्षिणात्यभूमिपाः, म्लेच्छभूमिपाः, आर्यभूमिपाः, वनशैलान्तवासिभूमिपाः, अन्यभूमिपाः च सर्वे तं दशरथं नृपं उपासांचक्रिरे,

  

keeping this in mind, the commentator substituted म्लेच्छ्प्रभवः for
म्लेच्छभूमिपाः .
