This is a celebration of the vastness of power of an emperor.

  

As such, it celebrates varieties of diversity among the kings that were under the emperorship of the emperor.

  

One diversity is geographical or directional.

  

प्राच्योदीच्याः प्रतीच्याश्च दाक्षिणात्याश्च भूमिपाः।

  

Another is , in today's parlance,

  

tribal, non-tribal diversity . वनशैलान्तवासिनः is mentioned for that purpose.

  

Similarly,

  

the binary Aarya -Mleccha is mentioned as part of celebration of lifestyle diversity or cultural diversity.
