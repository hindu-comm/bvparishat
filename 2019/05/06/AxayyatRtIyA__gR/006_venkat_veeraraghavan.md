[[venkat veeraraghavan	2019-05-08, 21:04:02 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/gRfRCdHEC0c)]]



Apparently अक्षय्यतृतीया was the day when Draupadi received the Akshaya patra from Surya. The purpose of Akshaya patra was to feed the retinue that Yudhishthira maintained during vanavasa.

The property of this patra was that any quantity of food that Draupadi put into the patra lasted till Draupadi herself had her meal.

  

Also, this was the tithi of the birth of Lord Parashu-Rama. Since this avatara of Vishnu is linked with Shukra /Venus, it is traditional to give daana of white substances like Payasam, Buttermilk, Silver, Gold etc....yathashakti.

  

Accumulation of Gold / Silver is a modern marketing gimmick of Jewellers and little else. Eventhough the Jewellers are not giving daana of Gold/Silver since they give away the metal on that day, the unwashed masses are contributing to the prolonged wealth of these jewellers....my 2 cents.
