Giving below a list of 65 modern Sanskrit novels compiled from various sources. A few of them have not yet been published.   

  

I humbly request scholars of BVP to point out titles of Sanskrit novels that are not included in this list. Also, kindly point out if any titles in this list are wrongly classified as novels.
