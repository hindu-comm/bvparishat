"*In the var**ṇamālā a, ā, i, ī, u, ū, and then ऌ; la, lā, li, lī, lu, lū and then लृ.** So, these are not meant to be pronounced alike, in my humble view.*"

  

Correction:

In the varṇamālā a, ā, i, ī, u, ū, **ṛ,** and then ऌ; la, lā, li, lī, lu, lū and then लृ. So, these are not meant to be pronounced alike, in my humble view.

  

Sorry for leaving out **ṛ** because of hasty typing.
