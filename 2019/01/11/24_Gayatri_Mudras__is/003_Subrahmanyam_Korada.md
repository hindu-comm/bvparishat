नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

24 मुद्राः before गायत्रीजप are prescribed in **विश्वामित्रस्मृति - 6 अध्याय ।**

  

A simple नित्यकर्म is made complicated by adding things that are not necessary .

  

सन्ध्यावन्दनम् , with its essential parts , is discussed in postings referred to by Vid Nagaraj .

  

I may suggest - many scholars do not study वेद and शाश्त्रs and offer उपदेश - such people are not to be

taken seriously .

  

युक्तियुक्तं वचो ग्राह्यं न ग्राह्यं गुरुगौरवात् ( याज्ञवल्क्यशिक्षा ) ।

  

धन्यो’स्मि
