**Vrindavan, 2019.01.10 (VT):**The 408th disappearance day of Srila Jiva Goswami was celebrated at the[Jiva Institute](http://www.vrindavantoday.com/jiva-institutes-english-bhagavat-sandarbha-released-today/)with a short play in the Sanskrit language depicting a portion of his life. The students studying Sanskrit at the Institute were the main actors.The play was called “Jive Daya Natakam”, a play on the words “Compassion to Jiva” and “compassion to all living beings.”
