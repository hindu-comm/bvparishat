नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

For ladies , as per पूर्वमीमांसा, वेद is not to be recited . They can study / recite पुराणानि , इतिहासौ etc.

  

Some priests argue that ladies should not recite विष्णु / ललिता सहस्रनाम and it has no प्रमाणम् ।

  

Without having स्नानम् one should not perform any कर्म --

  

सन्ध्या स्नानं जपो होमो देवतानां च पूजनम् ।

आतिथ्यं वैश्वदेवं च षट्कर्माणि दिने दिने ॥ **स्मृतिः**

  

**पाठक्रमात् अर्थक्रमो बलीयान् -- पूर्वमीमांसा (5-1)** - so first it is स्नानम् and then सन्ध्या ।

  

स्नानस्य शुद्धिहेतुत्वात् , शुद्धस्यैव सन्ध्यावन्दनाधिकारित्वात् -- **पराशरमाधवीयम्**

  

I shall discuss six types of स्नानम् separately .

  

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

  

 Hope that 'pravachana-karta' is not aware of :  

  

भक्तिमान् यः सदोत्थायशुचिस्त्द्गतमानसः

सह्स्रं वासुदेवस्य नाम्नामेतत् प्रकीर्तयेत् ॥

  

That is the reason, I have stopped following the bhakti pravachans in TV...

  

                                   
    --- Vid Venkata Sriram

  

The same प्रवाचक ( I rather think so) also said during गोदावरीपुष्कर , a couple of years ago ,

that people should take a dip at a particular moment in order to get the स्नानफलम् .

  

As a result there was stampede and 29 people had died .

  

The प्रवाचक studied neither ज्योतिषम् nor व्याकरणम् --   

  

सिद्धान्तस्कन्ध (ज्योतिषम् ) says -- if बृहस्पति / गुरु enters मेष then there will be गङ्गापुष्कर , if it is सिंह then गोदावरीपुष्कर etc .

Since ब्रुहस्पति stays for one year in a राशि the पुष्कर will be for the complete period .

  

**कालाध्वनोः अत्यन्तसंयोगे (द्वितीया ) says Panini** ( 2-3-5) -- so every moment thruout the year is पुष्करकाल ।

Then how - with what प्रमाणम् - the प्रवाचक said that it is a particular moment ?

  

His lack of knowledge of शास्त्रs had cost 29 lives besides injuries to many .

  

Another point he made - people asked me -- if you light a single lamp during कार्तिकमास , all your sins

will be washed away !! Is it not indirectly encouraging people to commit sins ?

  

This is due to lack of the knowledge of पूर्वमीमांसा -- if you come across such sentences in पुराणs etc

you have to take them as अर्थवादs - used just to create an urge in the minds of people - not to be taken

the वाच्यार्थ ।
