\> Bhagwat Purana was mistranslated as not yet entered puberty

  

A girl of 8 years (aṣṭavarṣā) is spoken of as gaurī. The meaning of gaurī in such contexts is "A young girl prior to menstruation, virgin" (Āpte's dictionary).

  

Below are some unambiguous quotes from law books and other texts on this matter (all available from the GRETIL database).

  

Kind regards, Walter Slaje

  

**Parāśarasmṛti**



**aṣṭavarṣā** **bhaved gaurī** navavarṣā tu rohiṇī \|\| 7.4 \|\|  
daśavarṣā bhavet kanyā **ata ūrdhvaṃ rajasvalā** \|  
prāpte tu dvādaśe varṣe yaḥ kanyāṃ na prayacchati \|\| 7.5 \|\|  
māsi māsi rajas tasyāḥ pibanti pitaraḥ svayam \|  
mātā caiva pitā caiva jyeṣṭho bhrātā tathaiva ca \|\| 7.6 \|\|  
trayas te narakaṃ yānti dṛṣṭvā **kanyāṃ rajasvalām** \|  
yas tāṃ samudvahet kanyāṃ brāhmaṇo madamohitaḥ \|\| 7.7 \|\|  

asaṃbhāṣyo hy apāṅkteyaḥ sa vipro vṛṣalīpatiḥ \|
