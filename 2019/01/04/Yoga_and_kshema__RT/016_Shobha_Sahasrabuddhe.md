Respected Scholars,

I wish to share some interesting details related to the concept /meaning of the term 'योगक्षेम' as reflected through कौटिलीय अर्थशास्त्र.

The following words appear in connection with the education of the prince - आन्वीक्षिकीत्रयीवार्त्तानां योगक्षेमसाधनो दण्डः, तस्य नीतिर्दण्डनीतिः अलब्धलाभार्था लब्धपरिरक्षणी रक्षितविवर्धनी वृद्धस्य तीर्थे प्रतिपादनी च l (KA 1.4.3)

The above four components - acquisition, protection, growth and proper utilization (of resources) can very well be considered as essential elements underlying the concept of योगक्षेम.
