Sundareswaranji,

  

As per the information available at worldcat site, Ajita commentary on Tantravartika has been published only upto chapter 2. It was publsihed by Ganganath Jha Rashtriya Sanskrit Vidyapitha in 2 volumes. Kunio Harikai has also edited some volumes of Ajita and also Vijaya, a sub-commentary on Ajita, which too has not been published beyond the first chapter of Tantravartika.

  

2 vols of Ajita vyakhya published by GJRSV are available online.
