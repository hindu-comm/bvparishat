\> I came across the word दुष्टतानः in Valmki today.  
  

The critical edition (Rāmāyaṇa 4.2.26) reads \[...\] vijñeyā  
duṣṭatānayoḥ (duṣṭatā + anayoḥ "wickedness of these two"). Another  
possible word division is vijñeyāduṣṭatānayoḥ = vijñeyā + aduṣṭatā  
("innocence") + anayoḥ.
