##### Kalpadruma

शृङ्गारः  
, पुं, (शृङ्गं कामोद्रेकमृच्छतीति । ऋ गतौ + “कर्म्मण्यण् । ” ३ । २ । १ । इत्यण् । यद्वा, शॄ हिंसायाम् + “भृङ्गारशुङ्गारौ । ” उणा० ३ । १३६ । इति आरन्प्रत्ययेन साधुः । )

This is the etymology of शृङ्गार given in Kalpadruma.

  

There seems to be place in Guntur diatrict Andhrapradesh by name Srungarapuram of which much is not known. But there doesn't seem to be any Sharada temple. But in some places where the verse occurs, this is assimilated with Shrungeri.
