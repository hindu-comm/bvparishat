Respectedsir

            There exists a kruti namely

  

श्री चक्र साम्राज्य संचारिणींं

शृंगार शृंगेरि पुरवासिनीम् ।

  

In this kruti also sringeri is referred as Shrungara sringeri puram.

  

Thank you
