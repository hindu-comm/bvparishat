Dear Professor Jha,

  

  It may very well be that झा is derived from अध्यापक, though I have seen the forms उवज्झाय etc. in Prakrit literature. Of course, the syllable ध्या in both अध्यापक and उपाध्याय will result in ज्झा>झा in Prakrit/Apabhraṃśa, as we see ध्यान \> झान.
