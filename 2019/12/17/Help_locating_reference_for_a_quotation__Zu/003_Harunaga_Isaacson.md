[[Harunaga Isaacson	2019-12-18, 09:36:15 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/ZuHgxZJ0vvc)]]



A variant form of the verse can be found in the Sūktimuktāvalī, the great anthology of verses  
compiled in the middle of the thirteenth century CE by Jalhaṇa. It is the fifth verse of the क्रियागुप्त्यादिचित्रपद्धतिः. See the following, from a scan of the 1938 edition.  

![Suuktimuktaavalii_ed_1938_p\_414.jpg](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/1a9e3c00c45b8/Suuktimuktaavalii_ed_1938_p_414.jpg?part=0.1&view=1)

  

(Presumably one should read ऐवेति क्रिया, ऐव being the उत्तमपुरुषद्विवचन form, परस्मैपद, of the लङ्-लकार of इङ् गतौ; this being the क्रिया that is indeed rather hidden in the verse, since one does not at first expect that there might be a क्रिया in नैव ...)  
  
  
Harunaga Isaacson
