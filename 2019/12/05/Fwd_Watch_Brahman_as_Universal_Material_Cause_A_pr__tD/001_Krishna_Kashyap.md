Several years back some of us took extended lessons on important topics in Vedanta from**Sri U.Ve. K.S. Varadacharya Swami,**a renowned expert on Vedanta, Sribhashya of Bhagavad Ramanuja, and Tattva Mukta Kalapa of Swami Desika. Swami is a deeply spiritual personal in private life. He is one of the most senior scholars alive and has an unparalleled grasp of key Vedanta teachings.

  

He has written a work: satyasiddhi which attempts to bridge the 3 systems namely Advaita, Visistadvaita and Dvaita. This work is not published yet. Sri KSV is about 98 years old now. He is not able to teach now due to health reasons. He had great respect for Sri Shankaracharya, Sri Ramanujacharya and Sri Madhvacharya. He has studied all these schools in depth.
