Interesting discussion on the wordkaṣāyam.  Kaṣāyam (കഷായം कषायम् ) is used in Malayalam for Ayurvedic decoctions. Various kaṣāyam-s are very popular in Kerala as part of Ayurveda treatment for different ailments.
On the other hand, kāṣāyam with a long ‘a’ (as in kāṣāya vastram) indicates the color of the robe of sannyasins.
