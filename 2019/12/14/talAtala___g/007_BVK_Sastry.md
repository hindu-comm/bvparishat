Do I understand that your question comes in the backdrop to translate a poetic expression \< talAd api atalam = a deeper underworld far below the ground level' \> ?



The first 'tala' may refer to ' ground level' and below ground, the first hell begins with name 'atala'. 

The poetic expression may be using the alliterating sounds to the term 'tala' meaning the ' bottom, the ground level' ??



The seven ' naraka - lokas ( = hells) title listed are : 1. Athala,
2. Vithala 3. Suthala 4. Talaathala 5. Mahaathala,6. Rasaathala
7.Paataala .

There may be no need to go for derivations of the terms.



The Lexicon listing : पाताल \[Cologne record ID=31604\] \[Printed book page [4299](https://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/scans/VCPScan/2020/web/webtc/servepdf.php?page=4299),b\]  
पाताल न० पतत्यस्मिन्नधर्मेण पत—आलञ् । १ भुवनस्याधो-  
भागे २ सर्वाधोभागस्थे स्थानभेदे । तानि च सप्तैव यथा  
“पातालानि च सप्तैव मुनयः संप्रचक्षते । अतलं  
वितलञ्चैव सुतलञ्च तलातलम् । महातलञ्च विख्यातं  
ततो ज्ञेयं रसातलम्” । “ततः पातालमित्येवं सप्त  
पातालसंज्ञकाः ।
