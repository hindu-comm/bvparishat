In the Holi festival of our country stealing wooden articles for burning Kaama is allowed. It is not crime to steal for this. Sometimes children will break wooden parts from the gate or door of a house in the night and steal it. This is like the trick or treat custom. 'Give me something nice otherwise I will do something bad.' Rudram says Taskaraanaam Pataye Namah
