A program that is intended to bring to the lime light the salient features of and the distinction between Buddhism and Advaita. The program has Sri Mani Dravid Sastrigal giving the keynote address in Sanskrit Other noted scholars M.L.Narasimhamurthy, Ganesha Ishwara Bhatta and Mahabaleshwara Bhatta will be speaking in Kannada. Presiding the sessions are noted Dvaita scholars Sri Haridas Bhatta Acharya, Sri Nagasampige Acharya, etc.  

  

Dr.S.R.Leela and Shatavadhani Ganesh will be participating. There will be scholars from Buddhism too.
