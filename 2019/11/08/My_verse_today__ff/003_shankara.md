Abhyankar ji,

  

According to Kalidasa, 'in the mass of merits a single fault disappears just as the dark spots on the moon disappear among in its rays.'  

एको हि दोषो गुण संन्निपाते। निमज्जतीन्दोः किरणेष्विवांकः ।   

  

regards  
shankara
