One is reminded of the Saundaryalahari verse

दृशा द्राघीयस्या दरदळितनीलोत्पलरुचा

दवीयांसं दीनं स्नपय कृपया मामपि शिवे।

अनेनायं धन्यो भवति न च ते हानिरियता

वने वा हर्म्ये वा समकरनिपातो हिमकरः ॥

When the holy waters of Ganga come into contact with a dog, the waters are not polluted. On the other hand the dog gets rid of all its pollutions and impurities.

This idea might be the बीजम् of the maxim.
