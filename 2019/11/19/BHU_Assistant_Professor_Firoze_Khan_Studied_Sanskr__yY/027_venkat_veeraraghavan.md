Dear Shri Deva Pattanayak ji-

  

I think around the second post on this thread a member indicated that the appointment might have been made out of turn from motives of favoritism.

While I agree that being a Muslim does not and should not prevent someone from specialising in any shastra, it should still be an appointment based on MERIT and not favoritism in which case the students are justified in protesting.

The other thing that made this issue smell for me were tear jerking statements like "never felt like a muslim" etc instead of talking about his strongpoints. generally when someone goes for an emotional reason that would imply their logical reasons for being in a certain position are relatively weak.

My 2 cents.

  

Also Kindly read on the background of the "Vasudeva Kutumbakam" refrain that has become so popular despite having negative roots in literature before using it as an argument. It is not apt at all.

  

Also in a mundane issue like university appointment kindly do not bring in extrasensory issues like punarjanm etc.

We are not debating here whether Dr. Firoze Khan is Kabir or Mahavatara Babaji reborn....

  

That would be beyond the scope of any online or offline group.
