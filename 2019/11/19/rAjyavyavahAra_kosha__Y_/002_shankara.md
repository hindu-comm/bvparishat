Marathi translation of the verse by A.D. Marathe from his 'Raaj Kosh' is given below. According to Marathe, the minister to whom the work of compiling the Kosa was entrusted was Raghunath Narayan Hanmante. Please see the [link](https://books.google.co.in/books?id=5D5KBwAAQBAJ&lpg=PT71&ots=-ZXMWmqOk8&dq=%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%83%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%20%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%9A%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%9B%E0%A5%8B%E0%A4%9A%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%9B%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%A6%E0%A5%87&pg=PT14#v=onepage&q=%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%83%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%20%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%9A%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%9B%E0%A5%8B%E0%A4%9A%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%9B%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%A6%E0%A5%87&f=false).  

  

"या पृथ्वीतलावरून म्लेंच्छांचा पूर्ण उच्छेद केल्यानंतर सूर्यवंशाला ललामभूत ठरलेल्या त्या छत्रपती शिवाजी राजांनी यवनांच्या भाषेने लोपून गेलेल्या राजव्यवहारपध्दतीचा संस्कृत भाषेतून प्रसार करण्यासाठी (रघूनाथ) पंडिताची नियुक्ती केली."  

  

regards
