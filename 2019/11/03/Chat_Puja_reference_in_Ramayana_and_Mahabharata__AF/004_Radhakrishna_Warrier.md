As I have been told Chhath ( छठ) is derived from Sanskrit षष्ठी (sixth). In south India also it is celebrated as Skanda Shashthi ((സ്കന്ദ ഷഷ്ഠി स्कन्द षष्ठी). In Bihar and UP, it is the worship of Chhathi Maiya (छठी मैया = षष्ठी देवी) where as in the south it is the worship of Skanda (Skanda स्कन्द = Subrahmanya सुब्रह्मण्य, Kaartikeya कार्तिकेय, Murukan मुरुकन्)
