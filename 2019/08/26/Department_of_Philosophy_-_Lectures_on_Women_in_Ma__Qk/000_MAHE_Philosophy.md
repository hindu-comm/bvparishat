Dear all,  
  
We cordially invite you to the seminars by Anusha Rao, Canada to be held on Wednesday, 28 August 2019 at MAHE, which are open to all. You may please find the attached invitations and also share with the interested people.  
  
**Seminars**:  

1\) **Hierarchy and the Deification of the Women in the Mahābhārata-tātparyanirṇaya** at 4 pm  
2) **The Problem of Evil in Hindu Myth** at 10 am

  

**Speaker**: *Anusha S Rao*, University of Toronto, Canada

  
**Venue**: MCES - LH1, Department of Philosophy, 5th Floor, Advanced Research Centre, MAHE  
  
These seminars are ongoing activities of the Manipal Mahabharata Lecture Series organised as a part of MAHE Mahabharata Digital Concordance Research Project available at <http://mahabharata.manipal.edu>. **MAHE Mahabharata** is the first digital concordance of highly cited three critical editions that helps researchers to conduct critical study on textual variations of Mahabharata recensions. The lecture by Anusha is eighth in the series, which hosted lectures by scholars of international repute like Padmashri Bannanje Govindacharya, Prof Ashok Aklujkar (Canada), Dr Nirmalya Guha, Dr Aleix Ruis Falques (Spain), Prof Lidia Sudyka (Poland), Prof Blanka Knotková-Čapková (Czechia) and Vidvan Lakshmish Tholpadi from India and abroad. The Department of Philosophy of MAHE - Faculty of Liberal Arts, earlier known as the Dvaita Philosophy Resource Centre (DPRC) offers a Master’s in Indian Philosophy and also conducts the MAHE Mahabharata research project with the support of Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE).
