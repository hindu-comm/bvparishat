Namaste,  
  
Giving below a short list of books translated into Sanskrit from English as well as Indian languages.  
  
1. Yoginah Atmakatha, Translation of Autobiography of Yogi by Paramahansa Yogananda.  
2. Rajayoga, Translation of Rajayoga by Swami Vivekananda  
3. Siddhartha, Translation of Siddhartha by Hermann Hesse; L Sulochana Devi (2008) & Muni Kalyankirtivijay (2013). The latter is available online at <https://jainelibrary.org/book-detail/?srno=009972>  
4. Sagaravihangam, Translation of Jonathan Livingston Seagull of Richard Bach by Meera Bhatt, Muni Kalyankirtivijay, et al., available online at <https://jainelibrary.org/book-detail/?srno=001165>  
5. Gita Pravachanani, Translation of Discourses on the Gita of Vinoba Bhave by Swami Sankarananda Bharati available online at <https://archive.org/details/Gita_Pravachanani_Sanskrit_Vinoba_Sankarananda_Bharati_1962>  
6. Agnimantramala, translation of Sri Aurobindo's Hymns to mystic Fire by Jagannatha Vedalankar (1976)  
7. Yajnaseni, translation of Yajnaseni by Pratibha Ray. Digital text in Devanagari Unicode available at <http://www.sanskrit.nic.in/ASSP/yagyaseni/index.htm>  
8. Ponniyin Selvan Tamil novel in 5 vols rendered into Sanskrit by Rajalakshmi Srinivasan, and published by Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan, New Delhi in 2015.

  

  

regards  
shankara
