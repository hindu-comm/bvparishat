Many kinds of food including paayasa are referred to in Mahabharata also. "परमान्नं तु पायसम्"।। २.७.८५८ ।।Amara lists in food items as above in the context of foods for yajna. Ramashrami explains as पयसि संस्कृतम्। "संस्कृतं भक्षाः" इत्यण्। द्वे क्षीरान्नस्य।
