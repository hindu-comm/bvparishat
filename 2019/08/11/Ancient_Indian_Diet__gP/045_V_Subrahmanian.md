Among Tamil Iyers there is a sub-sect known as 'vaDaman' colloquially called 'vaDamaaL' (in plural). These people are said to have migrated to Tamil Nadu from the banks of the Narmada in the North. In their sandhyavandanam there is a special namaskara for Narmada.
