Begin using vyādha-śva-śāvaka/śiśuḥ or a similar formations and it will gradually gather around itself the shades of meanings that are associated with the English phrase ‘Basset Hound puppy’.  
  
yasminn uccarite śabde yadā yo ‘rthaḥ pratīyate /  
tam āhur arthaṁ tasyaiva. nānyad arthasya lakṣaṇam //  
  
a.a.
