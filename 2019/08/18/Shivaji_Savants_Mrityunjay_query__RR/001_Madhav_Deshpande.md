Dear Irene,

  

  If you read Marathi, I would recommend a book by Daji Panshikar titled कर्ण खरा कोण होता?. This is not a novel, but a critical evaluation of Karṇa based on a study of the Mahābhārata, a counter to the one-sided glorification of Karṇa seen in some Marathi novels.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
