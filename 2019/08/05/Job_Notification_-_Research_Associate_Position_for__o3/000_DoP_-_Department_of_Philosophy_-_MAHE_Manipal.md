The Department of Philosophy invites application for the *Research Associate* position for its research project "[MAHE Mahabharata](http://mahabharata.manipal.edu)". BORI, Kumbhakonam and Sastri-Vavilla.

  

We are looking for researchers who have,  

-   Adequate knowledge of Sanskrit -   Experience in proofing and editing of the Sanskrit texts -   Good knowledge of computer and keying mainly Indic scripts -   Ability and willingness to work in teams in multicultural
    environments
