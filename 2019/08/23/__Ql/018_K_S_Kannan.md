error:

In verse 8 above, the wording should be

*citta-nirvR**ti**-vidhAyi*

(not *citta-nirvR**tti**-vidhAyi*)

\- ie. no double *t*

as *nirvRti* and *nirvRtti* have different senses.

(The metre will also be violated if we have it there as *nirvRtti*).
