[[Irene Galstian	2019-08-11, 11:02:31 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/mfzQIQtmrUg)]]



It's not easy to describe. Approximately speaking, the so-called  
'reality' becomes more dreamlike, and the nonphysical becomes more  
'real', with the effect that you perceive a wider range of energies,  
you might say. Rather than being busy with inferences between things  
already within the detection range of sense organs, you start seeing  
more and more how 'things' move from beyond this detection range into  
it. At the same time, whatever 'happens' becomes virtually  
unimportant. Thinking is less, since it's rather restrictive and less  
efficient than focus, which creates much quicker, with virtually no  
effort. Like that, approximately. It's not easy to describe, at least  
for me. Maybe someone else would do it better.  
  
Irene
