It is especially because Krishna is Bhagavan that I do not see Him transgressing the letter and spirit of the dharma shastras. So if He did something, the act must have been in conformance with dharma shastras of His time.

  

This is encapsulated in several instances in His leela, where He takes a Guru Sandeepani and plays faithfully the role of a śiṣya ...
