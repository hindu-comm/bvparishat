In the Brihadaranyaka Upanishad instance of Ajatashatru, a Kshatriya, instructing Baalaaki, a braahmana, on Brahma vidya, it has been pointed out that while a lower varna can be an instructor to an upper varna, the namaskara, etc. honor (by the upper varna to the lower varna) are excepted.
