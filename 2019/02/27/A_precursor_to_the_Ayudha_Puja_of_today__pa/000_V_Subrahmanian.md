In the Chaturvarga Chintamani Vol.2. Part 1, (Vrata kANDa), Hemadri details the Mahanavami vrata. At the end he cites a mahanavami vrata from the Atharvana Veda Gopatha Brahmana:

  

There is a AtharvaNa Veda Gopatha Brahmana reference to Mahanavami puja: The words 'vaahanaani snapayitva..' reminds us of the present day Ayudha puja observed by us at the end of the Navaratri, by washing the vehicles....
