Here is the most recent extensive study of Sanskrit gerund \[-tvā, -ya\] formations:

  

# The Sanskrit gerund: a synchronic, diachronic and typological analysis. By Bertil Tikkanen. (Studia Orientalia, edit, by the Finnish Oriental Society, 62.) pp. vi. 378 + corrigenda slip. Helsinki, Finnish Oriental Society1987. 200 Finnmarks.

The bibliography of this book is the best exhaustive listing of publications using the term "gerund" to refer to Sanskrit forms in -tvā/-ya.
