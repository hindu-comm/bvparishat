Rāmakr̥ṣṇa in his Saṃskāragaṇapati on the Pāraskaragr̥hyasūtra quotes a verse of Kātyāyana to this effect:

  

yannāmnātaṃ svaśākhāyāṃ paroktaṃ avirodhi ca /

vidvadbhis tad anuṣṭheyam agnihotrādikarmavat //
