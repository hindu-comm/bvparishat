Regarding Vasishtha making preparations for Rama’s coronation, which got aborted Bhoja says in Champu-ramayan, beautifully employing slesha,

“कान्तारकन्दखननं रचयेति नूनमाबद्धवान् प्रतिसरं भगवान् वसिष्ठः”
