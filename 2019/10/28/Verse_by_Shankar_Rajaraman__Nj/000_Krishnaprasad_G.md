Dear all

Dr Shankar Radharaman on dipavali

दीपावलीमहेऽस्मिन्नायान्त्या गेहमब्धिकन्यायाः ।

विस्फोटकशब्दानां मध्ये शृणुयाम नूपुरनिनादम् ॥

  

As Lakshmi comes to our homes during this

Dipavali, may we hear the jingle of her

anklets amidst the noise of crackers.
