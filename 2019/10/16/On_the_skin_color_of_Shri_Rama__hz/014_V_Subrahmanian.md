There is an idiom in Hindi: aankhen laal laal karna which means 'to look at someone furiously', or simply, to be angry.
<https://hindistudent.com/hindi-vyakaran/hindi-muhavare/aankhe-laal-karana/>

  

In the Bhagavadgita verse: 5.23:

शक्नोतीहैवयःसोढुंप्राक्छरीरविमोक्षणात्।  
कामक्रोधोद्भवंवेगंसयुक्तःससुखीनरः॥ २३ ॥

  

to describe the 'lakshana' for 'krodhodbhava vega', Shankara writes:  

  

गात्रप्रकम्पप्रस्वेदसन्दष्टोष्ठपुट*रक्तनेत्रादि*लिङ्गःक्रोधोद्भवो वेगः,
