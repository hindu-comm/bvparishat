[[venkat veeraraghavan	2019-10-24, 00:19:17 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/ZiKE_YEmocc)]]



Not related to the root question here...but it is interesting how re-readings bring out nuances from Valmiki's Ramayana  

तस्यर्षेः परमोदारं वचः श्रुत्वा नरोत्तमौ \|

*स्नात्वा कृतोदकौ* वीरौ जेपतुः परमं जपम् \|\| १-२३-३

  

A reference to snaanaanga tarpana as done by Rama and Lakshmana from Balakanda just before sandhyavandana.
