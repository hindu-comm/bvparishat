Hare Krishna.  
  
Just information for Sivasenani's mention of yayavara:  

  
\>\> Yaayavaaram is also allowed, I believe. I am sure that we can find supporting evidence  
  

SB 7.11.16  
ārtā vicitrā śālīna-yāyāvara-śiloñchanam  
vipra-vṛttiś caturdheyaṁ śreyasī cottarottarā  
  
Sridhara:  
yāyāvaraṁ pratyahaṁ dhānyayācñā  
  
Anvitartha prakasika & Visvanatha:  
yāyāvaraṁ pratyahaṁ dhānyamātrayācñā  
  
Sukadeva:  
yāyāvaraṁ bhīkṣāṭanam  
  
Thankyou,  
Damodara das
