The*Yoga-Kundalini Upanishad*is a common era text, composed sometime after[Yogasutras](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yoga_Sutras_of_Patanjali "Yoga Sutras of Patanjali").^([\[14\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yoga-kundalini_Upanishad#cite_note-14))Banerjea states that the Yoga-Kundalini text, like many late Yoga Upanishads, deals with yogic concepts and methods taught by Siddha Yogi teachers such as[Gorakhnath](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gorakshanath "Gorakshanath"), an 11th-century yogi.^([\[15\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yoga-kundalini_Upanishad#cite_note-15))
