I think Sanskrit paṭa (पट) means a membrane, cloth, curtain etc. It could mean screen, but not a board. In Malayalam, paṭam (പടം) has all the above meanings plus the meaning “picture”. Additionally pāmpiṉṯe paṭam (പാമ്പിൻറെ പടം) means the skin of a snake.
