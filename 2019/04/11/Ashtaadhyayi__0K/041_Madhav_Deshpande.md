Dear Achyut Ji,

  

  Please read the Mahābhāṣya on P.1.2.29-30 (p.206, Vol 1, ed. by Kielhorn, re-edited by K.V. Abhyankar, BORI, 1962): न पुनरन्तरेणाचं व्यञ्जनस्य उच्चारणमपि भवति । ... स्वयं राजन्ते स्वरा: अन्वग् भवति व्यञ्जनम्. This should answer the doubt you expressed: "however I never noticed any statement to the effect that a consonant cannot be phonated without a suffixed vowel."
