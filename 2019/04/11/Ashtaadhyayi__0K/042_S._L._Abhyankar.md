Consonants at the end of a word can be pronounced, even if they are without vowels, e.g.

-   सुप् तिङ् अच् rather
-   अनुबन्ध-s of all शिवसूत्र-s are हल्-s only and they are pronounced
    without vowels.
-   सर्वे प्रत्याहाराः अपि हलन्ताः - न किमपि काठिन्यं तेषामुच्चारणे (!)
