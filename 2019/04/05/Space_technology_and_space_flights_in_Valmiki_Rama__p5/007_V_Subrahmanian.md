The fourth chapter of the Gaudapada karika of the Mandukya upanishad is termed 'alAtashAntiprakarNam.' The analogy of the alAta is employed here. One example is:

  

ऋजुवक्रादिकाभास*मलातस्प*न्दितं यथा।  
ग्रहणग्राहकाभासं विज्ञानस्पन्दितं तथा ॥ ४७ ॥

[भाष्यम्](http://advaitasharada.sringeri.net/display/bhashya/Mandukya#bhashya-MK_C04_K47)

यथोक्तं परमार्थदर्शनं प्रपञ्चयिष्यन्नाह — *यथा हि लोके ऋजुवक्रादिप्रकाराभासम् अलातस्पन्दितम् उल्काचलनम् ,* तथा ग्रहणग्राहकाभासं विषयिविषयाभासमित्यर्थः।

  

There are many more occurrences of this term in this chapter. I have also seen a reference to this alAta in the Srimadbhagavatam, Uddhavagita context.
