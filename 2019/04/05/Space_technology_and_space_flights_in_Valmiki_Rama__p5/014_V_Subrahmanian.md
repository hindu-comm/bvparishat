Namaste,

  

To my knowledge there is no such upanishad by the name 'Alaata-shanti-upanishad'. If this name is found in any authentic text, it must be enquired into. But there is an opinion among some historians thus:

  

The fourth chapter of this Upanishad entitled*Alata Shanti Prakarana*(Quenching the Firebrand) talks about the theory of non-creation.

  

Sri Vidhushekhara Bhattacharya and others held that the four chapters of the Gaudapada Karika are actually four upanishads or together an upanishad.

  

The Srimadbhagavatam reference to alata chakra may be examined. I shall give the exact verse later.
