Very nicely put. Actually the pancha-avayava-vakyam of anumaana is a fine example for this:

  

१)प्रतिज्ञावाक्यम्‌=पर्वतो वह्निमान्‌

२)हेतुवाक्यम्‌=धूमवत्त्वात्

३)उदाहरणवाक्यम्‌=यो यो धूमवान्‌ स स वह्निमान्‌ यथा महानसम्‌

४)उपनयवाक्यम्‌=तथा च अयम्‌\|वह्निव्याप्यधूमवान्‌ अयं पक्षः\|

५)निगमनवाक्यम्‌=तस्मात्‌ तथा\|तस्मात्‌ धूमवत्त्वात्‌ तथा वह्निमान्‌\|

  

The first part, pratijnaa, claim, is systematically developed and the fifth part, nigamana, re-stating the very first part, is significant. For the development please read here:<https://sites.google.com/site/samskritavyakaranam/10---nyAyashAstram/23---anumAnapramANam---vibhAgaH-atiriktaH-guNAH>
