The late Sri K T Achaya, the distinguished food technologist and food historian, always quotes *Mānasollāsa* in his works**when he traces the history of most dishes that are prepared today in western and southern India. I suppose he was working off of an English rendering of the*Mānasollāsa.*He does not give any direct quotes however.
