चित्तं तु चेतो हृदयं स्वान्तं हृन्मानसं मनः ।। १.४.३१५ ।।  
  
मानस and मनस् are synonyms. But मनस् and मन are not.

And the explanation given also wrong. सोल्लास doet mean rejoicing, but one having happiness.
