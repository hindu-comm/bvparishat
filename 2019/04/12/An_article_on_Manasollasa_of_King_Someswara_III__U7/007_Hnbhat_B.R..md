The Wikipedia article has correct title with diacriticsThe***Mānasollāsa but***

***has wrong explanation as compound of manas and ullasa without***diacritics***.***

This would not give the correct compound***Mānasollāsa***

but would give मनउल्लास instead. There correct one is given as an alternative:

Alternatively, the compound word can be broken as*manasa*and*ullasa*, which mean "happiness of mind".^([\[5\]](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manasollasa#cite_note-5)[\[6](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manasollasa#cite_note-6))

Only wrong explanation taken with some correction to make the title look correct, but failed.
