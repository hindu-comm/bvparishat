In the Dharma Shaastra there is the prescription of 'इष्टापूर्तकर्म' where the pUrta is described as:

  

वापीकूपतडागानि देवतायतनानि च। अन्नप्रदानमारामः पुर्तमित्यभिधीयते ।। 
\[Construction wells, tanks, temples, free-food distribution centers, parks, etc.\]

  

There are many Smriti texts of very ancient periods that prescribe this. Also we find in the Mahabharata and other Puranas many instances of the wordदेवतायतनानि:
