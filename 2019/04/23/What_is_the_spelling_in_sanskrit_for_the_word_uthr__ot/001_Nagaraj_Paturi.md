[[Nagaraj Paturi	2019-04-23, 15:04:30 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/otgeSjrbTo8)]]



It is pronounced as उद्धरण uddharaNa by Telugu people.

  

It makes sense because uddharaNa means lifting up , picking up etc. (ut + haraNa )

  

UddharaNi is also heard.

  

It is possible that uddharaNi changed into utrani .
