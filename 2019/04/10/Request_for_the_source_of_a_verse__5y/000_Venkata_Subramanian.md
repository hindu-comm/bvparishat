The shloka given below is सिद्ध मन्त्र for bala kanda and sundara kanda for Valmki Ramayanam

  

सुप्रसन्न: शान्तमना:सत्यसन्ध:जितेन्द्रिय:।

धर्मज्ञो नयसारज्ञो राजा दाशरथिर्जयी॥

The source of this shloka is not known

It is not in valmki ramayana or any related work.

I request scholars to inform the source

N S Venkata Subramanian
