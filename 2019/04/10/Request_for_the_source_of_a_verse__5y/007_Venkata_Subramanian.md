This is a shloka from Aromas Bhagbavagham.  
In Prathama Amanda this appears as shloka no.3i in chapter or adhyaya 17.  
The full shloka is  
" abhyarthithastadaa Tasman sthanaani kalaye dadau\|  
Durham paanam sfriyaha suunaa yatraadharmashchatur vidaha\|\|"  
This verse is not from MahaBharatham  
N S Venkata Subramanian
