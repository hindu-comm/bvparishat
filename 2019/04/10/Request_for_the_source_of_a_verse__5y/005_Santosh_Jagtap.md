I request scholars to inform the source of following text which is said to be in Mahabharata. There may be some mistake in devanagri verse written below. I have seen it on wall of Radha Govind Temple, Jaipur.  
  
द्युतं पानं स्त्रिया सुना यत्र अधर्माश्च चतुर्विधाः  
  
It tells about 4 regulative principles : No meat eating, No Gambling, No Intoxicatiion, No illicit sex.

  

Thanking you,  
Santosh Jagtap
