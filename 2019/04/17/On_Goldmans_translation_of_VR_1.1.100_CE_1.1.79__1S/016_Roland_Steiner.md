\> Similarly , the विशेषणम् ॑ नीलः ॑ in ' नीलः काकः ॑ is also unmeaningful as  
\> a काक is always नील only and therefore there is no व्यभिचार to the विशेषणम्  
\> - नीलः ।  
  

Counterexample:  
  
athāhaṃ dṛṣṭavān \[...\]  
kālakākolavalayam ... (Mokṣopāya VI.15.23-24)  
  
"Then I (Vasiṣṭha) saw a flock (valaya) of black (kāla) ravens (kākola)."
