The word *ardhatisraḥ*in the sense of 2.5 sounded interesting to me.
The Marathi word for 1.5 is दीड \< द्वि+अर्ध, and दियड्ढ is attested in Pali. Similarly, Marathi for 2.5 is अडीच \< अर्धत्रि. Pali uses अड्ढतेळस \< अर्धत्रयोदश in the sense of 12.5.
