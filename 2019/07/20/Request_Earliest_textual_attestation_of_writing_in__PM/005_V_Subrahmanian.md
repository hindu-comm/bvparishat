There is this famous verse on how the Veda should not be recited. This verse contains 'likhita-paaThakah' = one who reads out from what is written:

  

गीती शीघ्री शिरःकम्पी तथा लिखितपाठकः ।  
अनर्थज्ञोऽल्पकण्ठश्च षडेते पाठकाधमाः ॥  

  

I do not know the source of this verse and its antiquity.
