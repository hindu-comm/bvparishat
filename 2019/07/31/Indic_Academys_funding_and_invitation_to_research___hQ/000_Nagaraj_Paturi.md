There is a 1980 book

  

" Advaida-Vedanta Literature

  

A Bibliographical Survey"

  

written by

  

Sri R Thagaswami

  

(Nyaaya VyaakaraNa Vedaanta S'iromaNi

  

RaashTrabhaashaa praveeNa, Hindi Vidvan)

  

Lecturer in Sanskrit

  

Sanskrit Department, University of Madras

  

published by

  

University of Madras

  

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

The book is in Sanskrit.

  

There is a Foreword in English bySri K Kunjunni Raja

  

Introduction by the author is in English.

  

There is a huge chart of Advaitagranthakartrgurus'ishyaparamparaavrikshah tree diagram of the guru-s'shya lineage of the authors of Advaita books attached to the last page .

  

The book is in 454 pages with an additional 62 pages of Indexes of Authors, books, names and places, quotations etc.
