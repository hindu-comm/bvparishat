Bijoy ji,

  

So the ASI is cognizant of this site, but they are not doing more the bare minimum - is what I gather from this thread.

If you are still in Odisha, perhaps you could consider getting in touch with the curator of the State Museum at Bhubaneswar and share your photos with a note on site deterioration.

Utkal University has a PG Deptt of Ancient Indian History, Culture and Archaeology. Perhaps the HoD/research scholars may be able to help out in this regard.

Utkal University of Culture has PG and PhD programs in Culture Studies and South and South East Asian Studies. Perhaps the faculty/research students there might be interested in taking this further.
