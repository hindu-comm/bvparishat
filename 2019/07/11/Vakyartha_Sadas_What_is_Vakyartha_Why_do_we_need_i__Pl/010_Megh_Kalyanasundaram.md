# [*5/5: Vakyartha Sadas \| Brahmasri Mani Dravid on Traditional Pedagogy*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mCoAuDlwhIo)

  

"Prof. K.S.Kannan summarises the event. Brahmasri Mani Dravid, speaks in Hindi on the nature of traditional pedagogy. The reason for much of Western Indology’s misinterpretations derive from the lack of a valid context in their learning and understanding. Irrespective of the motives of the (western or otherwise) scholars, knowledge is to be acquired based on its actual basis and evolution from the actual practitioners of the knowledge.The guru-sishya parampara and the primarily oral tradition of pedagogy are explicated.
