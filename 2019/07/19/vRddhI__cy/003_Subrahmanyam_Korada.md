नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

What are the two parts that make the आ a vruddhi swar?

  

                   ----- Vid Acyut Karve

  

वृद्धिः -- **वृद्धिरादैच् (पा 1-1-1 )**

  

वृद्धि is a संज्ञा ( designation / name ) -- i e one should not take its literal meaning - Nor is it a स्वर .(prefer Sanskrit to Hindi).

  

आ , ऐ and औ - are called by the name वृद्धि in my शास्त्रम् -- says Panini -- ’ **प्रत्येकं वाक्यपरिसमाप्तिः** ’ -- so each of the three has got the name ---

  

to give a name is for ' brevity ' -- **लघ्वर्थं संज्ञाकरणम् (महाभाष्यम् )**

  

प्रयोजनम् --

  

**इको गुणवृद्धी ,मृजेर्वृद्धिः , वृद्धिर्यास्याचामादिः तद् वृद्धम् , वृद्धिरेचि**

  

If you take the meaning there will be problem with -- **गुणः , टि , घु , भ , अङ्गम् , निष्ठा , सत् , अपृक्तः** etc .

  

There may be some exceptions -- **सर्वेषां नामानि सर्वनामानि** -- but one has to check with **महाभाष्यम्** ।

  

**A suggestion -- just sit down with a qualified गुरु and study the fundamentals of the शास्त्रम् ।**

  

As you know it is difficult to discuss basic issues - hope you understand .

  

No doubt , any serious question will be attempted by any one of the galaxy of scholars .
