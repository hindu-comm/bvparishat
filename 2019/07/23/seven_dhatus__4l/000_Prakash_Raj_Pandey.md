Pranam to all learned scholars,

  

Bhagavat (2.10.31) enumerates seven dhatus by saying:

  

त्वक्चर्ममांसरुधिरमेदोमज्जास्थिधातवः ।

  

Now, you can certainly explain difference between त्वक् and चर्मन्; I have regarded both of them to be "skin".

  

विदुषाम् अनुचरः

प्रकाशः
