The word paṇḍita looks to me as another candidate of a word that probably originates in vernacular Prakrit usage and then enters into Sanskrit. The Pali word for prajñā is paññā and the Ardhamāgadhī word is paṇṇā. The word paṇḍā seems to me to be a closely related vernacular variant.
