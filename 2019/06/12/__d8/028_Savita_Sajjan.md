    Regarding अभिप्रायिक नाम - 1st letter should be**घोषवदादि--
Middle word should beअन्तःस्थ (as per translators ) Last word should be -ऊष्मान्तम्**

 

     Ex -गजानन , रमा ,

      **Q 1) Following above principle /sutra/rule , Practically, whether many names can be framed ?  
**

**      Q 2) Gajaanan - name will not fit in the second or middle word and last word/ criteria ?**

**      Q 3) What is the importance of this rule in keeping अभिप्रायिक नाम?**

**       Q 4) Whether all our names follows above rule? **

  

**(घोषवदादि**-- the name should start with a letter having a बाह्यप्रयत्न , called घोष , i e any one of the following letters --

ग घ ज झ ड ढ द ध न ब भ म ह य र ल व ( हश् प्रत्याहारवर्णाः -- ङ and ञ are not counted for obvious reasons).

**अन्तःस्थान्तम्**-- the name should end with any one of the following letters -- य र ल व ।

or

**ऊष्मान्तम्****वा**-- should end with any one of the following letters -- श ष स ह ।)
