नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

**नामकरणम् --- व्याकरणम् - ज्योतिषम् - कल्पः (गृह्यसूत्रम् )**

**  
**

**At the outset one should note that he / she cannot give any name of his / her choice - such as - डित्थ , डबित्थ , टिंकु ,**

**रिंकु etc -- although it is called नामकरणम् , a नाम is being put rather than given -- पुत्रस्य नाम निदधाति (न तु करोति )**

**- सुदर्शनाचार्य , the famous commentator , clarifies - why ? शब्दस्य नित्यत्वात् ( आपस्तम्बगृह्यसूत्रम् ) ।**

**According to पाणिनीयम् , संज्ञाशब्द-s are those which are already there in language - so not to be created.  
**

  

1) As per charak Abhiprayika name should be (**घोषवदाद्यन्तस्थान्तमूष्मान्तंवाऽवृद्धं^(\[[४](http://localhost:4001/ecaraka/?mod=read#foot_4)\])त्रिपुरुषानूकमनवप्रतिष्ठितं,नाक्षात्रिकंतुनक्षत्रदेवतासमानाख्यं^(\[[५](http://localhost:4001/ecaraka/?mod=read#foot_5)\])द्व्यक्षरंचतुरक्षरंवा\|\|५०\|\|)**

                                   
        ----- विदु सविता सज्जन्

  

आभिप्रायिकं नाम means the name liked by father and mother (of the child) - elsewhere (लघुमञ्जूषा etc) it is called

व्यावहारिकं नाम - the name to be used by all .  

  

The other one is नाक्षत्रं नाम -- the name that follows the जन्मनक्षत्रम् of the child -- some scholars said that the नामनक्षत्रम्

should not be revealed -- जन्मनाम तु गोपयेत् । This , they think is simply to save the child from अभिचारहोम (**अथर्ववेदे -**

**अभिचारः = हिंसा** ) by enemies .

  

नाक्षत्रनाम - is also useful , in case the person ,due to lack of familiarity with ज्योतिषम् , forgets his जन्मनक्षत्रम् , then the

पुरोहित will make out from his नाक्षत्रनाम ।

  

This practice of having two names is not there in Telugu states - most of them refer to the horoscope - or (wrongly)

follow the नामनक्षत्रम् ( got by reverse gear - referring to the chart by the व्यावहारिकनाम ) ।

  

For नाक्षत्रनाम -- these are available in any पञ्चाङ्गम् (Almanac ) ---

  

चू चे चो ला -- अश्विनी

  

पे पो रा रि -- भरणी etc

  

Example -- चू चे चो ला - अश्विनी means if the child is born in अश्विनीनक्षत्रम् , which has got four पाद-s (legs) , -- first पाद

\- the name should start with the letter चू ( चूडामणिः etc ) and so on .

  

**It is not possible to provide all names for all नक्षत्र-s -- 27 नक्षत्र-s x 4 पाद-s = 108 ( there should be some choice for each- again male and female ) .**

  

**घोषवदाद्यन्तस्थान्तमूष्मान्तंवाऽवृद्धं^(\[[४](http://localhost:4001/ecaraka/?mod=read#foot_4)\])त्रिपुरुषानूकमनवप्रतिष्ठितं,नाक्षात्रिकंतुनक्षत्रदेवतासमानाख्यं^(\[[५](http://localhost:4001/ecaraka/?mod=read#foot_5)\])द्व्यक्षरंचतुरक्षरंवा**  

  

**घोषवदादि** -- the name should start with a letter having a बाह्यप्रयत्न , called घोष , i e any one of the following letters --

ग घ ज झ ड ढ द ध न ब भ म ह य र ल व ( हश् प्रत्याहारवर्णाः -- ङ and ञ are not counted for obvious reasons).

  

**अन्तःस्थान्तम्** -- the name should end with any one of the following letters -- य र ल व ।

or

**ऊष्मान्तम्****वा** -- should end with any one of the following letters -- श ष स ह ।

  

**अवृद्धम्** -- the first letter should no be any one of -- आ ऐ औ ( **वृद्धिरादैच् , वृद्धिर्यस्याचामादिः तद् वृद्धम् -- पा सू** )

  

**त्रिपुरुषानूकम्** -- the name should indicate three four fathers of the father .

  

**अनरिप्रतिष्ठितम्** ( there is an error in the text - even the commentators - **both Sanskrit and Hindi on चरकसंहिता**

- left this ) - the name should not be that of an enemy (of the family) .

  

**नाक्षात्रिकंतु नक्षत्रदेवतासमानाख्यम्--**But for नाक्षत्रनाम --it should coinside with the name of the देवता of the नक्षत्रम् -- अग्निर्नः पातु कृत्तिका (**कृष्णयजुर्वेदः**) - if the boy is born in
कृत्तिकानक्षत्र any name of अग्नि will be okay. It means one need not follow the above said नियम-s - but has to follow in आभिप्रायिकम् नाम one should follow the same .

  

**द्व्यक्षरंचतुरक्षरंवा --**In any case **the name should have two or four letters - not more than that .**

दशमेत्वहनि-- after tenth day ( **दशम्युत्तरकालम् -- Patanjali under
' दशम्यां पुत्रस्य ' - पस्पशाह्निकम् , महाभाष्यम् ) -- because  
**

**there will be अशौचम् ( मैला in Hindi and मैल in Telugu ) for ten days - inclusive .**

  

सपुत्रास्त्री - the woman with the child ( male / female ) - **लिङ्गम् अविवक्षितम्** ।**  
**

**  
**

सर्वगन्धौषधैर्गौरसर्षपलोध्रैश्चस्नाता-- having had bath with all powders , herbs , white musturd and Lodhra (Symplocos Racemosa)  

  

लघ्वहतशुचिवस्त्रंपरिधाय-- having put on a dress that is light , new and clean  

  

पवित्रेष्टलघुविचित्रभूषणवतीच - also having adorned ornaments that are pure , liked , light and of different types  

  

संस्पृश्यमङ्गलानि -- having touched auspicious things  

  

अर्चयित्वाचदेवतांम् -- having worshiped the Godess  

  

शिखिनःशुक्लवाससोऽव्यङ्गांश्चब्राह्मणान्स्वस्तिवाचयित्वा-- having taken blessing of ब्राह्मण-s , who - have tufts , with white  

cloths and not handicapped

  

कुमारमहतानां^(\[[**३**](http://localhost:4001/ecaraka/?mod=read#foot_3)\])चवाससांसञ्चये---
making the child put on new clothes  

  

प्राक्शिरसमुदक्शिरसंवासंवेश्य--- having made him / her sit facing east or north

  

देवतापूर्वंद्विजातिभ्यःप्रणमतीत्युक्त्वा-- having said (made) him / her salute God and ब्राह्मण-s  

  

पिताद्वेनामनीकारयेन्नाक्षत्रिकंनामाभिप्रायिकंच-- the father should
arrange to put two names - नाक्षत्रिकम् and आभिप्रायिकम् ।
