## HRD Minister moots Sanskrit villages

HRD Minister Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ on Thursday said that at least two Sanskrit-speaking villages must be developed near the central institutes promoting and preserving the language. The Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan, the Lal Bahadur Shastri Rashtriya Sanskrit Vidyapeeth in Delhi and the Rashtriya Sanskrit Vidyapeeth in Tirupati are the three central institutes promoting the language.PTI
