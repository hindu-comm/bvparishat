> नि: + शब्द: = नि:शब्द:

It is just an orthographic style to write "**निःशब्दः**" in order to represent the sound "**निश्शब्दः**" ।

  

In fact the actual spoken word is **nishshabdaH** & not **niHshabdaH**(in ITRANS style) due to the sandhi transformation (as explained by Shivakumari ji).
