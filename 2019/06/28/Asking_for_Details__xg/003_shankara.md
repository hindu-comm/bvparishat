Giving below bibliographical details of various editions of Pavanaduta and related studies.  

  

Pavanaduta Editions:

  

1\. Pavanaduta edited by Manomohan Chakravarti in 1905, published in JASB

2\. Pavanaduta edited by Chintaharan Chakravarti 1926 published in Samskrita Sahitya Parishad Granthamala -<https://archive.org/details/pavanaduta>  
3. Pavanaduta edited and translated into Hindi by Umesh Chandra Sharma and Girish Chandra Sharma in 1978 - <http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/23539143>  
4. Pavanaduta edited and translated into Hindi by Maheshanatha Chaturvedi and Sivabalaka Dvivedi in 1982 - <http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/312563196>  
5. Kaviraj Dhoyi's Pavanadutam translated into English by Pabitra Mohan Nayak 2005 - <http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/855779385>  
6. In 'Messenger poems' translated into English Verse by James Mallinson published in Clay Sanskrit Library Series in 2006   

  

Studies on Dutakavya literature:

  

1\. Origin & Development of Dutakavya Literature in Sanskrit by Chintaharan Chakravarti published in Indian Historical Quarterly Vol 3 in 1927 - <https://archive.org/details/IndianHistoricalQuarterlyVol.3/page/n293>

2\. Vamana Bhatta Bana's Hamsa Sandesa and Its Intertexts - Yigal Bronner (JAOS 2013)  

3\. A New Composition of the Dutakavya Genre - Rathangaduta - Daniela Rossella 2008  
4. Elephants in Sandesa Kavyas - Dhanesh Wisumperuma_JRASSL_57_2012  
5. History of the Duta Kavyas of Bengal - JB Chaudhuri 1953  
6. Keraliya Samskrita Sandesa Kavya Samiksha - NP Unni 1996 (RSS)  
7. Megha Sandesa A Critical Appreciation - M Rangacharya 1967  
8. Meghadut Ki Parampara Me Rachit Adhunik Sandesh Ka Anushilan - Narendra Dev 2015 (Thesis)  
9. Meghduta Evam Sarika Sandesa Ka Kavyasastriya Tulanatatmak Adhyayan - Suman Sukala 2005 (Thesis)  
10. Pavana Dutam Ka Samikshatmak Evam Tulanatmak Adhyayan - Neetu Devi 2007 (Thesis)  
11. Sanskrit Ke Sandesa Kavya - Ramkumar Acharya 1962 (Thesis)  
12. A Critical Study of the Sanskrit Dutakavyas of Kerala - Sudha Garg 1985 (Thesis)
