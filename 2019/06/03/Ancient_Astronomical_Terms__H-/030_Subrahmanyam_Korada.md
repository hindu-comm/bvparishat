नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

I would appreciate if the question was the other way round - rather the question itself exhibits the sorry state of affairs with regard to the great शास्त्रम् - ज्योतिषम् ।

  

अथाधिज्यौतिषम् , पृथिवी पूर्वरूपम् , द्यौरुत्तररूपम् ... **शीक्षावल्ली , तैत्तिरीयोपनिषत्** ।

  

ज्योतिषामयनं चक्षुः -- **पाणिनीयशिक्षा** -- ज्योतिषाम् अयनम् -- लक्षणा - तद्बोधकं शास्त्रमित्यर्थः - it is the eyes of वेदपुरुष ।

  

ज्योतिषं नेत्रमुच्यते (वेदपुरुषस्येति शेषः ) -- another **शिक्षा** ।

  

निष्कारणं ब्राह्मणेन षडङ्गो वेदो’ध्येतव्यो ज्ञेयश्च -- Patanjali in **पस्पशा , महाभाष्यम्** । निष्कारणम् - not expecting anything in return (कारणशब्दः फलपरः) ।

  

**Panini** touched all the three **स्कन्ध-s of ज्यौतिषम्** -- मुहूर्तस्कन्धः , जातकस्कन्धः (होरास्कन्धः) , सिद्धान्तस्कन्धः । ( he recorded that people were running with horoscopes ) .

  

Unlike other शास्र-s this is useful on a regular basis -- तिथि , नक्षत्र , दुर्मुहूर्त , वर्ज्य etc .

  

Question -- then why such a sorry state of affairs with regard to ज्यौतिषम् ? व्याकरणम् , मीमांसा etc are better .

  

One may go thru the so many works / commentaries related to व्याकरणम् / पूर्वमीमांसा / न्यायवैशेषिके / वेदान्त etc -- no scholar takes up any issue related to ज्यौतिषम् seriously !

  

Why ? They could not master this शास्त्रम् --

  

वराहमिहिराचार्य declares that he was compiling the popular work बृहज्जातकम् (फलितज्योतिषम्) for scholars of व्याकरणम् / मीमांसा / न्याय etc since they miserably failed in mastering ज्यौतिषम् , preferably the होरातन्त्रम् --

  

भूयोभिः पटुबुद्धिभिः पटुधियां होराफलज्ञप्तये

शब्दन्यायसमन्वितेषु बहुशः शास्त्रेषु दृष्टेष्वपि ।

होरातन्त्रमहार्णवप्रतरणे भग्नोद्यमानामिदम्

स्वल्पं वृत्तविचित्रमर्थबहुलं शास्त्रप्लवं प्रारभे ॥ 2

  

पटु means the one who , in spite of hurdles , can finish the job - they have the mental capabilities .

  

ज्ञप्तिः = ज्ञानम् ( **प्रौढमनोरमा** ) । शब्दन्याय - refers to व्याकरणम् , मीमांसा and न्याय -- दर्शन-s .

  

No doubt , they dedicated their lives to propagate / illuminate
different शास्त्र-s .

  

A couple of years ago I have exhibited to one of my students as to how to discuss the compatibility between the horoscopes of braid and groom on Bluetooth , while negotiating blind turnings with a car in Hyderabad .

  

Actually , some centuries ago there used to be scholars who learnt both वेद and शास्त्र-s -- as the time passed there has been a vertical division between scholars of वेद and शास्त्र - if at all scholar of a शास्त्र learns वेद it is confined to just पुरुषसूक्तम् , शतरुद्रियम् ( नमकम् and चमकम् ) , शान्तिमन्त्राः and something that is useful for शिवपञ्चायतनम् / विष्णुपञ्चायतनम् and so on . I have been watching for the last sixty years .

  

The Sanskrit terminology of technical terms of ज्योतिषम् is known to us ( people of this generation ) since some decades .

  

I herewith attach four pages of material where one can find a number of terms related to ज्योतिषम् - only drawback is it is English and Telugu (तत्समशब्दाः) and I do not have time to offer the Sanskrit equivalents - I beg some scholar may do a favor .

  

While Latitude is अक्षांश / शर , Longitude is रेखांश । Ecliptic is क्रान्तिवृत्तम् ।
