In प्राज्ञ पाठशाला of वाई, तर्कतीर्थ लक्ष्मणशास्त्री जोशी often used to read with his classmates as instructed by his गुरू केवलानन्दस्वामी whenever he was preoccupied with senior students.Further, चिन्तनिका or discussion among the शिष्यs on the topic taught by गुरू on the same day was an integral part of learning.
