Brahman, the word is neuter. The entity Brahman does not have any gender. Yet we see this entity being referred to through all the three genders:

  

सेयंदेवतैक्षतहन्ताहमिमास्तिस्रो देवता अनेन जीवेनात्मनानुप्रविश्य नामरूपें व्याकरवाणीति ॥ २ ॥ Chandogya Upanishat 6.3.2 Here it is referred to in the feminine gender: devataa.

  

In this mantra, again, from the above upanishad:स य एषोऽणिमैतदात्म्यमिदꣳ सर्वं तत्सत्यꣳ स आत्मा तत्त्वमसि श्वेतकेतो we have, in one mantra itself, both the masculine - saH aatmaa and the neuter tat satyam.
