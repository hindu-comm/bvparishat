For word "param-parā " meaning given is correct ,but "sampradāya" is special word with different grammatical & other traditional meaning or interpretations (like this <http://spokensanskrit.org/index.php?tran_input=sampradA&direct=se&script=hk&link=yes&mode=3> ) one meaning or interpretation for "sampradāya" (which i love) given by Acharya Vinoba Bhave is "what ever knowledge I got that I should give to my colleague & be free" in hindi \*मुझे जो ज्ञान प्राप्त हुआ है , वह मै साथी को दे कर मुक्त हो जाऊ\*
