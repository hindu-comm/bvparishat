Pranam to all learned scholars,

  

In the 6th Sarga of Kishkindha-kanda of Adhyatma-ramayana, I found this:

  

न लक्ष्यसेऽज्ञानदृशां शैलूष इव रूपधृक् ।

  

In Gita Pres's Hindi translation, शैलूषः becomes मायावी.

  

Can there be a better word to carry the meaning?

  

Prakash
