Namaste,

  

AFAIK, the sanskrit equivalent for 'saffron' is 'काश्मीरज'.

  

In our tradition, we chant the गुरुपादुका स्तोत्रं and one of the lines in the couplet is 'काश्मीरज-पुष्पसार-सुलेपिताभ्याम्' ie., those holy pair of guru sandals that are

smeared with saffron.

  

rgs,
