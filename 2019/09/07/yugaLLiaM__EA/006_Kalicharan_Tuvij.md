The meanings can be reconciled.

yuj if understood in the sense of sexual union, can mean suitability, agreeableness, pairing etc.

ArAt if understood as : at some distance, can mean either near or far.

dhruva if understood in the context of spin, can mean both (spinning) motion or (a point on the axis) rest.

savya already means both left and right together.
