In this auspicious occasion of Navaratra - I am happy to share the link of Yoga vaisharadi - <https://kymyogavaisharadi.org/>

(a free search able web repository of Classical yoga literature)  

The site has been further refined, the texts have been re-organized and the home page wears a new look.  

  

Following are the features -  

**Yoga-vaiśāradī - A Free Searchable Comprehensive Web-repository of Classical Yoga Literature.  
An Initiative of Research Department of Krishnamacharya Yoga Mandiram  
**

-   15 Classical Samskrita Yogasutra-Commentaries– spanning nearly 1700
    years in one place -   Hatha-yoga-pradipika with Samskrita Commentary -   All 700 verses of Bhagavad-gita -   All texts in Devanagari, Roman, Tamil, Kannada and Bengali Scripts -   Audio Rendering of Entire Vyasa’s Yogasutra commentary making it
    more accessible -   English & Tamil Translations of Select Commentaries -   All texts Searchable in Roman and Devanagari scripts

I give two images - in Samskrita and English of the above, so that it can be circulated among interested people.
