Namaste,

  

shrI Alangudi Periyaval (Swayamprakashananda Saraswati Swamiji) attained Jivanmukti and used to roam like Avadhuta in Alangudi. He got initiated into Bhagavatam from Balakrishnananda Saraswati Swamiji..Since then, He used to do parayana of Bhagavatam only and nothing else.

Additional Info:

  

Shri Jayendra Saraswati Swamigal of Kanchi Mutt, was a devotee of Krishna...and had by-heart the Sridhara's commentary of Bhagavatam. His Holiness had great command on Bhagavatam's Sridhara's commentary and it was He who initiated into secrets of Bhagavata to the great advaitin scholar Mahamahopadhyaya, Advaita Samrat, Abhinava Shuka Shri.Maddulapalli Dattatreya Sarma Garu. He was initiated into the Bhagavata secrets with all the commentaries in Vijayawada from His Holiness.
