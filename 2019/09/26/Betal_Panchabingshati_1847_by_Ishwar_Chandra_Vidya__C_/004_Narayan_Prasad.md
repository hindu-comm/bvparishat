The first critical edition of वेताल पञ्चविंशति in Sanskrit in the devanagari script was edited by N.A. Gore and published from Poona in 1952.

  

He has mentioned also the version prepared and published by Pt Jibananda Vidyasagara from Calcutta in 1873.

<https://ia800402.us.archive.org/5/items/Complete_Works_of_Jibananda_Vidyasagara/Vetala_Pancha_Vimsati_-_Jivananda_Vidyasagara_1873.pdf>  

  

Its 2nd edition came out in 1907.

<https://ia800402.us.archive.org/5/items/Complete_Works_of_Jibananda_Vidyasagara/Vetala_Pancha_Vimsati_-_Jivananda_Vidyasagara_1907.pdf>  

  

The 3rd edition of 1924 also contains meanings of certain terms as footnotes which are useful for students.

<https://archive.org/stream/Complete_Works_of_Jibananda_Vidyasagara/Vetalpanchavimshati_-_Jivananda_Vidyasagara_Bhattacharya_1924#page/n5/mode/2up>
