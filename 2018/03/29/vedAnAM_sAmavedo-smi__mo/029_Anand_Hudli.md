Dear Scholars,

  

Here is a book with an excellent article on Soma.

  

<http://sanskrit.bharatavani.in/view-pdf/?pdf=vyaakhyaan-vallaree>

  

Please see the article by Shri Pattabhirama Sastri -सोमस्वरूप विमर्शः, page 41.

  

On page 46, he says:

ऋग्वेदे पवमानं मण्डलं समग्रमपि महिमानमेतस्यैव (=सोमस्यैव) वर्णयति। सामवेदस्य तु कैव कथा? सौम्यं गुणगणं गौरवं च वर्णयितुमेव स प्रवृत्त इत्यपि वक्तुं शक्यते। अत एव सामवेदः सोमवेद इति शब्देऽपि नातिभेदमुपलभामहे।

  

He has a discussion onसोमरसो मादको वा?, where he gives many western scholars' arguments for intoxication by Soma consumption. Next, he presents arguments why Soma is not intoxicating likeसुरापान.

An interesting article!

  

Anand
