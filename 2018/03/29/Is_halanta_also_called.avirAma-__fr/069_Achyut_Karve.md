To be more pointed in my query my question is are there only 26 basic lingual sounds in the English language? If there are more sounds how were they reduced to 26 to put them down in writing? Even though it has come down to us by convention the question still remains how did the convention evolve. 

  

It is interesting to observe that though the written Roman alphabet is the same it is recited differently in English French or German. 

  

However in India even though the recited alphabet is the same it is written in different scripts.

  

Why this contrast?
