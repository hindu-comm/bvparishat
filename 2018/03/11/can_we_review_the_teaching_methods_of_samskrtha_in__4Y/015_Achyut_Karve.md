Why should every Indian learn Sanskrit?

  

This is exactly the question we need to ask ourselves. This question needs to be answered by Sanskrit pundits and Sanskrit scholars. How has the Sanskrit language transformed thier lives not because of the information they got by coming to know Sanskrit but its effect on their personality. Education is not just about information and logic. Its primary target is to mould personalities. That was the reason why Socrates had to drink poison.

  

How well Sanskrit as a medium of instruction can serve this purpose is the question.
