नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

are different formats of sankalpa available?

  

Also how would one construct a sankalpa citing jitendriya (conquering one's senses) and conquering hunger, lust and sleep?

  

                                   
    ---- Vidvan Niranjan

  

संकल्प has got only one format .

  

Why should one construct a different संकल्प than the one accepted as शिष्टाचार ?

  

कालः , नाम , गोत्रम् (धर्मपत्नीसमेतः) - are to be mentioned . Even if one is a यति / संन्यासी the same is the procedure .

  

No विशेषणs are to be added to the नाम / गोत्रम् etc.

  

Someone may want to add something else - then there will be अव्यवस्था ।

  

These issues need वेदशास्त्रप्रामाण्यम् and प्रयोजनम् ।

  

धन्यो’स्मि
