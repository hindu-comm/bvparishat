Sankalpa is a statement of one's wish to do a सत्कर्म stating the देश, काल, one's Name and Gotra and the Prayojana. However you can do a Sankalpa for things which are कृतिसाध्य. Jitendriyatva is only a wish (इच्छा) and hence not कृतिसाध्य. Hence no Sankalpa is possible.
