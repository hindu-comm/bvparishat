Dear colleagues,



I am looking for contributors for an edited volume on the Bhagavad gita; the publisher is interested in various topics and the focus at this point is on the following topics:



1. Medieval Gita commentaries; Shankara and Ramanuja.

2. Modern Gita commentaries; Gandhi, Tilak, Sri Aurobindo.

3. Gita and the Indian National movement ( such as Bankim Chandra Chatterji).

4. Gita by 20 c. gurus such as Bhaktivedanta Swami, Maharishi Mahesh Yogi, Swami Chinmayananda.

5. Gita in Western culture, literature and Philosophy.
