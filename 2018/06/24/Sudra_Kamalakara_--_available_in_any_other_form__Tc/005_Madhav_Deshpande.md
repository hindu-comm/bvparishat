Here is some information regarding Kamalākarabhaṭṭa:

  

![image.png](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/6d15e564f29ba/image.png?part=0.1&view=1)  

His other famous work is Nirṇayasindhu. He was the uncle of Gāgābhaṭṭa alias Viśveśvara Paṇḍita who performed the Rājyābhiṣeka for Shivaji in 1674.
