\>\> Respected Vidvaj-janas,  
\>\>  
\>\> Is there a sanskrit subhasita equivalent to the phrase --  
\>\>  
\>\> "If wealth is lost nothing is lost,  
\>\> if health is lost something is lost,  
\>\> if character is lost everything is lost"  
\>\>  
\>  
\>  
\> not exactly what you are looking for, but this one from विदुरनीतिः goes  
\> somewhat closer -  
\>  
\> वृत्तं यत्नेन संरक्षेद् वित्तमेति च याति च। अक्षीणो वित्ततः क्षीणो  
\> वृत्ततस्तु हतो हतः ॥  
\>  
\> Protect the character with efforts. Wealth will come and go. If wealth is  
\> lost, nothing is lost. If character is lost, everything is lost.  
\>  
\>
