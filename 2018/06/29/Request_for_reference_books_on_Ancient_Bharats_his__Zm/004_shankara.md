Kunwarlal Vyasasishya has written the following books in Hindi dealing with Indian history based on Puranas.  

  

1\. Puranon Mein Itihas

2\. Puranon Mein Vanshanukramik Kalakram  

3\. Bharatiya Itihasa Punarlekhan Kyon Evam Puranon Mein Itihasa vivek  

4\. Puranon Mein Aitihasik Privart Yug   

  

Links:

<https://archive.org/search.php?query=Vyasasishya>
