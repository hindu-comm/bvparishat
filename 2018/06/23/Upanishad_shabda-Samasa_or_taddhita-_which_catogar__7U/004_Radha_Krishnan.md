Upanishad = upa + ni + sad + kvip

  

Upa and ni are the two upasargas or prefixes. Upa= near; ni=surely.

  

Sad (सद्) is the verb which denotes three things. 1) to destroy completely 2) to reach or to understand 3) to loosen.

  

Kvip is a krit-pratyaya or suffix. This pratyaya, when added to the verb, denotes the doer of the action.

  

In the first meaning of the verb, Upanishad means, the "Complete Destroyer". And etc.

  

  

  

Note that this pratyaya is a sarvalopi pratyaya, that is the entire pratyaya will be deleded.

  

I wrote what I understood. Scholars may rectify the mistakes.
