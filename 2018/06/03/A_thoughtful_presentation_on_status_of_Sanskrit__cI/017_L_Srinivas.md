It may be a good idea to read Prof Deshpande's article on the subject of Sanskrit in Maharashtra following the 1817 defeat of the Peshwa. The article also has plenty of references if one were interested in following up further on the subject.

  

M.M.Deshpande, 'Pandit and Professor: Transformations in the 19th Century Maharashtra', in A.Michaels (ed.),The Pandit: Traditional Scholarship in India (Delhi, MRML, 2001).

  

Hope this helps,
