At the end of Narayana Sukta we have:

  

।नीलतोयदमध्यस्था विद्युल्लेखेव भास्वरा ।



  

But in the dakshina paaTha we say: At the end of Narayana Sukta we have:

  

नीलतोयदमध्यस्थाद्विद्युल्लेखेव भास्वरा । If the 'd' is inserted, will there be any correct meaning? Sayana's reading is the first shown above.

  

I request scholars to clarify.
