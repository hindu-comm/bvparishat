Namaste,

  

A similar use of the word मठिका is found in the first chapter of Hathayogapradipika. There मठिका is used in the sense of a small cottage. The commentator says - अल्पो मठो मठिका.  

एकान्ते मठिकामध्ये स्थातव्यं हठयोगिना॥ हयो-१.१२॥  

  

regards  
shankara
