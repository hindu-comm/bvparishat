This is indeed a laudable effort. Even in this material age, that such people as these are working for the conservation of our history and dharma is praiseworthy.

  

The govt should institute a massive effort to enlist on proper salaries such motivated persons and entrust them with the discovery and archiving of all surviving manuscripts and materials available across India.

  

A separate Department of Ancient Knowledge Systems at a central ministry level should be raised and given adequate funding.
