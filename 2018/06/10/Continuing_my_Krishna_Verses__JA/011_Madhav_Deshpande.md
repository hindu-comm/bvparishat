Continuing my Krishna Verses:

  

जगन्मोहन कृष्ण त्वं मोहयित्वाखिलं जगत् ।

गूहसे हृदयाकाशे भव मे दृष्टिगोचर: ।।४०८।।

O Kr̥ṣṇa, Enchanter of the world, having enchanted the entire world you hide in the space of my heart. Be visible to me.

  

आगत्य माधव यदा पुरतो मम तिष्ठसि ।

त्वयापहृतचित्तोऽहमशक्त: शब्दयोजने ।।४०९।।

O Mādhava, when you come and stand in front of me, with my mind stolen away by you, I am unable to find words.

  

धन्यं दिनमहं धन्यो दृष्टोऽसि त्वं सुदर्शन ।

एकमेवाभ्यर्थनं मे जगतीं मधुरां कुरु ।।४१०।।

Fulfilled is the day and fulfilled I am. O Beautiful Looking Krishna, I have seen you. I have only one request. Make this world sweet.
