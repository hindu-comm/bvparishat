(We, they) could be a natural characteristic of human sensing.

India struggled hard to discover that the difference does not exist.

The west has not reached there. It may.

We have to discover how the perceptual information was analyzed in India to reach the larger conclusion.

We have only some logistical arguments in the Upanisad by creating counterarguments.

Even if a philosopher discovered it, possibly it can only be taught through cultural outreach.

I get lost when I think about the possible processes of educational outreach.

No, it is not religion. It is cosmology, it is science.

Pollock's book gets irritating with "we, they". There is no "our gravity" and "their gravity."
