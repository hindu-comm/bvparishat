शारदा शारदाम्भोजवदना वदनाम्बुजे।  
सर्वदा सर्वदास्माकं सन्निधिं सन्निधिं क्रियात् ॥1॥  
  
शारदा-अम्भोज-वदना The face of the one which resembles the autumnal lotus  
सर्वदा the giver of everything  
शारदा Goddess Sharada (Saraswati)  
अस्माकम् our  
वदन-अम्बुजे in the lotus like mouth (speech)  
सर्वदा always  
सत् निधिम् great wealth  
क्रियात् make  
  
भावार्थ: -  
Let Goddess Saraswati whose face resembles a beautiful lotus of the autumnal season, who bestows everything make her presence, which is veritable great wealth, in our speech.
