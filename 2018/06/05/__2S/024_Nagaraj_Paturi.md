These are not mere repetitions. If we call them repetetions, it becomes punarukti which is a dosha. But what we have here is Yamaka, an alamkaara.

  

There are four not two yamakas here.

  

शारदा as vyasta. शारदाas samasta in शारदाम्बोजवदना

  

सर्वदा as a vis'eshaNa सर्वदा as avyaya.

  

वदना as the last word in शारदाम्बोजवदना . वदना as the first word in वदनाम्बुजे

  

सन्निधिं as a noun in accusative case. सन्निधिंas adverb (as residence)
