Also Yudhishthira declares qualities determine who can qualify as a brahmana (in the yaksa prasna episode of the Mahabharata)

  

But the mere fact that it was felt necessary to articulate this indicates that the tendency to equate birth with qualification was already in motion  
  
of course, if we take birth as synonymous with training and virtue inculcation (which is what good nurturing is, basically) then it does make sense to make birth the qualifying factor

  

but this heuristic shortcut of taking birth as the determinant of brahmanahood ceased to be accurate when the upbringing/nurture aspect broke down sometime in the past
