It gives me immensepleasure to announce that the ensuing issue of theResearch Journal of the Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Sansthan, entitled ***Dhīmahi******Vol- 10*, ISSN 0976-3066 for the year 2018-19 is inviting articles.



**The standardresearch paper should reach us on or beforeAugust 31, 2018.**



**Guidelines: **

Ø Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Sansthan invites innovative original contributions in the fields of Sanskrit language and linguistics, Sanskrit literature, Indian philosophy, comparative philosophy, Veda-Vedangas, ancient Indian scientific literature having relevance in the modern context including Āyurveda, Mathematics and indigenous medical practices and knowledge systems, commentaries, ancient Indian art and architecture and their comparison with forms of world art and architecture, book-reviews, creative writings in Sanskrit and Indology, unpublished/rare manuscripts, comparative and inter/multi-disciplinary research findings wherein all the above areas and similar western contributions are compared/critically studied/edited etc.

Ø Articles shall be in Sanskrit, Hindi and English languages containing a maximum of 4000 words with ample margins providing contributors’ complete address, phone number and E-mail id.

Ø Notes and references should appear at the end of the articles as ‘Notes’.

Ø Only unpublished and original contributions will be considered for publication. Should there be major changes, the article will have to be revised by the author.

Ø Copyright on articles published in the journal shall remain vested with the Shodha Sansthan.

Ø For the papers written in English, quoted words from Sanskrit, Ardhamagadhi, Prakrit, Apabhramsa, Arabic or Persian etc. should be with diacritical marks with normal text if rendered in Roman Script. *The name of the texts or compositions, journal and complete sentence other than English should be in italics. *

Ø Authors should submit the original manuscript, typed on one side of A4 paper along with a soft copy in CD ROM or through E-mail may be sent to the Director/Editor, [dhimah...@gmail.com]().**** ****

Ø The article should be typed in Devanagari Mangal or Arial Unicode MS or Kokila or Sanskrit 2003 font, size 14-16. In English, Times New Roman with diacritical font, size 12 preferably in MS Word along with PDF.

Ø A copy of the printed journal will be provided to the contributor.
