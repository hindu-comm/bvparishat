Dear Prof. Upender Rao,

  

  Thanks for your concern. What I mean by कवीनां च कपीनां च पदानां नास्ति बन्धनम् is identical with what Mammaṭa expressed with his verse: नियतिकृतनियमरहितां ह्लादैकमयीमनन्यपरतन्त्राम् । नवरसरुचिरां निर्मितिमादधती भारती कवेर्जयति ।। I intended to refer to the inherent freedom of a poet. I hope that explains my intention. With best wishes,

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus

Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan
