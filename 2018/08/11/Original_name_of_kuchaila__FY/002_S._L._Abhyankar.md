Since चैलम्means garment (See चैल*a.*Made of cloth.**-लम्**A piece of cloth, garment)कुचैल can be simply an adjective, denoting a person, whose attire was unbecoming. In most Sanskrit stories a Brahmin is always a poor Brahmin दरिद्रः ब्राह्मणः. By corollaryकुचैल becomes a fitting adjective forदरिद्रः ब्राह्मणः, in turn, maybe सुदामा.

  

Actually प्रातिपदिकम् ofसुदामा isसुदामन्. (See दामन्*a.*Liberal, donor; Mb.12.92.17.Also दामन्*n.*\[दो-मनिन्\]**1**A string, thread, fillet, rope.)

  

I have been thinking thatदामन् should be from धातुः दम् (To control, to regulate See दम् 4 P. (दाम्यति, दमित, दान्त)**1**To be tamed.**-2**To be calm or tranquil; Ms.4.35;6.8;7.141.**-3**To tame, subdue, conquer, restrain; यमो दाम्यति राक्षसान् Bk.18.29; दमित्वाप्यरिसंघातान् 9.42,19;15.37.**-4**To pacify.*-Caus.*(दमयति-ते)**1**To tame.**-2**To subdue, conquer, overpower; अस्त्वयं सर्वदमनः सर्वं हि दमयत्यसौ Mb.1.74.8.**-3**To afflict, crush down; अतिभारेण दमयति Pt.4.)

  

Takingदामन् to be from धातुः दम् the wordसुदामाshould mean one, who has good (self-) control.
