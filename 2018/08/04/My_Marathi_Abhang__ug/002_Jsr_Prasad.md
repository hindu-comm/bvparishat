Respected Prof. Deshpande Ji!

  

It is very nice to read a couple of your abhangs in BVP. English translation helped to realize the profundity of these abhangs.  

Chanting 'Vitthala' not only brings us puNya but also physical health, is researched by Pt. Prasad Joshi *[et.al](http://et.al).* They published a [clinical research report](http://vedvidnyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Vithal.pdf) on effect of chanting Vitthal on heart (2014). This is quite encouraging and some of our members might be aware of this news.

  

Is abhang, a Sanskrit word?  

  

Thanks and regards,

Prasad
