'தொனி விளக்கு' (doni viLakku) 1944

  

Dr. P. S. Subrahmanya Sastri was Prof.of Sanskrit, who was the first to be awarded [Ph.D.in](http://Ph.D.in) Tamil by the University of Madras for his comparative study of Tamil grammar with Sanskrit grammar.

This is available for sale in the Kuppuswami Sastri Research Institute, Chennai.

He was the first to translate Tolkappiyam into English ( Eluthu and Porul ) available with KSRI for sale.

He has also translated the entire Mahabhashya in English, published by the KSRI in 14 Volumes.

He was rewarded with many titles including by the great Paramacharya of Kanchipuram...
