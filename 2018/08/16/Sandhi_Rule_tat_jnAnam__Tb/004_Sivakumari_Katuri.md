> >   The sounds represented by the letter ज्ञ are ज्+ञ. Therefore, > तत्+ज्ञानम् is phonetically the same as तत्+ ज्+ञानम्. Here there > are two processes, 1) voicing of त् before ज् \[by झलां जश् झशि\] and > the change of the resulting द् to ज् before ज्+ञानम् \[by स्तो: श्चुना > श्चु:\]. Thus we get the final result तज्+ज्ञानम् which is > phonetically the same as तज्+ज्+ञानम्.
