Thanks, I thought થકી was an old form of[થાકવું](https://gu.oxforddictionaries.com/થાકવું)and not a*vibhakti* suffix. With this, જિહ્વા થકી અસત્ય ન બોલે can be rendered as **जिह्वया वदति यो नासत्यं**, with

  

जिह्वया वदति = ८ मात्राः

यो नासत्यं = ८ मात्राः  

  

This will also have the*karaṇatva*intact as in the original. Thanks to Dr. Bhatt and Sh. Ujjvala Rajput for the help.
