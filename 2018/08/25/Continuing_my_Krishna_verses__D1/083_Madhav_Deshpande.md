Continuing my Krishna verses:

  

चञ्चलं मे मन: कृष्ण बध्नामि तव पादयो: ।

अयि माधव मा धाव कुत्र कर्षसि मे मन: ।।५६४।।

O Krishna, I tie my fickle mind to your feet. O Madhava, don’t run. Where are you dragging my mind?
