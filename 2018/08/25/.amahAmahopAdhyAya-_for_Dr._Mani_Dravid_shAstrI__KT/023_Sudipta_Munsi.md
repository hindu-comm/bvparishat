I just saw Prof. Varakhedi's email. Heartiest congratulations to Prof. V. N. Jha. I met him in Calcutta in August, 2016, when he came to deliver the key-note address to a seminar on Translation Studies, organised by the Calcutta Sanskrit College and University - Prof. Godavarisha Mishra introduced me to him at the Ramakrishna Mission Institute of Culture, Golpark (I don't know if he remembers, though).
Here is a verse on him -

  

śāstraikanetraḥ khalu tarkasūriḥ

sacchātravṛddhyai bahulekhakartā/

vidyāprabhābhiśca ya eva nityaṃ

cakāsti so’yaṃ vibudho vasiṣṭhaḥ//
