Thank you very much for informing about this recognition of the great scholarship of these stalwart intellectuals. I have already written about Prof. Mani Dravid but I got to know about the richly deserved honour to V.N.Jha through this communication. One is so happy to see such recognition to these scholars and when it happens to a personal friend like Jha it is doubly gratifying. Jha, I remembered the days we spent with you in Poona (as it was called then) and, even though we hardly communicate, I keep in touch with all your scholarly activities not only through BVP news but also through our common friends. I am sure this is only a prelude and both these "Maṇis"are bound toreceive many such accolades in the future.

om
