Namaste

  

My intention is certainly not to support the claims being made in such forums. I only wanted to inform the group that engages such people who malign the Hindu society to be aware of texts such as that I posted and see how best to properly interpret them and send a correct message to society. We can also see in the Shaankara Bhashya that the parama purushartha, moksha, is happily admitted for shudras. Only that it is not through the vedic path but through the means of the puranas. This was the situation during the times of Shankaracharya that shishTas admitted.
