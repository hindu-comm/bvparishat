Bhashapariccheda( popular in Bengal) is another name of Karikavali of Vishwanatha Panchanana on which Vishwanatha himself wrote a commentary called Nyayasiddhantamuktavali.

Navya Nyaya Bhasha pradipa is by Maheshachandra Nyayaratna. Available with English translation and elaborate notes with diagrams by Prof. Mrs. Ujjwala Jha, published by the Asiatic Society, Kolkata under the title : A Primer of Navya Nyaya Language and Methodology.
