\<\< Panini has used only 41 pratyaharas. Out of the 42, the no. 19, viz. चय् has been used only in the varttika.>\>

  

If we count "ञमन्ताड्डः" (पञ्चपादी उणादि 1.111), then there is the 43rdप्रत्याहार ञम्.
