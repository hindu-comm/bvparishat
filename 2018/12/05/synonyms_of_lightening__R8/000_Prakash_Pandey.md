Pranam to all learned scholars,

  

अमरकोषः gives 10 synonyms for lightening, and तडित् विद्युत् सौदामिनी are the three of them.

  

Now we have these examples from Bhagavat Puran:

  

कालः प्रादुरभूत्काले तडित्सौदामिनी यथा ॥ (1.6.28)  

  

ततश्चाविरभूत्साक्षाच्छ्री रमा भगवत्परा ।

रञ्जयन्ति दिशः कान्त्या विद्युत्सौदामिनी यथा ॥ (8.8.8)

  

Is there some subtle differences in the meanings of these synonyms, or the use of one of them renders the other redundant?

  

Prakash
