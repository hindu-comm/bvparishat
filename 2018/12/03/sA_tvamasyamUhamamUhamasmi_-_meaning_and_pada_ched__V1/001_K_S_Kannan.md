Let me answer in brief and in essence.

  

सा**and अम form a pair,

respectively the feminine and masculine.

"You are सा**and I an अम."

  

Sandhi:

अमः + अहम् = अमोऽहम्*.*

sandhied form in संहिता-पाठ = अमूहम्*.*
