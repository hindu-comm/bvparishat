\> "\*न देवा यष्टिमादाय रक्षन्ति पशुपालवत्। यं यं रक्षितुमिच्छन्ति  
\> बुद्ध्या संयोजयन्ति तम् ॥". \*Could the original source be identified?  
  
See Mahābhārata 5.35.33 (crit. ed.: na devā yaṣṭim ādāya rakṣanti  
paśupālavat / yaṃ tu rakṣitum icchanti buddhyā saṃvibhajanti tam).  
Moreover, the critical edition of the Mahābhārata records an  
additional stanza following 5.34.79, numbered 192\* in the critical  
apparatus (2nd line: yaṃ hi rakṣitum icchanti buddhyā saṃyojayanti tam).
