This is simply a misunderstanding of Boehtlingk's explanation. Boehtlingk does not say here that पित् "can be oxytone (उदात्त)", but that the elements धि in the quoted "examples" (रारन्धि, यन्धि, युयोधि) are "Oxytona" (Plural of "oxyton") - "despite पित्" (which is per se indicative of an अनुदात्त accent).
