[[Ganesh Ramakrishnan	2018-12-28, 17:10:13 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/q9gSevKZKqI)]]



Namaskaram to all scholars, guides and well wishers on this group.  

  

This is to invite you to the Lokarpan (launch) of Dharmawiki on 6th January, Sunday between 9:30 AM and 1 PM:<http://dharmawiki.org/dharmawiki/index.php/Main_Page>. You all have been and are always already part of this effort from its inception. Dharmawiki is afterall, **our** platform! If anyone of you would like to join and need any kind of assistance, please donot hesitate to contact me or Smt Nischala Mahodaya (in CC).

  

The Venue will be Y B Chavan Auditorium,7/8, General Jagannath Bhosle Rd, Nariman Point, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400021 (<https://goo.gl/maps/STmH9V9sErp>)  

  

The Lokarpan will start with Veda chanting at 8:30 AM. Prof. Vaidyanathan, Dr. Madhukishwar, Dr. Subramanian Swamy and Mr Ratan Sarda have all agreed to speak on the occasion. We also have two panel discussions, of which one of them, titled "Divided We Fall" will feature discussions between some Sampradayik scholars.

  

Please find attached the introductory slides of Dharmawiki and a few article samples highlighting our ideas given below. We need contributions from all of you as well as your friends to edit this respositoryboth in quality and quantity (which can be done collaboratively just like Wikipedia can be).

  

While we have attempted to present thoroughly researched articles for a starting point (see several articles at<http://dharmawiki.org/dharmawiki/index.php/Main_Page>), the crux of dharmawiki is to engage our scholarly community in coming together to strengthen the Rishi Parampara and present points of agreement as our mainstay and points of differences only strengthened by the points of agreement.
