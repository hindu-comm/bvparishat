Dear Sir,

  

Is Sandhi Natural and spontaneous?

  

Suppose take the two words देव and इन्द्र.

देव + इन्द्र = देवेन्द्र

  

If we utter the two words देव and इन्द्र using our vocal cords so rapidly that there is no time gap between व and इ, then does it sound as देवेन्द्र?

  

Regards

Ramana murthy
