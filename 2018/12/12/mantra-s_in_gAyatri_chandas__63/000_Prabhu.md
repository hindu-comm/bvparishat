At the end of the medhA sUktaM we have a mantra in gAyatri chandas:

हंसहंसायविद्महे \|

परमहंसायधीमहि \|

तन्नोहंसःप्रचोदयात्‌ \|

ITRANS

haMsahaMsAya vidmahe \|

paramahaMsAya dhImahi \|

tanno haMsaH prachodayAt \|

  

The second pAda has 9 akshara-s, instead of 8.

  

Likewise in atharvaveda sUryopanishat, I saw what seems to be “sUryagAyatri” mantra:

  

आदित्यायविद्म्हे।

सहस्रकिरणायधीमहि।

तन्नःसूर्यःप्रचोदयात्‌।

ITRANS

AdityAya vidmhe \|

sahasrakiraNAya dhImahi \|

tannaH sUryaH prachodayAt \|

  

The first pAda has 7 akshara-s and

the second pAda has 10.

  

Aren’t the gAyatri mantra-s of the above type supposed to fit the format:

\< \> vidmahe

\< \> dhImahi

tanno \< \> prachodayAt

  

Where each of the 3 pAda-s has 8 akshara-s?
