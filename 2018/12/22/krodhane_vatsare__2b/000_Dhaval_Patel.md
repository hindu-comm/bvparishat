Recently while preparing a catalogue of manuscripts,

I came across the following two entries.

  

MS 49 - क्रोधने संवत्सरे फाल्गुने शुक्लपक्षे पौर्णमास्या दिने पूर्णं जातम् ।

MS 325 - इदं पुस्तकं शके १७२७ क्रोधननामसंवत्सरे आश्विनवद्यसप्तमी इन्दुवार तद्दिने समाप्तम् ।

  

Question - I do not know anything about the names of various samvatsaras. Is it safe to presume that the 'krodhana' samvatsara mentioned in MS 49 also refers to shaka 1727? Or the same names of samvatsaras are used cyclically? If so, what all years had the name 'krodhana'?
