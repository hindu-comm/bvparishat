At least in Telugu there is no elongation in pronouncing राम etc. The confusion is because the vocative in Telugu is the elongated end vowel.
 रामा in Telugu is हे राम! in Sanskrit. But the Telugu speakers usually do not elongate the last vowel if it is a Sanskrit bhajan as
हरे राम हरे कृष्ण .
