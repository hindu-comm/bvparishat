There is a great need to study Shastras in modern contaxt. There are lot more things ancient indian traditional knowledge can give to the world.Every sanskritist thinks that ancient Shastras and modern science should go hand-in-hand.With this thinking, department of Vastu in Shri lal bahadur shastri rashtriya sanskrit vidyapeeth singed MOU (momerandum of understanding) with renouned School of planning and Architecture (SPA), Delhi (institute with national importance) on 18th october 2018.

  

We want our traditional students should have knowledge of modern architecture along with ancient Vastu. And modern students of architecture are also feeling a need to study Vastu, (because of huge demand in modern time). So, This is the first initiative of our collaboration -

  

A workshop on " Vastushastra and modern architecture" will be held during 7-21 january, 2019. It's a joint program of Department of Vastu, Shri lal bahadur shastri rashtriya sanskrit vidyapeeth, New delhi-16 and School of planning and Architecture (SPA), Delhi (Institute with national importance).
