As far as I know the scriptures do not favor organ donation. In orthodox vedic circles there is the thinking that donating an organ from a dead body renders the body disqualified for 'offering' to Agni for obsequies. However, there are other views too as for example,

  

# Message on Organ Donation by Shri Visvesha Teertha Swamiji of Shri Pejawar Mutt

  

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w1CFQBNF95E>  

  

In this message in Kannada, the Swamiji says 'It is quite a meritorious act to donate the organ to a needy person. The case of Shibi in the purana is pramana for us in this regard.'

  

There are other views too in the following list, which I have not studied:
