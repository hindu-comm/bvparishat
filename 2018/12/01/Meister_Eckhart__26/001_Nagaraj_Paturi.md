University of Madras published a book titled " Global Spirituality Towards The Meeting of Mystical Paths" under it's Philosophy department's SAP program. It has various comparisons. It compares the theology of St Grancisof Assissi with Visishtaadvaita and the last chapter is on the comparison of Miester Ekhart:s thought with Advaita. This was in early 80's.
