IAST transliteration could have beenఆచ్ఛికācchikaఅచ్చతెనుగుaccatenugu.

  

बालव्याकरणम् of परवस्तु चिन्नयसूरि deals with the grammar of these words in a separate chapter calledācchikaparicchēdamu.

  

The first sūtra of theācchikaparicchēdamu definesācchika words. In fact he uses the word accha.

  

The original Sanskrit word from older grammars of Telugu in Sanskrit medium was deerghaanta. It was acchā.

  

acchā \> ācchika in śanskrit

  

acchā (Sanskrit) \> accha (Telugu)

  

accha (tatsama) \> acca (tadbhava)
