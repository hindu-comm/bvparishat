नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

What Vid Paturi meant is -- आच्छिकशब्दाः called by the name अच्चतेलुगु - acca telugu (instead of achcha telugu)

and the 'cca' is to be pronounced as दन्त्य rather than तालव्य ।

  

The details are available in संज्ञापरिच्छेद of बालव्याकरणम् of परवस्तु चिन्नयसूरि ।
