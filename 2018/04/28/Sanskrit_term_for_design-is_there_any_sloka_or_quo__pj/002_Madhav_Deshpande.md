Dear Paturiji,

  

  The word रचना occurs in the Brahmasūtra रचनानुपपत्तेर्नानुमानम् in the sense of design or structure of the world. The argument is that the world has a well designed structure, which seems to indicate that it must have a thoughtful creator.
