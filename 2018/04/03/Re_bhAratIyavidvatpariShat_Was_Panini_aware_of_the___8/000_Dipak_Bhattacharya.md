A propos of R.Mohan's question on Paanini's awareness of *hanu**is* it not sufficient to assume that he regarded hanu as avyutpanna ,sabda?

  

2018-03-23 2:31 GMT+05:30 R Mohan \<[mohan.r...@gmail.com]()\>:  

> 
> > 
> > The word **हनुः** is usually well-known and its derivation is > recognized by the उणादिसूत्र “*शॄस्वृस्निहि*….”, which allows the > affix उ after the root हन्. >
> 
> > 
> > We also see the form **हनूः** in Vedic and post-Vedic literature. > E.g., >
> 
> > 
> > ‘***हनू**भ्याँ स्वाहोष्ठाभ्याँ स्वाहा*’ (तैत्तिरीयसंहिता ७.३.१६.१) >
> 
> > 
> > ‘*दद्भ्यस्स्वाहा **हनू**भ्याँ स्वाहेत्यङ्गहोमाञ्जुहोति*’ > (तैत्तिरीयब्राह्मणम् ३.८.१७.४) >
> 
> > 
> > ‘***हनू**मूले जिह्वामूलेन कवर्गे स्पर्शयति*’ (तैत्तिरीयप्रातिशाख्यम् > २.३५) >
> 
> > 
> > ‘*शक्तिरथी…. अनू**हनू**सूविभू इत्यवग्रहः*’ (तैत्तिरीयप्रातिशाख्यम् > ३.७) >
> 
> > 
> > ‘***हनू**कम्पयुतश्च कासः*’ (बृहत्संहिता ८.४८) >
> 
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > 
> > However, the वार्त्तिका to पा.सू. ‘*ऊङुतः*’ prevents ऊङ् from being > added to रज्जुः and **हनुः**, thereby disapproving the form **हनूः**. > From शब्दकल्पद्रुमः, we understand that भरतमल्लिकः in his अमरटीका > allows this affix (‘*पक्षे ऊङ्*’) to be added to **हनुः**. Knowing > that he follows वोपदेवः, he may not completely be in sync with > Pāṇinian system. >
> 
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > 
> > 1\) Is it right to assume that Pāṇini’s system did not recognize > **हनूः** as a valid word? >
> 
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > 
> > 2\) If so, what might be the logic governing that decision, given the > occurrence of its forms even in the Vedas? >
> 
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > 
> > 3\) Is there a reason to stipulate the form **हनू** through ‘शरादीनां > च’ before adding -मतुप्, for proper nouns (**हनूमत्**), when it could > have been easily accomplished by making ‘*ऊङुतः*’ more inclusive > towards **हनुः**, since both **हनुमत्** and **हनूमत्** are found in > शिष्ट literature? This would have made रज्ज्वादि- and शरादि-gaṇas > short of one word! >
>
