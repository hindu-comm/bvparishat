Yes, it is sddhe s'abdaarthasambandhe only articulated in vaakyapadiyam which is the aagamasangraha collected from the sutra-wise explanation in mahaabhaashya.

  

The point is that this view of s'abdaarthasambandha became well established almost as 'obvious' in the literary scircles of pre-modern India. One of the readers for such a scholarly popoularity of the idea could be Kaalidaasa's beautiful poetic articulation of this great s'aastric idea being popular among scholarly/poetic circles all over India.
