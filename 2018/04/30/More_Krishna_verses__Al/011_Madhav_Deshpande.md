Dear Pandyaji,

  

  Please correct my name from "Madhavi" to "Madhav". I am not sure about the identity or history of the author of this bhajan. There are several individuals with the name Brahmananda, and I am not sure which of these many Brahmanandas composed this bhajan. The bhajan itself is popular. It has been sung by Bhimsen Joshi and Ashwini Bhide, and perhaps by others.

  

Madhav Deshpande

Campbell, California
