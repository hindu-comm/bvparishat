[[Achyut Karve	2018-04-18, 11:33:34 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/y_8aJFMmcJk)]]



The sutra त्र***यम् एकत्र संयमः ॥ ४॥ is a very important one. A human mind can only do one thing at a time. Samyama can only be achieved if the Drastha of an individual is in place as it is only he who can alternate in the three. Hence the sutra.***
