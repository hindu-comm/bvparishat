Harsh Vardhan Ji,  
  

The first step is to restrict your consciousness to a chakra: देशबन्धः  

Second step is to achieve a state of one-thoughtedness: प्रत्येय एकतानता  

Third step is to bring the प्रत्येय एकतानता to a stage where you are aware of the meaning, but this meaning is not expressed in words and images: स्वरूप शून्यम्  

When these three are combined, the state of mind becomes संयमः  
  

Dogra, Chennai
