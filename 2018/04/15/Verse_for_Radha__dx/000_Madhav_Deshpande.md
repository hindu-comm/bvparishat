Verse for Radha:

राधे मनसिजविद्धे विपिने मा धाव यत्र तत्र त्वम् ।  
माधव एष निगूढो हृदये त्वं विन्द गोविन्दम् ।।  
O Rādhā, smitten with love, don’t run here and there in the forest. Mādhava is hiding. Find him in your heart.

  

  

Madhav Deshpande

Campbell, California
