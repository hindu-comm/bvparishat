कुलदैवतमस्माकं कोदण्डशरमण्डितम्।

इष्टदैअतमस्माकं इक्ष्वाकुकुलदैवतम्ं॥

  

This verse is recited 12 times minmum before commencing parayanam of SUNDARA KANDAM OF SRIMAD VALMIKI RAMAYANAṂ

LATE BRAHMASRI ANANTHARAMA DIKSHITAR specifies this shloka in his book.
