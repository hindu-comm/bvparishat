Respected Wakankar Ji, what you said simplifies the definition!

  

\- आचारप्रभवो धर्मः... (विष्णुसहस्रनामफलश्रुतिः)

\- ध्रियते पुण्यात्मभिरिति धर्मः (शब्दकल्पद्रुमः)

  

Considering the second definition:

  



Prof. Rajkumari ji's comment \<I feel Sanatan Dharma should be renamed as मानवधर्म\> may require a justification that all मानवs are पुण्यात्माs. Also, सनातन = सदातन, means eternally existing, which qualifies the word धर्म ।
