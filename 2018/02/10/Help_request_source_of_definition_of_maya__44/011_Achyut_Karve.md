[[Achyut Karve	2018-02-12, 09:38:08 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/44-jXhT-5Lo)]]



Dear Scholars,

  

All knowledge that humans acquire is through the medium of sense perceptions and actualised through the medium of language which is the only way we are able to appropriate reality. However no proof can be forwarded to suppose this to be the reality. This knowledge therefore is addressed as Maya. Not because it is false but because it is based on constraints on account of our existence.

  

It would be better if we try to understand the meaning of MITHYA In the context of 'Brahma Satya Jagan Mithya'.

  

Achyut Karve.
