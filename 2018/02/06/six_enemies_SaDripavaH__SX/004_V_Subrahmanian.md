Thanks Ramesh ji, for the references, especially the second one where the six 'ari-s' are present. Yet, the original poster's question was about the reference in the scriptures to these six 'enemies' with the nomenclature 'षड्रिपवः'. The Ramayana verse beautifully lists them and even calls their nivartaka-s ' षड् गुणाः' Thus we can say that 'षड्रिपवः व्यतिरेकमुखेन ज्ञापिताः वर्तन्ते रामायणश्लोके'.Here are some references that came up from a google search:

  

  

<https://www.facebook.com/groups/209689645806584/297692113673003>  

  

"षड् रिपवः--श्लो। "कामः क्रोधस्तथा लोभो मदमोहौ च मत्सरः"
