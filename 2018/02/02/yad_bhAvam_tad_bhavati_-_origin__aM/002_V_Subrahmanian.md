The purport / principle of this is stated by Shankara in the Bhashyas by citing a shruti passage in the Brahmasutra bhashya 1.1. Anandamayadhikarana introduction:  । एक एव तु परमात्मेश्वरस्तैस्तैर्गुणविशेषैर्विशिष्ट उपास्यो यद्यपि भवति, तथापि यथागुणोपासनमेव फलानि भिद्यन्ते ; *‘तं यथा**यथोपासते**तदेव भवति’ इति श्रुतेः,*[‘यथाक्रतुरस्मिँल्लोके पुरुषो भवति, तथेतः प्रेत्य भवति’ (छा. उ. ३। १४। १)](http://advaitasharada.sringeri.net/display/bhashya/Chandogya?page=3&id=Ch_C03_S14_V01&hl=%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%A5%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%81%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%B8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%81%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%8B%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%87%20%E0%A4%AA%E0%A5%81%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%81%E0%A4%B7%E0%A5%8B%20%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BF,%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%A5%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%83%20%E0%A4%AA%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%20%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BF)इति च। स्मृतेश्च —[‘यं यं वापि स्मरन्भावं त्यजत्यन्ते कलेबरम्। तं तमेवैति कौन्तेय सदा तद्भावभावितः’ (भ. गी. ८। ६)](http://advaitasharada.sringeri.net/display/bhashya/Gita?page=8&id=BG_C08_V06&hl=%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%BF%20%E0%A4%B8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%9C%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%20%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%AC%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8D%C2%A0%E0%A5%A4%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%B5%E0%A5%88%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BF%20%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%8C%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%AF%20%E0%A4%B8%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%A6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%83)इति।

  

In several other places too he cites this passage: *‘तं यथा**यथोपासते**तदेव भवति’ * and the ref is given as Shatapatha Brahmana 10.5.2.20. The Bh.Gita too has this idea in 8.6:

यंयंवापिस्मरन्भावंत्यजत्यन्तेकलेबरम्।  
तंतमेवैतिकौन्तेयसदातद्भावभावितः॥ ६ ॥

[भाष्यम्](http://advaitasharada.sringeri.net/display/bhashya/Gita?page=8&id=BG_C08_V06&hl=%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%BF%20%E0%A4%B8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%9C%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%20%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%AC%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8D%C2%A0%E0%A5%A4%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%82%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%B5%E0%A5%88%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BF%20%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%8C%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%AF%20%E0%A4%B8%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%A6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%83#bhashya-BG_C08_V06)

यंयंवापियंयंभावंदेवताविशेषंस्मरन्चिन्तयन्त्यजतिपरित्यजतिअन्तेअन्तकालेप्राणवियोगकालेकलेबरंशरीरंतंतमेवस्मृतंभावमेवएतिनान्यंकौन्तेय,सदासर्वदातद्भावभावितःतस्मिन्भावःतद्भावःसभावितःस्मर्यमाणतयाअभ्यस्तःयेनसःतद्भावभावितःसन्॥ ६ ॥

Maybe if a search is initiated with the word 'yadbhAvam' in a good Vedic/Upanishadic Concordance, there could be some result.
