> > > > > > The wordनिर्यातीभिमुख is an interesting combination of निर्यात > > > and अभिमुख. The wordनिर्यात has the oblique formनिर्याती- in > > > cases other than the nominative. Thus the formनिर्यातीभिमुख is > > > actually a combination ofनिर्याती andअभिमुख. A pure Sanskrit > > > sandhi would have given निर्यात्यभिमुख, but then the Marathi > > > reader would not recognize the first word asनिर्याती-. On the > > > other hand, the expression -भिमुखinनिर्यातीभिमुख is evidently > > > recognizable as अभिमुख.Therefore, the somewhat unusual sandhi > > > seems to have been adopted. There must be similar examples in > > > Marathi as well as in other Indian languages of limited > > > adaptations of Sanskrit. > > > > > >
