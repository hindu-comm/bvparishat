All the names अहमदाबाद, दौलताबाद, औरंगाबाद have the second component आबाद meaning "to populate a place". Note, आबादी means population. The first component is name of a Muslim person, after whom the locality is named.

  

बाद may be often pronounced as वाद, by the phonological dictum वबयोरभेदः
