Title - Status of Reality - Does everything "exist"?

Venue - NRCVEE seminar hall (IIT Delhi) - Block V, Room number 401.

Timing - 28th February 2018, Wednesday, 4 30 p.m.

Abstract - This talk will focus on various aspects of reality as discussed in various schools of Indian classical philosophies. Specifically, the definitions of reality from the perspective of Sankhyas, Bouddhas, Vendantins etc. will be discussed and followed by their significance in detail. Some aspects of the modern views including quantum view of the world will also be discussed.

Speaker - Prof. Veeranarayana Pandurangi a renowned Sanskrit scholar haling from Bangalore, Karnataka. He is presently the principal of Karnataka Samskrit University's evening college. He was awarded the Maharshi Badrayan Vyas Award for Sanskrit by Pratibha Patil, the then President of India, for the year 2011. He was previously the Dean of the Deapartment of Darshana, at the Jagadguru Ramanadacharya Rajasthan Sanskrit University, Jaipur. Pandurangi is a specialist in Darshan Shastras (Hindu philosophy). Has authored many scholarly books and articles on philosophy in multiple languages.
