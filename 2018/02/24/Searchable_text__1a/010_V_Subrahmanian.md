Here are two references that come close to what you may be looking for:

  

यद्यपि विविक्ताभिधायी भगवान् , तथापि मममन्दबुद्धेः व्यामिश्रमिव भगवद्वाक्यं प्रतिभाति । This is the thinking by Arjuna, as per Shankara, BG 3.2 bhashya  

  

रस्य ब्रह्मणो वाचकरूपेण, प्रतिमावत् प्रतीकरूपेण वा, परब्रह्मप्रतिपत्तिसाधनत्वेनमन्दमध्यमबुद्धीनां विवक्षितस्य ओङ्कारस्य उपासनं कालान्तरे मुक्तिफलम् उक्तं यत् ,  

  

BG 8.12 Here it is not a reference to Arjuna but a reference to those who are not adhikarins for Jnana Yoga now. They will have to engage in upasana by recourse to the Omkara and as a result of that samskaara, will become eligible for Jnana at a later janma/loka.

  

It is to be searched in Anandagiri's gloss to Shankara's bhashya if the term madhymaadhikaarin is used with ref to Arjuna. But that gloss is very likely post-Madhva.

  

Here is a closer ref: BG 2.47 which has the famous words: कर्मण्येव अधिकारः न ज्ञाननिष्ठायां ते तव।

  

Introduction to the third chapter: अर्जुनाय च[‘कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते. . . मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि’ (भ. गी. २। ४७)](http://advaitasharada.sringeri.net/display/bhashya/Gita?page=2&id=BG_C02_V47&hl=%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%A3%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%A7%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%B8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%20.%20.%20.%20%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%BE%20%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%87%20%E0%A4%B8%E0%A4%99%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%97%E0%A5%8B%E0%A4%BD%E0%A4%B8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%A3%E0%A4%BF)इति कर्मैव कर्तव्यमुक्तवान् योगबुद्धिमाश्रित्य, न तत एव श्रेयःप्राप्तिम् उक्तवान्। तदेतदालक्ष्य पर्याकुलीकृतबुद्धिः अर्जुनः उवाच।
