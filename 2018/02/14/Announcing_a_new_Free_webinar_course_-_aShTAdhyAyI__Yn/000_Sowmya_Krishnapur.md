Vyoma-Saṃskṛta-Pāṭhaśālā ([www.sanskritfromhome.in](http://www.sanskritfromhome.in)) is commencing a **Free webinar course** ****aṣṭādhyāyīpraveśaḥ****. The objective of the course is to introduce learners to the tradition of Pāṇinian grammar in an easy and effective manner. Participants will gain an understanding of all the major concepts of Sanskrit grammar. They can also practice applying the concepts through extensive exercises.  
  

The course is taught by **Dr. Tilaka M Rao,** Assistant Professor, MIT School of Vedic Sciences, MIT-ADT University, Pune. It is scheduled to start on **21st February 2018**. Classes will be held every Wednesday, 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM IST.   

  
Those who are interested can visit this page to know more and register for the course - <https://www.sanskritfromhome.in/course/ashtadhyayipravesha>
