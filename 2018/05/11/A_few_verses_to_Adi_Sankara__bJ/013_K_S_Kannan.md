*stAt* is the alternate form of *astu*.

Typically, while taking dictation, double *s* is heard as single *s*.

The printer too can be credited with such ingenuity, of course.

  

So then, *namaH astu* can be stated as *namaH stAt = namasstAt,*

approximated to* *namastAt.**
