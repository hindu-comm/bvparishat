Dear Shri Manjunath Bhat,

  

Kavirahasya is a short work of Halāyudha providing meanings for verbs. Its two recensions were published in 1900 in Germany by Ludwig Heller, and there are some Indian editions. I have the pdf of the edition published in Germany, and a paper copy of an edition published in Pune in the Shaka year 1811 \[ = 1889 AD\]. The verse you cite is no. 6 from the beginning:

  

गोपायति क्षितिमिमां चतुरब्धिसीमां पापाज्जुगुप्सतुदारमति: सदैव ।

वित्तं च गोपयति यस्तु वनीपकेभ्यो धीरो न गुप्यति महत्स्वपि कार्यजाते ।।६।।

  

The verse is designed to illustrate the various uses of the verb "gup".

  

Madhav Deshpande
