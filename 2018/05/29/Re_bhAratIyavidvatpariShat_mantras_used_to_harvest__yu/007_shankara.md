Namaste,  

  

Sometimes the website may be temporarily unavailable during maintenance. If this is not the case, the site would have been blocked by your ISP.  

  

Several websites including [archive.org](http://archive.org) were blocked by many ISPs in India after an order by Madras High Court in 2017 August.

  

<https://blog.archive.org/2017/08/09/statement-and-questions-regarding-an-indian-courts-order-to-block-archive-org/>

  

You could try to access the website using some other ISP like Jeo or Airtel which have not yet blocked [archive.org](http://archive.org).
