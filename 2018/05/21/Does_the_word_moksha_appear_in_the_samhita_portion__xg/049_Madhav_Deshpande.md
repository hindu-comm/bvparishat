The phrase पदप्रकृति: संहिता is much older than Bhartr̥hari, and is found in Yāska's Nirukta (1.17) and the R̥kprātiśākhya (2.1).

Please consult these two sources and commentaries on these for pre-Bhartr̥hari understandings of this phrase. With best wishes,
