Mahadevashastri narrates the Following story that sheds light on why ष is pronounced as ख.  

  

![Inline image](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/7425374ae0230/1536940972787blob.jpg?part=0.1&view=1 "Inline image")  
  

  

Another legend where the vomit from yaa\~navaalkya was consumed by "tittra bird" which became taiitariiya samhitaa.

  

This gets cross referenced:

  

यजूंष्यथ विसृष्टनि यज्ञवल्केन वै द्विज् ।  
जगृहुस्तित्तिरा भूत्वा तैत्तिरीयास्तु ते ततः ॥ विष्णू पु. ३.५.१२ ॥  

  

To me this could also be related to paaNyiiya shiksha   

##### अनक्षरं हतायुष्यं विस्वरं व्याधिपीडितम्‌ । अक्षता शस्त्रषरूपेण वज्रं पतति मस्तके ।।

It is said that since that time yaaj\~navaalkya stopped uttering ष and decided to pronounce it as ख  

  

ज्ञानेश्वर महाराज was यजुर्वेदी, in ज्ञानेश्वरी pronounces जो भजे आदि पुरुखी अखंडित
