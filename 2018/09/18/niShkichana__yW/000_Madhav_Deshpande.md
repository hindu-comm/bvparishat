On the analogy of निष्काञ्चन, my new word today is निष्किचन. Since the kitchen counters are being installed today in our house, all kitchen appliances have been disconnected, and the kitchen is unusable for a few days. My vigrahavākya for this new compound is: निष्कासित: किचनात्.☺☺☺
