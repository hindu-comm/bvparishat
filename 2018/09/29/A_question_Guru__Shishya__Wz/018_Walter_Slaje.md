Some might be interested in reading the below words on the precondition of ascertaining the qualification of a teacher by the disciple before accepting his instructions, and of ascertaining the qualification of a disciple by the teacher before imparting knowledge to him:

  

tattvaṃ jñātum ato yatnād dhīmān eva hi dhīmatā \|

prāmāṇikaḫ prabuddhātmā praṣṭavyaḫ praṇayānvitaṃ (II.11.43)

prāmāṇikasya pṛṣṭasya vaktur uttamacetasā \|

yatnena vacanaṃ grāhyam aṃśukeneva kuṅkumam (II.11.44)

atattvajñam anādeyavacanaṃ vāgvidāṃ vara \|

yaḫ pṛcchati naraṃ tasmān nāsti mūḍhataro 'paraḥ (II.11.45)

prāmāṇikasya tajjñasya vaktuḫ pṛṣṭasya yatnataḥ \|

nānutiṣṭhati yo vākyaṃ nānyas tasmān narādhamaḥ (II.11.46)

tajjñatātajjñate pūrvaṃ vaktur nirṇīya kāryataḥ \|

yaẖ karoti naraḫ praśnaṃ pṛcchakas sa mahāmatiḥ (II.11.47)

anirṇīya pravaktāraṃ bālaḫ praśnaṃ karoti yaḥ \|

adhamaḫ pṛcchakas sa syān na mahārthasya bhājanam (II.11.48)

pūrvāparasamādhānakṣamabuddhāv anindite \|

pṛṣṭaṃ prājñena vaktavyaṃ nādhame paśudharmiṇi (II.11.49)

prāmāṇikārthayogyatvaṃ pṛcchakasyāvicārya vā \|

yo vakti tam iha prājñāḫ prāhur mūḍhatamaṃ naram (II.11.50)

  

Regards, Walter Slaje
