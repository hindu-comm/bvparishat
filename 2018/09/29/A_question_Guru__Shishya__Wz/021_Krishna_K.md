Shri Madhvacharya, while commenting on Bhaagavata's 'लब्ध्वा च  
अव्यग्रं आचार्यं' (11th skandha, 3rd adhyaya, quotes a text called  
Karma-tantra, which states the following:  
  
परीक्ष्यैव गुरुः शिष्यं शिष्यो अपि गुरुमव्रजेत् \|  
अन्यथा नरकायैव प्रायश्चित्तं गुरोस्तथा \|\|  
  
His Mahabharata-tatparya-nirNaya defines guru as dispeller of doubts -  
asaMshayaH saMshayachChid gururukto manIShibhiH \| So that answers how  
a guru can be assessed (by a mature shishya).  
  
Regards,
