> > Knowledge went to the Brahmana and said, ‘I belong to you…. > Samhitopanishad Brāhmaṇa 3.9 >

> To be a very successful person the celibate student must in addition > have eight more qualities - intelligence, appreciation of teacher, > self-discipline, faith, critical approach to the subject, not > disclosing the knowledge to an undeserving person, retention power and > serving the teacher. Samhitopanishad Brāhmaṇa 3.20 >
