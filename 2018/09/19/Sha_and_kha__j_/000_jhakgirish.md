Shrimanto Vishvas mahaabhaagaah

Namo namah.

Aham taavat jnapayitum ichchhaami swakiyaam Devanaagari-tankanaanabhijnataam kshantavyo' aham atah.Kimapi nivedayitum aakaamkshe:

Ekaih praachinapanditair ekadaa ukto' aham:

Murdhanyasya sakaarasya uchchaaranam atipraachine kaal eva luptam( praayena Paanineh Praatisaakhyebhyah0 purvam eva).Tadanintanaah Aachaaryaah

Maadhyandinashaakhiyaah sambhaavanayaa antardristibhih samaadhaanam kartum etat aahuh shah khas tumrite iti.Punascha samasyodeti.Anyaasu shaakhaasu tu na drisyate.Ata eva aham na santusyaami.Jai SitaRam Jai Radhe Shyam.
