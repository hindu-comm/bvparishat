कोलम्बशताब्द refers to the Malyalam era, popularly known as Kollavarsham, which is said to have begun on the first day of solar month Simha in the year 825 CE. Hence अष्टम कोलम्बशताब्दादि means the beginning of the 8th century ME . It would correspond to the century beginning with the CE 1525.             Kolambabda 602 would correspond with 1427 CE.
