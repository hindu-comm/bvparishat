पञ्चम्यां सहस्राब्द्यां = In the 5th Millenium

पञ्चमे शतके = in the fifth century.

  

This just gives the approximate century when the person lived. In the fifth millenium, in the fifth century, which would be 4400 to 4500 kali era.

  

(This is similar to saying in the 5th year 4th month etc where 4 years are completed and in the fifth year April is the month specified.)
