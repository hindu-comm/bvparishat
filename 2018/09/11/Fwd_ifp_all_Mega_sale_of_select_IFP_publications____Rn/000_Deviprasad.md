Dear all,

As you may no doubt remember, we had organised a Mega Sale of some of our old publications last year, in order to reduce their stock. The initiative met with an enthusiastic response. So in order to take it forward, we are organising a 2nd edition of the sale this year, with discounts ranging from 20% to 80%. LIke last year, these discounts will be applicable only on select publications (see links below for the lists), for a limited period, i.e. from **24th September to 26th October 2018**, and till stocks last.

The consolidated list can be downloaded at: [**http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale_Sep-Oct2018.pdf**](http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale%20Oct-Nov2017.pdf)  
Department-wise lists are also available at the following locations:  
Indology: [**http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale_Sep-Oct2018_Indology.pdf**](http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale%20Oct-Nov2017_Indology.pdf)  
Social Sciences: [**http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale_Sep-Oct2018_Social_Sciences.pdf**](http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale%20Oct-Nov2017_Social_Sciences.pdf)  
Ecology: [**http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale_Sep-Oct2018_Ecology.pdf**](http://www.ifpindia.org/ifpsitedata/pdf/List_of_discounted_books_sale%20Oct-Nov2017_Ecology.pdf)  

Some more details:  
- The discounts will be applied on all orders received directly by us, whether by post or on spot.  
- The discounts will be valid only between 24th September to 26th October 2018.  
- The discount will be on rupee rates within India and on Euro rates outside India.  
- The discounts will be applied subject to a minimum order of 500 RS / 10 EUR (net total)  
- Packing and postage charges will be extra.  
- Discounts will be given only to those who make the full payment in advance.  

We request you to take advantage of this special sale to purchase some of the titles that may be of interest to you, and also to inform all those around you, friends, colleagues, who are likely to be interested.

Thank you,
