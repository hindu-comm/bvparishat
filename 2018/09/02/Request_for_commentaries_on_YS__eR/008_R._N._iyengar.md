In the list of YS versions and commentaries the work of Al-beruni deserves to find a place, though it is not in Sanskrit. My interest in YS is limited to find how star 'Dhruva' is understood by the commentators. I find Al-beruni's explanation more realistic than that available in the six Sanskrit commentaries (ed.Dhundiraja Shastri, Kashi Sanskrit Series no. 83).
