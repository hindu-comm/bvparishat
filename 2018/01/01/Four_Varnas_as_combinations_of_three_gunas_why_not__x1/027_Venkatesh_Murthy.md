Namaste

  

Sattva = Knowledge = Light. Tamas = Ignorance = Darkness. Sattva and Tamasare opposite qualities. They cannot be Primary and Secondary qualities in the same person. They are incompatible. But Rajas is Action oriented quality. It is compatible with Sattva and Tamas both and it can be Secondary or Primary. Tertiary quality is even less than Secondary. Therefore it is negligible in a person.
