In *Mahabhasya*, Paspasahnika defines *vyakarna* as a*laksana+laksya+uddharana+pratyuddharana 'suksama grantha', i*n which:

     laksana = 'rule/marker',  

     laksya = 'object of rules/markers=object language',  

     uddharana = 'example'  

     pratyuddharana = 'counter-example'.
