Kumbh is the same as what is called as Pushkara time for rivers in south India.

  

Hence the story for Pushkara time is the story for Kumbh too.

  

Astrlogically it is considered to be the time of entry of Guru (Jupiter) in each raas'i (sign of zodiac)
