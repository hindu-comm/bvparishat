क्रियार्थेmentions the purpose or the meaning intended.

  

But the purpose of the sutra is to describe/instruct the *location where* the suffixes occur.

  

That location isक्रियायां.

  

Purpose of vis'eshaNa is to specify the distinction of ekades'a from its group.

  

क्रियार्थायाम् is doing that.
