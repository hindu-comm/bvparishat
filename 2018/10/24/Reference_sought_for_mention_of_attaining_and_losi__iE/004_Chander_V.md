Respectful Salutations.  

  

After sending the links, I "brooded" on your friend's request again.

While I appreciate his search for authority of Pramanas, something struck me.

  

Understanding the Vedic (and esp Vedantic) texts, you agree, cannot proceed like the vyaavaharik sciences,

because they generously employ any of the four methods of communicating.  

So cursory reading can lead the "reader" astray (through misinterpretation of the purport).  

Even the one who has systematically started with Prakaranas (e.g. Tattva Bodha, Viveka Chudamani)  

needs constant Sravana to delve into deeper aspects.  

  

The recommended approach to understand the Sastras, which you may already know, is to approach a Srotriya Brahman Nishta. Any other "self study" may be catastrophic misunderstanding.

  

Hastening the process may not help.

  

With best regards,
