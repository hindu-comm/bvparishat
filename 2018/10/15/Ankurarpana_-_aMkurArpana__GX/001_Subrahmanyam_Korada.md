नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

It is अङ्कुरार्पणम् or अङ्कुरारोपणम् ।

  

पालिका -- a flat earthen plate/platter with rim in which नवधान्यानि are placed (to sprout) during उपनयनम् etc.

  

घटिका -- a small घट ( अल्पार्थे कन् ) \|

  

In ज्योतिषम् -- 1 घटिका = 24 minutes - 60 घटिकसs = one day.

  

घट is an earthen pot with big mouth - कुम्भ is an earthen pot with a narrow mouth .

  

शरावः -- a vessel in which something is boiled (मूकुडु in Telugu )

  

Since you are staying near Vijayawada town there are many पुरोहितs / श्रोत्रियs in and around

and anyone of them would enlighten you . You may also have a first hand knowledge by watching

an उपनयनम् / विवाह ।
