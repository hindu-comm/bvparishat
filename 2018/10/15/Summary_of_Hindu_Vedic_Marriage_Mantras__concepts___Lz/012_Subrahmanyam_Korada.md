नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

Vid Krishna Kashyap

  

Just see that the following four important aspects are highlighted in the explanation on विवाह (you may quote

me) --

  

1\. **विवाहः**-- **विविदिषार्थम्**

  

besides clearing the **पितॄणम्** , it is useful in generating **विविदिषा** in the minds of the couple .

  

वेत्तुम् इच्छा विविदिषा -- a desire to know - what is that to be known ? There is only one thing that should be

known - परब्रह्मन् ( although one knows many things in the universe it is of no use in the pursuit of

the ultimate - ब्रह्मन् ) - which is essential in attaining the ultimate goal of every human being - मोक्ष ।

  

2.**अर्धाङ्गी --**

  

This is a unique concept prescribed in **वेद** --

  

अर्धो वा एष आत्मनो यज्जाया -- **शतपथब्राह्मणम् , 5-2-1-10 ; 8-7-2-3**

  

अर्धो वा एष आत्मनो यत्पत्नी -- **तैत्तिरीयसंहिता , 6-1-8-5**

  

So the question of pocket money etc does not arise at all - the husband does not give money to the wife

rather she takes - - both of them have equal rights on everything - neither is superior / inferior to the other.

  

3\. **What kind of child is going to be born** ? ---

  

निषेकसमये यादृङ् नरचित्ते विकल्पना ।

तादृक् स्वभावसंभूतिः जन्तुर्वसति कुक्षिगः ॥ --- **गरुडपुराणम् , उत्तरखण्डः , 22 / 17,18**

  

गर्भोत्पत्तौ तु मनः स्त्रियाः यं जन्तुं व्रजेत् तत्सदृशं प्रसूते -- **गर्भोत्पत्तिः , चरकसंहिता , शारीरस्थानम् , 2-25**

  

  

The **child will be similar to the one that is there in the mind of the lady at the time of intercourse**

**- both in terms of physical and mental characteristics .**

  

4\. **How many children** one may produce ? It is ten --

  

इमां त्वमिन्द्र मीढ्वः सुपुत्रां सुभगां कुरु ।

दशास्यां पुत्रानाधेहि पतिमेकादशं कृधि ॥ **ऋग्वेदः , 10-85-45**

  

One may produce ten children (today it is useful for Europe , Russia , Japan , Koreas etc if not India).

The eleventh one will be the father himself -- **आत्मा वै पुत्रनामासि** (यजुर्वेदः) ।
