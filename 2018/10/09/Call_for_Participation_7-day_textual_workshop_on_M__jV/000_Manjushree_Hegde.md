We are pleased to announce that Amrita Darshanam — International Centre for Spiritual Studies — in association with Indian Council for Philosophical Research (ICPR) is organizing a 7-day textual workshop onMadhusūdana Sarasvatī’s*Siddhāntabindu*between 10-16 November 2018 in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore.

  

*Siddhāntabindu*or*Siddhāntatattvabindu*is Madhusūdana Sarasvatī’s commentary on Śri Śankarācārya’s*Daśaślokī*, also known as the*Cidānandastavarāja*or*Cidānandadaśaślokī*or*Nirvāṇadaśaka*.The text presents all the basic principles of*advaita*in the prima facie views and their refutations, and is a reliable and comprehensive digest of*advaita vedānta*.
