To critically edit texts manuscripts have to be made accessible in a convenient digital format for interested scholars to do the much needed work. Please share your thoughts on how to make manuscripts available across india accessible to interested scholars. Can we create a digital manuscript store or repository where all digitized manuscripts can be conveniently deposited by various institutions or individuals who have access to manuscripts. They can keep the physical copies and the digital versions can be shared on such a manuscript store. One can first start with published works and then gradually move to unpublished works on case to case basis
