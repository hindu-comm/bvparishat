by P Sreekumar

The grammar of a language generally prescribes or describes the essential and the observable system of the language. The art and craft of writing and the scientific studies on grammar is known as grammaticography. For a decade, grammaticography has been emerging as a major field in linguistics (Lehmann and Maslova 2004; Ameka, Dench et al. 2006; Payne and Weber 2006; Nordhoff 2012). Reflecting upon the new trends in grammaticography, an attempt has been made in this paper to review the grammaticography of Malayalam based on Kēraḷa Pāṇiṉīyam (henceforth KP).
