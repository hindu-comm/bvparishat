Both are grammatically correct.Ash bhaksane-root.nini suffic is added to ash by the rule nandigrahi.Bhiksaam ashnanti-iti is derivation.Bhiksaashin the base( praatipadika) in plural we find bhiksaashinah. On the other hand in bahuvrihi compound bhiksaa ashanam yeshaam iti is derivation.
