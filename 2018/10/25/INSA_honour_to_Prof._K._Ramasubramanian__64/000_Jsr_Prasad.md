Prof. K. Ramasubramanian, an eminent Sanskrit scholar, and our list member, has been elected as a Fellow by the Indian National Science Academy, New Delhi. Hearty congratulations to him! The [fellowship](http://www.insaindia.res.in/ref3.php) is effective from 2019 onwards. He is a [PhD](http://iks.iitgn.ac.in/prof-k-ramasubramanian/) in physics and obtained Advaita-Vedanta upadhi from H.H. Sri Sringeri Jagadguru Mahaswami.

  

His contributions to Indian Astronomy and Kerala School of Mathematics are remarkable. Earlier, he was awarded President's Certificate of Honor in Sanskrit in 2008.
