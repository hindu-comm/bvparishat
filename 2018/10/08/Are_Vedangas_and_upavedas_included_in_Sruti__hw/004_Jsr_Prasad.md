Namaste.

  

As per a general statement - स्मरन्ति वेदमनया इति स्मृतिः, they can be. It may be that, going by रूढि (convention) smrtis authored by Manu etc., could only be considered under this classification. Smrtis are dharmashastra texts. May I remind you this quote of Vyasa/Kautilya -

  

अङ्गानि वेदाश्चत्वारो मीमांसा न्यायविस्तरः ।

धर्मशास्त्रं पुराणञ्च विद्या ह्येता श्चतुर्दश ।।

  

Vedangas come under 14 vidyaas and the remaining are under 18 vidyaas.
