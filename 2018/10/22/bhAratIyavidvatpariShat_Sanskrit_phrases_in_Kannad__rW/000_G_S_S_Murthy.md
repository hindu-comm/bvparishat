Respected scholars,

In Kannada some Sanskrit phrases are seen to be used. Examples are given below. I find that the origin of one phrase, यद्वा तद्वा, appears to be the following verse:

यस्य कस्य तरोर्मूलं येन केनापि पेषयेत् ।

यस्मै कस्मै प्रदातव्यं *यद्वा तद्वा* भविष्यति ॥

Do other phrases also originate from some Sanskrit Sloka / quotable prose passage? If so, which are those slokas, prose passages?

शताय गताय; शतः सिद्ध; शाकाय लवणाय; शुभस्य शीघ्रम्, *यद्वा तद्वा*, पदे पदे.
