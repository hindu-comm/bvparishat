वृद्ध does not seem to be always in the sense of old age only.

  

For example, we have धर्मवृद्ध , आगमवृद्ध where age aspect does not fit.

  

'developed (in terms of ) ' wealth seems to fit in the case of धनवृद्ध

  

'Eminent in' seems to fit forज्ञानवृद्ध .
