I am in agreement with Prof. Kannan and other scholars that BVP should be a forum meant for academic debates. As and when quality of debates gets low the debates often results in mudslinging, making allegations and conspirator theory against scholars and individuals who may or may not be members of list BVP mailing list. This approach doesn't make sense to me.  

  

Can BVP change the way in which Government, universities or private appointments are done in this country is the question one should answer One can solve such issues affecting academia and students more outside the list than on the list. discussing issues making more noise on the list and being silent outside will not help. I am not corrupt, I am scholar but will do nothing on corruption and low level academic activities that are happening right in front of eyes and make big noises on the internet how much will such an approach help.

  

There were appeals by the list moderators to close this thread the reason being BVP as mailing list cannot solve recruitment issues of universities and private bodies. What BVP list can do is stir the conscious of academic decision makers who are members of their list is to start a clean up operation so that quality and rigorous Sanskrit and indological studies which are deteriorating in this country are restored.

  

This mail is written with a disclaimer. The views expressed are those of Ajit Gargeshwari and BVP as a mailing may or may not subscribe to views expressed.

Regards

Ajit Gargeshwari
