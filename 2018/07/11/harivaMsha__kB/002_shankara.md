Namaste,

  

Apart from BORI's critical editon, there are the following editions of Harivamsa, that I am aware of.  

  

1839 Asiatic Society - Harivamsa Sanskrit text  
1891 Gopal Narayan - Harivamsa Sanskrit text with Nilakantha's Tika  
1896 Lakshmi Venkatesvara Press (Gangavishnu Srikrishnadas) - Harivamsa Sanskrit text with Nilakantha's Tika  
1909 Ganpat Krishnaji - Harivamsa Sanskrit text with Nilakantha's Tika  
  
Harivamsa Sanskrit text with Hindi translation  

1967 Gita Press - HV Sanskrit text with Hindi translation.  

1985 Nag Prakashan - HV Sanskrit text with Hindi translation by Rajendranath Sarma in 2 volumes

  

regards  
shankara
