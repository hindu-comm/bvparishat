My Marathi Abhang this morning:

  

जगाच्या नौकेचा तूचि कर्णधार

वादळी वाऱ्यात तूची नेशी ।

O Lord, you are the captain of the ship of this world, and you lead it into stormy winds.

  

बुडावे की आम्ही तरावे केशवा

ठरविशी देवा तूची आता ।

O Lord, whether we sink or stay afloat, only you will decide.

  

सुखाचा तू कर्ता दु:खाचा तू हर्ता

दोहोंचा करविता नाही दुजा ।

You are the cause of happiness and remover of sorrow, but none else is the maker of both.

  

बसलो नौकेत तुझिया विठ्ठला

तुझ्या हाती आता भार माझा ।।२।।

O Vitthala, I am sitting in your ship, and my burden is now in your hands.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus

Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan
