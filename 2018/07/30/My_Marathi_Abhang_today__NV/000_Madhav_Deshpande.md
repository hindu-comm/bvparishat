My Marathi Abhang today \[inspired by the California wild fires\]

  

पंढरीच्या पांडुरंगा जग तूची निर्मियले

वणवे हे का पेटले चोहीकडे ।

O Lord of Pandharpur, you yourself created this world.

Why then are the fires burning everywhere?

  

घरे दारे जळताती वने मने ही पेटती

नाही आग विझवाया एक थैंब ।

Households are burning, and so are burning the forests and minds. There is not a drop to quench the fire.

  

अग्नी आज का कोपतो वारा वेडात धावतो

व्याकुळले जन सारे माझ्या देशी ।

Why has the fire become angry and the wind blowing crazy? People in my region are tormented.

  

नको राहू देव्हाऱ्यात ठेवू नको कर कटी

होउनिया आता मेघ घेई धाव ।।३।।

Don’t just stay on your pedestal and don’t just rest your hands on your waist. Now become a cloud and rush here.

  

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus

Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan

\[Residence: Campbell, California\]
