Re: shudra occupations - Smritis, e.g. Yajnavalya smriti say "serving dvi-jaati" or if that is not possible then 'vanijyam' as also 'vividhaih shilpaih vaa jiivet'.  

  

The commentator quotes Devala that the vividha-karma could be karshana, pashu-paalana, bhaara-vahana, panya-vyavahara, chitra-karma, nrtya, geeta, venu-viina-muraja-mrdanga etc vaadana.  

  

The list is obviously not exhaustive. Carpentry (takshana) **could** also be shudra occupation. The word shilpa includes all possible crafts and arts.
