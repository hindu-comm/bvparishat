Dear Sri Venkateswaran,

  

In the Gopalapurva tapini upanishat, the commentator Narayanashrama (13th Century CE) replies for a question:

  

![image.png](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/3b5598367ef95/image.png?part=0.2&view=1)  

  

The highlighted question: What causes fear to Death? \[Death is afraid of which entity?\]

  

  

![image.png](https://groups.google.com/group/bvparishat/attach/3b5598367ef95/image.png?part=0.1&view=1)  

  

Reply: Death is afraid of Govinda.

  

The commentator says: This is Sankarshana who has been stated in the Bh.gita 11.32 verse: I am Kaala, Time, the destroyer of creation, out to destroy the beings now/here.
