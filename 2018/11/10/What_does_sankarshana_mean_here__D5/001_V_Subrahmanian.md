In the Devibhagavata occurs a Shiva sahasra naama and there is a term 'vishva sankarShakaH' \[The one who draws in - sankarshanam - the entire universe during pralaya\]

<https://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_shiva/shivasahasranAmastotradevIbhAgavata.html?lang=sa>
 चित्रयोधी चित्रकर्मा विश्वसङ्कर्षकः स्वयम् ।

One may probe this further:<https://ramanan50.wordpress.com/2014/06/11/balarama-is-shiva-transferred-in-the-womb-sankarshana/>

  

The Amarakosha 48 gives Sankarshana as a name of Balarama.
