Namaste,

  

The शिवरह्स्यम् vide 39^(th) shloka of 10^(th) chapter says:



किं वेदैरखिलैः पुराणनिचयैरन्योन्यनिन्दापरैः

किं वा भाररयुद्धकीर्तनमुमे किं क्षुद्रधर्मादिभिः

यत्रेशानकथाप्रचारविधुरानिस्सारभूता कथा

**काव्येनापि किमत्र कथया दुःखोत्कटायाः फलम्** ॥ (१०.३९)



What is there is Vedas, puranas that engage in mud-slinging among each other (among the gods),

what is there is Mahabharata that only glorifies the war; what is there is poetry like Ramayana etc.

that speak of deep suffering (उत्कटदुःखम्).



However, I take the above shloka with a pinch of salt as this work is shiva-pAramya.



Rgs,

sriram
