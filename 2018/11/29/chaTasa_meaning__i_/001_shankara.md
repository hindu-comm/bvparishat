Pandurangiji,

  

This verse occurs in Samkalpasuryodaya 5.34 with minor variations. In its Adyar edition, V Krishnamacharya adopts the patha 'स्थपुटपुटसापेक्षः'. He explains in a foot-note that though some commentators explain चटसं पानपात्रमिति, he could not find चटसशब्दः in any of the kosas.
