Sir,

  

There are two critical editions of Srimad Bhagavata Purana.

  

i\) Critical edition of Bhagavata Purana based on printed editions of BP, published by TTD in 12 volumes 16 parts. Most of these volumes are available at <http://ebooks.tirumala.org/>

  

ii\) Critical edition of Bhagavata Purana based on manuscripts, published by B.J. Institute of Learning & Research, Ahmedabad in 4 vols, 6 parts. Digital copies of this edition are not found on the internet. If any member of BVP posseses the soft copies of these volumes, they may kindly share the same.  

  

regards  
shankara
