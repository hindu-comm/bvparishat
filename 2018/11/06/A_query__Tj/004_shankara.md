I have uploaded TTD's critical editon of Bhagavata Purana at the link given below.

<https://archive.org/details/Bhagavata_Purana_With_Multiple_Commentaries_TTD_Critical_Edition>

  

Volumes 3, 5 & 6 are missing. If any member of BVP happens to posses their soft copies, please share the links.

  

TTD's critical edtiion of BP, though not based directly on manuscripts in based on 10 well-known editions of BP including the Nadiad editon containing 11 commentaries.

  

Special features of TTD edition of Bhagavata Purana.

  

1\. There are 3 major commentaries by Sridharaswami, Viraraghavacharya & Vijayadhvajatirtha for all 12 skandhas. First 2 skandhas have 'Sudhisudha' vyakhya of Radhakrishna Sarma who himself edited the first 2 volumes.

  

2\. There are 2 appendices. Appendix 1 is an alphabetical index of slokapadas. Appendix 2 is an alphabetical index of quotations in the commentaries, which includes the source text references.  

  

**A request to scholars of BVP** - please let me know, if you are aware of any reviews on TTD's critical edition of Bhagavata Purana.
