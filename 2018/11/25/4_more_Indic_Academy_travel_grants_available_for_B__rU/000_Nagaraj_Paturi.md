As you all know, Indic Academy announced in all five travel grants to attend BVP conference at Udupi 4th -6th January, 2019.

  

One of these has been now allocated for a senior scholar.

  

*Four* more of them are now available.

  

Objective behind the grant is that the conference should not miss a great deserving scholar for want of funds for the travel of the scholar.

  

Each scholar is funded to the extent of Rs 10,000/- or the actual travel cost , whichever is less.

  

Members can indicate the names and ids of such deserving scholars requiring grant or the scholars themselves may apply for the grant.
