It is common nowadays for the normal civic life to be severely affected by public protests AKA “dharNa”. Most dharNa-s tend to be rooted in ill conceived motivations:  
  
धर्मस्य “धर्ण”-स्य च  
वर्णमात्रं विशेषत: \|  
धर्मो धारयते जीवा:  
दारयते परो खलु \|\|  
  
ITRANS:  
dharmasya cha “dharNa”-sya cha  
varNamAtraM visheShataH \|  
dharmo dhArayate jIvAH  
dArayate paro khalu \|\|  
  
May I request kind a review by the vidvajjana of the above 4-liner?
