When i recently studied Rramayana with the intention of knowing whether its true that Rama arrived in Ayodhya on the day of Deepavali. i dint
found any sources for this cliam. I found that Rama came to ayodhy on Shasti. Here are some references,पूर्णे चतुर्दशे वर्षे पञ्चम्यां लक्ष्मणाग्रजः। भारद्वाजाश्रमं प्राप्य ववन्दे नियतो मुनिम्। Hanuman
tells Bharatha that Rama will stay in bharadwajashrama for one night( panchami),and next he will land in Ayodhy( Shasti) . पञ्चमीमद्य रजनीमुषित्वा वचनान्मुने। भरद्वाजाभ्यनुज्ञातं द्रक्ष्यस्यत्रैव राघवम्।
