Dear all,

As per the practice of worship followed in Bengal, 16 upacaaras are aasana, svaagata, paadya, argha, aacamaniiya, madhuparka, punaraacamaniiya. snaaniiya, vastra, bhuuSaNa, gandha, puSpa, dhuupa, diipa, naivedya, vandana.

Best

Nabanarayan Bandyopadhyay
