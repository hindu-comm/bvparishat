But there is no Visarjana in Nitya Puja. Only when we are taking out the Devata Vigraha out of the house for immersion and so on we have to do Visarjana. Visarjana is like Goodbye. If you do Visarjana you cannot do Nitya Puja for same Vigraha next day.
