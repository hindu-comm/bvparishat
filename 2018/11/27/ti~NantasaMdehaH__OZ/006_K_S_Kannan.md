Neither *vyAyAmi* nor *vyAyacchAmi*,

but ***vyAyacche***.

And your query as to *laT prathama-bahuvacana* is also incompatible.

It is *laT uttamaikavacana.*

*(*If the former, the form would be *vyAyacchante.).*

*  
*

(But if you choose a *nAma-dhAtu* form, things can be different.)
