Prof. Aklujkar is correct.Jha,Ojha,Upadhye,Padhye,Paadhaa are from 'Upaadhyaaya' thru prakritization.'Daasha' in odisha is from 'Daashasharmaa'perhaps der. from 'daashri daane'.

For 'kara' i think in odisha people utter during rituals their name-endings as 'Karasharmaa','Devasharmaa' and 'Dharasharmaa',out of which short titles as 'Kara','Dhara' and 'Deva' came up.

In the treatises on Gotra,Pravara etc this can be better confirmed.
