Let us take up the issue afresh andsd sede what is said in Vyakarana, Kalpa,Purvamimamsa , Sribhasyam etc.



Vyakaranam :



brahmani sAdhu brahmanyam (manah), su brahmanyam subrahmanyam (yatpratyaya by 'tatra sAdhuh'

Pani 4-4-98 ) . subrahmanyo'm subrahmanyo'm subrahmanyom (Taittiriya Aranyakam- Arunam) , na subrahmanyAyAyAm svaritasyatUdAttah - Pani 1-2-37 . Itcan be in pumlingaalso .



Abrahmanyam (avadhyoktau - Amara) may be put under PrsodarAdi (Pani 6-3-109) .



Ramante yoginah asminniti rAmah - ramu kridAyAm, akartari ca kArake samjnAyAm, Pani 3-3-19 , ghan .

It is possible to offer derivation to most of the names which are Tatsamas . But the ending depends upon

the grammar of the vernacular .



In Telugu fololowing Mahat and Amahat dichotomy the Pratyayas, du-mu-vu, are added -- rAmudu, samudramu

/ samudrudu, vrksamu, Manuvu etc. (see Tatsamapariccheda, Balavyakaranam of Cinnayasuri). No doubt the Trimuni Vyakaranam is closely followed in Telugu but in order to certify certain usages/endings separate rules are prescribed .So far as the wordSubrahmanyam is concerned it has to be justified thru abhedopacAra . As a name of a man it should be in pumlinga, if we strictly follow Telugu vyakaranam.



(Nannaya, Atharvana and Ahobalapati are considered as Munitraya and they compiled Sutra, Vartika and Bhasya for Telugu in Sanskrit



Right from Katyayana the discussion has been there regarding the SAdhutva of SamjnAs -- nyAyyabhAvAt kalpanam samjnAdisu (Vartikam under rlk) . All the commentators discussed this aspect and Haradattamisra

coded the Siddhanta as follows -



anAditA cet sAdhutvam samjnAsabdesu kA gatih ? ucyate, namakarane grhyesu niyamasruteh II

yathAkAmam na samjnAnAm viniyogah samasti nah I tasmAdanAditA samjnAsvapi naiva virudhyateII

yastAttaddesabhAsabhih samjnastAsAm na sAdhutA I lukAravArtike caitat vyaktam katyAyano'bravIt II



YA punardesabhAsAbhihsamjnAh kUcimancItyAdayah na tAsAm sAdhutvam .....



Therefore the Samjnas made according to Vyakarana only are SAdhus .



Kalpah :



dvyaksaram caturaksaram vA (Apastambagrhya 5-25-9), krtam kuryAnna taddhitam (GrhyasU) etc has to be followed in NAmakarana . May be due to this reason people of certain regions such as Maharastra give two

names - sAstrIyam and vyAvahArikam .



Purvamimamsa :



In PikanemAdhikarana Jaimini compiled the following sutra (1-3-5-10) - coditam tu pratIyeta avirodhAt

pramAnena.

The meaning of certain words being used by Mlecchas in certain meanings can be taken as there is no any

contradiction withany strong PramAna(accepted by Aryas).

Therefore the words (including Samjnas) that are being used in DesabhAsas can be taken as they are.



Sribhasyam :



In Vedarthasamgraha Ramanujacarya asserts that all Vaidika and Laukika Sabdas end up in Paramatma --

vedAntasravanena ca vyutpatih pUryate I evameva vaidikAssarve sabdAh paramAtmaparyantAn svArthAn

bodhayanti. vaidikA eva sarve sabdAh vedAdevoddhrtyoddhrtya parenaiva brahmanA sarvapadArthAn purva-

vatsrstvA tesu paramAtmaparyantesu purvavannAmatayA prayuktAh .
