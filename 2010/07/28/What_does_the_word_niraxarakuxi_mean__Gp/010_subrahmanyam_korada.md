namo vidvadbhyah



kusyate bahiskriyate malamasmAsiti kuksih , kusa = niskarse .



picandakuksI jatharodaram tudam -- Manusyavargah, Dvitiyakandah, Amarakosah .



**Paniniyasiksa :**



AtmA buddhyA sametyArthAn mano yunte vivaksayA I

manah kAyAgnimAhanti sa prerayati mArutam II

mArutastUrasi caran mandam janayati svaram I

so'dIrno mUrdhnyabhihato vaktramApadya mArutah I

varnAn janayate........II



Here kAyAgni is jAtharAgni that is situated in kuksi (udaram) . So kuksi is the place where the physical process

of the generation of Varnas starts .Therefore -niraksarakuksi ( varnam vAhuh pUrvasUtre- Mahabhasyam) .



Let us see the **Philosophical** aspect --



parA vAnmUlacakrasthA pasyantI nAbhisamsthitA I hrdisthA madhyamA jneyA
vaikharI kanthadesagA II  
  
PasyantIvAk is situated at the navel .



Here is **JnAnArnava (23 rd Patala** , **7-12**) -



vaikhary hastinI jneyA sthUlA yasmAdvarAnane I caturvidhA hi dUtyastu sundaryascArulocanAh II

tathA vanaspatigatA madhyama vAgvarAnane I tesu sarvesu pasyantI jIvesu paramAsrayA II

parabrahmani samlInA parAvAk paramesvari I



**NityAsodasIkArnavah** **(VisrAmah -7, Slokah - 69)** -



mUlAdhAre tadidrUpe vAgbhavAkAratAgate I



**Setubandhah** (commentary) - vAgbhih parApasyantyAdibhih bhavatIti vAgbhavam trikonam I



**SrIprapancasAram (Patalah - 2, slokah - 19) -**



tacca turIyAtItam tasyApi bhavenna dUrato muktih I

athavA sUksmAkhyAyAm pasyantyAm madhyamAvaikharyAh II



**Sivadrsti** of **Somananda** vehemently refutes (Ahnikam - 2 ) the theory of Sphota of Vaiyakaranas --



asmAkam jnAnasaktiryA sadAsivarUpatA I VaiyAkaranasAdhUnAm pasyantI sA parA sthitih II (1)

tadaksaram sabdarUpam sA pasyantI parA hi vAk I (2)

prAno varnAnabhivyajya varnesveva pralIyate I (116)



By and large the Tantragranthas also endorse the PasyantyvAk .



There are other works like **SAradAtilakam** , **KAmakalAvilAsa** etc. where 'Sabda' is discussed .
