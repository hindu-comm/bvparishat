namo vidvadbhyah



mantrabrahmanayoh vedanamadheyam (Apastambah ) - this sentence simply says that both are called by the name Veda (AkhyAhve abhidhAnam ca nAmadheyam ca nAma ca - Amarah) .



There is problem with the definition of a Mantra - taccodakesu mantrAkhyA ( Purvamimamsa

2-1-7-32 ) – here the word ‘tat’ is pUrvaparAmarsI , as in the earlier SUtra , i.e. api vA prayogasAmarthyAt mantro ‘ bhidhAnavAcI syAt (PUr
MIm 2-1-6-31), the term abhidhana is used the same is retrieved and it means anustheyah padArthah . tasya smaranasya hetuh mantrah –is the SUtrArthah .



Here is SAbarabhAsyam ---



… taccodakesu mantrAkhyA I abhidhAnasya codakesu evamjAtIyakesu
abhiyuktA upadisanti -- mantrAnadhImahe, mantrAnadhyApayAmah,

mantra vartante iti .



According to Sabarasvamy, the VrttikAra ,i.e. Upavarsa ( Sankaracarya says Upavarsa , Ramanujacarya says Bodhayana ) says – the text ending in ‘asi’ ( medho’si ) , ‘tvA’ (isetvA) etc..are Mantras . He further says –



prAyikamidam laksanam I anabhidhayakA api kecinmantrA ityucyante , yathA ‘ vasantAya kapinjalAnAlabheta’ it I na sakyam prstAkotena ( prstasya Akotah kutilIkaranam , tena tadgatah

kleso laksyate yathA prthvIgatAnekapadArthanirIksane prstAkotajah klesah tathA pratyekam mantrapadavisayAlocane’pi klesasAmyAt laksanAbhidhAnam ityarthah – Bhasyavivaranam

of GovindAmrtamuni who follows Prabhakara School ) tatra tatra
upadestumiti laksanamuktam ,



rsayo’pi padArthAnAm nAntam yAnti prthaktvasah I

laksanena tu siddhAnAmantam yAnti vipascitah II



TantravArtikam :

adhyetrvrddhavyavahArasiddham cedam prAyikacihnayuktam laksanam lAghavArthamuktam I dharanigatAnekadravyapatyekanirIksane punah punah prstam kutilIkriyate iti tatsAmanyena prstAkotAbhidhAnam . slokascaivam drastavyah –



vrttau laksanametesAm asyantatvAntarUpatA I

Asisah stutisamkhye ca pralaptam paridevitam II

praisAnvesanaprstAkhyAnAnusangaprayogitAh I

sAmarthyam ceti mantrAnAm vistarah prAyiko matah II

Therefore Mantra in Veda is as good as Linga in BhAsA , i.e. vrddhavyavahAra eva saranam .



BrAhmanalaksanam :



Sese brAhmanasabdah ( Pur MIm 2-1-8-33 ) .



SAbarabhAsyam :



Atha kimlaksanam brAhmanam ? mantrAsca brAhmanam ca vedah I tatra mantralaksane ukte

parisesasiddhatvAt brAhmanalaksanamavacanIyam I …



vrttikArastu sisyahitArtham prapancitavAn –



heturnirvacanam nindA prasamsA samsayo vidhih I

parakriyA purAkalpo vyavadhAranakalpanA II

upamAnam dasaite tu vidhayo brAhmanasya tu I

etatsyAt sarvavedesu niyatam vidhilaksanam II



idamapi prAyikam I ……



BhAsyavivaranam : tallaksanasyAvaktavyatvam vaktum vedadvairAsyamAha I



TantravArtikam :

Kimartham punah sUtramArabhyate ? nArabheta , yadi mantrabrAhmanAtmaka eva veda iti sarvesAm prasiddham bhavet I yesAmtvaprasiddham tesAm trtIyadiprakAranirAkaranArtham dvairAsyameva vedasyeti pratipAdayitumAha – sese brAhmanasabda iti I
