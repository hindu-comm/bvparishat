\>Is there a specific rule or only regional oil.  
  

तैलदीप:&घृतदीप: are traditionally prescribed for worship according to different texts(Kalpa-Puja,Tantra texts) followed by the Parampara. Taila by etymology means tila-janya, sesame oil.  
नमोस्तु ते व्यास विशालबुद्धे फुल्लारविन्दायतपत्रनेत्र ।  
येन त्वया भारततैलपूर्ण: प्रज्वालितो ज्ञानमयप्रदीप:।।  
तैलदानम् is offering of Tila Oil only and not any regional oil.
