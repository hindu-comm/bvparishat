नमस्सर्वेभ्यः ।  
दीपानाम् आवली - दीपावली ।  
Somehow, I am not convinced of translating 'deepa' to a 'candle'. With due respect to other religions, using candle is not a Hindu tradition, since its made of wax. In many inaugural functions during deepa jwaalana, I observe that , people light the lamp with a candle. They can simply use a small deepa to make the event fully traditional. We practice it here.  
  
धन्योऽस्मि
