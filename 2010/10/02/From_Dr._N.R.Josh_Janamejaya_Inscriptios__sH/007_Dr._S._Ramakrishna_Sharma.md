**"तान्यहं वेद सर्वाणि न त्वं वेत्थ परंतप !"**  
#This post is timely warning against wasteful spending of / filling in  
the sacred space allotted by Time through Google Groups!  
  
#To those who have श्रद्धा in the **above** reassurance of "वासुदेवाख्यं परम् ब्रह्म "  
this post ought to serve as the notice to refine and enlighten the conscience to  
learn to listen to enlightened conscience through spiritual receptivity rather than  
scientific curiosity(where the ego asserts more; humility becomes a policy than a  
quality; surrender to the Higher becomes an intellectual exercise than the self-effacing  
act of the heart).  
  
# श्रद्धा-भक्ति-ध्यान-योगादवैहि !

तं ह देवं आत्मबुद्धिप्रकाशं  
मुमुक्षुर्वै शरणमहं प्रपद्ये ।।
