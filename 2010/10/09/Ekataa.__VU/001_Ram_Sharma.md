Thanks.Our Rgveda concludes with the following ekaatmtaamantra:  
 Samaanii va aakuutih samaanaa hRdayaani vaH/  
 samaanam astu vo mano yathaa vaH susahaa'sati//  
 Anyone who meditates on the sound and the import of this mantra at least once a day will defenitely develope the universal and wholistic attitude of ekaatmataa.  
 Humbly yours  
 Ram Karan Sharma
