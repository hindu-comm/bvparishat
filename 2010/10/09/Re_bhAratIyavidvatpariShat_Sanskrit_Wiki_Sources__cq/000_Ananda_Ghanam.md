Dear Scholars!  
It is high time to think about creating a error free scholar managed  
forum as an online source. The technical aspects have been thoroughly  
assessed and a prototype is ready.

We are ready to host, manage the free source online. However, the list  
of editors shall be approved and the editing will be allowed for the  
select group.

We can enlarge the editorial group, as needed. This applies for both  
encyclopedia, or large texts. All the interested scholars are welcome  
come together at a date deemed convenient at either Mysore, Hyderabad  
or Tirupati, for finalisation.

Please write to me for more details.
