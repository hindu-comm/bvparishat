The counry itself is badly in need of a good Dictator .

Long ago I discontinued my preparation for IAS (Civil Services) after  
coming to know that  
the entire seen is dominated by Politicians but the officers will be  
penalized for the mistakes etc. .  
Perfect examples for the manipulations enumerated by Kautalya in  
'Accounts' are available in 'Satyam' (just like Dvaitas' '  
atattvamasi' he follows 'asatyam') .

Democracy is not suitable wherein the rulers and the ruled do not  
conform to ethos .

If an academician interfers then under some pretext or the other he  
will be arrested and  
taken to Police Station , simply to see that he would lose his job .

Rajadharmas of Mahabharata are not followed and the Law is diluted .

'Dhrtih ksamA ...' - Manusmrti , that defines the ten factors of  
'Dharma' , is fogotten .  
'Dhrtih dhAranadhairyayoh' - Amarasimhah .

People at the helm of the affairs do not have guts to take decisions .

'dhanamArjaya kAkutstha ! dhanamUlamidam jagat I  
antaram nAbhijAnAmi nirdhanasya mrtasya ca II '
