Respected Sir,  
  
\* First स्रियां is the gender indicator of the word रीतिः. That means the gender of the word रीतिः is feminine.  
Second नस्रियां is the negation of the feminine gender for the word आराकूट. That means the gender of the word आराकूट is masculine and neuter.   
  
\* Medhiniikaara referred the word वरिष्ठं in ठान्त, in the same sense. The sloka is this :-  
..............................वरिष्ठं मरिचेऽपि च।  
ताम्रे क्लीबं तित्तिरौ ना वरोरुतमयोस्त्रिषु।।  
  
\* For the word द्व्यष्ट didn't get any reference from another lexicons.
