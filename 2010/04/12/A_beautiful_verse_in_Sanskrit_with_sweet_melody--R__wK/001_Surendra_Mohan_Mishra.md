Responding to the connoisseur spirit of Shri I V Nacharya in quoting a live gem from the celebrated Amaru I may draw attention to a verse which mentions who truly indeed deserves to relish poetry in its best :  
  
naiva vyaakaranajnam eti pitaram no bhraataram taarkikam  
miimaamsaa-nipunam napumsakam iti jnaatvaa nirasyaa 'daraat /  
duuraat sankucitaiva gacchati punas caandaalavac cchaandasam  
kaavyaalankaranajnam eva kavitaa-kanyaa vrinoti svayam //
