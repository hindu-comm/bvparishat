Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Sansthan is organizing  

One Week Workshop on

**Developing Sanskrit Kaaraka Analyser**

from 11^(th) to 16^(th) May 2010

in collaboration with Department of Sanskrit Studies, University of Hyderebad

Venue: Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Sansthan, Adisankaranilayam, Veliyanadu, Ernakulam

**Aim of the Workshop**

The main purpose of the workshop is to discuss the design of the kaaraka analyser for Sanskrit, evolve a methodology to develop the necessary language resources in the form of Kaaraka charts and manually annotated corpus. The participating institutes of the Sanskrit consortium are developing the manually annotated corpus. Durig this workshop, we will be discussing various problems that are being faced by these annotators while tagging the texts.  
  
It is envisaged that at the end of the workshop, we will know about the challenges one faces while developing these resources and come up with solutions and guidelines for handling them.

Daily, part of the time will be reserved for the studies in the field of Shabdabodha theories of vyaakarana and nyaya by the eminent scholars.
