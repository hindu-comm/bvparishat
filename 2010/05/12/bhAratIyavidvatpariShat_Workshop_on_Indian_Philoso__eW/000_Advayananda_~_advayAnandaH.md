Dear All,

Hari Om. Salutations.



Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Samsthan (CIFSS), Veliyanad, Ernakulam Dist., Kerala in collaboration with Indian Council of Philosphical Research (ICPR), New Delhi, is very happy to announce a ten day residential workshop on *'Indian Philosophy: A Brief Analytical Study'* from 5th to 14th June, 2010. This workshop will give a birds eye view of all the six darshanas as well as the six pramanas. Advaita Vedanta will get special attention even as the 6 darshanas are given an overview.



The topics that will be covered in this textual workshop are as follows:  
*Overview of Shad-darshanas*- Prof. R. Balasubramanian, Former Chairman, Indian Council of Philosophical Research, New Delhi  
*Adhyasa Bhashya with Purnanandiya* - Prof. Godabarisha Mishra, Member Secretary, Indian Council of Philosophical Research, New Delhi  
*Manamala with the commentary of Ramananda* - Prof. Revathy Sukumar, University of Madras  
*Vishaya Pariccheda in Vedanta Paribhasha of Dharmaraja* - Dr. Goda Venkateswara Sastri, Traditional Scholar, Chennai  

  
We take great joy and pleasure in inviting you for the programme. Please also pass on this information to your contacts.



One can register Online also for the programme @ [www.chinfo.org/indianphilosophy](http://www.chinfo.org/indianphilosophy)
