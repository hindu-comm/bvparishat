The Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Samsthan (CIFSS) in  
collaboration with Indian Council of Philosophical Research (ICPR) is  
very happy to announce a ten day residential workshop on 'Indian  
Philosophy: A Brief Analytical Study' from 5th to 14th June, 2010.  
This workshop will give a bird’s eye view of all the six darśanas as  
well as the six pramāņas with special focus on Advaita Vedānta. The  
sessions will be conducted by following scholars –

• Overview of Śaḍ̣-darśanas - Prof. R. Balasubramanian, Former  
Chairman, Indian Council of Philosophical Research, New Delhi  
• Adhyāsa Bhāşya with Purnānandiya - Prof. Godabarisha Mishra, Member  
Secretary, Indian Council of Philosophical Research, New Delhi  
• Manamāla with the commentary of Rāmananda - Prof. Revathy Sukumar,  
University of Madras  
• Vişaya-pariccheda in Vedānta Paribhāşa of Dharmarāja - Dr. Goda  
Venkateswara Sastri, Traditional Scholar, Chennai  
Please note that one can register for the workshop online too. For  
more information regarding the residential workshop, the CIF, the Ādi  
Śankara Nilayam, the academic team or visiting faculty, please visit  
our website [www.chinfo.org/indianphilosophy](http://www.chinfo.org/indianphilosophy).  
We would highly appreciate it if you could circulate the information  
regarding the workshop in your institution or to your friends
