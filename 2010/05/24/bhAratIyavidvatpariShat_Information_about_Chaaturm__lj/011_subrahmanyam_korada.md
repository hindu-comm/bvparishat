namo vidvadbhyah



The following text is there in Taittiriya Kaathaka Trtiyaastaka Dasamaprapaathaka (7) --



samvatsaro'si parivatsaro'si idaavatsaro'siiduvatsaro'si idvatsaro'si vatsaro'si tasya te vasantassirah grismo daksinah

paksah varsaah puccham saraduttarah paksah hemanto madhyam .....



Therefore the year starts with vasanta only .



Derivation :



vasati kaamah (manmathah) asminniti , vasanti sukham yathaatathaa asminniti vaa vasantah

vasa = nivaase .

girati jalam iti griismah ,gruu = nigarane .

varsanam varsah , so'treti varsaah , vrsu = secane .

srnaati pamkamiti sarat , sru = himsaayaam .

himena hantiiti hemantah , hana = himsaagatyoh .

sinotyamgam siitena iti sisirah , siin = nisaane .



Vaidyasastre -- daksinaayanam varsaassaraddhemantau tesuuttarottaram sarvapraaninaam

balam vardhate .

Refer to Caraka and Susruta for the food restrictions etc during different Rtus .
