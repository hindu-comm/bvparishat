Nothing could be more sad and depressing than this that a devoted
scholar like Varakhediji has to part with his closest companions one after another ! We can sincerely pray to God for his good karma to support and sustain him in this hour of distress :  
प्रारब्धं हा कठिनकठिनं भूय एवापि वज्रं

सोऽयं देवो विमृशतु किमत्रौषधं घोरदुःखैः  
संतप्तस्य प्रियमिह शमं प्रार्थयामः सुहृद्भिः ।।  
सादरं
