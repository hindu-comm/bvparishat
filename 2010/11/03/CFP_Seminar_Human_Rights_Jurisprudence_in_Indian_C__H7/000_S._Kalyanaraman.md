Human Rights Jurisprudence in Indian Constitution  
  
Location:India  
Seminar Date:2011-01-28  
Date Submitted: 2010-10-29  
Announcement ID: 180156  
  
The aim of the proposed seminar on Human Rights Jurisprudence in Indian Constitution is to encourage inter-disciplinary, cross-cultural discussions on the issues surrounding the field of Human Rights and to promote greater understanding. The seminar to be organized seeks to include the theme papers particularly in light of the development of rights jurisprudence in the Indian Constitution particularly the expansion of theright to lifeunder Article 21. Towards the outcome of the proposed Seminars/conferences the effort will be on to examine contemporaryhuman rights issuesfrom across the world and the effects of recent global transformations in strengthening the discourse on human rights jurisprudence in India in particular.
