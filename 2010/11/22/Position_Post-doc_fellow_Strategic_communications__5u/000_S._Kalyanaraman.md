The Centre of Excellence for National Security (CENS) invites applications for a 12-month Postdoctoral Fellow working on strategic communications vis--vis national security commencing in the first quarter of 2011.  
  
Areas of interest as it pertains to the field of national security could include, but are not limited to: rhetoric and public communication, crisis communication, intercultural communication and organizational communication. The Postdoctoral Fellow will not be expected to teach but will be expected to contribute significantly to the research agenda of CENS.  
  
The selected candidate is expected to be in residence. Remuneration is competitive and will be commensurate with experience and current employment.  
  
Please send curriculum vitae, research proposal, and two letters of recommendation to the Head of Administration.  
  
This is a rolling application and only short-listed candidates will be contacted.
