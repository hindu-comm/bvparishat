abhivaadya,

  
\>यः कण्ठं  
प्रविश्य अधस्तादगच्छन्नग्निरिव  
बाधां जनयति सः ब्राह्मणः"इति।   

This episode precisely points out that one who is  
a brahmana by aachaara and anuShTaanam is FIRE  
to be reckoned with. His body is not mere maamsapinda,  
but mantrapinda through UpAsanA.  
दैवाधीनं जगत्सर्वं  
मन्त्राधीनं तु दैवतं ।  
तन्मन्त्रं ब्राह्मणाधीनं  
ब्राह्मणो मम दैवतं।।(Mahabharatam)  
This is Yogeshwara Krishna's matam.  
The file containing the article on Identity  
of Brahmana can be read/downloaded at[Brahmodaya](http://www.brahmanworld.org/pdfils/publications/Brahmodaya2k7.pdf)
