Sialkot and Shaakal Dveepa



If you bring Shaakal Dveepa within the subcontinent, then you bring Shalya,Madra, Bactria all in the subcontinent. To the best of my knowledge Shaka and Shaakal come to next other. And Shaka Dveep was certainly not within the subcontinent. (In the known history Shakas entered the subcontinent.) I mentioned that connection of Shaakal Dveepa goes to Mangolia and we have not discussed that yet.



In the ancient Kazakhstan and the western Mongolia was populated by people of Caucasian features.

I asked the question earlier-where exactly were Meru and Gandhamadan parvata in Himalayas. Pandavas were going from Gandhamadan to Dvaitavana and on the way they came across country Tushara. Dr. R. N. Iyengar placed Dvaitavana on Indian Sarasvati. Assume Gandhamadan was in subcontinent Himalaya. Then Pandavas will go directly to Indian Sarasvati instead of visiting Tushara which was certainly out of the subcontinent in the northwest and then in northeast direction. Assume now that Gandhamadan was in the Iran. Then also they will come directly to Indian Sarasvati without going to Tushara. There is no Gandhamadan in central Asia. Now assume that Dvaitavana and Dvaita sarovara was in Central Asia. Then no matter where you put Gandhamadana in India or Iran, Pandavas would have come across the country of Tushara on their way to Dvaitavana.



Problem does not end here. Draupadi says to Arjun that you have concquered Shaka Dveepa on east of Meru parvata and Krauncha Dveepa on the west of Meru parvata. This places Meru in ancient Iranian land.

We did not talk yet about the Mahabharat dynastic names in the known history of Persia. It is Mahabharat-big. N.R.Joshi.
