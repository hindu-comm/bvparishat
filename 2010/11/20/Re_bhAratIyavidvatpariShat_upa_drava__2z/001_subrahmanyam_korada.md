namo vidvadbhyah  
  
**NidhAnam means nidhih** - .du dhAn(ca) = dharanapo.sa.nayo.h - nitAntam dhIyate dhriyate asminniti nidhAnam -- adhikarane lyu.t by '**[krtyalyu.to](http://krtyalyu.to) bahulam'** (PAn 3-3-113) .  
  
here is **Raghuvamsa (3-9)** --  
  
**nidhAnagarbhAmiva sAgarAmbarAm**  
s'amImivAbhyntaralInapAvakAm I  
nadImivAntassalilAm sarasvatIm  
nrpassasattvAm mahi.sImamanyata II  
  
**Bonus info** -- **nidAnam tu AdikAra.nam** (Amara.h) .  
  
**Upadrava.h - Meaning of Meaning** --  
  
1. **Yaugika.h (derivative )** - pacatIti pAcaka.h , dasarathasya apatyam pumAn dAsarathi.h .  
  
2. **RU.dha.h (popular)** - praveena.h = expert ( not-prakrs.tA vee.nA yasya sa.h ) ; kus'alah  
= expert ( not kus'An lAti ) .  
  
When there is a tie then **'rU.dha' prevails -- rU.dhi.h yogam apaharati / yogAdrU.dhirbalIyasI .**  
  
3. **YogarU.dha.h ( both Yaugika.h and rU.dh.h** ) - pa'nkajamukhI pArvatI - panke jAtam pankajam = padmam / insect . Here padmam only .  
  
4. **YaugikarU.dha.h ( some times Yaugika.h , sometimes RU.dha.h** --  
  
udbhit = udbhidya = Urdhvam bhitvA bhuvam Agacchati = tarugulmAdi **(Amara.h**) or khanitram ( **KumArila.h etc. in Udbhidadhikara.nam - PUrvaMimAmsAsUtram - 1-4-1) .  
**  
udbhit = name (**nAmadheya**) of a YAga -- 'udbhidA yajeta pas'kAma.h' (**TAn.dyabrAhma.nam - 19-7-3** ) .**Kha.n.dadeva of BhA.t.tadIpikA does not accept YogarU.dha etc. .**  
  
In the case of Upadrava , as it was suggested by Dr Ramanath , better to follow the **Prakara.nam (context)** - check with the commentary and it may be , as you pressumed ,  
'Ru.dha' rather than 'Yoga' .  
  
In the sense of trouble , **'upadrava.h' - is RU.dha.h .**
