Namaste Acarya



I have linited information with me (may not be too scintific) -- the following verse is found in Manusmrti (3-37) --



neksetodyantamaadiyam naastamyantam kadaacana I nopasrstam na varistham na madhyam nabhaso gatam II



( one should not stare at the Sun who is rising / setting /eclipsed /reflected in waters / at the center of the sky).



Generally speaking - the sight is affected in case one stares at the Sun . Long ago I saw some related material in Carakasamhita/

Susrutasamhita .



On the otherhand, the sentence is taken up by Sabarasvamy to discuss the concept of Mahavakya . Iiksanavidhi by avantaravakya,

i.e. iiksetodyantamaadityam, but Pratisedha by Mahavakya, i.e. neksetodyantamaadityam . The latter is Pramana.



dhanyo'smi
