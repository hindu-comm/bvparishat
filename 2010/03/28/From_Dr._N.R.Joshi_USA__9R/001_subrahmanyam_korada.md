namo vidvadbhyah



here is the derivation of the words --



it is bharga (name of siva - bhargastryambakah tripuraantakah -- Amarakosa)-- bharjate kaamakaalaadiini iti

bhargah- bhrjii = bharjane -- Bhvaadi( bhargya can be there in another Taddhita sense, but it is not a synonym of siva).



mrdati sukhayati bhaktaan iti mrdah - mrda = sukhane --Tudaadi



dhanyosmi.
