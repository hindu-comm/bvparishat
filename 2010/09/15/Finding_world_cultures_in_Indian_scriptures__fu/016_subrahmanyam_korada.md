namo vidvadbhyah  
  
AsurAh - na surA yesAm te , surebhyah anye , yajnabhAgAnnirasyante (asu = ksepane ) , asUn prAnAn rAnti grhnantIti (rA = AdAne ) .  
  
BrahmacArI - brahma = vedah ( brahma tattvam tapo vedah - Amara) , tadadhyayanArtham vratamapi brahma ( Gaun.I Vrttih ) , tat caratIti brahmacArI -- n.inih by 'supyajAtau n.inih tAcchIlye  
(Pan 3-2-78) -- KaumudI (samAsAsrayavidhi) under ' caran.e brahmacArini' (Pan 6-3-86).  
  
Tadasya brahmacaryam (Pan 5-1-94) - iti PAn.iniprayogAt yadantah .  
  
VrataniyamAh smrtisu drastavyAh .  
  
dhanyo'smi
