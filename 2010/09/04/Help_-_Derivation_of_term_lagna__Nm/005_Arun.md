In Orissa, a shorter term 'Vaha' only is used. One Pandita explained  
that marriage consists of two phases-Vivaha is mutual giving of words.  
Nirvaha is following them in practice. When both are joined, we can  
not use both prefxes and it remains 'Vaha' only-Arun
