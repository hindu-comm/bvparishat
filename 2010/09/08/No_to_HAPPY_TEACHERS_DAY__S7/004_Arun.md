Dr. S. R. Sharma has already explained 3-fold meanings of this verse.  
5 are stages of created world-Svayambhu (Universe), Parameshthi  
(Galaxy), Saura (Solar system), Chandra (sphere containing moon orbit)  
and earth. In Adhyatmika sense, these are 5 -fold meanings explained  
in Taittiriya Upanishad, Shiksha-valli. But creative stages are 9  
called Sargas. Bhagavata purana counts 10 sargas including Avyakta  
(abstract). For each of the sargas, there is a time scale-explained in  
my article -Aditya and varaha' on [www.scribd.com/Arunupadhyay](http://www.scribd.com/Arunupadhyay).  
Number 14 has been indicated by letter -Dha-14th consonant from ka.  
This system is attribute to Aryabhata only, e.g.-  
काहो मनवो ढः = Manvantara in day of Brahma are 14.  
The Damaru is called Dhakka as it was sounded 14 times-Arun
