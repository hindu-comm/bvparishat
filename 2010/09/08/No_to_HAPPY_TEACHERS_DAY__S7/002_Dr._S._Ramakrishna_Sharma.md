।। ओम् नमो विद्वद्भ्यो नमः।।  
  

I remember to have heard one of the saintly Masters  
mentioning that three different disciplines emerged  
out of the rhythms of the "ननाद ढक्कां नवपञ्चवारम्।"  
and that they are  
1)the ***Yoga Sutras*** of Maharshi Patanjali  
2)the ***Maaheshwara Sutras*** of Maharshi Panini  
3)the ***Naatya Shastra*** of Maharshi/Muni Bharata  
All these texts have in one way or other Lord Siva as  
the presiding Devata. He is Naada Tanu.  
Further,  
  
Why नवपञ्चवारम्? And not 8+4; 7+7;11+3; etc?  
Sri Ranga Maha Guru states that it is 9+5 because  
the secrets of Sri Chakram and Pra-Pancha are revealed  
to the deserving.
