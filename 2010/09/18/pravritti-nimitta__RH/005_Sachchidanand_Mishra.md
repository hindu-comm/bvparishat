Whenever we use a word to denote something, what is the reason we use the word. Let us take one example. Why we use the word cow to denote an special animal? The use of word is based on Pravrittinimitta. ThePravrittinimitta of cow is cowness therefore where cowness is present (inherent) to denote that person we use the word cow. Some times only universal is notPravrittinimitta but an action isPravrittinimitta as in the case of word पाचक whoever cooks is called पाचक. The Pravrittinimitta of this word is cooking. Some words are there whose Pravrittinimitta is a quality.Therefore, we find the deffinition ofPravrittinimitta in shastras वाच्यत्वे सति वाच्यवृत्तित्वे सति वाच्योपस्थितिप्रकारत्वम्।
