Namo vidvadbhyah

**Derivation of Pravrttinimittam** - sabdasya arthe(arthabodhane) pravrttih(behavior) , tasyA nimittam hetubhUtam jAtyAdi.

Under the **SivasUtra – ‘rlk’** in **MahAbhAsya , Patanjali**
offeres two guidelines **– catustayI sabdAnAm pravrttih**

**and TrayI sabdAnAm pravrttih . The term ‘Pravrttih’ is taken from the above.**

The term **‘nimittam’** is taken from **VAkyapadIyam (2- 172** etc.) –

**anvAkhyAnAni bhidyante sabdavyutpattikarmasu I**

**bahUnAm sambhave’rthAnAm** **nimittam** **kaiscidisyate** II

For the later commentators and authors it became **‘pravrttinimittam’** **( my discovery ?)**

**Origin and development** - there are two sides **- Avyutpattipaksa and Vyutpattipaksa** with regard to **Sabdas** .

**AVyutpatti - Aukthikya** etc , some **PrAcIna-VaiyAkaranas,**
argued that the nimitta in denoting the artha ‘ gauh’ by the Sabda ‘gauh’ – is the ‘SabdasvarUpam’ itself , no other thing like ‘jAti’ etc is required , i.e. Sabda is ‘Avyutpanna’ , and the **‘SabdasvarUpam ‘ is the Pravrttinimittam** (Vakya.2-175) –

**gaurityeva svarUpAdvA gosabdo gosu vartate I**

**vyutpAdyante na vA sarvam** , **kaisciccobhayathesyate** II

**Vyutpatti** -- the **SabdasvarUpam** as well as **the VAcya (artha) , both are nimittam (VAkya 2- 176, 170, 171, 174) –**

**sAmAnyenopadesasca sAstre** **laghvarthamAsritah I jAtyantaravadasyApi visesAh pratipAdakAh II**

Analysis thru **Vyakti** is cumbersome (**gauravam**) , so **SAmAnyam (jAtirjAtam ca sAmAnyam – Amara**) is taken up for **‘lAghavam’** (brevity) . **Vyaktivisesa** , such as Agnisabda, just like in the case of **ghatatvAdi**, would denote **‘agnisabdatva** ‘ etc. **Jati** also ( note – **VaiyAkaranas accept Jati in jAti , NaiyAyikas hold – nissAmAnyAni** **SAmAnyAni). GachatIti gauh (Niruktam**) – then it can be an ‘**asva**’ also . So the ‘gauh’ has got a specific type of **‘gamanam’** and thus this **‘gachati**’ in the above derivation is
different from the common ‘ **gacchati’** .

**anvAkhyAni** … the **SabdAnusAsanAni** differ in terms of the derivation of **Sabdas** . When there are a number of Arthas , one of them is taken as the **‘ nimitta** ‘ .

**vairavAsisthagirisAh tathaikAgArikAdayah I kaiscit kathancidAkhyAtA
nimittAvadhisamkaraih II**

The ‘**nimittam** ‘ need not be ‘**niyatam**’ - vIrasya karma / vIrasya vIrAyA vA idam / vIresu bhavam – **vairam** .

Vasisthena drstam / proktam / vasisthasabdo’smin – **vAsistham . Girisah** – girau sete / girim syati khandayati / girau sirah yasya sah .

**Kaiscinnirvacanam bhinnam giratergarjatergameh I gavatergadatervApi gaurityatrAnudarsitam II**

**Gauh** - grUn **= nigarane** (to swallow), garja = **sabde** , gaml = **gatau**, gun = **avyakte sabde**, gada **= vyaktAyAm vAci** .

**PAnini** follows both **Vyutpatti** **and Avyutpatti ( NAgesa , in Sekhara (Visarga), tries to defend that PAnini follows Avyutpatti only , but both are there ) –**

We come across **four kinds of Sabdas in AstAdhyAyI** and the same is said by **Patanjali (rlk**) –

**catustayI sabdAnAm pravrttih – jAtisabdAh , gunasabdAh , kriyAsabdAh , samjnAsabdAh caturthAh** .

**JAtisabdah** - - gauh = gotvajAti vatI . Due to **‘ nAntarIyakatvam** ‘ (inseparability) between **JAti and**

**Vyakti**, the latter is also denoted and as such there will not be a problem in cases **like pasunA ‘yajeta’** etc

(**see AkrtivAda of SlokavArtikam and TantravArtikam of KumArila**).

**Gunasabdah** - svetah , kapilah etc.

**KriyAsabdah** - pacati , gacchati etc. are in **SAdhyAvasthA** and ‘**pAkah**’ **'gatih**'etc are in **‘SiddhAvasthA’** .

**SamjnAsabdAh** - the SamjnAs like Caitrah , Kesavah etc . It means
that **'sabdasvarUpam' only** is taken as the ‘**Pravrttinimittam**’ for **SamjnAsabda**s .

**Hari in VAkyapadIyam** widely discusses the aspect of SabdasvarUpam to be nimitta in SamjnAsabdas .

It is just like some Samjnas in VyAkarana - ti , ghu , bha etc. which do not have any derivation - dittha, dabittha etc. . Others also like sarvanAma, samAsa etc. , which have YaugikArtha - in Loka Satrughnah(satrUn hanti) etc. (VAkya 2- 363,364,365,366,367) --

**gotrAnyeva tu tAnyAhuh samjnAsaktisamanvayAt I**

** nimittApeksanam tesu svArthe nAvasyamisyate II**

**vyavahArAya niyamah samjnAyA samjnini kvacit I**

** nitya eva tu sambandho ditthAdisu gavAdivat II**

**vrddhyAdInAm ca sAstre'smin saktyavacchedalaksanah I**

** akrtrimo'bhisambandho visesanavisesyavat II**

**samjnAsvarUpamAsritya nimitte sati laukikI I**

** kAcit pravartate kAcinnimittAsannidhAvapi II**

**sAstre tu mahatI samjnA svarUpopanibandhanA I**

** anumAnam nimittasya sannidhAne pratIyate II**

In the case of **SamjnAsabdas** also there will not be any problem to '**SabdanityatA**' - says Hari .

Therefore, **CatustayI sabdAnAm pravrttih is the SiddhAnta according to Hari (laukikI) .**  

**Haradattamisra of PadamanjarI** took an example - **kUcI MancI** ( in Andhra there is an AgrahAram , where Brahmanas only live, by the name KUcimacivAri AgrahAram , AmalApuram) etc. is not acceptable .

**‘TrayI sabdAnAm pravrttih** ‘ is taken as **SiddhAnta (ManjUsA**).

It is at once a vast subject and any other questions can be answered
extempore .

Now one may go to **NyAya (SaktivAda etc), MImAmsA, AlamkAra , VedAnta etc.**
