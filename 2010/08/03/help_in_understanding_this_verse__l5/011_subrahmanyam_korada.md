namo vidvadbhyah



it can be as follows -- let us pray some bigbelly , that has human-trunkand elephant-face, is composed , holy and with unimaginable glory, for clearing the darkness of obstacles .It would be a festival .



tundilam = tundam is 'udaram' and 'ilac-pratyaya' by ' tundAdibhya ilac' (Panini 5-2-117) , in

Matvartha. So it is a big belly .



Mahah means Utsavah - ' maha uddhava utsavah ' - Amarakosah . Maha = pUjAyAm (kandvAdi), pacAdyac .
