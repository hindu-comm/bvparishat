अन्तराय means obstacle and not inner. Vide Amara:विघ्नोऽन्तरायः प्रत्यूहः. Sothemeaning of the compound word अन्तरायतिमिरोपशान्तये
is, ‘‘forreducing the darkness of obstacle to nothingness’’.
