Respected Professor Joshi,



MM Williams Dictionary suggest संवादिन् for harmonics and the term is very close to the music terminology, as the change in frequency is counted as mandra, madhya and târa in an increasing order. Modern science has many devices to count in minute fractions through electronic detections. A harmonic of a wave is a component frequency of the signal that is an integer multiple of the fundamental frequency. The harmonics have the property that they are all periodic at the fundamental frequency, therefore the sum of harmonics is also periodic at that frequency. Harmonic frequencies are equally spaced by the width of the fundamental frequency and can be found by repeatedly adding that frequency.
