In the Somayaga Prakarana, Tait. Sam. 6.1.1.1, there is the query about  
heaven - को हि तद्वेद  
यद्यमुष्मिन् लोकेऽस्ति वा  
न वेति  
  
Similarly, in Tait. Brahmana also, there is a query about Brahman -  
सो अङ्ग वेद यदि वा न वेद  
  
Ramanujan
