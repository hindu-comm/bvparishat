Sorry I missed one more step. Though the above steps are seemingly correct, the strict order and process has got a delicate difference.

  

The reduplication of the stem takes takes place before the गुण and hence the steps are to be revised as follows:

  

कृ+लिट् \> कृ+णल् \> कृ+अ - Now the process starts:

  

By 6-1-8 लिटि धातोरनभ्यासस्य

  

कृ+कृ + अ -

कर् +कृ+ अ । By 7-4-66 उरत्‌ in the first part, and in the second 

7-3-84 सार्वधातुकार्धधातुकयोः - गुण, the ऋ changes into अर्.

Then the same process for चकार and चकर, with only the difference,

  

"अत उपधायाः" वृद्धि takes place regularly in the प्रथमपुरुष, due to णित्, gives you the form चकार only, while in उत्तमपुरुष, it is optional, as णित् is made optional as I have already explained in the earlier post.
