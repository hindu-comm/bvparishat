There is a Trust in the memory of my Late father, Mahavidvan, Salakshana  
Ghanapathi, Bhuloka NityaSuri, Parankushacharya Swami, called  
"Parankushachar Institute of Vedic Studies" (Regd.) with a website  
<http://www.parankusa.org>.  
  
We are arranging searchable content of all Veda bhashya and so far KYV  
Sayana Bhashya Samhita 6 kandas \[data entry by Sathya Sai Institute of  
[vedamu.org](http://vedamu.org)\],  
Taittireeya Aranyakam complete Sayana and Bhatta Bhaskara, SamaVeda Sayana  
Bhashya, Taittireeya Samhita Bhatta bhaskara bhashya 1st kanda 3 prasnas  
\[as of today\] are done and are being made available with all references  
hyperlinked \[with shadangas\].  
  
Additionally, the Arsheya krama \[ancient method of KYV study\] is fully  
mapped to present Sarasvata method.  
  
Many more such works with many years of my lectures on Vedangas, Veda  
bhashya, lakshana granthas etc. are being uploaded.  
  
Ramanujan
