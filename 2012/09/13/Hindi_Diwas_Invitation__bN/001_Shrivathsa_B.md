hariH OM,  
  
 hindii divas in samskR^ita pratiShThAna... the following came to my mind:  
संस्कृत संस्थान मे हिन्दी दिवस, कैसा यह उपहास।  
तिरुपति लड्डु\* मे जैसे मिला हो मैगी टोमेटो सौस\*\*?  
आगे चलेगी संस्थान मे हिन्दी भाषा क्लास?  
संस्कृत को अकेला छोडो, बन्द करो बकवास॥  
संस्थान को अकेला छोडो, बन्द करो बकवास॥  
\* tirupati laDDu = the prasAdam which one receives in tirupati which is of a divine taste.  
\*\* maggi tomato sauce = a popular brand of tomato sauce which has a tangy taste but is not fit to be given as an offering to bhagavaan. needless to talk of its incompatibility as an accompaniment to tirupati laDDu.  
  
svasti,  
 JAYA BHAVAANII BHAARATII,  

shrivathsa.
