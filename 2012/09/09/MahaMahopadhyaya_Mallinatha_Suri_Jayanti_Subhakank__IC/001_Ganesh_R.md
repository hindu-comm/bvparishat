It is very heartening to know that the birthday of Mallinaatha, the  
great commentator of the famous five epics is being celebrated. Pl  
accept my hearty congratulations. Let me greet you with a humble verse  
of mine:

मल्लिनाथमधुमल्लिकासुमामोदमेदुरमहामहॊsथ यत्।  
रूप्यतेsत्र भवता भवेत् भवे भावलोकविभवाय वायतम्॥

regards

Staavadhaani Dr.R. Ganesh
