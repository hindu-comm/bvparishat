Thanks for the information on Ved mantra-s.

  

Ved mantra-s Viniyog are prescribed in Brahman Grantha-s specifically for each Karm Kand they describe. Vidhan granths compile separate manuals from the collection of karm kand prescribed in Brahman granths.

  

The question was asked about Rig Ved and Rig Vidhan of Shaunaka is popular for the Vidhans for each mantrs.
