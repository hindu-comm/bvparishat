To be precise, the Varttika on 3.3.56 says only: अज्विधौ भयस्योपसंख्यानम् । However, Patanjali, in his Mahabhashya, adds the following:  
  
भयादीनामिति वक्तव्यम् । इहापि यथा स्यात् - भयम् , वर्षम् ।  
  
The next Varttika is: नपुंसके क्तादिनिवृत्त्यर्थम्  
  
Anyway, अच् (and for that matter, several other pratyayas) can be added to any dhatu since पचादि (3.1.134) . The Kashika quotes:  
  
अज्विधिः सर्वधातुभ्यः पठ्यन्ते च पचादयः । अण्बाधनार्थमेव स्यात्सिध्यन्ति श्वपचादयः ॥  
  
Now, the purpose of the उञ्छदिगण is to override rules that would have made them not अन्तोदात्त (for example, घञन्त would have ञित्स्वर which is आद्युदात्त). If वर्ष was चित् then it would be अन्तोदात्त anyhow and there would be no need to include it in this gana. So Bhattabaskara probably considered it to be घञन्त.  
  
As I mentioned earlier, वर्ष has prakriti swara (and not samasa svara) by 6.2.46 तृतीया कर्मणि ।  
  
However, we cannot use pratyayas arbitrarily since they can be used only in definite senses (कर्तृ, भाव and so on).
