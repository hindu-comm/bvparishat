The akṣaras have not been correctly counted. For example, 7.34.17 has 20 akṣaras two more than what is apparent. Read *mā*́ *no (á)hir budhn**_(í)o riṣé dhān*. It is a correct dvipadā virāṭ (10+10) included among the paṅkti class. The method of the accomplishment of the metres is available in the Ṛkprātiśākhya 17^(th) Paṭala.
