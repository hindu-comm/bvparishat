**Title** Dhanañjayakośa

**Remarks** The colophon gives the title Pramāṇanāmamālā. Other alternative titles of this text are: Nāmāvalī, Nāmamālā, Dhanañjayanāmamālā, Dhanañjayanighaṇṭu, Nighaṇṭu-samaya

**Subject** Kośa

**Language** Sanskrit
