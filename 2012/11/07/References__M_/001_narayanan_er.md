The Victoriyaacharitasamgraha (or Victoriashatakam?) in Vasantatilakam metre having each verse ending as: विक्टोरिया विजयते शरदां शतं सा।। of Kerala Varma Valiya Koyi Tampuran (1845-1904) is published from Trivandrum. This work will be available in the University of Kerala Library. Manuscript may also be at the Manuscript Library, Trivandrum, Kerala, India. His nephew Rajaraja Varma Valiya Koyi Tampuran has wrote the Aangalasaamraajyam (British Empire), a Sanskrit Mahakavya, printed in Malayalam script and published by the Cultural Publication Department, Govt. of Kerala. There he describes the match-box introduced from the West: (I am not remembering the canto/verse numbers), about the Lucifer Match-stick, in Drutavilambitam metre:

लसति लूसिफरानलदण्डके क्व तरुणा तरुणाग्निमुचा फलम्?।। (when the Lucifer Match-stick glows, what is the result of a wood (arani stick) which produces huge fire flame? )

Regards,

Narayanan
