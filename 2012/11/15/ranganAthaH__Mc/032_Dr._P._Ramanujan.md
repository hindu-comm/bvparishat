\> In the pattabhisheka sarga, it is mentioned that ’लब्ध्वा कुलधनं राजा  

\> लह्कां प्रायाद्विभीषणः’ .\*\*\*\*  

\>  
\> This (kuladhanam) refers to Lord ranganatha, the presiding deity of  

\> Ikshvaku dynasty.\*\*\*\*  

\>  
\> On the way, the Lord chose to remain between the two Kaveri-s at the  

\> present place, but is south-facing to oversee Lanka and bless Vibhishana.\*  
\> \*\*\*

\>  
\> In Hamsa sandesa, Sri Vedanta Desika mentions of there being (only) a  

\> sesha-pitha for Lord Ranganatha to arrive and take\*\*\*\*  
\>  
\> Archanas at the present place.\*\*\*\*  
\>
