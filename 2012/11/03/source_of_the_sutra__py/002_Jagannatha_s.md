Srimantah svaamivaryaah, svasti \|

  
Dr. G.B. Palsule, in his ***The Sanskrit Dhatupathas*: *A critical Study***(Pub. Deccan College Pune 1961)**critically examines these Dhatupathas: 1.Paniniya 2.Katantra(Actually prepared by Durga according to GBP)3.Chandra 4. Jainendra 5.Kashakrtsna 6. Katantra 7.Shakatayana 8.Hemachandra 9.Kavikalpadruma of Vopadeva(in verses). The learned professor has also prepared a complete list of the available Dhatus from these dhatupathas in his monumental work, ***A Concordance of Sanskrit Dhatupathas*.** Fortunately it is available at Digital Library of India ERNET (Barcode 5990010098720.)Most probably you can have answer to your question in this work.

SJ
