[[narayanan er	2012-05-02, 10:58:49 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/H5cK3nOGce4)]]



The term paśyantī is derived from the root dṛś as: yā paśyati sā paśyantī by adding śatṛ affix and the feminine ṅīp. An augmentation called n is inserted in between paśya and tī to make it intopaśyantī with the support of the rule śapśyanor nityam (7.1.81). The meaning is īkṣaṇakartrī, the female observer.
