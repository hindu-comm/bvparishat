नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

As we are aware the अतिरात्रः - 2012 has been performed at Bhadraacalam , Andhra Pradesh from 21 April to 2May 2012 .

  

I have participated in the same and my responsibilities were - fixing the land , thru measurements , for the construction of यागशाला , i.e. प्राचीनवंशः , सदस् and महावेदिः , supervising the digging of ponds , guiding transportation of त्रेता (three Agnis) and other material from Trissur, Kerala etc.

  

Preparation of day to day detailed program for the Press, offering commentary in Telugu , Hindi and English and recitation of Veda etc. were also my duties .

  

A book on यागः is under preparation .
