*tava ļ-kāraḥ *is given as example by Vāmana on P. 6.1.87 *ād guṇaḥ* enjoining guṇa ekādeśa when a vowel (effectively an ik-vowel) follows *a/ā* ; in *tava ļ-kāraḥ* *a* is followed by the vowel *ļ* that is such a vowel. Bhaṭṭoji ḍīkṣita S.K. 69 repeats Vāmana. *tava ļkāraḥ*
means ‘your ļkāra’.
