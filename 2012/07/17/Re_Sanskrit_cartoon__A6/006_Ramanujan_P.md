In Soma yaga context water, designated as ‘vasatIvarI’ is addressed and being feminine plural \[‘Apah’\], has the form हविष्मतीरिमा आपः ....
भागधेयीः स्थ यज्ञे जागृत in second person plural. अप्रमत्ता भवत is the commentary there. This is, of course, Vedic usage.

Ramanujan
