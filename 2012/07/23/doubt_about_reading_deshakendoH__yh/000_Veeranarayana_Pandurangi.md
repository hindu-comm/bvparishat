विद्वद्वरान्नमस्कृत्य

  

it is a sloka of bhavadevamishra in the end of his yuktabhavadeva

  

इषुयुगशरचन्द्रैर्लक्षिते **देशकेन्दो**स्त्रिभुवनमयमीशं वीक्ष्य दृष्टः स्वबुद्ध्या।

  

here it is given as shaka 1545 the year in which this work is composed. but देशकेन्दोः is not clear. is there any better reading possible?
