नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

बृव ईट् (पा सू 7-3-93) - is for ईडागम in ब्रवीति , unless one comments in line with वृद्धिः (वृद्धिरादैच्) , सत् (तौ सत्) , भू (भूवादयो धातवः) , अ (अ अ)   etc.  

  

ईशावास्योप्निषत् with me is in एकशृति (Samata Books , Chennai , 1999).

  

It seems Dr Nityananda Misra did not understand my point --

1\. I never proposed or supported अनित्यवाद (?) in this regard .

2\. The analysis of समास and वाक्य is simply to exhibit the difference in case one takes up a compound-word for interpretation .

  

**'Cannot beतृतीयातत्पुरुषःand वाक्यम् at the same time, can it be?'**

I did not say / imply so -- while discussing the difference between a समास and वाक्य , Patanjali stated some facts which are very useful - राज्ञः पुरुषः - राजपुरुषः।

  

In the present case -

one has to depend on the commentaries -- महाभाष्यम् --

  

सर्वसंदेहेषु इदमुपतिष्ठते -

व्याख्यानतो विशेषप्रतिपत्तिः न हि संदेहादलक्षणम् (परिभाषा)

  

Both the analyses are correct .

  

But if a commentator says -

  

'what Samkaracarya says is अनुचितम्’ - this itself is अनुचितम् ।

  

’अस्त्यप्रयुक्तः इति साहसमात्रमेव’(महाभाष्यम्) - stands .

  

All 1134 Branches of Veda are not available . Even if बृहस्पति is in बुद्धिस्थानम् (पञ्चमं लग्नात्) in परमोच्च , i.e. in कर्कटकराशि, 5'-00" , without any पापदृष्टि , it is impossible to claim knowledge of all Sabdas.

  

आकृतिगण , पृषोदरादि etc mean the same .

  

Panini registered Sabdas acceptable to other Rsis .

  

Under परोक्षे लिट् , Patanjali listed अपरे etc.- meaning of परोक्ष .

  

’show me the usage or quote the text ' - is rude .

  

Amarakosa does not (there are a few exceptions) register Vaidikasabdas - **this is declared by the author** .

  

प्रवर्ग्यः , पर्यग्निकरणम् etc.

  

हरिः means इन्द्रियम् in some Upanisat .

  

otherwise - शबरस्वामी says - य एव लौकिकाः त एव वैदिकाः etc.

  

If Srirama is taken as अवतार (सगुणः) then there will be problem - the clarification is nice - here he is परमात्मा ।

  

ईट् is क्विबन्त , Anandadgiri is right -- **in your opinion** - is again rude - it is according to Paniniyam .

  

I am just a student of Panini.

  

I request the members to be polite even when they differ and not to make sweeping statements .
