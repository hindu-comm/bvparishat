" Reincarnation and/or rebirth is a belief, not a scientifically established fact. It is not essential for a Hindu to believe in reincarnation-- or believe in anything that he or she is not convinced of."

  

"Even psychology, a bordeline science, if a science at all has not adopted the idea. Only parapsychologists on the fringes of psychology talk about it."  

  

---

  

नमो नमः

  

Dear Mr. Rajaram,

  

The belief in reincarnation need not brook any scientific rationale. It need not be justified other than as a tenet passed down by Seers of the Shruti.

  

The thin line of respect for a precept of our Sanatana Dharma in no way constrains the scientific temperament. If at all anything, it provides a benchmark. 

  

Regards,

  

Aditya.
