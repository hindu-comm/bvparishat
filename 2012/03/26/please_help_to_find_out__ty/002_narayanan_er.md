Respected scholars,

It is heard that the Sahṛdayadarpaṇa of Bhaṭṭanāyaka is not available since 1000 years. There are a few quotations from the Sahṛdayadarpaṇa like : धनिर्नामापरो योऽपि व्यापारो व्यञ्जनात्मकः। तस्य सिद्धेऽपि भेदे स्यात् काव्येंशत्वं *न रूपता।। *are seen in the Locana commentary by Abhinavagupta to the Dhvanyāloka of Ānandavardhana. We see round about nine partial quotations like this in the Locana. As far as my limited information goes, the Sahṛdayadrpaṇa of Bhaṭṭanāyaka is not available then. Mahimabaṭṭa (1000-1100 AD) says :  

*सहसा यशोऽभिसर्तुं* समुद्यताऽदृष्टदर्पणा मम धीः। स्वालङ्कारविकल्पप्रकल्पने वेत्ति कथमिवावद्यम्।।४।। that he had not seen the "darpaṇa".

Regards,

Narayanan
