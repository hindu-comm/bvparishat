Respected scholars,  

If the root जागृ निद्रक्षये from the अदादिगण in परस्मैपदम्, in लुङ् lakaara is providing the forms:  
अजागरीत् अजागरिष्टाम् अजागरिषुः  
अजागरीः अजागरिष्टम् अजागरिष्ट  
अजागरिषम् अजागरिष्व अजागरिष्म  
Then what could have made Max Muller to think in different way as far as these forms are accepted and used? Then where comes the scope for अजागारिषम् with a वृद्धिः?  

Regards,

Narayanan
