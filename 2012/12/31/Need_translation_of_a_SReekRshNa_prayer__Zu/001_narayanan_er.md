Source: http://www.khapre.org/portal/url/sa/sahitya/sukti/05/z80314052111(श्रीकृष्णसूक्तिः).aspx  
दोर्भ्यां दोर्भ्यां व्रजन्तं व्रजसदनजनाह्वानतः प्रोल्लसन्तं  
मन्दं मन्दं हसन्तं मधुरमधुवचो मेति मेति ब्रुवन्तम्।  
गोपालीपाणितालीतरलितवलयध्वानमुग्धान्तरालं  
वन्दे तं देवमिन्दीवरविमलदलश्यामलं नन्दबालम्।।२८।। (श्रीकृष्णसूक्तिः-२८)  
My attempt for translation (sorry for mistakes in English idiom if any): I salute the Lord, that Nanda boy, dark and handsome in pure lotus petal, moving by both hands, plays according to the calls from the people of Vraja-house, having a mild smile, says: 'don't, don't' with melodious speech, and one is astonished with the sounds between bangles being rubbed with the plates held by the female cowherds.
