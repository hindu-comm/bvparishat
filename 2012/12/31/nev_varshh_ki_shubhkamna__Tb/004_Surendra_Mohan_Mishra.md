//shreeH//

namo vidvatparishade !

I am inspired by Shri Rajakumar Mishra's appreciable verse to extend  
the same theme as under :

dushTaa yaantv atalaM punas'ca vitalaM dhUrtaaH prayaantvanjasaa  
yaantv addhaa sutalaM viveka-rahitaaH paakhaNDino naastikaaH /  
raashTra-droha-kritas talaatalam aghaa mohaan mahaantaM talam  
dharma-glaani-kalankinas tu sahasaa paataala-nirgahvaram //

naa'vedavin naa'tapasi prarUDho  
naa's'aastravin naa'jitadUshaNaatmaa /  
naa'tigmatejaa na na shaDguNaaDhyo  
raashTran ca vis'vaM hy abhayaM tv aven naH ! //

Let me formulate my new year wishes :

"vatsaro vatsalo vatsee ratnagarbho dhanes'varaH" /  
sarva-bhUta-svadharmaaryo bhUyaat sampatsukhaakaraH ! //

saprashrayavinayam,

su.mo.mis'raH  
dharmakshetram
