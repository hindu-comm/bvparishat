Sorry my bad, विद ज्ञाने is अदादिगण and not दिवादिगण. वेत्ति-वित्त:-विदन्ति and alternately वेद-विदतुः-विदुः. It is this root I was referring to.  
  
In दिवादिगण, it is विद सत्तायाम् which I did not intend to enquire about.  
  
Thanks for the correction, Nityanand
