The Mimamsa shastra has 907 adhikaranas, each discussing and arriving at  
one nyaya. Hence the reference to Mimamsa as sahasra-nyaya-sastra  
(figuratively) in scriptures.  
  
Bhatta-dipika makes such nyayas explicit in most cases.  
  
It is in the form of derivation of a maxim from the interpretation of a  
Vedic context with attendant discussions of prima facie and established  
viewpoints.
