Dear Shankarjī,

  

Thank you so much for this. I have so far gathered a few more references, mostly inMeulenbeld's "A History of Medical Literature". His short biography is included in the following works:

  

1\) विद्यालंकार, अत्रिदेव, आयुर्वेद का बृहत् इतिहास

2\) शर्मा, प्रियव्रत, आयुर्वेद का वैज्ञानिक इतिहास

3\) Srikatamurthy, K. R., Biographical History of Indian Medicine

  

Meulenbeld refers to a few more texts which I was unable to trace:

(1) आयुर्वेदमहामण्डल – निखिलभारतवर्षीय आयुर्वेदमहामण्डल का \[रजतजयन्ती ग्रन्थ\], edited by रसायनाचार्य कविराज प्रतापसिंह, 1^(st)ed., vol. I 1936 (pp. 275-277)

\(2\) Dravyaguṇavijñāna by Priyavrat Sharma vol. IV (pp. 305-306)

\(3\) शास्त्री, श्रीकान्त (1981) पौराणिक तथा आधुनिक युग के प्रमुख आयुर्वेद-सेवी, सचित्र आयुर्वेद 33, 9, 111-120. (p. 118)

  

I don't know if any memorial volume was published on Jādavjī.

  

With gratitude,

Deepro.
