Dear all,  
  
Prakrit Bharati Academy 13-A, Main Malviya Nagar, Gurunanak Path, Jaipur, in collaboration with the Rajasthan Sanskrit Academy, is organizing a two day National Seminar on March 13th - 14th, 2012. The theme of the conference is “DWADASHAARANAYACAKRA (by Mallavadi; edited by Jambuvijay) men Praachina Daarshanika Pramprayen” (i.e. Traditional philosophical system in Dwadashaaranayacakra)  
  
Proposal for paper are invited in English or Sanskrit or Hindi. You are requested to submit the paper by 12th of March, 2012 through e-mail.  
  
The text book is published in Three parts by Sri Atmanand Jain, Bhavanagar, Gujarat Rajya, Pin - 364001. The synopsis and summary of 12 chapters of the book is enclosed for your perusal. If you do not get the original text, please mail to \<[prabh...@gmail.com]()\> demanding the chapter number for which you want to write the research paper for the seminar.  
  
Though the given time for writing a paper is short but Prakrit Bharati Academy, requests your co-operation for holding this conference on schedule dates 13-14 March, 2012.  
  
The remuneration for Rs. 1000 is reserved for written papers only. The lodging and boarding will be provided by Prakrit Bharati Academy and Traveling allowance for 3rd AC/AC Bus etc. will be re-embersed.  
  
Please convey the mode and date and time for reaching at Jaipur.  
  
Please confirm your participation so that the arrangements for your stay may be done in advance by Prakrit Bharati Academy, Jaipur.
