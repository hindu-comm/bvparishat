You wrote , "Phonetics is the sole of Sanskrit language." I did not understand. Do you mean 'soul' or 'sole'? Please clarify.

  

I have seen many books on phonetics and sonograms of English words. To the best of my knowledge I have not seen any modern book about sounds of Sanskrit words and their sonograms. Please suggest the source if exists.

Indian civilization is started with phonetics for preserving correct uccara of Vedic literature. I do not understand neglect of sound section of Physics through many Indian colleges.
