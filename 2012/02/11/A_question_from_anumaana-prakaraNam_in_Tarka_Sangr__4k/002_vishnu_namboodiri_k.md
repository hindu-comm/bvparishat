Namaamsi.  
In the fourth step, the sentence is-  
"smoke(which never can exist without fire) is there in this mountain."  
The bracket is helping.The portion in bracket is just an anuvada of a  
fact previously known and not a new one.Hence -  
4.smoke is there in this mountain.So the conclusion is-  
5.fire is there.  
Evam bhavitumarhathi vaa?
