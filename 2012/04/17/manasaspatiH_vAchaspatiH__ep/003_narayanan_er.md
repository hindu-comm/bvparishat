Respected Sir,

Thanks for explanation. But the term बृहस्पति, a synonym of the term वाचस्पति has been considered in a different list of pāraskara group, and having an augmentation of suṭ between बृहत् and पति. Prastumpati (but not pratumpati) and taskara are also in that pāraskara group, and Vanaspati goes with the genitive compound in Vedic usages as in 8.3.53. But I wonder how the synonymous and Vedic Bṛhaspati historically differs from Vācaspati? Please explain.

Regards,

Narayanan
