\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

Research Associates sought for digital Sanskrit Library project

  

The Sanskrit Library seeks two research associates for one year, from 1 June 2012 to 31 May 2013, to assist in digital Sanskrit library development projects. One assistant will analyze and correct the association of headwords in digital dictionaries in a project that integrates Sanskrit dictionaries in a single interface. The other assistant will validate or correct morphological and syntactic tagging of words in their specific contexts in a project to create a syntactically tagged Sanskrit corpus. Both positions require fluency in reading Sanskrit texts, familiarity with traditional Indian and modern European linguistic terminology, and facility working in the digital medium. The positions carry a stipend of between INR 24,000--30,000 per month, depending upon the qualifications and rank of the applicant.

  

Ph.D. in Sanskrit and advanced doctoral candidates are invited to read the full text of the announcement of the positions under "Personnel" at<http://sanskritlibrary.org>and to apply by 10 May 2012.
