Respected Scholars, Namaskar!



After attending WSC in Delhi from Jan 4 to 7, I am now visiting Hyderabad. In WSC I had pleasant and informative interactions with Prof. Narang, Dr. Ravi prakash Arya, Dr, Sastyvrata Shastri, Dr. George Cardona, Dr. Ashok Aklujkar, Dr. Malhar Kulkarni, Dr. Muralimaadhava and with many other Sanskrit scholars. I met scholars who write on BVparishat and others who read my postings. Delhi University Norrh Campus arranged my lecture on "Mahabharata-History or Myth?" It was so refreshing to interact with young students of the university as well as those present in WSC. I presented my paper on Sphota Doctrine and Physics of Sound. In Hyderabad, one lecture in Sanskrit Academy of Osmania University was presented on Jan 13. The second is scheduled on Jan. 17. I am missing interesting discussions on BVparishat during my travel in India. I hope to catch on after returning to USA on Feb. 15. Thanks. N.R.Joshi.
