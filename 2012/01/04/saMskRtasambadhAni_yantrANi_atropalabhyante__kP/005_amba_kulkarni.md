In the transliteration menu, type the text you want to transliterate in the left box. Choose the name of this transliteration scheme in the from Menu. Then choose the transliteration scheme to which you want to transliterate in the To menu, and click on Transliterate. This should produce the transliterated text in the right box.  
  
For example type  
दशरथस्य पुत्रः रामः नगरे कोशात् हस्तेन ब्राह्मणाय धनं ददाति.  
in the left box, and select Unicode-Devanagari in the 'From menu'. Select Unicode Roman Diacritic in the 'To Menu', and click on Tranliterate button.  
It should produce the following in the right box.  
daśarathasya putraḥ rāmaḥ nagare kośāt hastena brāhmaṇāya dhanaṃ dadāti.
