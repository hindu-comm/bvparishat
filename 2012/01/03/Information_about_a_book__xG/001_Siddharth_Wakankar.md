Dear scholar,  
The Harivijaya is a very famous Marathi book,read by the orthodox  
Maharashtrian families. It is printed many times and you may get this  
book from any bookshop in Maharashtra,esp.Mumbai,Pune,Kolhapur etc.OR  
at any Tirthakshetra in Maharashtra.Alternately,you may refer to it  
any Library in Maharashtra,say,Bhandarkar Institute,Pune,Bombay  
Uni.Library etc.Information about this book will be available in any  
book on History of Marathi Lit OR Marathi Sahityakosha etc.  
With New Year Greetings and best luck in your efforts.
