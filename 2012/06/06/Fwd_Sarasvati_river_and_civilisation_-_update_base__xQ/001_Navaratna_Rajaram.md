 I did receive your message text and I agree with most of your conclusions. But I feel we need to pay more attention to Southeast Asia, especially the Mekong Valley. My field studies in Cambodia suggest that a primitive form of rice cultivation might have been prevalent in Cambodia and Thailand during the Ice Age that later made its was to India by two routes-- the southern or the oceanic and the northern or the land route. This would be 12,000 BC or so.
