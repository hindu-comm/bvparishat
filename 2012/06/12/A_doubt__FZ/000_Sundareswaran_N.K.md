Dear Scholars,

There is a verse prescribed by tradition to be recited daily which runs as

ब्रह्माणं शंकरं विष्णुं यमं रामं दनुं बलिं / सप्तैतान्संस्मरेन्नित्यं महापातकनाशनं //

Here who is referred to by the term Danu ?

Kindly enlighten.

N. K. Sundareswaran
