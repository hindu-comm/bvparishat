When more degrees than one are given, that follows the norm of the Section where the Candidate studied. In the BHU, for example, the Oriental Section awards Acharya while the general section awards MA in Sanskrit. Acharya and MA,BA Courses are different also in the Sanskrit College, Kolkata. The MA degree is (was) awarded by the University of Kolkata and the Acharya degree by the Bangiya Siksha Parishad.
