> > My question: निःश्वासः and निश्वासः are these two different words? If > they are different what is the difference in their meanings? >
> 

  

  

निश्वस्*niśvas*\[[*ni*](http://sanskrit.inria.fr/DICO/36.html#ni)-[*śvas_1*](http://sanskrit.inria.fr/DICO/64.html#zvas%231)\] v. \[2\] pr.*(niśvasiti)*pp.*(niśvasita)*inspirer; siffler.

निश्वसित*niśvasita*\[pp.[*niśvas*](http://sanskrit.inria.fr/DICO/36.html#nizvas)\][n.](http://sanskrit.inria.fr/cgi-bin/SKT/sktdeclin?q=nizvasita;g=Neu)inspiration; respiration.

निश्वास*niśvāsa*\[[*niśvas*](http://sanskrit.inria.fr/DICO/36.html#nizvas)\][m.](http://sanskrit.inria.fr/cgi-bin/SKT/sktdeclin?q=nizvaasa;g=Mas)inspiration; respiration; soupir.

Apte doesn't find much difference between the two:

  

निःश्वासः niḥśvāsḥ निश्वासः niśvāsḥ

निःश्वासः निश्वासः 1 Breathing out, expiration.**-2**Sighing, a sigh, breath.**-Comp. -परम***a.*melan- choly.

> No doubt both are correct घञ्ञन्त forms but result from different > prefixes. निस् + श्वस् + घञ् gives निःश्वासः or निश्श्वासः. Whereas > नि + श्वस् + घञ् gives निश्वासः.  
>   
> निः + श्वासः cannot become निश्वासः by the वार्त्तिक on 8-3-36 which > permits the optional elision of विसर्ग. The reason is that even though > श्व् is a शरादिसंयुक्त, it is not a खर्परशरादिसंयुक्त as required by > the वार्त्तिक since the consonant व् is not included in the खर् > प्रत्याहार.
