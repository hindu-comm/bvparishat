Dear Sh. Nityanand,

I liked your Gurudeva Jagadguru Ramanadacharya Svami Rambhadracharya's Śrībhārgavarāghaviyam very much. I did save that pdf. Well a talented poet and no dearth of vocabulary and scholarship!!!, and that every Sanskrit student should imitate most of his usages. I humbly salute him. Here are two verses by me which suit to the context that you were mentioning:  

  

निःक्षत्रामिति संकलय्य विधिवद्रामाभिभद्रः कवि-  
र्निःक्षत्रां पृथिवीं विधाय भगवान्नः पातु वो भार्गवः।  
भद्रो रामकविर्निधाय कवने निःक्षत्रियं भूतलं  
भर्ग्यो राममुनिः क्रुधा व्यरचयन्निःक्षत्रियं भूतलम्।। १।।

  

Moreover, I submit this Radhoddhata for you, you call it-the mnemonic-and-a fun tongue-twister:

  

शर्परे खरि च खर्परे शरीत्येवमेव हि शर्विसर्गते।  
संहिते हि भवतस्तदाश्रिते सूत्रवार्त्तिकविकल्पकल्पिते।। २।।

  

With regards, your friend,

Narayanan
