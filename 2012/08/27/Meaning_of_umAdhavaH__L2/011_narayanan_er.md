Dear Shri. Shrivathsa,

The following is also recorded as the 44th verse of Vallabhadeva's Subhasitavali, Verses 1-1040 of 3527 verses according to the edition by P. Peterson, Bombay 1886, Bombay Sanskrit Series-31.

yena dhvastamanobhavena balijitkāyaḥ purāstrīkṛto  
yaścodvṛttabhujaṃgarahāravalayo gaṅgāṃ ca yo'dhārayat /  
yasyāhuḥ śaśimacchiro hara iti stutyaṃ ca nāmāmarāḥ  
pāyāt sa svayamandhakakṣayakarastvāṃ sarvadomādhavaḥ // 44//

The index of the 1925 edition of the Dhavanyaloka with Locana and Balapriya published from Chowkhambha Varanasi suggests that is is a verse of \[चन्द्रककवेः\] Candrakakavi, but the work has not been mentioned there.

Regards,

Narayanan
