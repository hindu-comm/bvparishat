> Can you let me know the source of following three shlokas:  
>   
> *1. na satyaṃ kevalaṃ satyaṃ anṛtaṃ na tathānṛtaṃ \|  
>  hitaṃ yat sarvalokasya tat satyaṃ śeṣaṃ anyathā \|\|  
> *

  

न सत्यं केवलं सत्यमनृतं न तथानृतं ।

हितं यत्सर्वलोकस्य तत्सत्यं शेषमन्यथा । । १३.४०२ । ।

[क्षेमेन्द्रस्य भारतमञ्जर्याम्](http://www.google.co.in/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=1&ved=0CCMQFjAA&url=http%3A%2F%2Fsanskritworld.in%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2011%2F04%2F%25E0%25A4%2595%25E0%25A5%258D%25E0%25A4%25B7%25E0%25A5%2587%25E0%25A4%25AE%25E0%25A5%2587%25E0%25A4%25A8%25E0%25A5%258D%25E0%25A4%25A6%25E0%25A5%258D%25E0%25A4%25B0-%25E0%25A4%25AD%25E0%25A4%25BE%25E0%25A4%25B0%25E0%25A4%25A4%25E0%25A4%25AE%25E0%25A4%259E%25E0%25A5%258D%25E0%25A4%259C%25E0%25A4%25B0%25E0%25A5%2580.doc&ei=79YgUJzuCeejiAevmoGQAQ&usg=AFQjCNHcGy1pEMZFJHYas7kJjWz9UM6MqQ) ।  
  

> ** *2. pulaka iva dhanyeṣu putrikā iva pakṣiṣu \|  
> * *tadvidhas te manuṣyāṇāṃ yeṣāṃ dharmo na karānaṃ *\|\|  

  

पुलाका इव धान्येषु पुत्तिका इव पक्षिषु।12-179-8a  
तद्विधास्ते मनुष्येषु येषां धर्मो न कारणम्।।12-179-8b

[महाभारते शान्तिपर्वणि](http://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%B9%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AD%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8D-12-%E0%A4%B6%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B5-179)  
  
  
  

> *3. dharmam yo badhate dharmo na sa dharmaḥ kuvartma tat \|  
> * *avirodhāt tu yo dharmaḥ sa dharmaḥ satyavikramā \|\|*  

  

बहून्यो बाधते धर्मो न स धर्मः कुवर्त्म तत्।  
अविरोधी तु यो धर्मः स धर्मः सत्यविक्रम ।।

3-133-11a
