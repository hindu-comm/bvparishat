I do not venture to explain the meaning without knowing the scope and nature of the deity referred to as चक्रेश्वरी as it seems to describe. Barely the अन्वय will be as follows with the correction in the third पाद as अघनुदे - for destroying the sins.:

  

स्वस्वामिभावपरिजृम्भणसारचक्र-

चक्रेश्वरीमयतया नवधा स्वरूपम् ।

  

The first explains the nature nine fold division due to manifestation of the nature स्वरूप with the means ofस्वस्वमिभाव etc. In other words the appearance in different nine चक्र of the चक्रेश्वरी is the स्वरूप. And this स्वरूप is taken in accusative as the object of the verb aअघनुदे आभासयन्ति त्रिपुरास्तु सर्वे,

सर्वात्मकत्वकलनेन निजाक्षराणाम् is the cause of their ability of the बीजाक्षर-s of the mantra. Here the निज may refer to चक्रेश्वरी herself, the central चक्र of the nine चक्र or circles of manifestation due to स्व-स्वामिभाव. Thus with this view, the अन्वय will be

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

सर्वेत्रिपुरास्तु 

निजाक्षराणां सर्वात्मकत्वकलनेन

स्वस्वामिभावपरिजृम्भणसारचक्र-

चक्रेश्वरीमयतया नवधा स्वरूपम्।

अघनुदे आभासयन्ति



would be the proper अन्वय। Many others may be possible if the context requires as per the मन्त्रशास्त्र which is beyond my reach. I leave it to other members to explore the other possibility. Anyhow, the correction would अघनुदे and

as it is अधनुदे seems to mean nothing.

  

नुदतीति नुत् - णुद प्रेरणे। भावे क्विप्।

अघस्य - पापस्य, नुत् - अघनुत्. पापापहरणाय। will be the normal meaning with the correction.
