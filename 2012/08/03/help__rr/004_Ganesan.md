Dear Friend,  

> I just want to add a correction here: The Vishnudharmottara is neither > an appendix to nor a supplement of VishnupurANa. It is a supplement to > the Vishnudharma.

These texts, as also the Sivadharma and the Sivadharmottara, strictly speaking, are not Purana-s or UpapurANa-s since we do not come across any legend or other subjects characteristic of the PurANa.  
At the most they are a class by themselves containing rich source of ancient material dealing with a form of Vaishnavism and Saivism respectively which, we may say, are prior to the Agamic/Tantric form.  
  
The following verses are from the unpublished Sivadharmottara, 12th adhyAya which also speak 'the cows as Mother of the universe':  
  

नन्दा भद्रा च सुरभिः सुशीला सुमनास्तथा ।

इति गोमातरः पञ्च शिवलोके व्यवस्थिताः ।

एता दिव्या जगद्धात्र्यः सर्वलोकस्य मातरः ।

तर्पयन्ति जगत् कृत्स्नं नित्यमात्मरसामृतैः ।
