नमस्काराः विद्वांसः - Subrahmanyan , Narayanan and Vineet Chaitanya --

  

I request Prof Vineet Chaitanyaji to help(/arrange for) me to create a unicode text format and see the the book is available to as many people as possible across the globe .

  

In other words , I put the book in the hands of Scholars and any help in propagation of the concept will highly be appreciated - I do not have any reservations in this regard .

  

I personally do not have the expertise .

  

And I must confess for the delay- why was not this done long ago ?

  

More discussions , involving English are incorporated in Theories of Language : Oriental & Occidental (DK Pub , New Delhi) .

  

वाकोवाक्यम् (question and answer) also falls under Mahavakyam . Here is an example by Bhojaraja in Srngaraprakasa (8) --

  

बाले ! नाथ ? विमुञ्च मानिनि रुषं , रोषान्मया किं कृतम् ?

खेदो’स्मासु ! न मे’पराध्यति भवान् , सर्वे’पराधा मयि !

तत्किं रोदिषि गद्गदेन वचसा ? कस्याग्रतो रुद्यते ?

नन्वेतन्मम ! का तवास्मि ? दयिता ! नास्मीत्यतो रुद्यते !!
