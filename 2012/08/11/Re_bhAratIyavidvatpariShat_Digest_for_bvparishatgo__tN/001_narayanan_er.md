Namaste,

  

It is a न् ending word in Neuter gender. स्यन्दू प्रस्रवणे (to trickle out, to flow) स्यन्दते with a prefix नि makes निष्यन्दते, and स्य becomes ष्य here due to इ of नि। In neuter gender its forms in Nominative Case is: निष्यन्दि निष्यन्दिनी निष्यन्दीनि। रस is essence or an experience of aestheticism as the meaning varies to some contexts. स्यन्द is the secretion or flow of rasa.  

स्यन्दिनी is feminine, निष्यन्दिनी also.  

स्यन्दी is masculine, निष्यन्दी also.  

स्यन्दि is having the suffix णिनि which makes it स्यन्दिन् whereas in Nominative singular it is heard as स्यन्दि, and with नि it is heard as निष्यन्दि what which makes flow of.

So रसनिष्यन्दि is what which makes flow of rasa. As we hear स्यन्दि from the 1st canto of the Uttararamacarita of Bhavabhuti:

  

जीवयन्निव ससाध्वसश्रमस्वेदबिन्दुरधिकण्ठमर्प्यताम्।  
बाहुरैन्दवमयूखचुम्बितस्यन्दिचन्द्रमणिहारविभ्रमः॥  

  

Regards,

Narayanan
