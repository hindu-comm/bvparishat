Sarvebhyo Namamsi.

This happens to be one of the most important speeches in the history of Samskrit.

It is the first time that such such a high scholar accepts/ announces that"Not all Vyakaran students need to study Mahabhashya". Hitherto people were afraid of openly speak about it.

This speech should be listened by all the Vice Chancellors Professors and students alike. It gives a new roadmap to Samskrit studies.
