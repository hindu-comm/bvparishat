तत्रोप॑वर्णितम्—

  

This is the third Sanskrit feature film of the world Produced by Baby Mathew Somatheeram and Directed by Vinod Mankara, a multiple National Film Award winner. The film bagged many awards including the National Film Award and was the opening film in the Indian Panorama. The film was selected most of the major International film festivals of India and abroad. cast by Rajesh Hebbar, Pratheeksha Kashi, Meera sreenarayanan etc. Music by Pt. Ramesh Narayan and Sreevalsan J Menon. Script by Vinod Mankara.  
  

The movie focuses on one of the greatest creative geniuses who lived in Kerala during the late 17th century, Unnayi Warrier and his masterpiece, the incredible romantic composition "Nalacharitham Attakadha". The film gives a true and authentic record of the agony and ecstasy experienced by the poet Unnayi Warrier during the creation of Nalacharitham Attakadha. The film is set in the serene background of Travancore in the later part of the 17th century.
