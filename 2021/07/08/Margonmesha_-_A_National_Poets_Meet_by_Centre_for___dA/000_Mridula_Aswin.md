कालिदासदिनमनुलक्ष्य आषाढस्य प्रथमदिवसे चिन्मयविश्वविद्यापीठस्य नूतनतया प्रारब्धस्य अवधानकेन्द्रस्य प्रप्रथमोयं नवोन्मेषेण प्रकल्पितः कार्यकलापः मार्गोन्मेषनामा कविसमवायः। तत्र भवतां समेषां समोदं सविनयं च आह्वानेन सम्भावयितुमिच्छुकास्मि।  

  

The Centre for Avadhanam, that has been established recently in association with Indic Academy, hopes to, become a centre of excellence for the promotion, patronage and performances of 'avadhanam' a unique ‘performance’ art-form. The Centre further aims to re-energize the study of kāvya, sāhitya, purāṇa and itihāsa, create a resurgence in the culture and arts of a past era and create avenues for intradisciplinary and interdisciplinary research in all the components that go into the performance as well as the performer, including but not limited to memory, creativity, etc.
