भरतस्यान्तिकं रामः प्रेषयामास मारुतिम् ।

रामस्यादर्शनाद्वह्निप्रवेशं काङ्क्षतो भृशं ॥ ५५॥

  

bharatasyāntikaṃ rāmaḥ preṣayām āsa mārutim /

rāmasyādarśanād vahnipraveśaṃ kāṅkṣato bhṛśaṃ

  

It is customary to have (undocumented, yet followed by and large) the halant-form (m) instead of the anusvāra (ṃ) at the line-endings of the verses - would you agree with Nāgabhūṣaṇarāvu Kālepu? It makes sense to me.  

  

M.
