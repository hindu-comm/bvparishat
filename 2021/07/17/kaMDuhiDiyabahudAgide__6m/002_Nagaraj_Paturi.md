ಆಗಿದೆ = ಆಗು+ ಇ+ ಇದೆ

  

ಆಗು = to become

  

ಆಗಿ = क्त्वार्थक form of ಆಗು  

  

ಇದೆ = past tense form of ಇರು = exist, stay used in the simple present sense, 'is' .  

  

ಆಗಿದೆ = it has come to be as-- , it became ---
