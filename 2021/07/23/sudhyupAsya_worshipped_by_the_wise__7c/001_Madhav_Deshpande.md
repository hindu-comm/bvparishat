सुध्युपास्य "worshipped by the wise"

  

शब्दव्युत्पत्तिमात्रेण कोऽर्थ: सिद्ध्यति माधव ।

विरामे सर्वशब्दानां सुध्युपास्यं हरिं भज ।।

  

O Madhava, what purpose is achieved by your word-derivations? Devote yourself to Lord Hari, worshipped by the wise, where all words go silent.
