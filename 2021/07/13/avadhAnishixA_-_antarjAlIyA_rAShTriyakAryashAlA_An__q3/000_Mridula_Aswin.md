सुधीभ्यः सादरं नमोवाकाः।

  

अवधाननामा कलाविशेषः स्वीयैः नानाप्रकारकैः वैशिष्ट्यैः सहृदयानाम् आदरपात्रं विराजते । तथापि कलामिमाम् आत्मसात्कृतवन्तो विरला एव। साहित्यावधानं निर्वोढुं या प्रतिभा या धारणा यानि च कौशलानि आवश्यकानि तद्वन्तः सन्त्येव भूयांसः। परन्तु योग्ये काले अस्याः कलायाः परिचयः मार्गदर्शनं च तैर्न प्राप्यते। तदेतद् वैरल्यं दूरीकर्तुम् चिन्मयविश्वविद्यापीठम् -इण्डिक् अकादमी इत्यनयोः संयुक्तोपक्रमेण अवधानकेन्द्रेण आविष्कृतोऽयं प्रकल्पः । अत्र हि अवधानित्वं प्राप्तुम् आवश्यकानाम् अंशानां व्यवस्थितं मार्गदर्शनं सङ्कल्पितं वर्तते । **साहित्यावधानविषये ईदृशः व्यवस्थितः पाठ्यक्रमः।  
**

Sāhityāvadhānam, a classical art form unique to India, is an awe-inspiring literary performance, that anchors itself in a long tradition of exceptional performers. It exhibits an individual’s superior cognitive abilities such as powers of observation, memory, multitasking, and creativity, through tasks that draw upon multiple frontiers of intelligence. However, there are very few performers, even though there are many potential Avadhānis who have the qualities aforementioned.

In an effort to offer exposure and training to interested candidates, the Centre for Avadhanam, a joint initiative of Chinmaya Vishwavidyapeeth and Indic Academy, is glad to announce yet another Online workshop where the participants will learn to be an Avadhānin. **It is noteworthy that this is the first time that such a subject is being offered as a curriculum.** Under the guidance of Shatavadhani Dr R Ganesh, eminent Avadhānis like Dr Ramakrishna Pejathaya, Sri Shashikiran BN and others, will teach and train the participants, in various aspects of Avadhāntva.
