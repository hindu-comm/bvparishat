Devotion in Grammar

  

सुध्युपास्यो हरिर्लब्ध: पठता कौमुदीं मया ।

मनस्तो न बहिर्याति तस्मिन् मे रमते मन: ।।

  

While studying Sanskrit grammar, I found Lord Hari, worshipped by the wise. Now he does not leave my mind and my mind rejoices in him.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
