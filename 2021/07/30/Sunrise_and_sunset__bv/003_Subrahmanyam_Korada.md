नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

आयुष्मान् भव वेङ्कट

  

\>उत्तमसन्ध्यावन्दनम् is defined as arghya when nakShatrAs are visible at prAtaH-kAla and japa till sunrise (उपस्थानम् afterwards ) and arghya before suryAstaH and japa until nakShatrAs are visible(उपस्थानम्
afterwards). This would imply उपस्थानम् happens some time after complete sunset at sAyamkAla. \<  

  

Yes - this is the actual position . I did not give much श्रद्धा and thought may elaborate if there is any further question .

  

प्रातःकाले one has to offer अर्घ्यम् when there are नक्षत्राणि and keep doing गायत्रीजप till सूर्योदय - उपस्थानम् follows .

  

सायंकाले one has to offer अर्घ्यम् before सूर्यास्तमय and keep on doing गायत्रीजप till नक्षत्रदर्शनम् - उपस्थानम् follows .

  

Following हरि and पुण्यराज , I discussed ' नक्षत्रदर्शनम् ’ under ' नियमः ’ - वेदे in **मीमांसान्यायाः** ---

  

’ नक्षत्रं दृष्ट्वा वाचं विसृजेत् ’ - is a नियम for a मौनव्रती -- he should speak after नक्षत्रदर्शनम् only -- what does it mean ?

  

On a day of सूर्यग्रहणम् , नक्षत्रदर्शनम् can be there during day time .

  

On a दुर्दिनम् (the sky is overcast) there may not be नक्षत्रदर्शनम् for many hours or even days .

  

Today we have to add the condition of city-dwellers -- constantly
enveloped by concrete jungles . Even if one wants to go to a river-bank it is not advisable to touch the water for obvious reasons -- ’ शरीरमाद्यं खलु धर्मसाधनम् ’ -- शिव ( मायाब्रह्मचारी ) tells पार्वती
in कुमारसंभवम् ।

  

Then what to do ? कर्मलोप / कर्मवैगुण्यम् is not acceptable .

  

Therefore the term ' नक्षत्रदर्शनम् ’ is to be taken as a नियम and then it means ' नक्षत्रदर्शनयोग्यकालः ’ । So one need not necessarily wait forनक्षत्राणि in the sky . Rather

can decide the approximate time - during summer / winter .

  

This is how **मीमांसा / वाक्यशास्त्रम्** is useful in deciding
वाक्यार्थ ।

  

Example from पाणिनि -- ’ ते प्राग्धातोः ’ पा सू

  

Example from लोक -- पार्थ एव धनुर्धरः ।
