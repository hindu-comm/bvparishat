Miserable Grammarian!

  

नेतुं वैकुण्ठमायाते स्वयं विष्णौ दयानिधौ ।

तपस्वी शाब्दिकोऽपृच्छत् “एकधो वा द्विधो भवान्” ।।

  

When the compassionate Lord Vishnu came himself to take the devotee to heaven, the miserable grammarian asked him: “Are there one or two “dh”s in your name?”
