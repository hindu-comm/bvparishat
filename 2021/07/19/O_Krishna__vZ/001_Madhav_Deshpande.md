O Krishna!

  

कृष्ण: स्थितो वचसि मे हृदये च गूढ:

अङ्गेषु च व्रजशिशु: प्रसभं प्रविष्ट: ।

यद्यप्यधीरहमधीर इवास्मि तिष्ठन्

न त्वं बहिर्भवसि माधव सुध्युपास्य ।।

  

Krishna resides in my speech

And hides in my heart

That child of Vraja has forcibly entered my body

Though in ignorance I wait with anxiety

O Krishna, you, worshipped by the wise,

Are not anywhere outside
