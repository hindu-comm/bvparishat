Krishna!

  

यावच्चित्तं समन्ताद् विषयगिरितटज्वालमालावलीढम्

तावन्नायाति दृष्टिं हरिचरणयुगं संस्थितं मत्समीपे ।

शान्ते चित्ताग्निजाले तव वचनसुधास्नाननिर्धौतवृत्तौ

कृष्णेति द्व्यक्षरं ते श्रुतिसुखमधुरं श्रूयते नाम दिक्षु ।।

  

As long as the mind is encircled

With burning fires on the hills of objects

So long I don’t see the feet of the Lord

Standing next to me

When the engulfing fires calm down

With the mind bathed in your words

Of pure ambrosia

Only then do I hear your name

Pleasing to my ears

O Krishna!

Resounding in all directions
