Krishna!

  

अन्तश्चरो भवसि यद्यपि जीवभूत:

कस्मान्न यासि पुरतो मम चक्षुषोस्त्वम् ।

आयाहि भिन्द्धि तमस: पटलं हृदिस्थं

याचे न किञ्चिदपरं तव सुध्युपास्य ।।

  

O Krishna, worshipped by the wise, though as my life you reside in me, why don’t I see you in front of my eyes? Please come and clear the covering of darkness in my heart. I am not asking you for anything else.
