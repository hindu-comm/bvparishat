Krishna!

  

किं कारणं मयि हरे कुरुषे ह्युपेक्षां

किं ते मया प्रणमतापकृतं मुकुन्द ।

कस्यावनाय गतवानसि मां विहाय

धाव त्वरस्व भगवन्निह सुध्युपास्य ।।

  

O Krishna, is there any reason

why you pay no attention to me?

Did I offend you while worshipping you?

Setting me aside

Who are you rushing to protect?

O Krishna, worshipped by the wise,

O Lord, rush here please!
