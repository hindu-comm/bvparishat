Krishna!

  

यावत्कृष्णाननेन्दुर्मम हृदयनभस्युन्नतो भाति नित्यम्

यावत्तस्य प्रभाभिर्भवति च सततं चित्तवृत्तिप्रसाद: ।

आकाशे वारिवाहावरणमथ दिवि स्याच्छशी वा रविर्वा

नित्यं मे पौर्णमासी विलसतु सुखदा माधवस्यैव सख्यात् ।।

  

As long as the moon of Krishna’s face

Rises constantly in the sky of my heart

As long as its beams spread happiness

Through the modulations of my mind

Let there be a thick cover of clouds in the sky

Or there may be the sun or the moon

May the full moon always shine for me

Granting me bliss

Through my companionship with Krishna!
