Krishna!

  

न श्रीर्ममास्ति न च धीरपि गोकुलेश

त्वद्दर्शनाय करवाणि हरे किमत्र ।

त्वां ध्यायतोऽपि मनसा वचसा सदैव

नायासि मे नयनयो: पथि सुध्युपास्य ।।

  

O Lord of Gokula, I am neither wealthy nor bright. What can I do to seek a vision of yours? O Krishna, worshipped by the wise! though I constantly meditate upon you with my mind and words, you don’t appear in front of my eyes!
