It's difficult because it has been written wrongly by OP. *क्क्ङिति च kkṅiti ca* is the correct reading—*super* difficult.

  

> 
> >  style="font-family:Calibri,Helvetica,sans-serif;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-size:12pt"> >
> What does the word क्ङिति (kṅiti) mean, by the way? >
> 
> > 

  

*kkṅiti* means“before a *pratyaya*having *g*, *k*or*ṅ*with the *it*qualifier”. This is the fifth*sūtra* of *Aṣṭādhyāyī*.
