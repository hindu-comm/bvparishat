Chitradurga: Shivamurthy Shivacharya Swamiji of Sirigere Taralabalu Bruhanmutt has been selected for the 'Adikavi' award given by Akhila Bharatiya Sahitya Parishad. Famous Sanskrit scholar, Dr. from Bangalore. Shankar Rajaraman has been awarded 'Vagdevi' award.
