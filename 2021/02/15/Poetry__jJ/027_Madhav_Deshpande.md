Poetry

  

अयि कमलिनि सौन्दर्यं कमलानां ते न हीयते किञ्चित् ।

रसहानिरधन्यानामपश्यतां मास्तु ते चिन्ता ।।

  

O lotus plant, the beauty of your flowers does not diminish. It is a loss to those unfortunate ones who don’t see it. Do not worry!
