Poetry

  

रौद्रं बीभत्सं वा रसयति सुकविं विना न कोऽप्यन्य: ।

अक्षौहिण्योऽष्टादश युद्धे व्यासेन वर्णिता: कविना ।।

  

No one other than a great poet can turn a terrifying or disgusting subject into aesthetic delight. Vyasa described the eighteen great armies in the battle of Kurukshetra.
