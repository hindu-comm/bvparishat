Poetry

  

दृष्ट्वा निशि चन्द्रमसं कविरूचे केयमागता रङ्गे ।

आयाति यदा कविता मनुते चन्द्रोदयं सुकवि: ।।

  

Looking at the moon at night, the poet says: “Who is this lady arriving in my courtyard?” When the poem arrives there, the poet thinks the moon has risen.
