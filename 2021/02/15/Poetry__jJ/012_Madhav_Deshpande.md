Poetry

  

उच्छलितभाववीचिप्रसराकुलितं यदा कवेश्चेत: ।

कवितोदयस्तदानीं भावानामक्षरीभाव: ।।

  

When the heart of the poet is affected by the spreading waves of rising sentiments, then poetry arises as the transformation of sentiments into letters.
