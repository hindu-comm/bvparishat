Poetry

  

न करोति कवि: काव्यं हृदि समुदितमस्य लेखनी लिखति ।

व्यर्थं कवे: प्रशंसा लेखनमात्रं तु य: कुरुते ।।

  

A poet does not author a poem. His pen simply writes it down when a poem arises in his heart. It is futile to praise the poet. He just writes it down.
