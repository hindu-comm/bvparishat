Poetry

  

मानसे कमलानीव काव्यानि कविमानसे ।

रसिका उभयत्रापि त्वरन्ते मधुलोलुपा: ।।

  

Poems in the mind of a poet are like lotuses in the Manasa lake. Connoisseurs lured by sweetness rush to both.
