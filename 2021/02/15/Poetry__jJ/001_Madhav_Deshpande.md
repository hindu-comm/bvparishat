Poetry

  

वनितेव साभिमाना कविता नाराधिता समायाति ।

प्रीणयितुं तां कुरुते सुकविर्नवमालिकां वचसाम् ।।

  

Like a proud lady, poetry does not arrive without being propitiated. In order to please her, a good poet creates a new garland of words.
