Poetry

  

ब्रूते कविता कविमयि यासि कथं मां सखे विहाय त्वम् ।

त्वामन्तरेण को मां नेष्यति हृदयेषु रसिकानाम् ।।

  

Poetry says to the poet: “O darling, how can you leave me and go somewhere else? Without you, who will take me to the hearts of the connoisseurs?”
