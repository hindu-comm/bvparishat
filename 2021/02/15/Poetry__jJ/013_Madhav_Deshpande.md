Poetry

  

चन्द्रोदये यथा जलराशेरुद्यान्ति वीचिकल्लोला: ।

किञ्चिदालोक्य परित: कविचित्तात् काव्यमुद्याति ।।

  

As rolling waves arise from the ocean with the sight of the moon, so by watching something around, poetry arises from the poet’s heart.
