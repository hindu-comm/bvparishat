The poet and his wife

  

आर्या त्वमेव भार्ये कविता मे नात्र कापि संशीति: ।

इत्यूचे वनितां प्रति सुकवि: प्रतिवासरं शान्त्यै ।।

  

Everyday, seeking peace, the good poet said this to his wife: “O dear wife, there is no doubt that you are my verse and you are my poem.”
