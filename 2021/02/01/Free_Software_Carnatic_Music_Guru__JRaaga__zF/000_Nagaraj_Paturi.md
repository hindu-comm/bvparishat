Carnatic Music Guru is a tutor/player/lesson generator.  
  
YOU NEED Java Runtime JRE1.6+ TO USE THIS.

Features

-   It will generate / play carnatic music lessons for all 72 melakartha
    raagas and raagas with 6 or 7 notes in their aarogana/avarogana -
    for any thaala/jaathi combinations -   It can generate Kalpana swaram using Markov Chain model either from
    a notation file or from lessons -   It supports Veena, Violin, Sitar, Sarod and Piano as instruments for
    playing. You can select upto two instruments at the same to play the
    notes. -   It supports percussion Using Mridangam, Tabla. You can configure
    off/on percussion. -   Supports playing solkattu alone (very limited feature as of now) -   Have your own notes and play them too. -   Save the music as wave file, Western Staff notation (You need
    LilyPond software installed in your computer) -   Save the lyrics as a PDF. The notes will have subscripts,
    double/triple speed as overlines. -   You can also sing and check your Shruthi and set it for instruments. -   The software is supported in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, French,
    German, Spanish and English. -   Play krithis (available in internet) for selected raaga. Download
    the krithis to your computer. Use as MP3Player for music files in
    your computer.
