The poet and his wife

  

कवितास्वादस्तावद्यावद्वनिता न योजयेत् कार्ये ।

कुर्वन् करेण कार्यं मनसा कवितां स चिन्तयति ।।

  

The enjoyment of poetry goes on until the wife enjoins the husband in some task. While doing work with his hands, the poet keeps on thinking of poetry in his mind.
