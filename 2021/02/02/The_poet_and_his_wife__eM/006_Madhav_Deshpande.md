The poet and his wife

  

कविकर्मणि सक्त: कविरुदयास्तं दिनमणेर्न जानाति ।

तावत्करोति कवितां यावद्वनिता न वारयति ।।

  

A poet engrossed in composing poetry loses track of the sunrise and the sunset. He keeps on writing poems till his wife stops him.
