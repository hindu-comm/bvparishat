The poet and his wife

  

धन्या केयं कविता नाम्नाह्वयसि प्रतिक्षणं यस्या: ।

स्मरसि न मे नामापि प्रतिभेयं कीदृशी भर्त: ।।

  

The wife says to her poet-husband: “Who is this lucky Kavita \[=poetry\] by whose name you are calling every moment? You don’t even remember my name. O husband, what sort of creative imagination is this?”
