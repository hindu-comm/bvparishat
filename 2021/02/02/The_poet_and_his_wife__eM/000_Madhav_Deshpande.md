The poet and his wife

  

निकटे वनिता शेते कविता जागर्ति मानसे तु कवे: ।

उत्थाय मध्यरात्रे कविताया लेखनं कुरुते ।।

  

While the wife is sleeping next to him, it is the poem that keeps the poet awake. Getting up in the middle of the night, he writes it down.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
