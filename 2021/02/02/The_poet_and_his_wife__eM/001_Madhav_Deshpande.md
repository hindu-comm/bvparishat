The poet and his wife

  

कवितारचने मत्तो जानाति कविर्न वास्तवं किञ्चित् ।

कविता वा वनिता वा को भेदो मानसे वसत: ।।

  

Intoxicated with his poetry, the poet loses track of reality. For someone living only in his thought, what is the difference between the poem and the lady at home?
