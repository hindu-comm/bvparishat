कविभवने शुकशावा: कविता: शृण्वन्ति सन्ततं सुकवे: ।

विस्मृतकवितापादान् शुकवाचा स्मारयन्त्येनम् ।।

  

At the home of the poet, the baby parrots constantly hear the poems of this good poet and with their parrot-voice they remind him of the lines that he has forgotten.
