my pranams.

  

you will have to refer to Mimamsa-kosah (available in 7 vols. on archive) for specific Nyāyas. For example, it says —सावकाशनिरवकाशयोर्निरवकाशो बलीयान् इति न्यायः — betweenसावकाश and निरवकाशवाक्य, the निरवकाश is stronger. What does सावकाश and निरवकाश mean? It says —सम्भवद्विषयान्तरः सावकाशः अतथाभूतो निरवकाशः — A statement expressing possibility of change ofविषय is known as सावकाश. A statement which is not like that is निरवकाश .

  

For example:मा हिंस्यात् सर्वभूतानि — do not commit violence towards all living entities — is सावकाश whereasवायव्यं श्वेतमालभेत — "offer a white goat in sacrifice unto Vāyu" is निरवकाश .

  

Between the सावकाशand निरवकाशstatement, the निरवकाशis stronger. This is the न्याय.

  

sādhu-caraṇa-rajo'bhilāṣī,

  

hari pārṣada dāsa.
