Indica Renaissance Fund is pleased to announce a grant of Rs. 50,000/- to the event[Kala Drishti](https://nbf.cvv.ac.in/talks)at the 11th Naada Bindu Festival (NBF).<https://nbf.cvv.ac.in/>  
  
The 11th Naada Bindu Festival is a celebration featuring the best of the best performances and artist-reflections by globally acclaimed artists who have graced the NBF stage over the last decade. NBF is organised by Chinmaya Vishwavidyapeeth at Chinmaya Naada Bindu Campus, Pune.

Along with performances, NBF this year, offers its participants and audiences —a specially commissioned series of art-talks called ‘Kala Drishti’.

Kala Drishti brings multi-dimensional insights into the arts as seen through the eyes of the propagator. Eminent personalities from different fields, speaking about different subjects, bring in a common thread – the art that elevates the practitioner and the listener. The art-talks organized this year from Feb 11- 13, will host Shri Dushyanth Sridhar- author and public speaker on Sanātana Dharma, Smt. Mandakini Trivedi – recipient of the Sangeet Natak Akademi Puraskar for her contribution to Mohiniyattam, and Pandit Ritwik Sanyal- the outstanding Dhrupad singer, Guru, professor, composer and writer.
