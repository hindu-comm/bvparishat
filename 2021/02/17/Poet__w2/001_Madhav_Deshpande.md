Poet

  

कवितामनुधावन्तं वनिता ब्रूते क्व यासि रे कान्त ।

वपुषा निकटे स्थितोऽपि मनसा त्वं कुत्रचिद् भ्रमसि ।।

  

The wife says to her husband running after poetry: “O dear, where are you going? Though you are physically nearby, you are wandering somewhere else with your mind."
