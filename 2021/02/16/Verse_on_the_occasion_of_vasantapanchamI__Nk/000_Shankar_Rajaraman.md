सर्वेभ्यो वसन्तपञ्चम्याः शुभाशयाः

  

उद्गतपक्षेणेव स्वकीर्तिपुञ्जेन पृष्ठमारोप्य । सञ्चारिता प्रतिदिशं वाहमरालेन जयति वाग्देवी ॥

Her own white fame, having developed wings as it were, carries her on its back to every corner of the world by taking the form of a swan. Hail Sarasvati! (Poetic covention: fame is white)  

  

Regards

Shankar Rajaraman
