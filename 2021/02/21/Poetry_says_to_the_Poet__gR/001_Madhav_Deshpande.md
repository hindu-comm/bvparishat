Poetry says to the Poet

  

कवितान्विष्यति कविवरमयि यातं तावकं मन: कुत्र ।

ब्रह्मास्वादं हित्वा कथमन्यच्चिन्तयसि कान्त ।।

  

Poetry is looking for the poet. “O dear poet, where is your mind wandering? How can you abandon the ultimate bliss and think of something else?”
