The poet and his poetry

  

आयास्यति किं कविता नायास्यति वेति सर्वदा चिन्ता ।

सा दुनोति कविहृदयं यन्नान्यच्चिन्तयति किञ्चित् ।।

  

The poet is always worried: “Will my poem arrive or not?” It is this worry that bothers the heart of the poet which is not worried about anything else.
