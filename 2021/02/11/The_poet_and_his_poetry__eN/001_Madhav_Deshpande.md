The poet and his poetry

  

अयि कविते कुत्रासि त्वामन्वेष्टुं भ्रमाम्यहं जगति ।

क्वचिदायासि स्वापे नायासि यदास्मि जागरित: ।।

  

“O Poetry, where are you? I am wandering all over the world to find you. Sometimes you arrive when I am asleep, but don’t arrive when I am all awake.”
