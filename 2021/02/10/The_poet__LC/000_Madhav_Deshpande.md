The poet

  

उदितोऽनुदितो दिनमणिरिति चिन्ता नास्ति कापि कविचित्ते ।

कवितागवाक्षदृष्टिश्चन्द्रधिया यामिनीं मनुते ।।

  

The mind of a poet does not worry about whether the sun rises or not. However, looking through the window of his poem, the poet thinks of the moon and then of the night.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
