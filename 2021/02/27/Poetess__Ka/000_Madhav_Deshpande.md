कवयित्री यदि भार्या पतिरकवि: कीदृशं तयोर्भवनम् ।

चालयति लेखनीं सा भाण्डानि क्षालयत्यन्य: ।।

  

If the wife is a poetess and the husband lacks poetry, how is their home? She writes with her pen and he washes the dishes.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
