This is a wonderful project, Prof. Kannan ji, a great seva for Sanskrit.

Someone may also see the verses relating to Rama's viraha in Aranyakanda or Kishkindha kanda of Valmiki. Verses like ...

सारसाराव-सन्नादैः सारसाराव-नादिनी।  
याऽऽश्रमे रमते बाला साऽद्य मे रमते कथम्॥  

Some years ago I heard a Ramayana scholar singing this. Such passages can be brought into life in this project.
