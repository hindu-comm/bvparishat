Some people indulge in loud thinking in public;

but it may not always be a useful exercise.

"Speak less than thou knowest"

\- so goes the adage.

Should we take pleasure in advertising  

the boundaries of our knowledge?

  

Sometimes अव्याहृतं व्याहृताच्छ्रेय आहुः ...
