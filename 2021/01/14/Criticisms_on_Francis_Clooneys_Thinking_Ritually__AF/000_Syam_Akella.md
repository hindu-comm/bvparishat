Namaste Respected Scholars

Dr. Francis Clooney’s Thinking Ritually is an attempt by him to independently interpret and understand Jaimini’s Mimamsa Sutras without accepting the interpretative exegesis of the important commentaries of Sabara Swamin, Prabhakara and Kumarila etc but still relying on them to understand Jaimini. From our non-academic read of the work, it seems to us that he is laying a foundation for an alternative to Sabara Swamin.



Author - Dr. Francis X. Clooney, S.J.

Publisher – De Nobili Research Library

Date – 1990 (Vienna)

ISBN – 3 900 271 21 6



A few requests for information please:

1.  Any insight into this from the Vidwans and/or Purva Mimamsikas will
    be greatly appreciated. 2.  Are there any critical works written in response to this work? 3.  Are there any specific university departments in Bharat and/or
    non-academic private organizations with focus on Purva Mimamsa?
