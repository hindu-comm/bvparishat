Poetry

  

कर्णाभ्यां श्रूयते शब्दो बुद्ध्या ह्यर्थोऽधिगम्यते ।

शब्दार्थानां परे तीरे न रसो हृदयं विना ।।

  

One hears sounds with one’s ears and the meanings are understood with intellect. However, beyond words and meanings, there cannot be any aesthetic enjoyment without one’s heart.
