Poetry

  

रसहीनं कवे: काव्यं यदि शुष्केक्षुखण्डवत् ।

शीर्षस्य वेदना श्रोतुर्वृथैवैष मषीव्यय: ।।

  

If the poetry of a poet is joyless like a dried up piece of sugar cane, then it is just a headache for the listener and a total waste of ink.
