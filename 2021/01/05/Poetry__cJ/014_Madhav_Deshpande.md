Poetry

  

शब्दैरभिहितार्थेषु रसं मान्विष्य माधव ।

व्यञ्जका एव हि शब्दार्था: प्रतिभानाद् रसो हृदि ।।

  

O Madhava, don’t search for aesthetic enjoyment in meanings denoted by words. Those words and meanings are merely suggestive. Seek the aesthetic enjoyment in your heart through your own creative imagination.
