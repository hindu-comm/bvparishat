Poetry

  

मलयानिलवत्काव्यं शमयत्यनिशं मन: ।

रमयत्यपि कान्तावद् गुरुवद् बोधयत्यपि ।।

  

Like the breeze from the southern mountains, poetry always calms down our minds. Like a beloved, it is enchanting, and it instructs like a spiritual teacher.
