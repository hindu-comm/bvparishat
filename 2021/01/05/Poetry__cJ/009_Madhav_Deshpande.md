Poetry

  

अविनाशि चकास्त्येतन्मह: सारस्वतं सदा ।

हृदयेषु कवीनां तन्निर्मिमीते तरङ्गिताम् ।।

  

The everlasting light of Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, always shines brightly, and it produces waves in the hearts of the poets.
