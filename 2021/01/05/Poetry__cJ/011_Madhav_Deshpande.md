Poetry

  

कण्ठीकृत्याखिलान् कोशान् कवित्वं यदि लभ्यते ।

गौरश्व: पुरुषो हस्तीत्येतत् काव्यं भविष्यति ।।

  

If one could become a poet by memorizing all the dictionaries, word-lists like “ cow, horse, man, elephant” would have to be treated as poems.
