Poetry

  

शब्दास्त एव ते चार्थास्तथापि कविकर्मण: ।

वैचित्र्यगुणतो वाचां रसवत्तोपजायते ।।

  

The words and meanings are the same, and yet through the work of a poet, with the variety of expression, language becomes aesthetically delightful.
