Poetry

  

कवितावनिता धत्ते रसस्य चषकं करे ।

रसैषिण: समायान्तु मरन्दं मधुपा इव ।।

  

The Lady Poetry is holding a cup of joy in her hand. Those who desire joy may assemble, like bees coming for honey.
