Poetry

  

सालङ्कारौ सगुणौ शब्दार्थावुत्थितौ कवेर्हृदयात् ।

बिभृतो रसवत्तां तौ सहृदयहृदयानि रञ्जयत: ।।

  

The words and meanings, with poetic ornaments and virtues, arising from the heart of a poet become bearers of aesthetic enjoyment enchanting the hearts of the connoisseurs.
