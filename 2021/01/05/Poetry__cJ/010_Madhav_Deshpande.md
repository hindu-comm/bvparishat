Poetry

  

रसवत्ता कवेर्वाचि सरस्वत्या: प्रसादत: ।

शुष्कानां वर्णसङ्घानां श्रवणे रसना कुत: ।।

  

The words of a poet become joyous only through the grace of Sarasvati, the goddess of speech. Otherwise, how could there be aesthetic delight by listening to a bunch of dry sounds?
