Poetry

  

सालङ्कारा सुगुणा कविता वनितेव याति ललितपदा ।

रमयति सहृदयहृदयं नृत्यन्ती सा मनोरङ्गे ।।

  

A virtuous poem with ornaments arrives like a beautiful woman with delicate steps and dancing on the stage of mind it enchants the heart of a connoisseur.
