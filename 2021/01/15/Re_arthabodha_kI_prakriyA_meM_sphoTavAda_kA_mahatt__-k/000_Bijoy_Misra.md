Dear Prof. Bhatt,

Thank you for the video. I did view it and appreciated the lucid style

of presentation. A few syntax errors (Hindi) exist which may be corrected.

I admire the effort and the well-prepared slides.  

We are developing a view that the वर्ण is a neurological entity derived  

through the partitioning of cognition. वर्ण is a physiological element  

for expression. The transfer of neurological syllable to an acoustical

syllable is the process of स्फोट. The modalities and the parameters  

of the cognitive space are communicated through स्फोट which carry  

the information to the receiver (ear) for reconstruction.

Partition of cognition determines the semantic unit of a word or a sentence.

This is the over-all model we are working on. I may have more update

by the summer.  

Happy Sankranti.

Bijoy Misra
