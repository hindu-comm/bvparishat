500TH JAYANTĪ CELEBRATIONS OF SRIMADAPPAYYADIKSHITENDRA

  

With the blessings of Ubhaya Jagadgurus of Sringeri, Tattvāloka and Sringeri Gurubandhava are hosting a series of lectures by eminent scholars on the works of SrimadAppayyaDikshitendra.Dikshitendrawas a doyen of multiple śāstras viz., vyākarana, pūrvamīmāmsa, dvaita, viśishtādvaita and advaita.
