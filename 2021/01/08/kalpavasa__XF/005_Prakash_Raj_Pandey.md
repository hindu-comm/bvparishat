Here is what know about kalpavasa.

  

During the month, sun is in the Makara Rashi (Makaramasa), people go to the confluence (sangam) of holy rivers. If they spend the whole month there, it is known kalpavasa. Prayag, being the famous confluence, many regard kalpavasa to be that of Prayag.
