[[K S Kannan	2021-01-09, 14:41:11 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/SAv3rsVWj_I)]]



If a text has a Kannada translation of a Sanskrit text,

it is quite likely the original **text is in Kannada script**.

This applies more or less to all Indian languages  

that have a non-Devanagari script (e.g. Bengali, Telugu).
