No, to the best of myknowledge. However, in the south, in the district of Thiruvananthapuram, *ē* is pronounced somewhat like the English 'a' in hat and *ō* is pronounced somewhat like 'wa'. They pronounce words like c*ē*ci (older sister) like cyaci where the 'a' is like the English 'a' in hat and p*ōḍi* (go away, girl) as pwaaḍi.
