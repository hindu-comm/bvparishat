सादरं कुकवे याचे मा माऽकाव्यानि ते लिख ।

अलेखनेन काव्यानां रसिकान् नन्दयिष्यसि ।।

  

I respectfully beg, O Bad Poet, not to write your noetry \[= bad poetry\]. By not writing your poetry, you will delight the connoisseurs.
