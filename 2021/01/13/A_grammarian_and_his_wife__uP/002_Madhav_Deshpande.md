A grammarian and his wife

  

अइउण् ऋलुगिति कश्चिद् वैयाकरणो निशि स्वपन् प्राह ।

निद्राभङ्गाद् रुष्टा भार्या शषसर् हलित्याह ।।

  

Asleep at night, a grammarian said “a i u n ru luk.” Upset with her sleep being disturbed, the wife said “sha sha sar hal.”
