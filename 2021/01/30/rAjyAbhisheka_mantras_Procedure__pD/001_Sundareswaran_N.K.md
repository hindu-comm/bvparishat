The Śrautasūtra-s and the Brāhmaņa-s deal with the conduct of Rājasūya, a complex śrauta ritual which contains abhişeka and mahābhişeka, rituals for coronation as a king. And the Abhişecanīya somayāga is a part of Rājasūya. For some details please refer to this:

  

<https://www.academia.edu/35959329/Elements_of_polity_in_the_Vedic_literature>  

  

There is another paper on the Rājasūyaprabandha, s campū work dealing with the conduct of Rājasūya by Yudhişțhira. It is here:

  

<https://www.academia.edu/32903807/Rajasuyaprabandha_of_Melputtur_Narayana_Bhatta_A_note>
