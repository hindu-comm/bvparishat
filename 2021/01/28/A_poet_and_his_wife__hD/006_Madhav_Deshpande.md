A poet and his wife

  

मलये भिल्लपुरन्ध्री चन्दनतरुकाष्ठमिन्धनं कुरुते ।

कवितापत्रिकया कविभार्या शाकान् निबध्नाति ।।

  

The wife of a Bheel living in the southern mountains uses branches of a sandalwood tree as fuel. Everyday, the poet’s wife wraps vegetables in sheets of poems.
