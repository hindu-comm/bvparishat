A poet and his wife

  

रात्रौ सुप्तोऽपि कवि: कवितापादं विचिन्तयन् शेते ।

“हे कविते क्वासी”ति श्रुत्वा जागर्ति तद्वनिता ।।

  

The poet, even while sleeping at night, lies in his bed thinking of the line/foot of the poem. Hearing his words “O poem/Kavita! Where are you?”, the wife stays awake.
