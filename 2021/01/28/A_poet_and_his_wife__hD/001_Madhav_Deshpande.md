A poet and his wife

  

अतिपरिचयादवज्ञा कविभवने जायते कथं पश्य ।

कविना लिखितां कवितां प्रातर्वनिताऽपसारयति ।।

  

See how too much familiarity works in the house of a poet. The poet writes a poem during the night, and the wife throws it away in the morning.
