A poet and his wife

  

कवितारचने सक्त: कविरनिशं विस्मरत्यखिलमन्यत् ।

निजकविताया नाम्नाह्वयति सदा यो निजां वनिताम् ।।

  

A poet always engrossed in composing his poems forgets everything else. He often calls his wife using the name of his poem.
