A poet with his wife

  

“मा भूत् कालव्यय: काव्यैर्गच्छ शाकार्थमापणम्” ।

बुभुक्षितानामस्माकं काव्यैर्नोदरपूरणम् ।।

  

The wife says to the poet: “Don’t waste time with your poetry. Please go to the market for vegetables. We are hungry and cannot fill our belly with your poems.”
