A poet with his wife

  

“अलमेतेन काव्येन निद्रासुखविनाशिना” ।

भार्यायै श्रावयन् काव्यं स तया तर्जित: कवि: ।।

  

“Enough with your poetry that is destroying my pleasure of sleeping.” The poet, who was making his wife listen to his poem, was thus scolded by her.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
