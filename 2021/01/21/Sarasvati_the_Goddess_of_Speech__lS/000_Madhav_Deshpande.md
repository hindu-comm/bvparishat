Sarasvati, the Goddess of Speech

  

वाग्देवीं सततं वन्दे कवितारूपधारिणीम् ।

प्रसादकलया यस्या: लभन्ते कवयो यश: ।।

  

I always offer my obeisance to the Goddess of Speech who assumes the form of poetry. It is only with her grace that poets achieve success.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics
