श्रीराम जय राम जय जय राम



Greetings from Pūrṇapramati - A Center for Integrated Learning, Bengaluru ([www.purnapramati.in](http://www.purnapramati.in)) for the auspicious Śrī Rāma

Navami of Śaka 1943, that is 21st of April 2021 CE.



Pūrṇapramati has a tradition of working on yearly themes while imparting education to our children. The themes chosen for each year are related to the significant occurrences in the course of the times we live in. This academic year 2020-21 CE, despite the pandemic, we chose to focus on the Śrīmadrāmāyaṇam to keep abreast with Śrī Śrī Viśvaprasanna Tīrtha

Svāmī-Ji, who continuing the path tread by His Guru and widely revered Śrī Śrī Viśveśa Tīrtha Svāmī-Ji gave this clarion call: "Our aim is not building Rāma Mandir alone. Our priority is establishing Rāma Rājya."



We, at Pūrṇapramati, also attempted a reflective engagement on Rāma Rājya in our times through a 7-weekend Online Web Course under the title: 'Sense of Past: Towards Realizing Rāma Rājya'. We believe we have been successful in initiating our children to recite, learn, narrate as well as reflect on Śrīmadrāmāyaṇam of Vālmīki.



This spirit of learning and joy, we are happy to share with all of you as a continued 'Rāmōtsava' that begins with this Anugraha Bhāṣaṇam of Śrī Śrī Viśvaprasanna Tīrtha Svāmī-ji Pejāvara Adhōkṣaja Math, Udupi (Member, Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra Trust) titled वर्तमान से रामराज्य की ओर.



Time: 4 PM - 5 PM

Date: 21st April 2021 CE

(Śrī Rāma Navami of Śaka 1943)



Live Streaming from Pūrṇapramati YouTube Channel

We hope to have a brief Q&A.



We recall this famous description of Śrī Rāma:



रक्षिता जीव लोकस्य स्व जनस्य च रक्षिता \|

रक्षिता स्वस्य वृत्तस्य धर्मस्य च परम् तपः \|\|



Whose reign creates a people who are



प्रहृष्टोमुदितो लोकः तुष्टः पुष्टः सुधार्मिकः ।

निरामयोह्यरोगश्च दुर्भिक्षभयवर्जितः ॥



And the following is the memory of that reign of yore:



धर्मनित्याः प्रजाश्चासन् रामे राज्यं प्रशासति ॥



संतुष्टाः सर्वसिद्थार्थाः निर्भयाः स्वैरचारिणः ।

नराः सत्यव्रताश्चासन् रामे राज्यं प्रशासति ॥
