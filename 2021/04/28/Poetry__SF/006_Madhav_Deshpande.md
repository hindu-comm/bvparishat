Poetry

  

सहृदयहृदयाह्लाद: कविवचसां रसनया समुद्याति ।

शब्दार्थजवनिकां यदि नि:सार्य रसो बहिर्याति ।।

  

From the words of a poet bliss arises in the hearts of the connoisseurs if the aesthetic joy comes forward setting aside the curtain of words and meanings.
