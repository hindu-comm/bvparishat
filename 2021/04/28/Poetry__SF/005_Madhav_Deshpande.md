Poetry

  

नेत्रे निमील्य पश्यन् पश्यति जगतीमनन्यपरतन्त्राम् ।

यक्ष इव रामगिर्याश्रमे कवि: प्रातिभं कुरुते ।।

  

With his eyes closed, a poet looks at the world without any restrictions, and like the Yaksha on the Ramagiri hill, he creates something with aesthetic imagination.
