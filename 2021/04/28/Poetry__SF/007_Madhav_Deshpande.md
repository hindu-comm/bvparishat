Poetry

  

निर्मथ्य लोकवृत्तं पदचषकान् पूरयन् रसैर्नवभि: ।

वितरति रसिकवरेभ्यो मधुशालायां कविर्नित्यम् ।।

  

After churning the behavior of the world, a poet always fills the cups of his words with nine nectars, and offers them to the connoisseurs in his house of wine.
