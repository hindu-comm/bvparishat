Poetry

  

जानाति को न यक्षं शकुन्तलां वा सुवर्णितां कविना ।

रसिका रसनासक्ता विरतान्यकथा: भवन्त्येव।।

  

Who does not know the Yaksha or Shakuntala beautifully described by the poet?. The connoisseurs delighting in poetry of course stop talking about other matters.
