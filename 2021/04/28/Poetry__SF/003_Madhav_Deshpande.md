Poetry

  

नवरसपूर्णा काचिद्रचिता कविना मनोहरा सरसी ।

यत्र रमन्ते रसिका हंसा इव काव्यपद्मेषु।।

  

The poet creates a beautiful lake filled with nine nectars, where the connoisseurs delight like the swans roaming among the lotus flowers of poems.
