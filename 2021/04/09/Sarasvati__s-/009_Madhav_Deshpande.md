Sarasvati

  

उद्यन्ति कुतो वर्णा देवि सरस्वति विना प्रसादं ते ।

आदिक्षान्तां मालां लभते कविरन्यथा कस्मात् ।।

  

O goddess Sarasvati, how can the sounds of a poem arise without your grace? Where else would a poet receive the garland of letters from “a” to ‘Ksha”?
