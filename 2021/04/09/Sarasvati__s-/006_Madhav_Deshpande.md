Sarasvati

  

क्वणयन्त्या निजवीणां वाग्देव्या मन्दहासनिक्षेपा: ।

बिभ्रति कवितारूपं रमणीयं मानसे सुकवे: ।।

  

The smiles cast by Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, as she plays her Veena turn into beautiful poems in the mind of a good poet.
