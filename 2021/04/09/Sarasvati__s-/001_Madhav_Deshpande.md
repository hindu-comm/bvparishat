Sarasvati

  

सरस्वतीं वरदात्रीं हृदयस्थां भावयामि वाक्कुसुमै: ।

तस्या: कटाक्षपातो भवतान्मयि सन्ततं सुभग: ।।

  

I always devote myself to the Goddess Sarasvati residing in my heart with the flowers of my words. May her auspicious gaze always fall on me.
