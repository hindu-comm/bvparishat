Sarasvati

  

निजनेत्रपातमात्रप्रकटितवरदा सरस्वती देवी ।

उन्मीलयति कवीनां हृदयेषु सुशब्दपद्मानि ।।

  

The goddess Sarasvati bestows her boon just by casting her glance and she makes lotus-like words bloom in the hearts of poets.
