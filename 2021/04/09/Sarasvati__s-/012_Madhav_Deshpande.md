Sarasvati

  

तवाक्षमालामणयो राजन्ते कविवरा जगत्यस्मिन् ।

ये लेभिरे कवित्वं तव करकमलस्य संस्पर्शात् ।।

  

Great poets, the beads of your jeweled garland, shine in this world, as they attained their skill in poetry by the touch of your hand.
