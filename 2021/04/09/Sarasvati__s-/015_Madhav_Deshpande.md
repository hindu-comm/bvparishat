Sarasvati

  

जननी त्वमसि कवीनां देवि सरस्वति पदानि दधती त्वम् ।

सुमनस्सु सत्कवीनां कवयन्ती तान् कवीन् तनुषे ।।

  

O Goddess Sarasvati, you are the mother of poets. Taking your steps into the hearts of the poets you inspire poetry and help them become poets.
