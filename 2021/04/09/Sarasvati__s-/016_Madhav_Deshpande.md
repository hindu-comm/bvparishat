Sarasvati

  

यां चिन्तयामि सततं मयि सा विधत्ताम्

वाग्देवता करुणया निजनेत्रपातम् ।

तद्रोचिषा विकचकुड्मलवच्चकास्तु

माधुर्यनिर्भरगिरां हृदि मे विकास: ।।

  

May the goddess of speech, Sarasvati, whom I constantly hold in my thoughts, cast her compassionate glance upon me. By the brilliance of that glance, may sweet voices bloom like buds in my heart.
