Sarasvati

  

आरूढा यं हंसं विहरति सा मानसे गिरां देवी ।

सोऽहं भूयासमिति प्रत्यहमुद्याति मे काङ्क्षा ।।

  

Everyday, a desire arises in me that I should become the swan that Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, rides in the Manas lake.
