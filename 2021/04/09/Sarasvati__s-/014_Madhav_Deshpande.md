Sarasvati

  

कविमानसे वसन्तो जागर्ति सदा सरस्वतीस्पर्शात् ।

कवितास्पर्शो हृदये रसिकस्य वसन्तमुन्नयति ।।

  

The spring season always blossoms in the heart of a poet due to Sarasvati's blessings. The touch of a poem causes the spring to blossom in the heart of the connoisseur.
