Sarasvati

  

आदिबीजाक्षराणामुदयादुद्याति विश्वमिदमखिलम् ।

योनिस्तवाक्षमाला जगतो जननी त्वमेवासि ।।

  

With the rise of the seed-syllables like “a”, the entire universe is born. Your garland of beads is the source of this world. O Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, you are the mother of the world.
