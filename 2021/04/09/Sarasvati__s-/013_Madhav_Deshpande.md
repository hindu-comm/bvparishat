Sarasvati

  

कविमानसे वसन्ती क्वणयति वीणां सरस्वती देवी ।

सैव प्रतिभा सुकवे रसिकस्य च कीर्तिता विबुधै: ।।

  

Residing in the heart of the poet, Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, plays her Veena. She is said to be the aesthetic inspiration for the poets and connoisseurs.
