Sarasvati

  

यां तव करेऽक्षमालामादिक्षान्तां बिभर्षि देवि त्वम् ।

तस्या: किरणप्रभया कविता समुदेति कविचित्ते ।।

  

O goddess Sarasvati, you hold a string of precious beads in your hand, extending from the letter “a” to “ksha”, and due to the brilliance of its rays poems arise in the heart of a poet.
