Sarasvati

  

अथ चेत् स्फुरति हृदि कवे: सरस्वती भारती गिरां देवी ।

कवितारूपं बिभ्रति तस्या एते हि नि:श्वासा: ।।

  

If Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, manifests in the heart of the poet, then her breaths assume the form of his poetry.
