13 August is celebrated as Patriots Day in Manipur. On this day in 1891, at the end of Anglo-maNipur war crown Prince Tikendrajit, Gen Thangal along with 3 others were hanged by the British at Kangjeibung, the oldest pologround in the world, just outside the palace. King Kulachandra was exiled to Kala Pani.
