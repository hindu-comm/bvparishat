प्रकृतिप्रत्ययादीनां ये चर्चां कुर्वतेऽनिशम् ।

रसं विहाय, धिग्धिक्तान् काव्यास्वादपराङ्मुखान् ।।

  

Abandoning the aesthetic joy, some always continue the discussion of stems and suffixes. Shame on those who have turned their back on the enjoyment of poetry.
