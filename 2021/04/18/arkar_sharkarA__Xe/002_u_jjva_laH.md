**Sugar**

From[Middle English](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_English)*[sugre](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sugre#Middle_English)*,*[sucre](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sucre#Middle_English)*, from[Middle French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_French)*[sucre](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sucre#Middle_French)*, from[Old French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_French)*[çucre](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%C3%A7ucre#Old_French)*(circa 13th century), from[Medieval Latin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medieval_Latin)*[zuccarum](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/zuccarum#Latin)*, from[Old Italian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_language)*[zúccharo](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/zucchero#Italian)*, from[Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic)[سُكَّر](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%D8%B3%D9%83%D8%B1#Arabic)‎(sukkar), from[Persian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_language)[شکر](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%D8%B4%DA%A9%D8%B1#Persian)‎(šakar), from[Middle Persian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_Persian)(škl),[𐫢𐫞𐫡](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=%F0%90%AB%A2%F0%90%AB%9E%F0%90%AB%A1&action=edit&redlink=1)‎(šqr/šakar/), from[Sanskrit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sanskrit)[शर्करा](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E0%A4%B6%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%BE#Sanskrit)(śárkarā,“ground or candied sugar", originally "grit, gravel”), from[Proto-Indo-European](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proto-Indo-European_language)*[\*ḱorkeh₂](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/%E1%B8%B1orkeh%E2%82%82&action=edit&redlink=1)*(“gravel, boulder”), akin to[Ancient Greek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ancient_Greek)[κρόκη](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%CE%BA%CF%81%CF%8C%CE%BA%CE%B7#Ancient_Greek)(krókē,“pebble”).[Doublet](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#doublet)of*[jaggery](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/jaggery#English)*.

  

**Jaggery**

From[Indo-Portuguese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indo-Portuguese_creoles)*[jágara](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/j%C3%A1gara#Indo-Portuguese)*,*[jagra](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/jagra#Portuguese)*, from[Kannada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kannada)[ಶರ್ಕರೆ](https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=%E0%B2%B6%E0%B2%B0%E0%B3%8D%E0%B2%95%E0%B2%B0%E0%B3%86&action=edit&redlink=1)(śarkare)or[Hindi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hindi)[शक्कर](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E0%A4%B6%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B0#Hindi)(śakkar)/[Urdu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Urdu)[شکر](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%D8%B4%DA%A9%D8%B1#Urdu)‎(śakkar), from[Sanskrit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sanskrit)[शर्करा](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E0%A4%B6%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%BE#Sanskrit)(śárkarā).[Doublet](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#doublet)of*[sugar](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/sugar#English)*.
