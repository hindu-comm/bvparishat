When reading the classic preface to sAmavedasaMhitA, I noticed the below प्रतिज्ञाs made by (90 year old?) shrIpada sAtvaLekar in 1956 -

  

``` तानि तु सर्वाण्येव यथाक्रमं ग्रामारण्योहोह्येषु प्रकरणेषु सस्वरम् एव मुद्रापयिष्यन्ते । ```

``` सर्वाण्येतानि गानानि सविस्तरेण गानग्रन्थेषु मुद्रापयिष्यन्तेऽस्माभिः । 
```

``` सर्वेऽप्येते (ब्राह्मण)ग्रन्था यथाकालं मुद्रापयिष्यन्ते । ```

*I was wondering if these fine ambitions were ever met?*

  

  

Further, there was an entire section on "वेदादि-धर्मग्रन्थानां अल्पमूल्येन वितरणम्". I was wondering if there were any plans by the svAdhyAyamaNDalato pursue those noble goals by publishing free copies of their works on the internet (eg. by uploading to [archive.org](http://archive.org)).
