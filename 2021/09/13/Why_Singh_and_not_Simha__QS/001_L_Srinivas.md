\> If you note, in Punjab the middle/last name ‘Singh’ is spelt ਸਿੰਘ (सिंघ) with a ‘ṭippī’ in Gurmuki. This Gurmukhi spelling is closer to the actual  

\> pronunciation.

  

In Punjabi itself, I've often observed that the 'singh' is pronounced 'सियों'. Thus, 'Mohinder Singh' is मिन्दर सियों.
