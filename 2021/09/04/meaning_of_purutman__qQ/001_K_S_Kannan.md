*tman* and *atman* are more or less the same.

*puru* is many, *poly*-

*poly*- is almost cognate with *puru*.

*plenitude/plenum/plenary* are all kindred terms.

It can be taken as reflecting multiplicity or abundance.

  

"multi-selved" may do as a rendering.

The reference may be to the unity at the heart of diversity.

This is what I may attempt off-hand.

  

Looking into the full sentence and context of the original

can perhaps get a more nuanced sense.

  

My two pence.
