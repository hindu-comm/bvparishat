Greetings of the day,

  

Manifestation of Sri. Parusharama form of Sri. Vishnu took place in 28th maha-yuga.

  

The background for manifestation of Sri. Parusharama form of Sri. Vishnu is to free the world from the grip of wicked kings.

  

King Kartaveeryaarjuna had killed Jamadagni, the father of Sri. Parashuraama. In turn, Sri. Parashuraama kills King Kartaveeryaarjuna and other wicked kings.

  

King Kartaveeryaarjuna had requested for a boon to the effect that his end should come at the hands of one of the manifestations of Sri. Vishnu, and it was bestowed upon him.

  

King Kartaveeryaarjuna's end came at the hands of Sri. Parashuraamawho is an 'avataar' of Sri. Vishnu.

  

Searching epic and/or puraanic antecedents of King Kartaveeryaarjuna and story around him will give us details of "Sri. Parashuraama avataara".

  

श्रीमन्-महाभारत-तात्पर्य-निर्णय and श्रीमद्-भागवत-तात्पर्य-निर्णयof श्रीमद्-आनन्दतीर्थ-भगवद्पादाचार्य and it's commentaries gives copious details of histories relating the epic and puraana era.

  

Many knotty issues relating such history is resolved in these works.

  

These works are of high research value as भगवान्-आचार्य-पूज्यचरण informs that he had compared manuscripts from nook and corner of the country before arriving at the निर्णय. 850 years ago, he had undertaken research of massive propositions going by what he has told the posterity in his own words,  

  

"देशे देशे तथा ग्रन्थान् दृष्ट्वा चैव पृथग्विधान्।यथा स भगवान् व्यासः साक्षान्नारायणः प्रभुः" ॥

जगाद भारताद्येषु तथा वक्ष्ये तदीक्षया । सङ्क्षेपात् सर्वशास्त्रार्थं भारतार्थानुसारतः                महाभारततात्पर्यनिर्णय २.३ – ८
