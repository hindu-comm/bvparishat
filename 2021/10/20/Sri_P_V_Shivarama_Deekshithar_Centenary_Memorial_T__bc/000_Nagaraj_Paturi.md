Topic

**Sri P V Shivarama Deekshithar Centenary Memorial Talk on Dakshinamoorthi Stotram**

Description

Sri P V Shivarama Deekshithar Centenary Memorial Talk is a memorial talk being organized by Advaita Academy in collaboration with P S Ramanathan and P S Somasekharan in the memory of their father, Sri P V Shivarama Deekshithar.  
  
In this talk, Sri MK Ramanujam will be speaking about Sri Dakshinamurti Stotram.  
  
The Dakshinamurti Stotra is a Sanskrit stotra to Shiva Dakshinamurthy written by Adi Shankara. It explains the metaphysics of the universe in the frame of the tradition of Advaita Vedanta.  
  
Sri MK Ramanujamji (Sri Ramuji) is a disciple of His Holiness Maharanyam Sri Sri Muralidhara Swamiji. Sri Swamiji taught him Vedanta, Srimad Bhagavatham, Bhagavad gita, works of Sri Adishankara bhagavadpadal and works of Sri Ramana Maharshi like Upadesa Saaram, Akshara manamalai and Sad Darshanam.
