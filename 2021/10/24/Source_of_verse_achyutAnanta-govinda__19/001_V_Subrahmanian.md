In a forum, this verse has been identified as from the Pandava Gita:

  

<https://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_giitaa/pandavagita.html?lang=sa>  
69th verse

  

The forum discussion:<https://hinduism.stackexchange.com/questions/27983/what-is-the-origin-of-the-roga-nivaran-stotra>

  

Here it is said that this verse is from the Brihannaradiya Purana:

  

<https://www.wisdomlib.org/hinduism/book/hari-bhakti-vilasa-sanskrit-text/d/doc501635.html>

  

Here it is said to be from the Indrakshi stotram:
<https://jainqq.org/explore/020204/97>

  

It is found in the Agnipuranam too, with a slight variation:<http://satsangdhara.net/agni/adhyaya-31.htm>

  

अच्युतानन्तगोविन्दनामोच्चारणभीषिताः ।  
नश्यन्ति सकला रोगाः सत्यं सत्यं वदाम्यहम् ॥ २६ ॥
