In cases of *savarṇa-dīrgha*,

the originally existent *dīrgha* of *uttara-padādi* is indicated by

this double *avagraha*. (Else, single *avagraha*.)  

  

And it is not only for ā;

it is used for ī, ū also

(& why not for r̥' also?)
