Also: Bright (1958) uses the term apocope in *An Outline of Colloquial Kannada*. Verma (1989) also uses the term apocope in *Modern Linguistics*. His example is from Kannada: *nənnə* 'my' + *pustkə* 'book' → *nənpustkə.*
