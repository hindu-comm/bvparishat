Reminds me of my visit, more than four decades ago, to both Patan (પાટણ) and Prabhas Patan (પ્રભાસ પાટણ ) where the Somnath temple is.
Both are separated by approximately 460 km. Patan always reminds me of the folk songપાટણથી પટોળા મોંઘા લાવજો (Pāṭaṇ thī paṭoḷā moṅghā lāvajo – "Please bring for me costly (high quality) paṭoḷā from Pāṭaṇ"). Patan is famous for Patola (પટોળા) which is a type of silk cloth particularly used for wedding sarees.
