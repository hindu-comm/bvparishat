Grammarian poets

  

वैयाकरणकवीनां प्रेमालापं सखे शृणुष्वैनम् ।

खफछठथेति वदन्तं भार्या शषसर्हलित्याह ।।

  

O friend, listen to this conversation of the grammarian poets. When the husband says “kha pha cha tha tha,” the wife responds by saying “sha sha sar hal.”

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
