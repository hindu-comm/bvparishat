There are two types of संकर प्रयोगs in Marathi -

  

कर्ता-कर्म & कर्म-भावे

  

कर्ता-कर्म:

तू खीर खाल्लीस, तू भात खाल्लास (Verb agrees in gender and number with the karma and in person with the kartaa)

I have come across this only in the 2nd person.

  

कर्म-भावे:

आईने मुलीला निजविली (The verb agrees with the gender of the karma, but this is a bhaave prayoga) instead of आईने मुलीलानिजविले, which according to me is grammatically correct, and a preferred usage.

  

Other commonly heard sentence are रामाने रावणाला मारला. कृष्णाने राक्षसीलामारली.

  

I remember, in our school days, we were taught that the कर्म-भावे संकर is not good.

But I do not remember any such comment for the कर्ता-कर्म संकर.
