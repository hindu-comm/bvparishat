\<\< तू खीर खाल्लीस, तू भात खाल्लास (Verb agrees in gender and number with the karma and in person with the kartaa)\>\>

  

Thank you very much Professor for the explicit expression regarding the agreement issue of verb-form.

  

\<\<I remember, in our school days, we were taught that the कर्म-भावे संकर is not good.\>\>

  

In शास्त्रीय मराठी व्याकरण (1925), the संकर प्रयोग has been discussed on pp.777-783 and 987-988.

  

On p.777, मोरो केशव दामले remarks:

  
**कर्तृकर्मसंकर आणि कर्मभावसंकर ह्या दोन संकीर्ण प्रयोगांचा व्यावहारिक भाषणांत बराच प्रचार आहे.  
**
