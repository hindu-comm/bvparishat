[[Deva Pattanayak	2021-05-19, 12:16:20 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/CGNMBaqAqcQ)]]



I am very saddened to know that Kaul_ji passed away due to Covid-19. I remember his reply to my intuitivefeeling that atma has to go in and out of the body from a classical point of view, but from a quantum theory perspective one can talk about the probabilityof it being inside or outside the body. 

I suffered from this deadly disease and felt I am going in and out of my body ( dying) and wondered to myself why I am not feeling any pain. Now I have recovered and plan toread his wise reply on this forum on 8/17/2019 and continue my personal understanding of life and reincarnation.

My heartfelt condolences to his family and friends and pray for his atma to effortlessly transition to it's reincarnation (sadgati).

Deva
