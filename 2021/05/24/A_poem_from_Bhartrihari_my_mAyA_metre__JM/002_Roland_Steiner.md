It is an Upajāti (a mixture of Indravajrā and Upendravajrā lines).  
  
  
\> māyā  
  
The samacatuṣpadī metre otherwise known as Mattamayūram (ma ta ya sa  
ga; with a caesura after the 4th syllable in each pāda) is called māyā  
in the Prākṛta Paiṅgalam (2.141).
