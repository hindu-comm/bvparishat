Pāṇini, the Grammarian

  

मुखात् सिंहस्य सूत्राणि मुने: श्रुत्वैव कानने ।

आजीविका अकम्पन्त जीविताशङ्कया भृशम् ।।

  

Just by hearing the rules of Pāṇini coming from the mouth of the lion in the forest, the mendicants shook wildly with fear for their lives.
