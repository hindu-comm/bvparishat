Pāṇini, the Grammarian

  

मुखात्सिंहस्य सूत्राणि मुने: श्रुत्वापि कानने ।

गौडपादोऽब्रवीज्जातो नैव सिंहो न पाणिनि: ।।

  

Even after hearing the rules of Pāṇini coming from the mouth of the lion in the forest, Gauḍapāda said: “Neither a lion nor Pāṇini were ever born.”
