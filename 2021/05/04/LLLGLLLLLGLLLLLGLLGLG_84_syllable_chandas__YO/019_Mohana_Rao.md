[[Mohana Rao	2021-07-06, 09:19:45 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/YO5aRpjUK94)]]



This metre is called madanasAyakam that has the rhythm of charcharI (charchariis also called vibudhapriyA with a different yati, mattakOkila in telugu, mallikAmAle in kannaDa, mallikA in malayALam, ramal musaddas mah^zuufin urdu). It is mentioned by this name in marAThI ChaMdOrachanA. This metre was used by jagaddhara in the Sanskrit stutikusumAnjali.  

J K Mohana Rao
