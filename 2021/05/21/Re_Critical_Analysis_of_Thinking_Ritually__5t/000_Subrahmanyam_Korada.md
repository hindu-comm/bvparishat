## Critical Analysis of Thinking Ritually

  

Dr. Francis Clooney’s Thinking Ritually attempts to independently interpret and understand Jaimini’s Mimamsa Sutras without accepting the interpretative exegesis of the essential commentaries of Sabara Swamin, Prabhakara, and Kumarila, etcetera but still relying on them to understand Jaimini. From our non-academic read of the work, it seems to us that he is laying a foundation for an alternative to Sabara Swamin.  



We are looking for Purvamimasaka experts within the academia to produce an academic critical analysis of this work. We will support such a project as a Publisher by bringing this work to the Indology Academic space.

Before looking into any work ,we have to take a look at the qualifications of the author -- how good is the knowledge of संस्कृतम्
-- वेदाः , वेदाङ्गानि , दर्शनानि etc of विद्वान् Francis -- where , under which गुरु (s) he studied

etc .

I have been observing for the last forty years (especially in India) - some ' scholars' ( पण्डितंमन्याः / पण्डितंमानिनः) , without proper qualifications , start commenting / interpreting / translating great works such as ब्रह्मसूत्राणि

and get them published .

  

Even to study the essential basics one has to learn पदवाक्यप्रमाणशास्त्राणि ( व्याकरणम् , पूर्वमीमांसा , न्यायवैशेषिके ) -
and when one wants to take up a शास्त्रम् like पूर्वमीमांसा , one has to learn वेदाः ।

  

If anybody can interpret any शास्त्रम् , why to approach a गुरु and learn for years ?

  

This is totally unacceptable and looking into such a work is merely a waste of time -- hope people would not publicizesuch works , which do not serve any meaningful purpose whatsoever , and instead put in some effort to

learn and propagate the traditional knowledge .

  

I wonder whether the author in question knows - whereto use श्वेताक्षताः and where हरिद्राक्षताः ।

  

Did he study ज्योतिषम् ? -- अग्निर्नः पातु कृत्तिका , नक्षत्रं देवमिन्द्रियम् (कृष्णयजुर्वेदः) ?

  

And what about कल्पशास्त्रम् -- since you mentioned - he deals with कर्मकाण्ड ?

  

I toiled for decades to learn 18 विद्यास्थानानि - and still have doubts !

  

**I do not believe in fever / infection but I should not refute the सिद्धन्ताः of चरकसंहिता - offered by महर्षयः through योगिप्रत्यक्षम् !**

**

पूर्वमीमांसा is a great शास्त्रम् useful in the interpretation of any piece of literature ( it is universal - not only for संस्कृतम् ) and the सूत्राणि of जैमिनि are commented accordingly .
