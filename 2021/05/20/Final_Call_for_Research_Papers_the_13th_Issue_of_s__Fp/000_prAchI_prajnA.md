All the research papers undergo a review process. The papers (without the name of the contributor) are sent to the reviewers having expertise on the field, and considered for publication only on the recommendations of the reviewers on following parameters:

1. The Research Paper must have a link to any branch of Sanskrit Studies, i.e. Vedas, Vedāṅgas, Upaniṣads, Philosophies, Purāṇas, Smṛtis, Literature, Poetics, Grammar, Tantras, Astrology, Astronomy and Modern subjects like Computational Linguistics, Environmental Science and allied fields. In any case linkage to Indology is essential.

2. The Research Paper must be an original contribution of the author.

3. The discussion should develop in the order from Statement of Problem to→ assumption or hypotheses to → critical evaluations → to a conclusion with the findings of research.

4. The Research Paper must present a discovery / invention of a new - idea / thought /design / plan / awareness / inkling/ indication / appreciation / conception / understanding / opinion / view/ concept / notion / theory / principle or even a new style of interpretation.

5. The language must be lucid and must meet the standard of presentation.

6. The Research Paper must have contemporary relevance, contributions to knowledge, clarity and logic in analysis, methodology and appropriateness of references.

7. If the author has quoted more than 500 words / a table / a figure from a published work, in the article, a copy of permission obtained from the respective copyright holder needs to be enclosed.

8. The copyright is transferred to the Journal if article is accepted for publication in प्राची प्रज्ञा / Prachi Prajna, ISSN 2348 – 8417.

9. All the correspondence should be made through the same email ID only by which the paper has been submitted.

10. All the process will be made online only and no printing media will be undertaken. The selection of the Research paper is entirely entrusted to “प्राची प्रज्ञा”.

If the paper meets the preliminary requirements, the article will be processed for review, which could take up to 3 months. The accepted articles are scheduled for publication in the alphabetical order.
