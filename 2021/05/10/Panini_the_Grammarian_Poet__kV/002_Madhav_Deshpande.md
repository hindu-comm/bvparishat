Panini, the Grammarian Poet

  

कविश्च शाब्दिकश्चासौ पाणिनिस्तस्य विश्रुता ।

देहलीदीपवद्बुद्धिरुभयत्र प्रकाशते ।।

  

The great Pāṇini is said to have been both a poet and a grammarian. His highly praised mind shines in both fields like a lamp placed on the threshold.
