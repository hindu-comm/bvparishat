Panini, the Grammarian Poet

  

शाब्देऽभूत् कर्कशा बुद्धि: काव्ये तस्यैव कोमला ।

वन्दे शालातुरीयं तं महान्तं शाब्दिकं कविम् ।।

  

I offer my obeisance to Pāṇini, a resident of Śalātura, who was a great poet and grammarian. His mind was sharp in grammar and tender in poetry.
