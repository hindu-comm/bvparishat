Panini, the Grammarian Poet

  

“अय्युण्सुप्तिङ्डुकृञ्लुङ्लिड्” इत्यादि सततं वदन् ।

चित्रं स पाणिनिश्चक्रे काव्यं जाम्बवतीजयम् ।।

  

It is amazing that Pāṇini, the grammarian constantly uttering technical terms like “a i u ṇ,” “sup,” “tiṅ,” “ḍukr̥ñ,” “luṅ” and “liṭ,” created the poem titled “Jāmbavatījaya.”
