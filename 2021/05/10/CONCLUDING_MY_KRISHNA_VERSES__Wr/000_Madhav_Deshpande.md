CONCLUDING MY KRISHNA VERSES

  

माधवनामा मुरलीधरतनयो वासुदेवपौत्रोऽहम् ।

कृतवान् माधवकर्णामृतमेतन्माधवप्रीत्यै ।।१४६५।।

  

I, named Madhav, the son of Murlidhar and the grandson of Vasudev, composed this Nectar for the Ears describing Mādhava for the delight of Mādhava.

  

इति कृष्णतनय-सखारामसुत-रामचन्द्रप्रपौत्रेण वासुदेवपौत्रेण मुरलीधरसुनुना देशपांडे-कुलजेन मन्दाकिनीतनूजेन माधवशर्मणा विरचितं माधवकर्णामृतकाव्यं समाप्तम् । श्रीकृष्ण: प्रीयताम् ।

  

Thus ends the poem “Madhava/Krishna, The Ambrosia for the Ear” composed by Madhav Deshpande, the son of Murlidhar and Mandakini, the grandson of Vasudeva, the great-grandson of Ramachandra, the great-great-grandson of Sakharam, and the great-great-great-grandson of Krishna.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
