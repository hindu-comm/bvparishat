**एकम् हन्यान्नवा हन्यादिषुर्मुक्तो धनुष्मता।बुद्धिर्बुद्धिमतोत्सृष्टः राष्ट्रं हन्ति सनायकं॥चाणक्य**

  

ekam hanyānnavā hanyādiṣurmukto dhanuṣmatā।buddhirbuddhimatotsṛṣṭaḥ rāṣṭraṃ hanti sanāyakaṃ॥cāṇakya

  

It is possible that an arrow shot even by the most efficient marksman may miss its target and may fail to kill. But the sparks of intelligence emitted by the highly learned and efficient people, if they are oriented against a nation and the king, would, without fail, cause the destruction of the whole kingdom with its leader .

  

धनुष्मता by the archer मुक्तः released इषुः arrow, dart एकं one
हन्यात् may kill  न हन्यात् वा or may not kill (परन्तु) however 
बुद्धिमता by a person of intense intellectual capacity उत्सृष्टः generated बुद्धिः thoughts, thought process, plans सनायकं along with the leader राष्ट्रं the nation हन्ति (ध्रुवं) would surely kill, annihilate
