पिष्ट्वा स्वचापं बहुवर्णयुक्तं

मेघेषु वज्री बत मेलयित्वा ।

वर्षन्निवाभाति समन्ततो यत्

क्रीडन्ति रङ्गैरिह होलिकायाम् ॥

The way people here are playing with colours all around during Holi, it seems as though Indra himself has ground up his multicolour bow, mixed it into the clouds and made it rain!
