नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

However, has the issue been sorted out as to whether the reading(s)  

अनतीतोपधान् or अनतीतोपदान् could still be right?  ---- Vid Kannan

  

In both the cases योग्यताराहित्यम् is conspicuous . So no analysis is attempted .

  

अर्थात्प्रकरणाद्वा -- **महाभाष्यम्** ।

  

संयोगो विप्रयोगश्च साहचर्यं विरोधिता ।

अर्थः प्रकरणं लिङ्गम् ....

शब्दार्थस्यानवच्छेदे विशेषस्मृतिहेतवः ॥ -- **वाक्यपदीयम् , वाक्यकाण्डः , 315 , 316**

  

Here , due to both साहचर्यम् and प्रकरणम् , the above readings cannot be taken as the right ones .

  

The candidates ought to fulfill some formalities - prescribed by
धर्मशास्त्रम् or अर्थशास्त्रम् , in the absenceof which the rest of the qualifications will be null and void .

  

This is not to downplay anybody's scholarshipbut , may be , the Editor (Gita Press etc ) did not pay adequate attention in this regard .

  

उपदा fits in the sense of उपहार only .

  

धन्यो’स्मि
