गृहकार्यं कुर्वन्ती रचयति कविताक्षराणि कवयित्री ।

गायति मुखेन कवितां करेण शाकं पचन्ती सा ।।

  

While doing the housework, the poetess composes letters of her poetry. While cooking vegetables with her hands, she sings poetry.
