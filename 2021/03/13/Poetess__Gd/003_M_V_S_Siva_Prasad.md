करेण कैलाश कलाप कुशलां

कुचाग्रचुम्बितस्कन्धाभिदायिनीं ।

पदेन परमार्थतत्वप्रबोधिनीं

पति सेवापरां पार्वतीं भजेहम्।।

  

I worship the goddess parvati who by hands cares the kailasha mountain while feeding skandha along with the lecture to the ganesha on paramartha with the service towards the husband lord mahadeva.

  

Sorry for wrongs of any.
