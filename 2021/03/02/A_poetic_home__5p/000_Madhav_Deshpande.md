A poetic home

  

कुटुम्बिन: कवे: सर्वे यदि काव्यं वितन्वते ।

मार्जारा: कुक्कुराश्चापि कुर्यु: काव्यं शुकै: सह ।।

  

If all family members of a poet could compose poetry, the cats and dogs in the house would also compose poetry along with the parrots.
