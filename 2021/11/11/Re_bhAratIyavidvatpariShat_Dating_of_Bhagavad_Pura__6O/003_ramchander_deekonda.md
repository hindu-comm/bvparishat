Respected Scholars,

SrimadbhAvatam has this statement.

  

***कृष्णे स्वधामोपगते धर्मज्ञानाधिभिस्सः !***

***कलौ नष्टदृषामेष पुराणार्कोsधुनोदितः !!***

  

in the light of this sloka, we can conveniently presume that Srimad-Bhagavatam came into circulation at the beginning of the kali yuga.

Dhanyo'smi

Ramchander Deekonda
