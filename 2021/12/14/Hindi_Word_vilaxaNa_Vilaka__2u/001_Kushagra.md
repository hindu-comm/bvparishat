It is due to the multiple meanings of the उपसर्ग “वि”. विलक्षणम् can be explained as one of the following:

  

1\. विशिष्टं लक्षणं यस्य = विशेष-लक्षण-युक्त=remarkable/extraordinary/special

  

2\. विगतं लक्षणं यस्य=लक्षणहीन=devoid of any qualities or features

  

3\. विभिन्नानि लक्षणानि यस्य=विभिन्न-लक्षण-युक्त=possessing varied qualities

  

Hindi speakers use विलक्षण in the first sense. In Sanskrit, all three meanings are found.

  

1\. नमस्ते स्वविलक्षणात्मने=Salutation to you, the one with an extraordinary nature

  

2\. नयनैर्भयादिव विलक्षणैः=through eyes that were lusterless due to fear

  

3\. विलक्षाणि कर्माणि=various duties
