Here is an exampleof the dialect that Modi ji spoke:

  

हम बाबा विश्वनाथ दरबार से देश दुनिया के उन श्रद्धालु जनन के प्रणाम करत है, जो अपने अपने स्थान से इस महा यज्ञ के साक्षी बनत होन । हम आप सब काशी वासी लोगन के प्रणाम करत है जिनके सहयोग से इस शुभ घड़ी आयल ह, हृदय गद्गद ह, मन आह्लादित ह, आप सब लोगन के बहुत बहुत बधाइयाँ ।  

  

This is easy to understand, very close to standard Hindi, but not standard Hindi. In standard Hindi we don't say, for example,करत है,बनत होन, orआयल ह.

  

Modi ji began in standard Hindi, and then briefly spoke in the above dialect, then continued in standard Hindi interspersing his speech in standard Hindi with brief periods of speaking in the dialect.

  

My question is if this is a modern dialect or an old dialect that is no longer spoken. What is the name of this dialect? If it is a modern dialect, is it the present dialect spoken in the Kashi region?
