Dear scholars,

  

IIT-Madras has taken up a large-scale Project on Natural Language Processing.

This will be based on a vast corpus.

  

Scholars knowledgeable in both Sanskrit and English have good opportunities

to contribute to the work which will have an appropriate compensation.

  

The requirement is at multiple levels, roughly three:

  

**Ad-hoc or Part-time Annotators**, from among whom some  

**Full-time Annotators** would be selected. There is also a requirement of **  
**

**Project Coordinators** who will supervise the above two.

  

**Part-time Annotators** need to  

\- record Sanskrit sentences,  

\- record extempore conversations in Sanskrit on given topics,

\- translate simple sentences from Sanskrit etc.

  

**Full-time Annotators** need to

\- ensure the quality of the work above

\- annotate and verify the same

\- do translations, question-answer tasks

\- transcreate etc.

  

**Project Coordinators** need to

\- hire and manage the two above

\- set monthly goals for them  

\- assign tasks to annotators, and

\- ensure timely completion of the task

\- process payrolls for the above two at the end of each month, and

\- ensure the quality of the project.

  

The positions are immediately available,

and the project is to last for about a year or slightly more.

Respond early if interested, and contact me,  

though I am not directly/formally involved in the Project.
