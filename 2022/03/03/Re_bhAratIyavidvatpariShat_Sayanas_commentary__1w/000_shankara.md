It seems the complete Sayana-bhashya of Rigveda is not yet translated. Chaukhamba has published a Hindi translation of Rigveda by Bhishmadatt Sharma in 5 vols in 2013.

<https://chaukhamba.co.in/rgveda-samhita-%E0%A4%8B%E0%A4%97%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B5%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%A6-%E0%A4%B8%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%B9%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BE-buy-book-online-best-price-chaukhamba>

  

There is Peter Peterson's translation of Sayana's preface to Rigveda-bhashya and first two suktas, in addition to a selection of hymns from Rigveda and also C. Kunhan Raja's translation of 'Asya Vamasya' sukta. Links below.

  

<https://archive.org/details/handbooktostudyo01peteuoft/page/n12/mode/1up>

  

<https://archive.org/download/Bibliotheca_Indica_Series/FirstTwoLecturesOfTheRigvedaSamhitaWithSayanabhashyaEnglishTranslation-ERoer1848bis.pdf>

<https://archive.org/details/in.ernet.dli.2015.326152>
