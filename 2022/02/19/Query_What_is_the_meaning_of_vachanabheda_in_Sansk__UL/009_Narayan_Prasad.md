\>*6. vacana*is expression;*vacana-bheda*is "a different type/way of expression".

\>There would have been no*vacana-bheda*if both the compounds were of the same subcategory.

\>

\> .... ....

\>*Vacana-bheda*could perhaps be translated as "Variation in the mode of expression".

\>

  

But Hemacandra appears to have used*vacana-bheda*in the sense of "Variation in the grammatical number" under the sūtra 5.4.86.

Consider the following two sūtras:

कृगोऽव्ययेनाऽनिष्टोक्तौ क्त्वा-णमौ (हैम॰5.4.84)  
स्वाङ्गतश्च्व्यर्थे नाना-विना धार्थेन भुवश्च (हैम॰5.4.86)  
कृग् of हैम॰ = कृञ् of पाणिनि.

णम् of हैम॰ =णमुल् of पाणिनि.  

  

The above two sūtras of हैम॰ have the following parallels in पाणिनि -

अव्ययेऽयथाभिप्रेताख्याने कृञः क्त्वा-णमुलौ (पा॰3.4.59)  
स्वाङ्गे तस्प्रत्यये कृभ्वोः (पा॰4.4.61)  

  

Under the sūtra स्वाङ्गतश्च्व्यर्थे नाना-विना धार्थेन भुवश्च (हैम॰5.4.86), remarks of Hemacandra of his auto-commentaryतत्त्वप्रकाशिका (बृहद्वृत्ति) and of विजयलावण्यसूरि of his शब्दमहार्णवन्यासानुसन्धान are attached here.
