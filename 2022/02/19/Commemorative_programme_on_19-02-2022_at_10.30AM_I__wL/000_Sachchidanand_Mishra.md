Dear Scholars,

  

Padmashri Professor Karl H. Potter, the renowned philosopher, orientalist and Sanskritist passed away on January 11, 2022. Professor Potter contributed immensely in translating and preparing original Sanskrit texts and works as ‘Encyclopedia of Indian Philosophies’ in 25 volumes. His efforts for the sake of preserving and disseminating the philosophical contents of classical Indian Philosophies will be remembered for generations.

  

Indian Council of Philosophical Research (ICPR), in collaboration with Central Sanskrit University, is going to organise a commemorative programme to pay tribute to this great scholar. The programme is scheduled to be organized **online from 10.30 am, on February 19, 2022.**
