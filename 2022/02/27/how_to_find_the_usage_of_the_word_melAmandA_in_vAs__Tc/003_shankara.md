Namaste,

  

The word occurs only in some editions of Vasavadatta. In the Bibliotheca Indica edition of Vasavadatta published in 1859, मेलानन्दा occurs on page 239 - नभः पत्रायते सागरो मेलानन्दायते. Other editions of Vasavadatta have नभः पत्रायते सागरो लोलायते in its place.
