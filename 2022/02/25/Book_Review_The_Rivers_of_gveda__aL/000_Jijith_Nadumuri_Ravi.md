About the Book:-

It geographically analyzes 30 plus rivers mentioned in the Ṛgveda. The book contains 63 maps of the Ṛgveda Geography and has 70 plus figures and charts. Around 89 Rgvedic divinities, 424 Ṛṣis, 213 Rgvedic kings who patronized these Ṛṣis, around 60 Rgvedic meters including Triṣtubh, Gāyatri, Anuṣtubh, and Jagatī. The book has 362 pages, 128 footnotes, 17 tabulations of data. It also provides interesting insights into the chronology of Ṛgvedaby analyzing the expansion of the Bharatas and the other clans of people, across the rivers. The analysis connects the chronology of Ṛgvedawith the current archaeology as of 2021-2022. It also connects the Ṛgvedic events with the events pertaining to the two Itihasas:- Ramayana and the Mahabharata.  
  
The first few chapters serve as an introduction to the readers who are not familiar with Ṛgveda.
