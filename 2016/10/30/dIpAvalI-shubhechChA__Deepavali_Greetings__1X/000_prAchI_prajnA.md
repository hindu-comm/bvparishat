**दीपावली-शुभेच्छा**

Deepavali Greetings

 दीपानां स्नेहधाराप्रगुणितमहसां सन्ततिः सन्तनोतु

 द्वेषध्वान्तं तमिस्रं निगिलतु सततं दीपदीप्तिः प्रकामम् ।

            भावानां भव्यतायां मधुमधुमधुराणां माधुरी वृद्धिमेतु

             दीपावल्युत्सवोऽयं वितरतु भवते ऋद्धिसिद्धिप्रकर्षान् ॥{dīpānāṃ snehadhārāpraguṇitamahasāṃ santatiḥ santanotu,dveṣadhvāntaṃ tamisraṃ nigilatu satataṃ dīpadīptiḥ prakāmam.bhāvānāṃ bhavyatāyāṃ madhumadhumadhurāṇāṃ mādhurī vṛddhimetu,dīpāvalyutsavo'yaṃ vitaratu bhavate ṛddhisiddhiprakarṣān ॥}

 May the collection of the lights, having the luster overblown by oil of love and affection, proliferate. May the glow of the candles swallow entirely, the darkness of hostility. May the sweetness of sweets upsurge in the magnificence of the sentiments and emotions. May this Deepavali, the festive of lights, bestow on you the glory of success and opulence.

 आराष्ट्रं भारतीयाभिजनपदकृतां वस्तुनां पण्यमस्तु

 आतङ्कानां विनाशेऽखिलजनमनसामैक्यभावः विभातु।

 गैर्वाण्यां वाचि लोके विलसतु नितरां प्राच्यविद्यासु प्रीतिः

 दीपावल्युत्सवेऽस्मिन् भवतु च भवतां संघसंकल्पशक्तिः॥

{ārāṣṭraṃ bhāratīyābhijanapadakṛtāṃ vastunāṃ paṇyamastu,ātaṅkānāṃ vināśe' khilajanamanasāmaikyabhāvaḥ vibhātu.gairvāṇyāṃ vāci loke vilasatu nitarāṃ prācyavidyāsu prītiḥ,dīpāvalyutsave'smin bhavatu ca bhavatāṃ saṃghasaṃkalpaśaktiḥ॥}

 May there be the trade of Indigenous articles throughout the country, India. May there be unity to destroy the militancy. May there be the devotion to Sanskrit language and eastern knowledge in the country. May this be your determination as a whole in this auspicious festive of Deepavali. 

With Best Regards



**Dr. Sugyan Kumar Mahanty**
