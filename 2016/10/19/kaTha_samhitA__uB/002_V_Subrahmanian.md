Thanks to Sri Madhav Deshpande's help, the above cited passage could be traced to the

p.174 of the pdf of the Kāṭhaka samhitā 12.12:

  

The passage in the shruti is:‘तस्माद्ब्राह्मणः सुरां न पिबति’.

  

It appears that Shankara has done अर्थतः अनुवादः of the passage and not शब्दतः अनुवादः .

  

The index at the end of the pdf was useful in locating the passage.
