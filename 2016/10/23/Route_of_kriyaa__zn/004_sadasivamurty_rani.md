**॥अथ श्री देव्याः कवचम्॥**

ॐ अस्य श्रीचण्डीकवचस्य ब्रह्मा ऋषिः, अनुष्टुप् छन्दः,  
चामुण्डा देवता, अङ्गन्यासोक्तमातरो बीजम्, दिग्बन्धदेवतास्तत्त्वम्,  
श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीत्यर्थे सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोगः

**॥अथार्गलास्तोत्रम्॥**

ॐ अस्य श्रीअर्गलास्तोत्रमन्त्रस्य विष्णुर्ऋषिः, अनुष्टुप् छन्दः,  
श्रीमहालक्ष्मीर्देवता, श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीतये सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोगः॥

**॥अथ कीलकम्॥**

ॐ अस्य श्रीकीलकमन्त्रस्य शिव ऋषिः, अनुष्टुप् छन्दः,  
श्रीमहासरस्वती देवता, श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीत्यर्थं सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोगः।

  

Dear Sri Kamalesh Pathak JI,

Pranams. As mentioned in the above source in the Kavachaश्रीजगदम्बाप्रीत्यर्थे सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोगः

is used.

  

In the Argala Stotraश्रीजगदम्बाप्रीतये सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोग: is used.

  

In the Kilakamश्रीजगदम्बाप्रीत्यर्थं सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोगः is the phrase found.

  

As it has been clarified by Prof. Korada Ji and Prof. Sunil Ji there is no difference in the meaning and thought. The difference is only in coinging the word phrases: श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीत्यर्थे, श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीतये and श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीत्यर्थं \|  The sadhaka takes this Sankalpa to do JAPA of Chandi Saptasati to please Jagadamba (As the main purpose of JAPA). Ofcourse there can be many contextual purposes to be fulfilled through this JAPA process which shall be resulted only from the favour of the pleased Jagadamba.

  

Here there is a little more to be understood by the word JAPA. Usually in case of other Mantras (which are small in size having limited number of Syllables) JAPA means the recitation of any particular (chosen) Mantra for a required number of times.

  

But in case of Lalitha Sahasra Nama Stotram or Chandi /(Devi) Saptasati one time recitation or Parayanam of the entire text is considered as JAPA. In this lenghty process the variation of phrases in the Sankalpa doesn't matter. Only pleasing the Goddess Jagadamba final objective intended in mind.

  

Hope this will be of some considerable help in this context.

Pranams,

**Prof. Rani Sadasiva Murty**
