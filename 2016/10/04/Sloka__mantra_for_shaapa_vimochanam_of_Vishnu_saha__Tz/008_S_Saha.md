There is another rudra-zApa-vimocana mantra. It is attributed to the agastya samhitA,a pAJcarAtra text.  
  
This is the text of the nyAsa  
  
OM zrigaNezAya namaH. OM asya zrIvizNoH sahasranAmnAM rudrazApavimocana mantrasya. mahadeva RsiH. anuSTup chandaH. zrIrudrAnugrahazaktirdevatA. surezaH zaraNaM zarmeti bIjaM. anantaputabhugbhokteti zaktiH. zurezvarAyeti kIlakaM. rudrazApavimocane viniyogaH.  
  
karanyAsa:  
  
OM klIM hrAM aGguSThAbhyAM namaH. OM klIM hrIM tarjanibhyAM svAhA. OM klIM hruM madhyamAbhyAM vaSaT. OM klIM hraiM anAmikAbhyAM hrum. OM klIM hrauM kaNiSThAbhyAM vauSaT. OM klIM hraH karatalapRSThAya astrAya phaT. iti karanyAsaH.  
  
SaDaGganyAsa  
  
OM klIM hrAM hRdayAya namaH. OM klIM hrIM zirase svAhA. OM klIM hrUM zikhAyai vaSaT. OM klIM hraiM kavacAya huM. OM klIM hrauM netrAbhyAM vauSaT. OM klIM hraH astrAya phaT.  
  
dhyAnam  
  
Take a flower in your hand,and meditate thus:  
  
OM tamAla-zyAmala-tanuM pItakauzeyavAsasaM.  
varNamUrtimayaM devaM dhyAyennArAyaNaM vibhuM..  
  
japa  
  
10 or 108 times  
  
OM klIM hrAM hrIM hrUM hraiM hrauM hraH svAhA  
  
After that  
  
zrIviSNoH sahasranAmastava rudrazApavimukto bhava..  
  
  
iti agastyasaMhitAyAM zrIviSNoH sahasranAmnAM rudrazApavimocana vidhi.  
  
Here ends the zApa-vimocana-vidhi.
