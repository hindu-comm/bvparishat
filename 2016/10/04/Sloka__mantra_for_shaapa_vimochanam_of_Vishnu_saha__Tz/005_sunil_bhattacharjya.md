It is true that Vishnu has been cursed by many including Gandhari. Vedavyasa says in the Mahabharata that Lord Krishna could have annulled the curse but allowed it to fructify. He used that to help his objective, for which he prayed to Lord Shiva and Shiva granted him a son Shamba, who by his actionbrought the end of the Yadus.  
  

I feel that the action of cursing the Vishnu sahasranama may not be the same as cursing Vishnu.
