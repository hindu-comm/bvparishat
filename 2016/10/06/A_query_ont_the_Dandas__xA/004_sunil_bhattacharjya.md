Dear Shri Sathyanarayana Sarmaji and Shri Rani Sadasiva Murtyji,  
  

Thank you very much for the details.

Secondly, is there any specification as to whether the danda is ekadanda or a composite of tridanda. If there is any such thing, what is the significance of variation in number?
