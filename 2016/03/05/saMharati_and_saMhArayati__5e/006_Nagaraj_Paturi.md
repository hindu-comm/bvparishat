In Kumarasambhava, immediately after गणान्व्यनैषीत् of 3.41, we get one of the most celebrated word-videos of Kalidasa, i.e., 3.42.



लतागृहद्वारगतो ऽथ नन्दी वामप्रकोष्ठार्पितहेमवेत्रः

मुखार्पितैकाङ्गुलिसंज्ञयैव मा चापलायेति गणान्व्यनैषीत् 3.41





निष्कम्पवृक्षं निभृतद्विरेफं मूकाण्डजं शान्तमृगप्रचारम्

तच्छासनात्काननमेव सर्वं चित्रार्पितारम्भमिवावतस्थे 3.42



It appears as though the "Silence!!" command to Ganas made vrikshas, dvirephas, anDajas, mrigas, frozen into silence and stillness.

There is तच्छासनात् also.



Here, the gaNas do not seem to be the 'destroying' (devolving) agents.



Butin many other descriptions, saprophytes (pis'aachas), scavenger-organisms etc. are mentioned as members of the army of S'iva.
