\> Why Draupadi was also called Yagyaseni?  
\>

Because she was the daughter of यज्ञसेन (=द्रुपद , Drupada, q.v.--Do.^2 = Kṛshṇa: XII, Bhā. X. 57. 10. \[1\]), she was called याज्ञसेनी and not यग्यसेनी.
