[[Kalicharan Tuvij	2016-03-04, 22:44:57 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/z2xT6XUlOBE)]]



शि = to lead (to)/ enter  
क्ष = action  
  
शिक्षा = that which leads to action = "enabler"  
  
I assume this method of etymology, since there is no derivation as such within Paniniya, is certain natural principle of Sanskrit, also given the name वर्णवाद (by shri N.R. Joshi, if I am not wrong).
