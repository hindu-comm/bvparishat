भो श्रीमन्तः, bhavatām sāhāyyenādya mama sutasyānnaprāśanam susampannam. tenāham dhruvamupakṛtaḥ.

  

<https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/kalpa-prayoga> iti janānām aviduṣāmapi kalpa-jijñāsāpūrtyai sṛṣṭam. (adya yāvattu mukhapustakasambhāṣaṇeṣveva \[facebook\] jñānasādhane prayatamānā āsma.) bhavanto jyeṣṭā mārgadarśakās tatrāgatya saṃyujya sahakurvantu. kalpanāmno vedāngasya puṇyāham bhavanto bruvantu!
