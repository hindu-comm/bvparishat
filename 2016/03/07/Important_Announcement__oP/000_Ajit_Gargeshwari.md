**Rules for Posting**

Please read the rules completely before posting. "नामूलं लिख्यते किंचित् नानपेक्षितमुच्यते।"Nothing is written out of focus and whatever is written is not without any authentic source.

**Encouraged forms of communication:**

****

Discussions should be in Sanskrit or English. Please use spell check and frame clear sentences before posting. Only accredited scholars Should start a new Thread (Post with a Subject Line) or post replies. Accredited Scholars can start discussions, ask questions, answer questions. Other members posts will be moderated.  

Members can post announcements of publications, meetings, conferences or other forms of events characteristic of academia including job announcements. Announcements of book publications as well as of new journal issues on the part of their authors or editors are welcome.

Your profile picture should be your photo or it should be blank. Post using you real names. Posting with Nick names is not allowed.

You can include job title (Designation) and place of work. Disclaimers after your signature are not allowed.

Post if you feel your post will clarify, expand, challenge, or forward your views or doubts. Don’t post queries without searching the web.  

Refer to group archives before posting a question. This will ensure that your questions are not already answered.

Do not Post without a subject line. Use the subject line to indicate the subject of your post.

Quote directly relevant portions of your previous posts if required. Always keep in mind the subject of the thread.

When you need to know about a book, search online libraries such as DLI (<http://dli.gov.in/>) or archives ( [www.archives.org](http://www.archives.org)) etc. Many books are available on online libraries for a free download.

**Discouraged forms of communications**

****

Members will be expected to maintain commonly-accepted standards of decorum in their postings to the group. Contributions are expected to be polite and well-considered. Members who violate these standards will be warned and may have their membership privileges suspended or revoked. In particular, personal attacks of an ad-hominem nature, rude language, and off-topic postings are violations of the list rules.

Cross Posting not allowed. Threads from other forums are normally not discussed in BVP. Threads should originate at BVP.
