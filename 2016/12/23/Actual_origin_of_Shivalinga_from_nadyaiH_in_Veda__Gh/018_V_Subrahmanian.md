We have in the tradition from time immemorial the practice of 'anga pūjā' during all vrata-s of all deities, male and female. As part of this anga puja every one of the body-parts of that deity is worshiped by naming those parts. So, 'मेढ्रं पूजयामि’ is a common component in all such puja-s.
