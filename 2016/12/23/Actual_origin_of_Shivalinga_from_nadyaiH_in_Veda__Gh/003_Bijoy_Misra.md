Extending Prof Paturi's post, I should add that various tribes in southern and western Orissa do consider areas and

forests auspicious because of their productivity and location of special herbs. There was a big agitation against the

Vedanta group of companies to restrain from developing Niyamgiri mountains in Koraput district which were considered

the abode of the Creator.
