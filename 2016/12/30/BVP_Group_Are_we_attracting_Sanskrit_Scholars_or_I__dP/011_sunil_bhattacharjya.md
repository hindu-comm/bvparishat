Dear Sir,  
  

I think nobody, worthy of being called a scholar, casts aspersion on another scholar by a general sarcastic remark, without substantiation. I request Sri Lalitaalitahji to voice his objections to my mail, rather than making such general assertions. Purana (fifth veda text) clearly states that itihasa is required for dhharma. In fact the Mahabharata also says in the same vein that one should read Purana and itihasas to understand the Veda,  
  

Regards,  

Sunil KB
