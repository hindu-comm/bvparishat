With reference to my comments above I have located the source. Sri Govindaraja, the commentator has this to say at the beginning of 59th sarga, Kishkindhakanda. "ततस्तदमृतास्वादमित्यारभ्य पञ्च सर्गाः प्रक्षिप्ताः। सर्वकोषेष्वदर्शनात् तान् विनापि कथासङ्घट्टनात् पूर्वोक्तार्थविरोधस्फारकत्वाच्च । तथापि तॆ व्याख्यास्यन्ते।". Then at the beginning of 64th sarga he says, "एवं प्रक्षिप्ताः पञ्चसर्गा गताः।".The passage under discussion occurs in 61st sarga. I refer to Valmiki Ramayan published by Gangavishnu Srikrishnadas, Kalyan, Mumbai.
