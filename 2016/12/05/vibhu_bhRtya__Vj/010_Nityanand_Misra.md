Yes, this is also one of the earliest meanings attested. In Nirukta (2.19), Yaska explains विभ्वा in RV 1.113.1 इदं श्रेष्ठं ज्योतिषां ज्योतिरागाच्चित्रः प्रकेतो अजनिष्ट विभ्वा as विभूततमम्, which Durgacharya further explains asविस्तीर्णतमम् (=omnipresent).
