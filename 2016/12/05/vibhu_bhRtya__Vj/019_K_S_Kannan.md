***saptaṛṣayaḥ pratihitāś śarīre...***

occurs already in *Vājasaneyi Saṁhitā* 34.55.

***  
***

***tiryag-bilaś camasa ūrdhva-budhnaḥ...***

occurs already in *Atharva Veda* 10.8.9.

  

***arvāg-bilaś camasa 'urdhva-budhnaḥ...***

occurs in *Śatapatha*-*Brāhmaṇa* 14.5.2.4

& in *Bṛhadāraṇyakopaniṣad* 2.2.4.5
