**Kuvalayänanda: **Entry nos. 287-303. Appayyadrksita (ca. 1520 1593) wrote this

commentary on Jayadeva's *Candräbka,* identifying 15 additional alamkäras and earning

a place as the standard work on the topic, especially in south India. See also

his *Citrarmmämsä* and *Vrttivärtika,* q.v.
