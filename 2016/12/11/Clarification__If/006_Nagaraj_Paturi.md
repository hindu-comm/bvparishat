Dear Sri Raviji,

  

In atha yōgānuśāsanam , the word means advice, instruction, teaching.

  

śās is the root. anu- is the prefix.

  

anu- + śās \> anuśās

  

śās has several meanings such as to instruct, to order, to control,to rule, to discipline etc.

  

anuśās is used more often in the sense of instructing, teaching and advising.

  

Similar use is śabdānuśāsanam. Grammar is called śabdānuśāsanam.

  

It is in Hindi that the word anuśāsan is used in the sense of discipline.
