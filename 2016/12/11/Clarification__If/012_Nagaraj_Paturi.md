Though I failed to understand your latest post, let me try to focus on Atha which you seem to be focussing on.

  

Atha is a Sanskrit expression used in the beginning of an exposition.

  

Ancient scholars have discussed this expression in a great detail. 

  

In essence, atha is used in the sense of "and now, " and means "whatever follows is ...."

  

Thus atha yogaanushaasanam means "and now follows instruction/advice on/of Yoga" or "What follows here-onwards is

  

instruction/advice on/of Yoga"
