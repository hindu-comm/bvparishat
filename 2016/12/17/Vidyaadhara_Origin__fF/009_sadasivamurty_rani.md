Here is the first sloka of Sivapaadaadikeshanta Stotra said to have been written by Adi Sankaracharya in which we have a reference to Vidyadharas as the popular clan of singers.

कल्याणं वो विधतत्तां कटकतटलसत्कल्पवाटीनिकुञ्ज-

क्रीडासंसक्त**विद्याधरनिवहवधूगीत**रुद्रावतार:।

तारैर्हेरम्बमादैस्तरळितनिनदत्तारकारातिकॆकी

कैलासस्शर्वनिर्वृत्त्यभिकनकपदस्सर्वदा पर्वतेन्द्र:॥

There are several slokas of this kind in our tradition even taken from puranas where a frequent reference to Vidyadharas is available.
