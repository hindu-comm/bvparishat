dear vidvaj-janas,  
  
i'm planning to start some threads on how to sing specific meters. Vidvāns can contribute their recordings of how they sing these meters so that we all can get to know different styles of singing the same meter in a single thread.  
  
The meter i am posting today is Sragdharā. The verse that i've sung is as follows:  
  
वन्देऽहं श्रीगुरोः श्रीयुतपदकमलं श्रीगुरुन् वैष्णवांश्च ।  
श्रीरुपं साग्रजातं सहगणरघुनाथान्वितं तं सजीवम् ॥  
साद्वैतं सावधूतं परिजनसहितं कृष्णचैतन्यदेवं ।  
श्रीराधाकृष्णपादान् सहगणललिताश्रीविशाखान्वितांश्च ॥  
  
**Link for recitation**: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jz_fHIaGBH8>  
  
Interested vidvāns kindly suggest your own styles of singing this meter by posting a recording here or on youtube. Please feel free to select any verse of your choice in the sragdharā meter.
