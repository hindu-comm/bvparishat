स्वाम् च पात्नीम् अभिप्रेताम् ताराम् च अपि समीप्सिताम् \|  
विहरंतम् अहो रात्रम् कृतार्थम् विगत ज्वरम् \|\| ४-२९-४

  

*abhipretaam* = one who is choicest; *svaam paatniim abhipretaam ca* = his own, wife; and; *samiipsitaam taaraam ca api* = highly coveted, Tara, also even; *praaptavantam* = one who gained; *ahaH raatram viharantam* = day, night \[day in and day out,\] merrymaking; *kR^ita artham* = one who is making much of, his fortune; *vigata jvaram* = gone, fever - getting rid of other problems;
