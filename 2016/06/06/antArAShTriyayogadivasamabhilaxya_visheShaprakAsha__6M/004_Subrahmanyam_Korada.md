Dear आचार्य

  

you are right - this term is imported from Hindi .

  

In Telugu etc we use राष्ट्र for a part of a country - state , whereas in Hindi they use the same word for country - राष्ट्रपतिः ।

  

अथ राष्ट्रो ’ स्त्री विषये स्यादुपद्रवे -- अमरः।

  

विषयो देशे (पा 4-2-52) - अणादयः ।

  

But outside they use देश for country . There is no व्यवस्था in this regard.
