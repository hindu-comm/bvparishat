नमोनमः विद्वद्‍भ्यः।

Kindly explain the the concepts of रूपसमृद्धि and रूपसम्पद्‌ (or रूपसम्पत्ति). Is there any difference between the two? In which context are these words used?

Monier Williams: रूपसमृद्धि = Perfection of form, suitable form. रूपसम्पद्‌ (or रूपसम्पत्ति) = Perfection or excellence of form, beauty.

Apte: रूपसम्पद्‌ (or रूपसम्पत्ति) = Perfection or excellence of form, richness of beauty, superb beauty.
