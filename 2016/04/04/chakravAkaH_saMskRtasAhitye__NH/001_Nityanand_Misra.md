A useful reference may be *Birds in Sanskrit Literature* (2005 revised edition, MLBD, ISBN 81-208-1842-3)byJ N Dave. Dave identifies the bird as*Tadorna ferruginea* (also known asRuddy sheldrake or Brahminy duck). Please refer pages 450 to 453 of his work where he cites mention of the bird in the Rgveda, Atharvaveda, Sukla Yajurveda (Vajasaneyi Samhita), Kumarasambhava, Nalacampu, Matsya Purana, Ramayana, Markandeya Purana, etc
