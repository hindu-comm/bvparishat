Dear Scholars,

The following two Sanskrit verses appear as benedictory verses of two Malayalam commentaries on the celebrated mathematical work Lilavati.

The language and script (of course Malayalam) employed in the manuscripts suggest that these commentaries are not later than the 1850 CE. The authors of these are not known.

I earnestly seek the help of learned members to know whether the very same verses appear in any of the literary/commentarial/sastric works in Sanskrit. :

  

१. अभिप्रेतार्थसिद्ध्यर्थं पूजितो यस्सुरैरपि।

सर्वविघ्नच्छिदे तस्मै गणाधिपतये नमः।।

  

२. योगाश्रयं पापवियोगहेतुकं

गुणालयं हारमशेषदुष्कृतामताम्।

कृतीश्वरं वेदविदां परं पदं

घनत्विषं गोपकुमारमाश्रये।।
