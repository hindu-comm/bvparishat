Both pagemaker and indesign don't support unicode. The best option is Latex (miktex in windows). It's freely downloadable.  

The other options are - MS-Word, MS-Publisher, Openoffice Writer.  

This is for publishing book.  
  

To create database MS-Excel, MS-Access, Openoffice Calc, Openoffice Base, SQL, PHP etc. may be used. All of them support unicode.
