Dear Dr. Joshi,  
  

Ganga is called Jahnavi, as daughter of Jahnu muni as Jahnu muni drank the river and then allowed her to come out from him, so the anecdote goes. So the correct usage is Jahnu.  
  

Prahlaada is the commonly used spelling and not Pralhaada.  
  

To my knowledge, in the word Nr'sinha, deergha r'ii is not used.
