How do you write and differentiate in pronunciation संघः - सङ्घः, संचयः - सत्र्चयः, संधिः - सन्धिः, संबन्धः - सम्बन्धः using roman script?

It is same अनुस्वारः in हंसः, संशयः संवत्सरः. It is different from the anunasika, in the listed words and the same is used गङ्गा, पञ्च, षण्ढः,
स्कन्धः, अम्बा etc. used as अनुनासिक in the final nasal consonant in the five rows. Before the consonants श, ष, स the अनुस्वार is assimilated to nasal consonants respectively.
