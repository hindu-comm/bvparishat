Namaste,  
  
I have subscribed the Sanskrit edition of the magazine and the amt. is Rs. 60/- per annum (annual subscription) which is extremely low and affordable even for a common man. Now-a-days, Rs.60/- amounts to nothing. Let us encourage this so that amt. would go to the TTD exchequer whose noble activities are laudable rather than suggesting free downloads.  
  
harayE namaH.
