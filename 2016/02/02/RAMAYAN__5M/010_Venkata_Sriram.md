मान्डव्याः पुष्करो ज्येष्ठः कनिष्ठस्तक्षनामकः - Mandavi's (ie., Bharata's) eldest son was "Pushkara" and younger was "Taksha" ;

  

श्रुतकीर्त्याः सुबाहुश्च यूपकेतुः परः स्मृतः - Shrutakirti's (ie., Shatrughna's) eldest son was "Subahu" and younger was "Yupaketu".
