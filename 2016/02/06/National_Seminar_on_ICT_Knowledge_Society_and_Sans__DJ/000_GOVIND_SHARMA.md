A two days National Seminar on 'ICT, Knowledge Society and Sanskrit' will be organised by the department of Sanskrit and the department of Mass Communication, Assam University at Silchar Campus that is sponsored by Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan, New Delhi. It is scheduled to be held on 11-12 March, 2016.  
Experts, Researchers from related fields are requested to contribute in Seminar.

Sub-themes  
• Sanskrit and modern knowledge society  
• New Media and Sanskrit: aptness and viability  
• ICT and oriental knowledge streams  
• Preservation and modernization of Sanskrit and the ICT  
• ICT for Sanskrit, Sanskrit for ICT  
• Digitization, e-learning and Sanskrit
