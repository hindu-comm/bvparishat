The verse



***uttaram yad samudrasya himAdrescaiva dakSiNam***

***varSam tad bhAratam nAma bhAratii yatra santatiH***



cited by Vidwan Sri Ramchander Deekondaji is from Vishnu Purana



The composition dates of Vishnu Purana mentioned by the various scholars include:

H. H. Wilson: first century BCE; one of the oldest of the Puranas

Hem Chandra Raychaudhuri: 320-255 CE

Rajendra Chandra Hazra: 275-325 CE

[Moriz Winternitz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moriz_Winternitz "Moriz Winternitz"): before 400 CE

Gavin Flood: fourth century CE
