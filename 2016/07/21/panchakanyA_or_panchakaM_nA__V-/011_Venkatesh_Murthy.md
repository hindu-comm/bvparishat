Apte Sanskrit Dictionary -

  

meanings of "kanyaa"

f.{a-stem}

1.an unmarried girl or daughter;

2.a girl ten years old;

3.a virgin;

4.a woman in general;

5.the sixth sign of the zodiac;

[6.name](http://6.name) of durgaa;

7.large cardamoms

  

No. 4 may be the meaning in the Pancha Kanyaa Sloka.

  
Or It may be Married lady following Pati Vrata Dharma is as good as Kanyaa just like married man following Dharma of Patnigamana in her Ritu Kaala is as good as Brahmachari according to Dharma Sastra.
