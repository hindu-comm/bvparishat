> > > > **  The main academic event on the first day was the Seminar in > > which three authorities onकूडियाट्टम, the traditional Sanskrit > > theatre, presented research papers dealing with the > > cultural,historical and technical aspects of the art form. The > > nuances of the performance were also seriously debated. Prof. C. > > Rajendran (Retd. from University of Calicut), Prof. K. V. Vasudevan > > (a reputed scholar in Sanskrit and a specialist onकूडियाट्टम) and > > Prof. M. V. Narayanan were the Resource persons.** > > > >

> > > >  
> > > > 
> > > >

Usha Nangyaar (उषा नंगियार ), > > a****कूडियाट्टमperformer of International repute.She talked on > > the women representation in theकूडियाट्टमstage. She opined that > > the Nangyaar Koothu, an offshoot ofकूडियाट्टम****, is the Unique > > theatre of the present day where women get exclusive prominence and > > complete independence**. Following her lecture, there was a Lecture > > demonstration by Aparnaa Nangiyaar (अपर्णा नंगियार) another lady > > performer and a researcher in the field. > > > >
