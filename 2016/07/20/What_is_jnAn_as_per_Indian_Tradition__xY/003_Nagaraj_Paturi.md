[[Nagaraj Paturi	2016-07-20, 11:41:51 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/xY1Y-wdPeSo)]]



ज्ञानम् is of two types : Knowledge in the form of information, knowledge in the form of experience. 'Knowledge about' is information. For example, knowledge *about* sweetness is knowledge of information type. *Experiencing* sweetness is knowledge of experience type.

  

In thepaaramaarthika saadhana world, knowledge of experience type isconsidered to be true ज्ञानम्.For example, experiencing ब्रह्मन्
is considered to be ब्रह्मज्ञानम् and *not* knowing *about* ब्रह्मन्.
