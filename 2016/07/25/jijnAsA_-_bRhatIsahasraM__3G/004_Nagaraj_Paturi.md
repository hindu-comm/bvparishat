[[Nagaraj Paturi	2016-07-25, 20:35:46 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/3GCewwUMOw8)]]



Since Aitareyopanishad forms the 4th, 5th and 6th Adhyayas of the dviteeya aaraNyaka of the Aitareya braahmaNa and since the preceding
2.2.3 to 2.3.8. are focussed on the pras'amsaa of brihateesahasra , Sankara says,

  

"कर्म चबृहतीसहस्रलक्षणं प्रस्तुत्य अनंतरमेव आत्मज्ञानं प्रारभ्यते। तस्मात्कर्म एव अधिक्रियते। "
