Seems Dr. Bhat copy-pasted the quote from one of the online sources. In addition, it should be मा भूत् and not माऽभूत् due to न माङ्योगे (पा॰सू॰ ६.४.७४), just like मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूः (गीता २.४७).

  

The mistake is rampant: a Google search for "माऽभूत्सकलं" gives 54 results, while that for "मा भूत्सकलं" gives only four.

  

I too have been a victim of copy-pasting the same quotation: the माऽभूत् mistake exists in the third edition ofअध्यात्मरामायणेऽपाणिनीयप्रयोगाणां विमर्शः.
