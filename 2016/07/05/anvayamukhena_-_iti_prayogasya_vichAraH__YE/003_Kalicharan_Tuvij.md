नमस्ते ।  
  
तृतीया doesn't equate with instrumental. A better translation IMHO is: "active constraint".  
  
बाणेन हत: means "killed by arrow" and not by a club, or by something else.  
The constrain is effective not merely on the verb of the sentence; it affects all the words.
