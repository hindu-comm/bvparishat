Prof. Unithiri is an eminent Sanskrit scholar,writing both in English and Malayalam (profusely in Malayalam). He has written many valuable works(Studies, Editions and Translations of significant works in Sanskrit). He retired as Professor of Sanskrit form University of Calicut in the year 2006. At present he is translating Rgveda into Malayalam. Other significant works authored recently by him include the translations (Malayalam) of 1) Six systems of Indian Philosophy, 2) Upanishads, and 3) Studies on selected hymns of Rgveda, all originally written by Max Muller.
