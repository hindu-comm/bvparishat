The University invites online applications for admission to the adcademic programme in PGDMP (1 Year Post Graduate Diploma in Manuscriptology and Palaeography) under the Faculty of Arts in the session 2016-2017. Admission Procedure: Admission to the Post Graduate Diploma in Manuscriptology and Palaeography will be taken according to the marks obtained in Master’s degree.
