\> I thought about my own question raised above and realised that Rishi Valmiki's use of Panchami is deliberate and not accidental.  
\>  
\> The Rishi did not create the story of Lord Rama, as a novelist creates a story. The story simply flowed from the Rishi to humanity and, therefore, Panchami is the most suitable vibhakti. He was a medium and not the creator. Hence, Tritiyaa would be inappropriate.
