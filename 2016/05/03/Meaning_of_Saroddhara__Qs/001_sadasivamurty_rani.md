Sara means 'gist' or 'essence' ;' uddhaara' means extraction

  

So saroddhaara can be - "the essence extracted" ;

In case of any text the "gist extracted'' of any particular text ,

Ramayana saroddhaara also is a book available where the essence of the Ramayana is explained of course in detail.
