This is not correct.A सकर्मक verb can be अकर्मक due to प्रसिद्धि or अविवक्षा of कर्म (VP 3.7.88) but not the other way round.A verb like जीवति or म्रियते or शेते cannot be सकर्मक. Please note the कारिका

  

लज्जासत्तास्थितिजागरणं वृद्धिक्षयभयजीवितमरणम्।

आत्मानम् तिष्ठति is wrong usage. It should be आत्मनि तिष्ठति (अङ्गुष्ठमात्रः पुरुषो मध्य आत्मनि तिष्ठति in Kathopanisad, कटे आस्ते in grammar commentaries are standard usages). Yes, आत्मानम् अधितिष्ठति is correct (due to अधिशीङ्स्थासां कर्म). Can you give an example of such a usage in a standard work.

  

I am amazed you can say things like “स: (तम्) आत्मानम् तिष्ठति uses nothing more than the basic कारक-structure based on naturalistic principles” without citing any authority or explaining what you mean. Either you do not understand what you are talking about, or you are building castles in the air. Please read and understand कारक प्रकरण in Sanskrit grammar works before making such bold and fanciful claims.
