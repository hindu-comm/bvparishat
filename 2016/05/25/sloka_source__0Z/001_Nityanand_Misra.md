प्रत्यक्षेणानुमित्या वा यस्तूपायो न बुध्यते।

एनं विदन्ति वेदेन तस्माद्वेदस्य वेदता॥

  

is cited by Sāyāṇa in the *bhūmikā* of his commentary on the *Aitareya Brāhmaṇa*. Original source is not known to me.

  

The image is from the Anandashrama Sanskrit Series number 32 edition of the AB, published 1896.
