The difference exists in printed versions also.

  

The 2007 edition with an English translation by Gita Press (38th reprint, ISBN 81-293-0437-6) has the reading ‘parantapa’ which is explained as ‘O King’.

  

Older Nirnay Sagar Press editions with 8 and 11 commentaries have the reading ‘parantapaḥ’.
