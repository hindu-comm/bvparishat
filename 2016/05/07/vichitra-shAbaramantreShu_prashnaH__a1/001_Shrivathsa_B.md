hariH OM,

 You will find many 'shaabara mantras'. There are Muslim 'shaabara mantras' also! In any good bookstore you will find a few compilations of the 'shaabara mantras'.

 There is an ullekha of shaabara mantra in rAmacaritamAnasa. This may be the reason for many incantations (even muslim ones) vying for the honour.

svasti,  
 JAYA BHAVAANII BHAARATII,  
 shrivathsa.
