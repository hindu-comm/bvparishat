Chinmaya International Foundation (CIF) in Kerala and the Karnataka Sanskrit University (KSU) are jointly organizing a 10 day international Summer School this year for university students and scholars on ‘Kalāyoga- The heart of Indian art’ from July 2-11, 2016 in Kerala, India.

  

Shatavadhani Dr. R Ganesh, a renowned poet and scholar and Prof Shrinivasa Varakhedi, (Dean), Karnataka Sanskrit University will lead the School as Course Directors and many eminent scholars will be delivering lectures and conduct intensive discussions on different aspects of Indian art – classical poetry, dance, music, sculpture and painting. The School will also provide highlights on the classical Greek & Latin literature and arts, along with those of the Indian vernaculars.

  

Please let us know if you would be interested and also share this with your department, friends, colleagues, and others.Scholarships and grants are available for selected students on a case-by-case basis.
