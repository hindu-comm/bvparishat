***This article will, hopefully, convince you that promotion of Sanskrit is much easier than we often imagine.***

Two of world's ancient classical languages ---- Sanskrit and Tamil ----- flourished in India. Both languages possess writings with great literary, philosophical, historical and scientific value. These languages also connect us to our cultural roots and thus enshrine the spirit of our national ethos. While Tamil has maintained steady growth, Sanskrit is having to struggle for survival. And what is responsible for this sorry state of the language is a minor historical quirk that can be set right. This article explores the issue in detail.
