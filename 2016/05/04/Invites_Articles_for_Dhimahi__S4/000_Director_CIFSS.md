**CHINMAYA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION**

**SHODHA SANSTHAN**

**INVITES ARTICLES FOR**

**Dhimahi Volume 8**

**For the year 2016-17**

Invites innovative original contributions in Sanskrit, Hindi and English in the fields of Sanskrit language and linguistics, Sanskrit literature, Veda-Vedāṅgas, Ancient Indian Scientific literature having relevance in the modern context including Āyurveda, Mathematics and indigenous medical practices and knowledge systems, commentaries and book-reviews, creative writings in Sanskrit and Indology, comparative and inter/multi-disciplinary research findings wherein all the above areas and similar western contributions are compared/critically studied/edited etc, and other ancient Indian art and architecture and their comparison with forms of world art and architecture.

Original manuscript in duplicate, typewritten only on one side of A4 size paper, double-spaced with wide margins shall be submitted along with a soft copy in a CD or through E-mail in MS Word or InDesign format. The Unicode FONT of Devanagari script used shall be included in the CD or sent through E-mail.

The first page of the peer-reviewed articles shall contain the title of the paper, the authors’ name, designation and address. The second page must contain the title, an abstract of not more than 100 words. Manuscript pages should be serially numbered. The text must have added with diacritical marks wherever required. All references should be cited in the text either in Arabic numerals in superscript. All references should be written at the end of the manuscript/text.

The last date of receipt the article before 15th June 2016.
