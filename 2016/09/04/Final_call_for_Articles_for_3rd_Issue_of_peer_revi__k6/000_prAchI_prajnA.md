महोदयाः !

 “प्राची प्रज्ञा” (ISSN 2348 – 8417) इति अन्ताराष्ट्रियान्तर्जालपत्रिकायां “शास्त्रमञ्जूषा” इति षाण्मासिकखण्डस्य तृतीयाङ्कनिमित्तं प्रकशनाय मौलिकाः शोधलेखा आविश्वं विद्यमानेभ्यो विद्वद्भ्य आमन्त्र्यन्ते। लेखाः अधिकाधिकं १५ पृष्ठात्मकाः निम्नप्रारूपेणैव टङ्कयित्वा [prachip...@gmail.com]() इति संकेतेन मृदुप्रतिकृतिरूपेण 30 सेप्टेम्बर २०१६ पर्यन्तं मुख्यसम्पादकं प्रति प्रेषयितुं शक्यन्ते।

Ø शोधलेखाः संस्कृतभाषया/ English / हिन्दीभाषया वा Unicode आधारितदेवनागरीलिप्या / रोमनलिप्या वा १२/ १४ अक्षरमानेन A4 पृष्ठे १.५ पङ्क्ति-अन्तरालेन न्यायसङ्गतपार्श्वसीमसहिताः टङ्किताः स्युः।

Ø Microsoft Word आधारितं सञ्चिकाप्रारूपं स्यात्।

Ø प्रतिपृष्ठम् (आद्यं विहाय) पृष्ठशीर्षे लेखस्य शीर्षकमवश्यं टङ्कितं स्यात्।

Ø पादटिप्पणी ग्रन्थकार-ग्रन्थ-प्रकशक-वर्ष-पृष्ठसंख्याक्रमेण सज्जिता स्यात्।

Ø शोधलेखस्य संक्षिप्तिकाऽपि प्रायः २००शब्दैः प्रेषणीया।

Ø शोधलेखस्य अन्ते लेखकस्य नाम, वृत्तिः, संकेतः, दूरभाषसंख्या, अन्तर्जालसंकेतश्चेत्यादिकमवश्यं लेखनीयम्।



 विशेषज्ञमण्डल्या परीक्षणोपरान्तं प्रकाशनयोग्याः शोधलेखाः प्रकाशयिष्यन्ते। प्रकाशनयोग्यतायाः मानदण्डाः निम्नवत् निर्धारिताः सन्ति –

**1. शोधलेखः संस्कृतवाङ्मयस्य केनाप्येकेनाङ्गेन सह सम्बद्धः स्यात्। यथा- वेदः, वेदाङ्गानि, उपनिषद्, दर्शनानि, पुराणानि, स्मृतिः, योगः, आगमः, साहित्यं, कव्यशास्त्रं, व्याकरणं, तन्त्रं, ज्योतिषं तथा संगणकीयभाषाविज्ञानाद्याधुनिकविषयाः।**

2. शोधलेखः स्वीयः मौलिकश्च स्यात्।

3. शोधलेखे विवेचनप्रक्रिया समस्याचयनत आरभ्य क्रमेण प्राक्कल्पना ततः समालोचनात्मकं मूल्याङ्कनं, तथा अन्ते शोधस्य मओलिकनिष्कर्षेण सहोपसंहारः इत्येवं क्रमवद्धतापुरःसरं विकसिता स्यात्।

4. शोधलेखे काचिन्नवीना अवधारणा, किमपि नवीनं तथ्यं, नवीनं मूल्याङ्कनं, नवीनः सिद्धान्तः, नवीना शैली, नवीनः संकेतो वा अवश्यमाविष्कृतः स्यात्।

5. शोधलेखस्य भाषा परिष्कृता, प्राञ्जला, स्तरानुगुणा च स्यात्।

6. शोधलेखेन सह घोषणापत्रमवश्यं स्याद् यद् – अयं शोधलेखः मदीयः मौलिकः तथा न अन्यत्र कुत्रचिदपि प्रकाशितः प्रकशयितुं वा चितोऽस्ति। यदि मम लेखस्य प्रकाशनं न भविष्यति तदाहं निराकरणस्य कारणं नैव अनुसन्धाष्यामि।

7. शोधलेखे समसामयिकी सम्बद्धता, तार्किकम् अनुशीलनं, तथा सन्दर्भाणामौचित्यमवश्यं स्युः।

8. शोधलेखे अन्यस्य प्रकाशनस्यांशोद्धरणे तदनुमतिः अवश्यं स्वीकर्तव्या यदि तादृशमुद्धरणं ५०० शब्देभ्योऽधिकमस्ति।

9. प्रकाशनात्परं शोधलेखस्य सर्वाधिकारः “प्राची प्रज्ञा” इत्यस्याः एव सुरक्षितः भविष्यति। शोधलेखस्य पुनः प्रकाशनस्य स्थितौ “प्राची प्रज्ञा” इत्यस्याः लिखितानुमतिः अवश्यं स्वीकर्तव्या।

10. शोधलेखस्य प्रकाशनयोग्यताऽयोग्यतानिर्धारणदायित्वं “प्राची प्रज्ञा” इत्यस्यामेव न्यस्तं भविष्यति।

Respected Scholars !

 Research Papers in Sanskrit/ English/ Hindi are invited from the scholars from the entire glove for the 2^(nd) Issue of peer reviewed International Research Journal **“****प्राची प्रज्ञा”** having ISSN no. 2348 – 8417, for its half yearly section **“****शास्त्रमञ्जूषा”. The Last date of receipt of articles is 30^(th) September 2016. **A **submission** to “प्राची प्रज्ञा” must be the original work of the author(s) and must have not been published elsewhere or be under consideration. All contributions should be submitted in soft copy only through email to the editor in chief on [**prachip...@gmail.com**](). The Maximum length of the paper must be 15 pages and fulfill the following criteria:

1. Format:

Ø All the fonts of Devanagari / Roman and Roman with diacritical marks, must be Unicode compatible. For Devanagari, Arial Unicode MS and for Roman, Times New Roman is preferable. The size of fonts should be 12 to 14.

Ø Format of file must be only Microsoft Word. Adobe page maker / pdf file formats are not acceptable.

Ø Page size must be of A4 or Letter typed on 1.5 line spacing and justified aligned and 1" margined format.

Ø Each page except the first should have a header containing the title of the article.

Ø References should be embedded in the text in a consistent style: Author, Title (bold if a book, “in quotes if an article, diss, etc.”), details of Publication (Place, Publisher, date if book; name of journal in bold, volume no. and date if article), p. or pp.

Ø All research papers should be accompanied by an abstract of 150-200 words.

Ø All contribution should also include (i) Name(s) of the author (ii) their professional affiliation (iii) postal address (iv) phone/mobile numbers & email addresses.

 All the research papers will undergo a review process. The papers (without the name of the contributor) will be sent to the reviewers having expertise on the field, and will be considered for publication only on the recommendations of the reviewers on following parameters:

**1. **The Research Paper must have a link to any branch of Sanskrit Studies, i.e. **Vedas, Vedāṅgas, Upaniṣads, Philosophies, Purāṇas, Smṛtis, Literature, Poetics, Grammar, Tantras, Astrology, Astronomy** **and Modern topics like Computational Linguistics etc.**

**2. **The Research Paper must be an original contribution of the author.****

**3. **Development of discussion should be from Statement of Problem to→ assumption or hypotheses to→ critical evaluations → to a conclusion with the findings of research. ****

**4. **The Research Paper must present a discovery / invention of a new - idea / thought /design / plan / awareness / inkling/ indication / appreciation / conception / understanding / opinion / view/ concept / notion / theory / principle or even a new style of interpretation. ****

5. The language must be lucid and must meet the standard of presentation.

6. The Research Paper must have contemporary relevance, contributions to knowledge, clarity and logic in analysis, methodology and appropriateness of references.

**7. **Each article should be accompanied with a declaration by all the authors that **“They are the authors of the article.**** The article is original and has neither been published earlier nor been submitted for publication elsewhere. They will not enquire the cause of rejection if not selected.” **

8. If the author has quoted more than 500 words / a table / a figure from a published work, in the article, a copy of permission obtained from the respective copyright holder needs to be enclosed.

9. The copyright of the article after it gets published is reserved by the “प्राची प्रज्ञा”. It should not get re-produced elsewhere without the written permission of the “Editor in Chief” of “प्राची प्रज्ञा”.

10. The selection of the Research paper is entirely entrusted to “प्राची प्रज्ञा”.



 If the paper meets the preliminary requirements, the article will be processed for review, which could take up to 2 months. All articles will be peer reviewed. The accepted articles are scheduled for publication in the chronological order in which they are accepted.
