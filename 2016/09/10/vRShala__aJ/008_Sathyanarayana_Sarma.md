नमो विद्वद्भ्यः  
  

Here is a passage which gives some explanation of the word vṛṣalī from the text Prāyaścittasamuccaya of Trilocanaśivācārya. I have recently published.  
  
वृषलीत्युच्यते नारी या पतिव्यभिचारिणी।  
तां ज्ञात्वा क्षमते यस्तु स विप्रो वृषलीपतिः।।  
v.r.saliityucyate naarii yaa pativyabhicaari.nii\|  
  
taa.m j\~naatvaa k.samate yastu sa vipro v.r.saliipati.h\|\| 304\|\|  
  
  

That lady is called a *vṛṣalī* who goes to others than her husband. A brahmin who knows her \[to be such\] and who tolerates \[her\], is \[called\] the husband of a *vṛṣalī*.
