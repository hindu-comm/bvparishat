Veda Vijnana Vedika, Hyderabad an Arya Samaj / Swami Dayanda Saraswati inspired forum located in Hyderabad had been organizing monthly seminars on **Scientific knowledge in Vedic Literature** for several years (1993-2003). There was a prolonged pause in the activities of the organization. Now the program is getting revived from this month.

  

The program for the month of September, 2015 is in the invitation being shared here. Since the invitation is in Telugu, brief details are being provided here:

  

Date : 11th September, 2016. Day : Sunday. Venue : Puchchalapalli Sundaraiah Vijnana Kendram, Bagh Lingampalli, Hyderabad. Time: 3 : 00 pm.

  

Inaguration, Chairperson, **Key Note Address**: Mataji Nirmala Yoga Bharati, Hyderabad.

  

Monthly Seminar : " **ProbingScientific knowledge in Vedic Literature - Challenges"** by Prof. Nagaraj Paturi, Former Senior Professor of Culture Studies, FLAME, Pune.

  

Chief Guest : Prof. CSR Prabhu, Former Director General, National Informatic Centre, Government of India. Presently Head, Department of Computer Science, Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology , Hyderabad. Lectures on : "**Vedic Sciences Database, Experiences with the project."**
