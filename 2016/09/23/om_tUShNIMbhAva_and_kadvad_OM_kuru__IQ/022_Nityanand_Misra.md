Thanks a lot, Dr. Ramanujan. Based on what we have seen so far on this thread, I agree with you on both कद्वद् here having the वति suffix explained by तेन तुल्यं क्रिया चेद्वतिः and om having the meaning of तूष्णींभावः only in the specific context ofprājāpatya hyms (ओमित्यस्य प्राजापत्येष्विव तूष्णींभावः अर्थत्वेन फलति).
