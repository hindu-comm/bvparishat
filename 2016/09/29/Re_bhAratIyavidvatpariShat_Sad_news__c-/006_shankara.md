b. 18.02.1936. Kohat (Now in Pakistan) Rtd. Prof. LBS Skt. Vidyapeetha.  

Gp. M.M. Parmeswaranand Pt. Parmanand, Dr. Hemraj Vaha, Dr. Deviraj Chanana.  

Sp. Prof. R. Devanathan, Prof. A.P. Sachidanand, Dr. Sudarshan Jha.  

Bks 03 Samskrita Sikshanavidhi. Ps. 25.
