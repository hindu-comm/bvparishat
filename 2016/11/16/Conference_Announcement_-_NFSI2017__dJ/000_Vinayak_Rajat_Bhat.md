Dear Sir/Madam  

  
Chinmaya Vishwavidyapeeth invites you to a 3-day International Conference on "*New Frontiers in Sanskrit and Indic Knowledge: Emerging Issues for Teaching Research and Applications"* from June 12-14, 2017 at its Chinmaya Eswar Gurukula, Kochi Campus.  
  
This conference, designed to address the issues and create a new agenda for the professionals and researchers in the field of Sanskrit and Ancient Indian Wisdom, will provide a platform for thought leaders, academic community and researchers to share new knowledge, best practices and gain from mutual interactions. The conference will also provide networking opportunities for those engaged in teaching and research in Sanskrit, Indic Knowledge and its Applications.  
  
Please circulate it among your faculty, researchers and students contacts. Please find attached flyer with this mail which you may post on your notice board.  
  

We are available for any queries or doubts you may have. Contact us on [nfsi2017@chinmayauniversity.ac.in]() or call us at **[+91-7558-896-000](tel:+91%2075588%2096000).**  
You may click [here](https://goo.gl/gbXwHt) or on the poster to visit the website.  
  

We look forward to your support and thank you for your time.
