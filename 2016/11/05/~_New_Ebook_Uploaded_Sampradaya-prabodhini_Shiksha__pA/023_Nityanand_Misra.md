Now that the source of the Wikipedia text has been traced to Vinayak Ganesh Apte, I have no issues in acknowledging the view that as per some (*iti kecit*) *ṭup* is a short form for*anuṣṭup*. This is still one of the best guesses, as Prof. Deshpande said, but at least we know that the view has been around for some time. With that, the [Wikipedian protestor](https://xkcd.com/285/)in me withdraws his protest.
