Dear Jayaprakashji,

  

  This is a beautiful verse. Do you know the source of the verse? 

   In September 1985, at the beginning of giving a lecture on Panini at the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute, Pune, I started with my own prayer to Sarasvati:

  

यां चिन्तयामि सततं मयि सा विधत्ताम्

वाग्देवता करुणया निजनेत्रपातम् ।

तद्रोचिषा विकचपङ्कजवच्चकास्तु

दाक्षीसुतस्य वचसां हृदि मे विकास: ।।

  

Madhav Deshpande

Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
