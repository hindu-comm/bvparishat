Namaste,

Institutions celebrate Sanskrit week in such a way that poornima intervenes in between. Or one can begin week long celebrations on shravana poornima day or culminate celebrations on that day.

Sanskrit day is conventionally performed on shravana poornima. It was announced by Govt of India (long back) thru' a G.O. that anytime during the preceding week or following week of full moon, Sanskrit day can be celebrated. Hence, we observe it to be on different times.
