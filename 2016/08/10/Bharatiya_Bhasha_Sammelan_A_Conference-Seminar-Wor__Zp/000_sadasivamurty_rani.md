Respected Members,

Pranams to all

I am glad to inform you all that **Bharatiya Bhasha Sammelan, a grand Conference-cum-Seminar-cum-Workshop,** is going to be organized by **Siksha Samskriti Utthan Nyas of New Delhi**, a National Non Government Organization for protecting all the Indian Regional Languages. The dates of the conference are 15th and 16th of October. Nearly 400 delegates particularly from Kerala, Karnataka, Tamilnadu and Andhra and also some members from North are going to participate in this huge confluence. It is an open invitation to all who ever want to participate.

The other details are given in the enclosed brochure.
