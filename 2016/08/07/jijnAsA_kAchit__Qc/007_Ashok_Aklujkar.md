Secondly, is it definite that the famous Mīmāṁsā author Kumārila-bhaṭṭa was also known asKumārila-svāmin? From Kamala-nayana Śarma’s edition of Laghu-vārttika published by the Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan (1987) under the title Kumārila-svāmi-praṇītamLaghu-vārttikam such does not seem to be the case, although the editor prefers to think thus.Kumārila-svāmin could very well be a (relatively minor)Mīmāṁsā author, later than the author of the Śloka-vārttika, Tantra-vārttika, etc.
