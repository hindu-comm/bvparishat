I am glad to inform that the following great personalities are recognised with “Vishishta Samskrita Seva Vrathi Samman” by Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan, New Delhi, for their remarkable service for Sanskrit. (Invitation is attached herewith along with their brief biodata)  
  
1. Prof. Chandragupta Shridhar Varnekar, Maharashtra
