Namaskar,

  

The etymological analysis of scientific terms brings great efficacy to particular shastra or science to understand in better way. This kind of innovative research should be appreciated and supported. At present, Professor J.S.R.A. Prasad, Dept. of Sanskrit Studies, University of Hyderabad, is encouraging this kind of work, particularly in Ayurveda. Right now I am working under him as a PDF scholar on the concept of etymological analysis of technical terms of Bhavaprakasha'. Here, the conceptual ontology is also introduced to show conceptual relation between terms. In this regard, if any collaboration is happened, this kind of works will be fruitful. Similarly valuable suggestions and comments are welcomed.

  

Thanks and regards

  

Dr. Siva Panucanti
