I suspect that Dr, Joshi read ङित्त्वम् in the original message as डित्त्वम्. The verse cited distinguished between two instances of आ, such that one instance is ङित् (एतमातं ङितं विद्यात्), while the other one is not (वाक्यस्मरणयो: अङित्).
