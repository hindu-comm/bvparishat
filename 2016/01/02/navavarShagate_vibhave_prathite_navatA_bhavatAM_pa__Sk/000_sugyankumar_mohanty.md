नवता भवतां पटुतां तनुतात्॥

(२०१६तमस्य क्रिस्तनववर्षस्य शुभाशयाः)

  

**मलता जडता शठताऽपगता**

**विपथे वितथे रसता शमिता।**

**नववर्षगते विभवे प्रथिते**

**नवता भवतां पटुतां तनुतात्॥**

****

** May the freshness and inventiveness augment your skill in a time, when the messiness, imprudence and deceitfulness are gone away, the amiability in erroneousness and fallaciousness are closed, the affluence of the New Year 2016 are approached.**

****

**समता-ममता-मृदुतासु रता**

**सुपथे जवतां श्रयते जनता।**

**विषयेषु रतिः रुचिता विरता**

**नवता भवतां पटुतां तनुतात्॥**

****

** May the freshness and inventiveness augment your skill in this New Year 2016 when the people are affianced in equality, affection and sympathy, inclined in the potency of righteousness and the inclination in the objects of senses are clogged. **

****

**सुज्ञानकुमारमाहान्तिः**
