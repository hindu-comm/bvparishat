Points to be noted from the above:



1\. A 'university' is called so because it teaches the 'universe' of disciplines in contrast to a school teaching a single discipline. Please note the line



nānāśāstreṣu mukhyaiś ca śuśrāva svanam īritam

Various recensions covered here cover a very big number of disciplines. One of them has



01,064.037c lokāyatikamukhyaiś ca samantād anunāditam  

2\. This need not be treated as 'history' and may comfortably be taken as mythological narrative poem. But this certainly is a reflection of a historical reality that was convincing for its readers asrealistic only because the readers knew such reality around them.



3\. Thisāśrama of kaṇva was compared to that of nara and nārāyaṇa:



naranārāyaṇasthānaṃ gaṅgayevopaśobhitam



4.The pair of nara and nārāyaṇais brought in as an ideal for an ideal pair of guru and śiṣya while talking about the pair of Krishna and Arjuna in the context of the Gita.



5.Even ifthis naranārāyaṇasthānaṃis taken as amythological one, it was certainly reflecting a historical reality analogous to it.
