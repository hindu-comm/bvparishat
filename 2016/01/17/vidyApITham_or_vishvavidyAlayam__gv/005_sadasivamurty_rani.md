The word आश्रम: may be a suitable one. The chief of the Ashram i.e. a sage is called कुलपति: in one of the ancient texts. Please the following verse which I saw somewhere in my collection from ancient texts. Now it source is not in memory. But this sloka has been used by me to explain an ashram for the last two and half decades.

मुनीनां दशसाहस्रं योऽन्नपानादिपॊषणात्।

अध्यापयति विप्रर्षिरसौ कुलपतिस्स्मृत:॥

This for information in the present context.

**Prof. Rani Sadasiva Murty**
