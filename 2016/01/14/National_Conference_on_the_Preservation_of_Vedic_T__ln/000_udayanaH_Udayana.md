I wish to share this information about an upcoming conference

National Conference on **Preservation of Vedic Traditions through the ages: With special reference to South India.**

Date: 28th to 31st January 2016.  

Place: Mysore

Conference Objectives: To provide a platform for traditional and modern scholars of all Vedas from South India i.e. Karnataka, Tamilnadu, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. To document all the Vedic traditions of South India in Audio/Video format. To create awareness about Vedic studies and research among the public. To encourage Vedic scholars by recognizing their service in the preservation of rich Vedic traditions. To promote all the branches of knowledge that are directly or indirectly related to the Vedas. To disseminate the Vedic knowledge and Vedic chanting traditions among the common people in a secular way.
