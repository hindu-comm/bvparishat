A small doubt on the subject discussed:

  
हिंस्रयज्ञवर्जनम् - non-performance of animal sacrifice.

As I can understand the word हिंस्र means wild beasts and like Tiger, Lion and such wild animals are never sacrificed, even by gross misunderstanding but only Aja़-s as the Subhasita says.

And there is no need to avoid the sacrifice of such हिंस्रपशु as the title gives. And the अज the goat is not a हिंस्र animal at all to be included in the list. How it could be included?
