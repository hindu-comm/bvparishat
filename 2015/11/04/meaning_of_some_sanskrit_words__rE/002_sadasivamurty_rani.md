ब्रह्म, उद्गाता, होता, अध्वर्यु:, ब्रह्मणाच्छंसी, प्रस्तोता, मैत्रावरुण:, प्रतिप्रस्थाता, पॊता, प्रतिहर्ता, अच्छावाक:, नेष्टा, अग्निध्र:, सुब्रह्मण्य:, ग्रावस्तुत:, उन्नेता are the names of 16ऋत्विक्s in the Yajnas. They have different functions to perform in the Yajnas.

Usually, out of these 16 Rtviks, the first four ब्रह्म, उद्गाता, होता and अध्वर्यु: are popularly known to all.

The direct or one word English equivalents for all these 16 words may not be possible.

So their functions can be obtained from Srauta Sutras and be translated into English.

Or commonly a phrase - one of the sixteen Priests of Sacrificial rituals. This can be a common meaning to use.

  

**Dr. Rani Sadasiva Murty**
