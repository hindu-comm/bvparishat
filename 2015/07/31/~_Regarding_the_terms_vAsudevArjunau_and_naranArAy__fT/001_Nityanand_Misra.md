[[Nityanand Misra	2015-07-31, 07:30:25 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/fTp0wsdyqE0)]]



  
  
The*pūrvanipāta prakaraṇa*is*anitya*. This means that in*śiṣṭa*usages, deviations from the*pūrvanipāta prakaraṇa*are sometimes seen. It does not mean we can take liberties and make deviations from the rules.  
  
For this and more, see examples 1.45 (pp. 161-162), 1.63 (178-179), and 2.89 (p. 300) in*Adhyātmarāmāyaṇe'pāṇinīyaprayogāṇāṃ Vimarśaḥ*,**and also myfootnotes: 116 on page 162 and 322 on page 300. The relevant pages are attached.
