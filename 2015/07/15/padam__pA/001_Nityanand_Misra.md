Meanings in lexicons may be added:

  

The *Agni Purāṇa* (AP 362.17) and the *Amarakośa* (AK 3.3.93) –

पदं व्यवसितित्राणस्थानलक्ष्माङ्‌घ्रिवस्तुषु

*padaṃ vyavasititrāṇasthānalakṣmāṅghrivastuṣu*

My translation: ‘\[the word\] *pada* \[is used\] in \[the meanings of\] (1) a business, (2) protection, (3) a place, (4) a mark, (5) foot, and (6) a thing.’

  

The *Viśvaprakāśa* (cited in the*Vyākhyāsudhā*):

पदं शब्दे च वाक्ये च व्यवसायापदेशयोः। पादतचिह्नयोः स्थानत्राणयोरङ्कवस्तुनोः॥

padaṃ śabde ca vākye ca vyavasāyāpadeśayoḥ। pādatacihnayoḥ sthānatrāṇayoraṅkavastunoḥ॥

My translation: \[The word\] *pada* \[is used\] in \[the meanings of\] (1) a word, (2) a sentence, (3) a business, (4) a cause, (5) foot, (6) a sign, (7) a place, (8) protection, (9) a mark, (10) a thing.

  

The Medinīkośa adds (also cited in the *Vyākhyāsudhā*):

श्लोकपादेऽपि च क्लीबं पुल्लिङ्गे किरणे पुनः॥

ślokapāde’pi ca klībaṃ pulliṅge kiraṇe punaḥ

My translation: also neuter in \[the meaning of\] a quarter part of a verse, and masculine in \[the meaning of\] a ray.
