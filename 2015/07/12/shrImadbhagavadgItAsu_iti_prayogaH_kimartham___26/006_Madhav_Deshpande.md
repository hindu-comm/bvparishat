The colophons at the end of each Adhyāya of the Bhagavadgītā run like:
śrīmad-bhagavadgītāsu upaniṣatsu \[brahmavidyāyām yogaśāstre Srīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde ...\]. So the word bhagavadgītāsu is literally an adjective of the word upaniṣatsu. That is the reason it is both feminine and plural.
