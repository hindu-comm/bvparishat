Dear scholars

  

May I point out Raghuvamhsa 14th sarga, 18th verse in this regard.  

  

सभाजनाय उपगतान् स दिव्यान् मनीन् पुरस्कृत्य हतस्य शत्रो:।

शुश्राव तेभ्य: प्रभवादि वृत्तं स्वविक्रमे गौरवमादधानम्॥

  

The first word सभाजनाय means 'to congratulate'. Here is R D.Karmarkar's translation of first line relevant part:

'Having honoured the divine sages who had come to congratulate him ....etc'  



Dr. Shrikant Jamadagni
