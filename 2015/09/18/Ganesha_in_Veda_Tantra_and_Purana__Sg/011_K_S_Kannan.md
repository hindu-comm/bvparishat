It may be desirable to look in this context into the theories of hylozoism and panpsychism.

  

Of course, Patan"jali discusses the view

***sarvasya vA cetanAvattvAt.***

  

Far from a primitive conception of animism, or a mere hypothetical vitalism, this may convey a deeper, and perhaps a loftier, vision of life, where nothing is unanimated by the Spirit that indwells all of the cosmos, (but for which all would be an antithetical chaos), a multi-verse rather than a universe.
