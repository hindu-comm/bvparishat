In the case of Skanda here is one information, from Shankara's Brahmasutra bhāṣya 3.3.32:

  

सनत्कुमारोऽपि ब्रह्मण एव मानसः पुत्रः स्वयं रुद्राय वरप्रदानात् स्कन्दत्वेन प्रादुर्बभूव ;  

Sanatkumāra, a mānasa putra of Brahmā, incarnated as Skanda, son of Rudra, due to a boon. 

The bhāṣya says all such cases are those of Jnani-s taking another body for some or other cause.
