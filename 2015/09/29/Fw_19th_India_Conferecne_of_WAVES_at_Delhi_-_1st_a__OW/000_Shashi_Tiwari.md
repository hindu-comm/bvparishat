*First Announcement & Call for Papers: *



(WAVES) Wider Association for Vedic Studies, Regd.

*(A Multi-disciplinary Academic Society)*

In association with

Delhi Sanskrit Academy, Delhi

19thIndia Conference of WAVES

on

“Science and Spirituality in Vedic Traditions: Modern Context”

*'Vaidika Parampara me Vijyana aur Adhyatma: Adhunika Paripreksha’*

at

Delhi Sanskrit Academy, Delhi

27th (Friday) – 29th(Sunday), November, 2015



The conference is nineteenth in a series of annual conferences of WAVES held in India. Conferences in the series have been held in Delhi, Srisailam, Bangalore, Puduchery, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Vrindavan, Haridwar, Luchnow, and Varanasi. The conference will have keynote address, plenary talks, invited talks, &contributed papers. It aims at bringing together scholars and researchers of diverse disciplines to discuss science and spirituality in Vedic traditions with a view to highlight its
importance to the present world.



Prizes for Young Scholars:

There shall be cash awards of Rs. 3000.00 (First), Rs.2000.00 (Second), Rs. 1000.00 (Third) & Rs.500.00 (2 encouragement) for outstanding research papers submitted & presented by scholars (up to 45 years).
