Thanks for the suggestion.  

I think I should mention both the suutras, because it is due to
ईदूदेद्द्विवचनम् प्रगृह्यम् (1।1।11) the first word gets प्रगृह्य sa\~nj\~naa, and then प्लुतप्रगृह्या अचि नित्यम् becomes applicable when such a word is followed by an अच्  

So accordingly I have made the corrections in the programme.

|      |     |          |                 | |------|-----|----------|-----------------| | गुरू | एतौ | गुरू एतौ | प्रगृह्य-सन्धिः |
