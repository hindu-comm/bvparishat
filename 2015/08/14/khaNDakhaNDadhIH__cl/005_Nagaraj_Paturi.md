> 
> > या धीः अखण्डं खण्डं खण्डं इव (अनेकखण्डरूपेण)पश्यति सा खण्डखण्डधीः > इत्यपि भाव्यं। >
> 



This helps in connecting the first half of the verse with thesecond half.



सर्वं खल्विदं ब्रह्म and सर्वमात्मा both are अखण्डत्व views only. Combining these two statements , we get ब्रह्म isआत्मा. Identifying ब्रह्मwith आत्मा is another level of अखण्डत्व view. धीः which can notrealize this is being called as खण्डखण्डधीः.



आत्मा is the third person form of the first person pronoun अहम् . The second half of the verse comes from this. इदं in thethird quarteris from the first quarter of the verse. Combining सर्वं खल्विदं ब्रह्म and सर्वमात्माand converting आत्मा.into अहम्, we get अहम् इदं. Third quarter अहमन्यदिदं चान्यत् is the negation of अहम् इदं. अहम् इदं view is अखण्डत्व view. So negation of that अहमन्यदिदं चान्यत्should be a result of खण्डखण्डधीः.
