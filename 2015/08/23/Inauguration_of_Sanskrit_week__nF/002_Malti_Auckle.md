Dear Scholars,

Namonamah.

I have the pleasure to announce that The Sanskrit Department of The School of Indological Studies is celebrating Sanskrit Day on Monday 31 August 2015 in the Auditorium of The Mahatma Gandhi Institute.

Please note that every year Sanskrit day is celebrated at the Mahatma Gandhi Institute.  

  

His Excellency The High Commissioner of The Republic of India in Mauritius will be the chief guest.

  

The programme includes apart from the traditional speeches of The Director General Of MGI/RTI , The Director MGI, His Excellency Shri Anup Kumar Mudgal and Professor D N Tiwari, ICCR Chair at the Institution,

  

1. Lighting of lamp followed Invocational bhajan and dance dedicated to Guru

2. Dance Ballet based on the hymn of Pingala adapted from श्रीमद्भागवतम्

3. Act I Scene I of कालिदास "अभिज्ञान शाकुन्तलम" (adapted)

  

The Department also organised verse recitation for secondary and tertiary students, essay writing and painting and drawing competitions.  

  

Bless us that we can keep Sanskrit, in all its forms, alive in Mauritius.

M. Auckle  

  

Mrs. Malti AUCKLE

Head Department of Sanskrit

Senior Lecturer in Sanskrit

School of Indological Studies

Mahatma Gandhi Institute

Moka

Mauritius.
