Dear all,

Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan, New Delhi, is going to organize a Sanskrit Week Programme and its inaugural ceremony will be held on 26 August 2015 at 5 p.m. (please see the programme attached). On this occasionthe Vishist Sanskrit Sevavrati Award will be presented by the dignitariesto the following great personalities :        

(I) Shri K. Lakshmi Narsinhan

(II) Dr. Jayant Kumar Dirghangi

(III) Dr. Amba Kulkarni.

  

We heartily congratulate all of the above-mentioned respectable persons dedicated to the service of promoting Sanskrit studies and research in India. Specially, I personally know Dr Jayanta Kumar Dirghangi, a renowned physician practising in Memphis, USA, and a Somayajin, who has established the Jayendra Saraswati Vedavidyalaya in a vast Vedabhavana complex situated at a remote village named Jayantipur, West Medinipur, West Bengal, along with the temples of Lord Rama and Sita, Vedic garden, sacrificial ground, Goshala, Guest house, well, pond, etc. 50 residential students under 5 teachers are studying one branch of each Vedain this institution with great care and affection. To know more about the institution please visit [kamakoti.org](http://kamakoti.org). We wish Dr Dirghangi and other recipients blissful life, good health & spirit and look forward to their further achievements.
