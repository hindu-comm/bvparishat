Namaste

  

Here is information that I have:

  

In Chennai: MM Sri R.Krishnamurthy Sastrṇaḥ - Advaita, (Nyāya etc. that is required for Advaita Vedanta)

  

Vidwan Mani Dravid Sāstriṇaḥ - All the four disciplines mentioned by you. 

  

Vidwan Goda Venkatesha Sastrinaḥ can teach Advaita and Nyaya \[All the above thee scholars can teach even Advaita Siddhi\]

  

Dr.Maheswaran Nambūdiri: Advaita and Nyaya

  

All the above persons reside close to the Madras Sanskrit College.

  

Vidwan Srihari Dhaigude in Chennai can teach Advaita and Nyaya

  

In Karnataka:

  

Bangalore: Vidwan Shivarama Agnihotri: Advaita

  

Vidwan Ananta Sharma Bhuvanagiri: Advaita

  

At Sringeri: Vidwan Ganesh Ishwara Bhat (a member of this forum): Advaita and Nyaya.

  

Veda Vijnyan Gurukulam, Chennenahalli, Bangalore: Advaita

  

Sri Mahabaleshwara Bhatta, Sringeri, Vyakarana \[Retd. from Sanskrit College, Bangalore\]

  

Somapura near Tirthahalli: Vidwan S.T.Nagaraja Bhatta, Vyakarana, (same as above)

  

There are several scholars associated with the Sringeri Peetham there who can teach Nyaya, Vyakarana and Advaita.

  

Another Sri Mahabaleshwara Bhatta, Sringeri, Advaita.

  

Mathur (Shimoga Dist.) Vidwan Ashwathanarayana Avadhani and his twin sons who can teach Advaita.

  

Dharwad: Vidwan Vāchaspati Joshi: Nyaya and Advaita.

  

Svarnavalli: Vidwan :Shankara Bhatta Baaligadde: Advaita

  

In Bangalore there is the Samskrita University, College, where there are scholars who can teach Advaita. \[Vidwan Manjunatha Bhatta and Subrahmanya Bhatta\]

  

  

Andhra:

  

Vidwan M.L.Narasimhamurthy: Tirupati: Advaita, Nyaya

  

There is an institution in Tenali, Andhra, where all the four disciplines are taught.

  

The above is not an exhaustive list; others can add to the list.

  

warm regards
