Author: Sri Raghunandana Gosvami

About the book: An excellent kāvya in the virudāvalī style dedicated to Gauranga (Chaitanya Mahaprabhu), written by Sri Raghunandana Goswami. This kāvya is written on similar lines to Sri Govinda Virudavali of Srila Rupa Goswami.  
  
About the author: He was born in a family descending from Lord Nityananda in the seventeenth Saka century. He made a notable contribution to the field of Gaudiya Vaishnava literature and attained great fame by composing the following works: Gaurangacampu; Gaurangavirudavali; Ramarasayana, Radhadamodara Kavya; Gitamala; Desikanirnaya; Samshayashaatani tika of Bhagavatam; Vyakhyanamanjari tika of Chandomanjari.
