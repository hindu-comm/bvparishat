In the currently discussed type, the author of the book is not anonymous. Author is known.

  

In the other variety, the author of the work is anonymous. Some other author is projected in his place as the author.

  

In the currently discussed type, the author owns up the writing/composing of the work. He just disowns the innovation of the ideas in the book.

  

In the other variety the actual author disowns his efforts of writing/composing the book.

  

In the other variety you do not know who is disowning.

  

In the currently discussed type, you know who is disowning.  

  

In the other variety, it is the researcher who identifies the need for investigation.

  

In the currently discussed type, the author is either providing good number of details or at least some leads. The researcher works based on the author-provided details.
