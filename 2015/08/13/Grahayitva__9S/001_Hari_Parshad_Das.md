\~\~ śrī-śrī-rādhā-dāmodarāya namaḥ \~\~  
  
Translation: Having made eight brahmanas completely accept this vidyā (specified in the cākṣuṣopaniṣat), one becomes a perfected soul in this (same vidyā).  
  
sādhu-caraṇa-rajo 'bhilāṣī,  
  
hari pārṣada dāsa
