I have been unable to find the location in the text.

Can you please help point out where "बह्वृची" ismentioned inअष्टाध्यायी (or महाभाष्यम्)?

                     --- vidvan Sreyas Sarangan

  

Panini offers Sabdas in two ways -

  

1\. व्युत्पत्तिः - प्रकृति and प्रत्यय are provided and one can have the परिनिष्ठितरूपम् of a पदम् ।

2\. निपातः -- it means उच्चारणम् - of a सिद्धशब्द and one has to deduce the प्रकृति and प्रत्यय from that.

कठी and बह्वृची fall under the first category .

  

Under जातेरस्त्रीविषयात् अयोपधात् - commentators explained as to what is जाति - there both these are examples for - गोत्रं च चरणैस्सह ।

  

These examples (also in माधवीयधातुवृत्ति (कठी) and कौमुदी) have been coming for centuries - it is difficult to go into details without proper प्रमाणम् ।
