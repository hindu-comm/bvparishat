Hari Om!  
  
We hope this letter finds you happy and well.  

Chinmaya International Foundation Shodha Sansthan is organising a 15 day residential course of "Learning Sanskrit Language Structure: Theory and Application (Level - III)" at Chinmaya International Foundation, Adi shankaranilayam, Veliyanad, Ernakulam, Kerala  

  

Course duration : 7 - 21 September 2015

  

Place :  Chinmaya International Foundation. the place where the great Philosopher Shankaracharya was born in Kerala.

  

Content of the Course :   Sanskrit Syntax Structure  
  

Course Acarya : Prof V N Jha  

  

We will learn basics of Syntax of Sanskrit Language in such a way that simultaneously we will learn how Panini has handle it in his Astadhyayi.

  

Like Level-1, the level-2 course will also focus on developing reading, comprehending, writing and speaking skills.

  

Who can join ?

  

Anybody interested in learning Sanskrit.

  

Those who could not join Level-1 can also join this. Most probably, the DVDs of the level-1 will be made available by that time.

  

Please let us know if you would be interested and also share this with your department, friends, colleagues, and others.  
  
Looking forward to having you with us at Adi Sankara Nilayam for a spectacular learning experience.
