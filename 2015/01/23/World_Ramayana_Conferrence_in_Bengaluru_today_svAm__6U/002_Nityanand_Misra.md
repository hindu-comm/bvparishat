The official list of the Padma awards is out on the website of the Press Information Bureau, Government of India. Gurudeva Svāmī Rāmabhadrācārya has been awarded the Padma Vibhūṣaṇa.  
Here is the link to the PIB release  
<http://pib.nic.in/newsite/erelease.aspx?relid=114952>  
  
Other Sanskrit scholars on the list are -  
1. Śivakumāra Svāmi: Padma Bhūṣaṇa  
2. David Frawley (Vāmadeva Śāstrī): Padma Bhūṣaṇa  
3. Svāmī Satyamitrānanda Giri: Padma Bhūṣaṇa  
4. Bettina Śāradā Bäumer: Padma Śrī  
5. Brahmadeva Śarmā: Padma Śrī  
6. Annette Schmiedchen: Padma Śrī
