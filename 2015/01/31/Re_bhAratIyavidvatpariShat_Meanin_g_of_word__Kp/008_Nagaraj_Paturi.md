Outside पूर्वमीमांसा, परिसंख्या is used as the name of an alankaara in alankaaras'aastra.



It is borrowed from पूर्वमीमांसा only into alankaaras'aastra.



While discussing the alankaara too, all aalankaarikas bring the original use of the word in पूर्वमीमांसा for discussion.



Ruyyaka, Jayakara,Jagannatha etc. opined that in alankaaras'aastra, the distinction between नियम and परिसंख्या can be ignored. But Shobhakara argued for a separate alankaara with the name नियम apart from the one with the name परिसंख्या.
