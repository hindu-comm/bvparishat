The number 48, according to some is arrived at, since the worship is through the observation of this vrata to propitiate the 27 nakshatras, the 12 rasis and the 9Grahas.  
  
The total of 27, 12 and 9 is 48 and hence this is considered, by some to be the duration of a Mandala.
