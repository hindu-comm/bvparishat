\~\~ śrī-śrī-rādhā-dāmodarāya namaḥ \~\~  
  
dear vidvaj-janas,  
  
my praṇāmas.  
  
I think the solution to the problem of ambiguity regarding the nomenclature of the various times is found in the following sūtra:  
  
**kālopasarjane ca tulyam (PS 1.2.57)**  
  
The point of this sūtra is that the definitions of the various measures of time should be taken from loka-pramāṇa and not śāstra-pramāṇa.  
  
sādhu-caraṇa-rajo 'bhilāṣī,  
  
hari pārṣada dāsa.
