'Subha Madhyahnah' is a literal translation. In Sanskrit Conversational camps the following expressions are in practice.

For Good Morning -सुप्रभातम्

For Good Afternoon or Good Day -शुभदिनम्

For Good Evening -शुभसायम्

For Good Night -शुभरात्रि:/शुभरात्री
