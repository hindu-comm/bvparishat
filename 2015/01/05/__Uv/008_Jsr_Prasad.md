Thanks for all scholars who have shared invaluable information about the next Jagadguru of Sri Dakshinamnaya Sringeri Matha. Indeed, as Prof. Nagaraj ji felt it, Kuppa family is one of the very few Vedic families in Andhra that nourish and sustain the *anuucAnaveda-paramparA.* My revered *namaskAram*s to Brahmasri Kuppa Siva Subrahmanyavadhani garu and mother Nagalakshmi for bestowing us with the next Jagadguru.  
  

In this context, as per the normal belief in the *matha-sampradAya*, I am told by elders that Sri SAradAmatA herself, informs about selecting next pontiff to the the present Jagadguru. Sorry, science can not validate this belief.  
  

महालक्ष्मी महोदया, तत्र भवन्तस्सर्वे कुप्पावंशीयाः शारदाम्बासंपूर्णकृपाकटाक्षेणानुग्रहीताः । मन्यामहे आत्मानं धन्यान् वयमुपि यतोहि भवादृशानां मित्रबृन्दे वयमप्यन्यतमा इति कृत्वा ।  
  

*अज्ञानां जाह्नवीतीर्थं विद्यातीर्थं विवेकिनाम् ।  
*

*सर्वेषां सुखदं तीर्थं भारतीतीर्थमाश्रये ।।*  
  

भविष्यमाणजगद्गुरुभ्यो सप्रश्रयप्रणामैस्सह ।
