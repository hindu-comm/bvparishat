Here are some important studies of the Gaṇapāṭha of Pāṇini, in addition to what is mentioned by Ashok Aklujkar:

  

[Sumitra Mangesh Katre.](http://mirlyn-classic.lib.umich.edu/F/AXBH3E7S7BNXRV18FR997J4IM8RXUU92J43NDNRHNRM4ALH6T8-07689?func=full-set-set&set_number=016672&set_entry=000002&format=999)1971,[Dictionary of Pāṇini :Gaṇapāṭha](http://mirlyn-classic.lib.umich.edu/F/AXBH3E7S7BNXRV18FR997J4IM8RXUU92J43NDNRHNRM4ALH6T8-07689?func=full-set-set&set_number=016672&set_entry=000002&format=999)  

  

Robert Birwe, 1961,[Der Gaṇapāṭha zu den Adhyāyas IV und V der Grammatik Pāṇinis;Versuch einer Rekonstruktion.](http://mirlyn-classic.lib.umich.edu/F/AXBH3E7S7BNXRV18FR997J4IM8RXUU92J43NDNRHNRM4ALH6T8-07697?func=full-set-set&set_number=016672&set_entry=000004&format=999)

  

Kapildeva, Shastri, 1961,[Saṃskṛta vyākaraṇa meṃ gaṇapāṭha kī paramparā aura Ācārya Pāṇini.](http://mirlyn-classic.lib.umich.edu/F/AXBH3E7S7BNXRV18FR997J4IM8RXUU92J43NDNRHNRM4ALH6T8-07702?func=full-set-set&set_number=016672&set_entry=000005&format=999)

  

and an article:

  

Ojihara_Yutaka-Les discussions patañjaliennes afférentes au remaniement du gaṇapāṭha  

  

Birwe's book is in German, and Ojihara's article is in French, but the books by Katre and Kapildev Shastri may be more easily accessible.
