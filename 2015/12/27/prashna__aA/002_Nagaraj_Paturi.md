1\. 9-5 is the middle of a unit made of 9-4, 9-5 and 9-6

  

2\. The issue is not whether bhagavān is inbhūtāni or his ams'a is in bhūtāni



3\. The issue is whether bhagavān is inbhūtānior bhūtāni are in bhagavān



4\. śankara explains this complex exposition of the Gita by using the word asamsargitvāt.



He says,



न हि असंसर्गि वस्तु क्वचित् आधेयभावेन अवस्थितं भवति।
