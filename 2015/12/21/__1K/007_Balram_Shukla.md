नववर्षस्य प्राभृतायमानमिवाद्य सूचितं यद् मम चिरप्रतीक्षितः काव्यसंग्रहः परीवाह-नामा साहित्याकादमी-तः प्रकाशमासादित इति। अवसरे,स्मिन् ममेदं कर्तव्यं यन्मदीयकाव्यस्यास्य प्रेरणाभूतं सर्वमपि धन्यतावचोभिः सभाज्येरन्निति।  
It was an exhilerating news for me when I came to know that long awaited collection of my Sanskrit poems -parIvAhaH has finally been published by prestigious[Sahitya Akademi](https://www.facebook.com/sahitya.akademi.9/). At this occasion I extend my sincere thanks to all my Acharyas and friends who have been a source of inspiration for my poems and also polished them.  

--  

Balram Shukla
