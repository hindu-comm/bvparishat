It is an editorial error that was missed the proofreading and made its way to the third edition. There will be रुत्व by ससजुषो रुः (८.२.६६), यत्व by भोभगोअघो अपूर्वस्य योऽशि (८.३.१७) and लोप of यकार by हलि सर्वेषाम् (८.३.२२). It should be तद्विरुद्धसङ्ख्याया द्वित्वादेः. The error will be corrected in the next edition. Thanks for pointing out.
