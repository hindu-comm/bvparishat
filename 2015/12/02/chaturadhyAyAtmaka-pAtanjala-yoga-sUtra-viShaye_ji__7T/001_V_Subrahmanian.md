<https://archive.org/details/PatanjalYogaDarshanm.with.Bhasyas.Sanskrit>

  

Patanjal-Yoga-Darshanam with Vyasa-Bhasya, Tattva-Vaisharadi by Vachaspati and Yogabhashyavarttika by Vijnanabhiksu  

by[Patanjali, Vyasa, Vachaspati Misra, Vijnanabhiksu](https://archive.org/search.php?query=creator%3A%22Patanjali%2C+Vyasa%2C+Vachaspati+Misra%2C+Vijnanabhiksu%22)

  

A commentary attributed to Shankaracharya is also available.

  

Apart from these, there is a 'Yoga maṇi prabhā' authored by Sri Govindananda, the author of the 'Bhāṣya Ratna prabhā' (on Shankaracharya's Brahma sutra bhāṣya).

  

There is a 'Yoga sutra vṛtti' by Sri Sadaśiva Brahmendra Saraswati (the author of the famous 'Brahma sutra vṛtti').
