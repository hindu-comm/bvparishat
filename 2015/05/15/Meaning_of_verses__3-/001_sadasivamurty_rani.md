गुरूर्द्विमात्रे वक्रः स्याद्रूजुर्लघुरुदीरितः।

ताटङ्कहारकेयूरनूपुराख्या गुरोः स्मृता।।

मेनुकाहलदण्डेति शरसंज्ञा लघोः स्मृता।

मयरसतजभणगणा इत्यष्टसंज्ञकाः।।

In this sloka in the second line the shape (वक्र)of GURU is compared toताटङ्कहार,

केयूर(Armlets) andनूपुरNupura which are like the crescent of the Moon when they are put on (as their half side only is visible).

Similarly in the third line the shape (Ruju) of LAGHU is compared toमेनु,काहलदण्ड

andशर which resemble a vertical straight line in appearance.
