Two translations of the above shlok :  
a) Om! We shall treat of the phonetics: sound, rhythm, quantity, strength, modulation, union. Thus has been declared the lesson on phonetics.  
  
b) We shall now explain the science of pronunciation (it consists of) the sounds, accent or the pitch, quality or the measure, the effort put in articulation, uniformity & continuity in pronouncing the letters. Thus has been explained the lesson on pronunciation.

Thanks. I looked at the MW dictionary and the grammar related meanings are given here:  
1) अठिल्ला or अतिलिहा f. N. of a Prakrit metre (of four lines each containing sixteen मात्रs)  
  
2) अर्धमात्रिक mfn. (= mātra mfn.) having the quantity of half a short syllable Comm. on vājasaneyi-saṁhitā-prātiśākhya  
  
3) गुण the peculiar properties of the letters (11 in number, viz. the 8 bāhya-prayatnās \[q.v.\] and the 3 accents) Kāṡ. on Pāṇ. 1-1, 9 and 50 (cf. -mātra)  
  
4) च्युतक अक्षर-मात्र ?  
  
5) मात्रा metrical unit, a mora or prosodial instant i.e. the length of time required to pronounce a short vowel (a long vowel contains 2 मात्राs, and a prolated vowel प्रातिशाख्य
