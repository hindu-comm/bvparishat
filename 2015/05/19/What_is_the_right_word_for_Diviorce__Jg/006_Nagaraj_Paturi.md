Sharing a snapshot of a relevant portion of Kasikhandam of Skandapuranam.

  

Please take special note of the highlighted portions:

  

The deekshita relenquishes his son. त्यजेदेकं, अपुत्रत्वं वरं

  

The procedure described has darbha, ambu and tila. आनय दर्भाम्बु तस्मै दद्यांतिलाञ्जलिम्

  

The deekshita marries another woman. श्रोत्रियस्य सुतां प्राप्य पाणिं जग्राह दीक्शितः

  

It is not mentioned whether he relinquished the first wife or not.

  

But the son says, "जननी न मे"
