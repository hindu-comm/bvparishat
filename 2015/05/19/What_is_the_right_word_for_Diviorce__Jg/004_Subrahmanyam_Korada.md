नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

What is the right word for divorce. I heard people using the word "ghatasphota". How does it differe from ghatashraddha (ghata-ashraddha?)  

                              

                          -- Vidvan Sunil Bhattacarya

  

The fact is that I could / did not try to look into the aspect of
घटस्फोट । 

  

Recently , while dealing with अन्त्येष्टि ( the last संस्कार ) for my lengthy Article on Sixteen Samskaras, I came across घटस्फोट in स्मृतिs \|

  

घटश्राद्ध , as far as I know , is not prescribed in any स्मृति - शिष्टs , that too in South India follow ( used to follow ?) this rite.

  

On the other hand घटस्फोट is very much there in स्मृतिs --

  

This is not akin to Divorce , which is ruled out in tradition.

  

घटस्फोट ( throwing / breaking a pot of water) is prescribed to snap all connections with a पतित who refused to undergo any प्रायश्चित्त --

  

the aspect of पतित is discussed by Patanjali under ज्ञाने धर्मः etc in पस्पशा of महाभाष्यम् ।

  

A person who commits certain sins and ' falls below the standard of आर्य ’ can roughly be called पतित - such a person in unfit for पङ्क्ति (- भोजनम्) and other व्यवहार (पङ्क्तिबाह्य is called निरवसित - ’शूद्राणां चानिरवसितानाम् ’ पा सू) .

  

भृगु in मनुस्मृति (11-183 , 184,185) explains the procedure of घटस्फोट --

  

दासीं घटमपां पूर्णं पर्यस्येत् प्रेतवत्पदा ।

अहोरात्रम् उपासीरन् अशौचं बान्धवैः सह॥

  

A slave girl , with her face south (like a dead body), made to push aside (पर्यस्येत्) an earthen pot filled with water (all water should flow out of the pot)

and the relatives have to observe a day and night of अशौच (impurity ) as he is dead for them.

  

Then there cannot be any social intercourse with such a person who is ex-communicated --

  

निवर्तेरंश्च तस्मात्तु संभाषणसहासने ।

दायाद्यस्य प्रदानं च यात्रा चैव हि लौकिकी॥

ज्येष्ठता च निवर्तेत ज्येष्ठावाप्यं च यद्धनम्।

ज्येष्ठांशं प्राप्नुयाच्चास्य यवीयान् गुणतो’धिकः॥

  

Can घटस्फोट be revoked ? Yes , if the person in question undergoes the prescribed प्रायश्चित्त (मनु - 11- 186,187) --

  

प्रायश्चित्ते तु चरिते पूर्णकुम्भमपां नवम् ।

तेनैव सार्धं प्रास्येयुः स्नात्वा पुण्ये जलाशये ॥

स त्वप्सु तं घटं प्रास्य प्रविश्य भवनं स्वकम्।

सर्वाणि ज्ञातिकार्याणि यथापूर्वं समाचरेत् ॥

  

प्रास्य = having thrown

  

For a lady ( पतिता ) the घटस्फोट is the same - but she has to be provided with clothes , food , water and shelter nearby the relatives' house (मनु 11-188) -

  

एतदेव विधिं कुर्यात् योषित्सु पतितास्वपि।

वस्त्रान्नपानं देयं तु वसेयुश्च गृहान्तिके ॥

  

कमलाकरभट्ट in his निर्णयसिन्धु quotes different स्मृतिs \|

  

याज्ञवल्क्य - वसिष्ठ - गौतम - स्मृतिs also registered this rite.
