I am looking for the original source for the oft-cited ‘śruti’ **ātmā vai jāyate putraḥ** (आत्मा वै जायते पुत्रः).  
  
A similar śruti is **ātmā vai putranāmā’si** (आत्मा वै पुत्रनामाऽसि) which is from Kauṣītaki Upaniṣad (2.11).
