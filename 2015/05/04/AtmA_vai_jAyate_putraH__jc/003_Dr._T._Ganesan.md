The Vedic passage, uttered by the piests in Tamilnadu as an aashiirvaada to a sumaNgalii woman reads thus:  
   
   दशास्यां पुत्रान् आधेहि । पतिमेकादशं कृधि।  
  
purports to say that the husband is to be treated as the eleventh child by the woman after begetting 10 children.  
  
  
Ganesan
