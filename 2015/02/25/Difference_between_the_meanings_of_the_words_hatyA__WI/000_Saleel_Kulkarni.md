Namo Namah.

I would like to know the exact difference between the meanings of the words हत्या and वध. Apparently, the word हत्या is commonly used in the case of a killing that is against the धर्मशास्त्र or the नीतिशास्त्र e.g. गोहत्या, स्त्रीहत्या, पितृहत्या, ब्राह्मणहत्या, अनागोहत्या etc. Of course, the words गोवध, स्त्रीवध, ब्राह्मणवध too may be found occasionally. But the word वध is invariably used in the case of the killing of a person, whether good or bad e.g. कीचकवध, शिशुपालवध, कंसवध, भीष्मवध, द्रोणवध, अभिमन्युवध etc.

Kindly let me know if my interpretation is correct.

Thanks.
