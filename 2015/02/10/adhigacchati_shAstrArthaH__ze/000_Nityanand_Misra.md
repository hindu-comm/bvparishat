Namaste  
  

I am looking for the original source of the verse  
  
adhigacchati śāstrārthaḥ smarati śraddadhāti vā  
yatkṛpāleśatastasmai namo’stu gurave sadā  
अधिगच्छति शास्त्रार्थः स्मरति श्रद्दधाति वा  
यत्कृपालेशतस्तस्मै नमोऽस्तु गुरवे सदा  
  

The first half is cited in the *Vaiyākaraṇasiddhāntakaumudī* on PS 3-1-87 (कर्मवत्कर्मणा तुल्यक्रियः), and the *Bālamanoramā* gives the second half.
