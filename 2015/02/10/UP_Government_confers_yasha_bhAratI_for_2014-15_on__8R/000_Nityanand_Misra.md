Namaste  
  

I would like to inform the list members that Jagadguru Rāmānandācārya Svāmī Rāmabhadrācārya was conferred the Yaśa Bhāratī award for the year 2014-2015 by the Government of Uttar Pradesh at Lucknow on February 9 2015 (yesterday). The Yaśa Bhāratī is the highest civilian award given by the Government of Uttar Pradesh. The award carries a citation and Rs. 11 lakh.  
  

News coverage on Dainik Jagaran's inextlive website:  
<http://inextlive.jagran.com/yash-bharti-samman-60197>
