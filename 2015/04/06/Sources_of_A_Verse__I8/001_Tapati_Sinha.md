I think the verse is as follows:

Sarve Bhavantu Sukhinam  
  
Sarve Santu Niramayah  
  
Sarve Bhadrani Pashyantu  
  
Maa Kashchit dukhabaag Bhayet  
  
Om shantih Shantih Shantih  
  
May all be happy;  
May all be without disease;  
May all have well-being;  
May none have misery of any sort.  
— Brihadaaranyaka Upanishad 1.4.14  
  
ऊँ सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनाम, सर्वे सन्तु निरामया  
सर्वे भद्रणिपश्यन्तु मा, कश्चिदुख भाग भवेत
