gītin, गीतिन् a. (-नी f.) One who recites in a singing manner; गीती शीघ्री शिरःकम्पी*तथा लिखितपाठकः*Śik.32.  
This verse is found in the पाणिनीयशिक्षा the authorship of which is ascribed to पाणिनि, though it can be debatable and the disqualification was at least by the late of the composition of this work, The date of Panini or later could be the date of this verse as the authorship tentatively accepted and if not accepted, it could be been composed or the verse could have been interpolated at later date.
