Chinmaya International Foundation (CIF) in Kerala and Manipal University (MU) are jointly organizing a two-week international Summer School this year for the university students and scholars on**The Word and the World – A Philosophical Inquiry****fromJuly 13-25, 2015.

[Prof Shrinivasa Varakhedi](http://www.ksu.ac.in/en/administration/vice-chancellor/), (Vice Chancellor) - Karnataka Sanskrit University and[Prof Ashok Aklujkar](http://www.asia.ubc.ca/people1/ashok-aklujkar/), University of British Columbia, Canada will lead the School as Course Directors and many eminent scholars will be delivering lectures and conduct intensive discussions on different aspects of Indian philosophies.

Please let us know if you would be interested and also share this with your friends, colleagues, and international office. Individual participation of students is welcome. Also we encourage groups of studentsled by international offices of institutes/universitiesto participate in this program.

For more details, click here[Summer School July 2015 - The Word and the World: A Philosophical Inquiry](http://data.axmag.com/data/201412/20141223/U107002_F317846/FLASH/index.html)for the**brochure**and those interested may please fill the[Application Form](http://chinfo.org/mu-cif/applicationform.php). Please feel free to contact us for any information.

You may be interested in seeing the success of our first summer school program on**The Living Philosophies and Cultures of India.**please visit[www.chinfo.org/summerschool2014](http://www.chinfo.org/summerschool2014).
