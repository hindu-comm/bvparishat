The same rule Vārttika is cited by Mallinātha in the Sañjīvinī on Raghuvaṃśa 3.21. The edition I have (2002: Chaukhambha Publishers, edited by H D Velankar) has the reading बल in the Sañjīvinī instead of बाल in the Kaumudī  
  
**बल**मूललघ्वलमङ्गुलीनां वा लो रत्वमापद्यते  
**bāla**mūlalaghvalamaṅgulīnāṃ vā lo ratvamāpadyate
