धर्मशास्त्रम् (कल्पः) - आयुर्वेदः - ज्योतिषम्

  

In most of the prescriptions / restrictions across Indian cultural texts the benefits are both mundane and spiritual .

  

The food restrictions , during ग्रहणम् , on the mundane front , are meant for maintaining good health -

  

what is health ?

  

mentally , physically and socially well being .

  

विघ्नभूता यदा रोगाः प्रादुर्भूताः शरीरिणाम् ।

तपोपवासाध्ययनब्रह्मचर्यव्रताजुषाम् ॥ -- चरकसंहिता , सूत्रस्थानम् 1-6

  

Health problems started towards the end of कृतयुग ।

  

Today people have everything except health.

  

रुजति = दुःखयति इति रोगः ।

  

आयुर्वेद follows - प्रक्षाळनाद्धि पङ्कस्य दूरादस्पर्शनं वरम् ( prevention is better than cure) . Even Patanjali (महाभाष्यम्) says - नास्य किञ्चित् रुजति रोगः ।

  

उपवास is one of them (preventive steps enumerated above ) .

  

What is उपवास ?

  

उपावृत्तस्य पापेभ्यः सहवासो गुणे हि यः।

उपवासः स विज्ञेयः न शरीरस्य शोषणम् ॥

  

पापेभ्यः = क्रोधादिभ्यः (लक्षणा - पापकारकेभ्यः) ; गुणे = सत्यादौ ।

  

If one is composed and follows truth etc that is called उपवास , but not weakening the body simply by food restrictions .

  

According to Indian tradition food is only twice a day .

  

सुश्रुत (सूत्रस्थानम् - 1-31, 32, 34) explains that the वैद्यकम् (Medicine and Surgery) deals with both मनस् and शरीरम् , as they are the अधिष्ठानम् ( base) of रोगs --

  

तद् दुःखसंयोगा व्याधय उच्यन्ते ।

ते चतुर्विधाः - आगन्तवः , शारीराः , मानसाः , स्वाभाविकाश्चेति ।

त एते मनश्शरीराधिष्ठानाः ।

  

( it is today that the English Medicine recognized the importance of मनस् compared to शरीरम् )

  

Today most of the people do not think about their मनस् and try to administer powerful injections and consume antibiotics, which are dangerous to all vital organs.

  

लंघनं परमौषधम् (skipping the meal is the best medicine)

  

Milk and milk products cause increased कफ ( phlegm) - चरकम्

  

Hot milk does not cause कफ।

  

There should not be any undigested milk left in the stomach by the time one goes to sleep (even any food - so one should finish his meal two hours before going for sleep).

  

चरकसंहिता (सूत्रस्थानम्19-1) enumerates the diseases caused by क्षीरम् -

  

अष्टौ क्षीरदोषा इति वैवर्ण्यं वैगन्ध्यं वैरस्यं पैच्छिल्यं फेनसंघातो रौक्ष्यं गौरवम् अतिस्नेहश्च ।

  

Why to select milk specifically ?

  

Generally , on a day of उपवास people take milk and therefore it is specially mentioned . This is one of the reasons .  

  

On spiritual front , मन्त्रोपदेश and मन्त्रजप is preferred on the day of ग्रहणम् as it gives more vigorous results .

  

In all such matters ऋषिवचनम् , which is said following योगिप्रत्यक्षम् is taken as an unquestioned authority --

  

मन्त्रायुर्वेदवच्च तत्प्रामाण्यम् , आप्तप्रामाण्यात् (न्यायसूत्रम् 2-1-68)

  

वेद is to be taken as authoritative , just like the मन्त्रs (for serpent etc in Atharvaveda) and आयुर्वेद ( which are proved to be effective) as they are said by आप्तs (रागादिवशादपि यः नान्यथावादी सः आप्तः - चरके पतञ्जलिः).

  

The above सूत्रम् is applicable to वेदाङ्गs , दर्शनs etc.

  

As far as ज्योतिषम् is concerned --

  

रवि is the cause of शरीरम् and चन्द्र is for मनस् --

  

आत्मा प्रभावः शक्तिश्च पितृचिन्ता रवेः फलम् ।

मनो बुद्धिं प्रसादं च मातृचिन्ता च चन्द्रमाः ॥ -- कारकनिघण्टुः , मुहूर्तदीपिका

  

चन्द्रमा मनसो’जायत - पुरुषसूक्तम् , all Vedas.

  

So for a healthy person it is good , to maintain balance between मनस् and शरीरम् , to be on fasting before and during ग्रहणम्।

  

In Indian tradition / culture both आरोग्यम् and धर्म are intertwined , just like in the case of भाषा and धर्म ।

  

Healthy people are required for a healthy and peaceful society.

  

एकक्रिया द्व्यर्थिकरी बभूव
