The Vaiṣṇava view is quite different from above. Tapta-mudra is considered by Vaiṣṇavasampradāya-s to be ordained by śruti, smṛti, and purāṇa. In the 86th stanza of the*Bhaktamāla* (c. 1600 CE), Nābhādāsa while eulogizing Kamalakara Bhaṭṭa (of the Madhva sampradāya) says this about the *tapta-mudra:*

  

*śruti smṛtī saṃmata purāna taptamudrā dhārī bhujā* (BM 86)  

  

Hindi translation from the [Mūlarthabodhini commentary](http://jagadgururambhadracharya.org/works/bmm/pada086.php):कमलाकरभट्टने श्रुतिसम्मत, स्मृतिसम्मत और पुराणसम्मत तप्तमुद्राको अपनी भुजापर धारण किया था।
