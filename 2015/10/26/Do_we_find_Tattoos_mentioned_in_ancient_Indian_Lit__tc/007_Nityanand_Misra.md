The mudrā made daily using sandalwood paste is called **śītalamudrā***,* in contrast with **taptamudrā** for which a hot metallic stamp is used. Both *taptamudrā*-s and *śītalamudrā*-s are usedtraditionally by Vaiṣṇava-s. And both are different from a tattoo.
