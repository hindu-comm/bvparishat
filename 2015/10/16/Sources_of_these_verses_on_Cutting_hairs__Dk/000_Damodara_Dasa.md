Hare Krishna.  
  
Dear vidvajjanas,  
I found these verses on ksaura karma and need original sources of it  
if anyone has some information on it:  
  
saptArkirekam ahimadyutirAyuSastu jnA pancasaptavidurindrA  
gurustrimAsAn ekAdazAsura guruzca vivardhayanti\|\|  
"If shaving done on Tuesday 11 months life reduces, saturday 7 months,  
Sunday 1 month. Vednesday increases 5 months and monday increases 7  
months, Thursday 3 months, friday 11 months increases."  
(quoted by Satyanarayana Acarya in DirghAyuSa rahasya, poornaprajna vidyapeeth)  
  
mAsi mAsi grhasthasya pakSe pakSe yajvanAm\|  
Rtu Rtu yatInAm tu yatheccham brahmacAriNAm\|\|  
(quoted by Satyanarayana Acarya in DirghAyuSa rahasya, poornaprajna vidyapeeth)
