The Padma awards for 2015 were presented by the Honourable President of India Sh. Pranab Mukherjee at Rashtrapati Bhavan yesterday (March 30). Among those awarded were Sanskrit scholars Jagadguru Swamī Rāmabhadrācārya (PadmaVibhūṣaṇa) and VāmadevaŚāstrī also known as David Frawley (PadmaBhūṣaṇa).

  

Links to news articles and photos from the event are below:

  

***Times of India***

Bharat Ratna for Malviya; Padma Vibhushan for Advani, Badal

Times News Network, new Delhi, March 31, 2015

*... President Pranab Mukherjee gave away the honours to senior political leaders L K Advani, Parkash Singh Badal, sportspersons P V Sindhu, Arunima Singh, academician David Frawley and film director Sanjay Leela Bhansali. ... Among the Padma Vibhushan awardees were Advani, Badal and Jagadguru Swami Rambhadracharya.*

Bharat Ratna conferred on Madan Mohan Malaviya

Express News Service, New Delhi, March 31, 2015

*The President also bestowed the Padma Vibhusan on BJP stalwart L K Advani, Punjab Chief Minister Parkash Singh Badal and Sanskrit grammar expert, Jagadguru Swami Rambhadracharya.*
