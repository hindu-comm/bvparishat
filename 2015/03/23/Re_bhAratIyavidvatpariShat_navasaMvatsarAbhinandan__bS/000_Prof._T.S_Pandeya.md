नवसंवत्सराभिनन्दनम्  
द्वासप्तत्यधिके सहस्रयुगले संवत्सरे कीलके  
वर्षेशो हि शनिः कुजश्च सचिवः शस्येशजीवस्तथा।  
धान्येशश्च बुधो निशाकरवरो मेघाधिपः संस्थितः  
स्याद्राष्ट्रे विभवं भवे च भवताद् भव्यान्विता बन्धुता॥  
May opulence and prosperity grow in our country and noble friendship  
and fraternity strengthen in the world, In this New Saṁvatsara  
(2072) named kīlaka, where Saturn is placed as the king and the Mars  
as the minister, Jupiter as the lord of food grains, Mercury as the  
lord of wealth and the moon as the lord of rain.  
आरोग्यं प्रथतां सुभिक्षमहिता सम्पत्सदा वर्धतां  
पर्जन्यः परितः सुवृष्टिकलितः सूयेत शस्यं सदा।  
गावो दुग्धभरा धरा सुमधुरा धाराभिरापः परा  
सुज्ञानश्रियमातनोतु भुवने संवत्सरो नूतनः॥  
May healthiness titivate; May the wealth of abundant harvest grow up  
; May the cloud help producing plenty of crops everywhere ; May there  
milky cows and earth beautiful with the stream of water ; May the new  
year spread the beauty of good knowledge all over the world.  
  
Sugyan Kumar Mahanty
