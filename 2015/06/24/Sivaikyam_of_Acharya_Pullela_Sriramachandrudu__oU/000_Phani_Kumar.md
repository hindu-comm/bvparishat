Mahamahopadhyaya Acharya Pullela Sriramchandrudu passed away at 4 pm in hyderabad this evening. He was 88 years old.

Prof Sriramachandrudu was a sanskrit scholar of national renown. He has published over 175 books in sanskrit. He was the recipient of Padmasri and Presidents award for his contribution to sanskrit literature. He was born in Konaseema in East godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. He joined the osmania university and served the sanskrit department as professor and HoD. He was also the director of the sanskrit Academy for some time

His death is an irreparable loss to the world of sanskrit literature.
