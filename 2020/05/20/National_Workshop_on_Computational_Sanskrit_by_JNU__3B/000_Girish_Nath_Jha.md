***National Workshop on Digitization and Development of E- Resources for Sanskrit (27-31 May)***

**(jointly organized by Jawaharlal Nehru University and Delhi University)**

  

National Workshop on Digitization and Development of E- Resources for Sanskrit is being jointly organized by the School of Sanskrit and Indic Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi and Department of Sanskrit, University of Delhi during **May 27-31, 2020 (11am-2pm)** through Video Conferencing mode. The major objective of the workshop is to provide hands-on training for e-resource creation and tools development for Sanskrit. The topics will include (but not limited to) digitization of Sanskrit texts including manuscripts, preservation and accessibility mechanisms,development of critical tools.**E-certificate will be given to participants who successfully complete the 5-day workshop.**
