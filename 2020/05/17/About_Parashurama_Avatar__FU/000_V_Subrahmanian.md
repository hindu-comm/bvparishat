In the Parashurama Avatar we hear that his 'darpa' was quelled by Rama.
Would it be proper to project Bhagavan as someone endowed with darpa to be quelled by himself? In all other avatara-s (excepting perhaps Balarama), the Lord is portrayed as blemishless, full of venerable qualities.
