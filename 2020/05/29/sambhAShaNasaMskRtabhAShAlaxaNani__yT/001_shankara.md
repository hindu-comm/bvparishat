You may find the following books and articles useful for your study / research on 'spoken Sanskrit'.

  

1\. 'Spoken Sanskrit in India, A Study of Sentence Patterns' by R.N. Aralikatti published by Kendriya Sanskrit Vidyapitha, Tirupati in 1989

  

2\. Chapter 3 titles 'Spoken Sanskrit' in 'History and Pre-history of Sanskrit' by Sukumar Sen 1958  

<https://archive.org/details/HistorySanskrit/page/n46/mode/1up>

  

3\. 'Vyavahara Samskritam' by Krishnasastri Kanitkar in Sampurnananda Commemorative Volume 2 published in 1993  

<https://archive.org/details/VishwaDrishtiDrSampurnandaCommemorativeVolumeIISampurnandaUniversity_201710/page/n328/mode/1up>
