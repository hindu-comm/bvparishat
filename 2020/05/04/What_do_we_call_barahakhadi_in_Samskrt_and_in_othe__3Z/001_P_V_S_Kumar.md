"barahakhadi" in Samskrit can be called as 'गुणिताक्षराणि' (meaning 'augmented letters') . As the consonants get augmented with the मात्राs of vowels, the resultants are called'गुणिताक्षराणि' \|

  

In Telugu, we call गुणिंतालु (గుణింతాలు).
