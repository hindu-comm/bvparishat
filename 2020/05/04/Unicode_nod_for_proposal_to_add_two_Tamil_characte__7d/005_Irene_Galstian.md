This cutting out of sounds is alarming, since it limits the possibilities, while pretending that nothing is lost, and even that something (being 'modern'?) is gained. Hopefully the publication will receive enough letters of objection to publish a response from someone who recognises the importance of the full gamut of sounds. My teacher insisted that khechari mudra should be diligently practised by speakers, to bring out the fullness of each articulation place, and here's someone saying let's cripple the language...
