This seems an appropriate occasion to bring to the attention of scholars here Navlipi (<https://navlipi.org/>),

described as thefirst universal, phonemic script that accommodates phonemic variations in all the languages

of the world. Developed by a technologist running mostly on enthusiasm, research and spare time - am sure

he will appreciate any and all scholarly feedback on his creation.
