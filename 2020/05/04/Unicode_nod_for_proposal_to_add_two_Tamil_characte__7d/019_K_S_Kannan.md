vAla brings in no pejoration.

It means tail, no doubt.

But, monkeys would love to flaunt their tails,

and perhaps consider their tails their *uttamAn'ga*!

  

If Mr. Good-neck is Su-grIva,

Mr. \[Excellent\] Tail is VAlin

*(ini-pratyaya* in *prAshastyArtha).*
