The exercise of poetry can become contagious creative exercise. Sanskrit  
poetry has a term ' Chitra-kavya' interpreted as ' ornate word jugglery  
dominant poetic pedantry' !  
Shatvadhani R.Ganesh seems to have triggered a new trend in presenting  
this model in a different light of ' Composing a poetry that resonates a  
picture; Painint a picture that resonates a creative idea. Dr.Shankar and  
others have made more experiments in this lane.
