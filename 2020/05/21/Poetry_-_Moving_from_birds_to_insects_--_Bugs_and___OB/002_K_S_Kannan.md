There is none to beat Dr. Shankar

in the simplicity of his *citra-kAvya*  

(in the proper sense, not BVK's version of it).

  

The poems of even some of the best poets seem belaboured

as against Shankar's effortless effluence,

a matchless mark of a glaring genius.
