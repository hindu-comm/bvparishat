नमः शङ्करमहोदयाय,

  

What we today call as [occultations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Occultation) of नक्षत्राणि by ग्रहाः, was considered earlier as ताप्यते, उपतापयति, गृह्णाति, etc.

In fact the name ग्रहः is a short form of ताराग्रहः, (तारा गृह्णातीति).
