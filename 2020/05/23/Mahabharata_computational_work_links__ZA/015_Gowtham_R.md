The most problems/issues mentioned in the previous conversations are related to the 'Search results'. Here, I would like to tell you about the specialty of the "Advance option". If the word ’व्यास" given to the search, the result shows only the exact matches of व्यास and not व्यासस्य or व्यासे. If the Advance option is selected, then it shows the other related results like व्यासः, व्यासम्, व्यासागमनम्, etc. Please try with the Advance option. Even, if any result is not found, pleaselet us know.
