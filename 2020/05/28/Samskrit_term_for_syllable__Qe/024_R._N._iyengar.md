Thank you Prof. Deshpande for the reference to Siddheshwar Varma's 'thinking' book on the subject. I was looking for such an original work on Akshara by an Indian author for a long time. AkSara is fundamental to several uniquely original Indic ideas including Time. Varma's discussion on the musical notes of SAmagAna and the three RV accents is useful to some work we are doing at CAHC. Thoughshort, the book is a refreshingly original research monograph. (The book available on archive is of poor quality but readable with some effort.)
