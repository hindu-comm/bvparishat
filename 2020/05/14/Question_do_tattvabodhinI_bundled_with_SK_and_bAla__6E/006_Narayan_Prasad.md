Sir, I do not have problem regarding script. If I am not conversant with any scriptthat has certain work of my interest, I learn that.

That is how I learnt theशारदा script and converted the Sanskrit dhAtupATha written in this script in a manuscript that is availableat BORI. Pt Yudhisthir Mimansak could not use this manuscript while editing theक्षीरतरंगिणी, because he could not find anybody who could read this script.

  

I give herewith a few sutras from क्रियापरिच्छेदमु of बालव्याकरणमु, which I read more than 20 years ago.

<http://www.bhimalapuram.co.in/oldbooks/grandhamulu/balavyakaranamu.html>

1. धातुवुनगु.  

2\. वर्तमानंबुन लट्टगु.  
3. भूतंबुन लिट्टगु.  
4. भाविनि लृट्टगु.  
5. तद्धर्मादुलंदु लाट्टगु.  
6. आशीश्शापसं प्रार्थनविधुलंदु लुट्टगु  
7. व्यतिरेकंबुन लङियगु.  
8. लकारंबुनकु डु ज्रु वु रु नु मुञ्ञगु.

  

The last one appears to be the Telugu equivalent of Panini'sलस्य, तिप्-तस्-झि-सिप्-थस्-थ- ... -इट्-वहि-महिङ्।
