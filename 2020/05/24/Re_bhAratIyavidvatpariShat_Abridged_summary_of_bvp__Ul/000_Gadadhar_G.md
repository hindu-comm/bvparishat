\*About S Ramachandran:\* Stuyding Astronomy is his hobby and predicting weather is his passion. For the last 15 years, he has been studying the weather charts of thepast century and combining that past information and blends it with the astronomy based formulas to predict our weather patterns. Based on his knowledge of Indian astronomy, he has now identified more than 45 formulas for predicting the weather. His reports have been published in many news papers over the years.
