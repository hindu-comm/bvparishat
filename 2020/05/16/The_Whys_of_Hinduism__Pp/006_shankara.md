Irene ji,

  

I have hard copies of all the three vols. All the three books are very much useful.  

  

While discussing a topic, the author provides reference Veda samhita, Brahmanas, Aranyakas, Puranas, Smritis, Kalpa texts, etc. Thus the book contains references from the complete range of Vedic literature.

  

These volumes are available for sale at exoticindia website.

<https://www.exoticindiaart.com/book/details/most-comprehensive-introduction-to-puranas-ever-HAA633/>

<https://www.exoticindiaart.com/book/details/most-comprehensive-introduction-to-vedas-HAA634/>

  

A scan of Purana Digdarshan is available online at

<https://archive.org/details/PuranaDigdarsanMadhavacharyaSastri1957_201805>
