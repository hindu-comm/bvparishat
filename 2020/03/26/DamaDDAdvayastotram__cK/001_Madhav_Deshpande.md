Dear Satyan Sharma Ji,

  

  A beautiful stotra composition. I have one grammatical question. In the first two lines \[जगदनुभाविन् परमनियन्तो,

निखिलविकल्पविहीन डमड्डम्\], I assume the form नियन्तो is from नियन्तृ.
The vocative form नियन्तर् will not change to नियन्तो before निखिल... The reading in my understanding will be जगदनुभाविन् परमनियन्तर्निखिलविकल्पविहीन डमड्डम्. With best regards,

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
