A distraction from Coronavirus

  

दृशापि स्पृश मां मा त्वं तिष्ठ दूरं वरानने ।

कोरोनानिर्गमादूर्ध्वं मीलनं नौ भविष्यति ।।

  

O Beautiful Lady, please stay away and do not touch me even with your glances. We shall get together after the Coronavirus goes away.
