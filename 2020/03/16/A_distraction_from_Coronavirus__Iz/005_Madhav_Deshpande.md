A distraction from the Coronavirus

  

स्वयंवरे वधू: काचिद् मालां क्षिप्त्वा विनिर्गता ।

“दुर्दिनेऽस्मिन् करोनाया वरयामि न कञ्चन” ।।

  

At the ceremony of choosing a husband, the bride threw down the garland and went away saying: "During such a difficult time due to Coronavirus, I will not choose anyone."
