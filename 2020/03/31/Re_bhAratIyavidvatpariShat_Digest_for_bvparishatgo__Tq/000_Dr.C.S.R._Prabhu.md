Vasanas drive Karma of a person in a given Varna.He attains perfection in fulfillment in his own Svadharma without attachment through Karma Yoga . He can progress parallely in spiritual path ,independent of his profession .We have many examples like Dharma Vyadha,Valmiki or many saints who were enlightened even though not belonging to Brahmana Varna.This is my understanding.
