Respected Vidwans,  
  
There is an excellent resource in kannada by Prof. S. K. Ramachandra Rao called ಅವಧೂತ (Avadhuta). It is book of 129 pages with 3 chapters. The first chapter is called ಅವತರಣಿಕೆ (Introduction), the second chapter is ಅವಧೂತೋಪನಿಷತ್ತು (Avadhutopanishat) and the third chapter is ಮಹೋಪನಿಷತ್ತಿನಲ್ಲಿ ಅವಧೂತ ಚರ್ಯೆ (Avadhuta's behavior in Mahopanishat).  
  
Regards,
