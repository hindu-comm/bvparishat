Inspired and encouraged by Prof Deshpande-ji and shrImAn GSS Murthy-ji,

here is a verse on the “Three-Monkeys” being unable to do what they are famously known for, due to Corona.

  

——————

**नाहं निधातुं वदनेंsगुलिम् मे**

**शक्नोमि पश्यन् अपि विस्मयेन \|**

**कर्णाक्षिवक्त्रै र्विनिवृत्तहस्तम्**

**\*कपित्रयं चाकुलितं करोनात् \|\|**

  

अन्वय:

पश्यन् अपि      in spite of seeing

विस्मयेन        with surprise,

कपित्रयं        the monkey-triad(which)

करोनात् आकुलितं  is fearful of Corona

च विनिवृत्तहस्तम्  and has removed-hands

कर्ण अक्षि वक्त्रैः   from ears, eyesand mouth

अहं न शक्नोमि   I am unable

निधातुं          to place

अंगुलिम्        finger

मे वदने        on my mouth{a gestureexpressing surprise}

             (due to Corona)

  

**\***The first akShara of the last pAda happened to be a laghu ("क")...

and this padya turned out to be in ”बाला” वृत्त instead of Indravajra!

I came to know of this serendipitously.
