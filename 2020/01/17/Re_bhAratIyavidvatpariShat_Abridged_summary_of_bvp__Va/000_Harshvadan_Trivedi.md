Dr. Hari Narayan Tiwari is a prominent expert on Sanskrit vyakaran. He is credited with translating the entire Vyakaran Mahabhashya by Patanjali, entire Laghu shabdendu shekhar,by Nagesh Bhatt, entire Vakyapadiyam by Bhartrihari and entire Vyuttpattivad in hindi. He is the first person to translate these texts in the national language in entirety. The other day, I was astounded to find a verbatim copy of the few passages from the preface of Pt. Suyranarayan Sukla's Vakyapadiyam vyakhya in the preface of Dr. Tiwari's hindi translation of Vakypadiyam. Look at the attached file.
