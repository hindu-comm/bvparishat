विद्यालयश्चिताभूमिश्चण्डाला गुरवस्तथा ।

पिशाचा दुर्धियश्छात्रा दह्यं वाण्याः कलेवरम् ॥

This University is a cemetery. The teachers therein are chandAlas. The students are wicked spirits. And Sarasvati's divine form is the body that must be burnt.
