Hare Krishna.  
  
Visnu Smrti is another good treatise on Dandaniti.  
  
Also Manu and Yajnavalkya are foremost authorities on dharmasastras  
and both their works have a dedicated section for vyavahara and  
prayascitta kandas which cover majority of rules for ruling a state  
and thus dandaniti also.  
  
Thankyou,  
Hari Guru Vaisnava das,  
Damodara das  
  
ps. For downloading these sastras, just go to [archive.org](http://archive.org) and search  
them. They are freely available in English as well as original  
sanskrit.
