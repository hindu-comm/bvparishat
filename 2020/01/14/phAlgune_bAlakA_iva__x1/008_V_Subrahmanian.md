Even I think it could be related to Holi. I also got a reference that might help:स्कन्दपुराणीयपुरुषोत्तममाहात्म्ये त्रिंशाध्याये । “फाल्गुन्यां क्रीडनं कुर्य्यात् दोलायां मम भूपते ! ॥ ” इति कृत्यतत्त्वम् ॥   The word 'kridanam' could hold a clue. Instead of doing the vrata, children would keep playing. Phalguni is also stated to be a pilgrimage centre in the Bhagavatam. Going to a pilgrimage to that place, with parents, while the elders do what is to be done, the boys while away their time there playing. So this could be the comparison in the subhashita. <https://kosha.sanskrit.today/word/sa/phAlguna>
