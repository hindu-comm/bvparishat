Please find attached updated information on the 4^(th) edition of our course on **Indian Knowledge Systems**, an elective course at IIT Gandhinagar with the theme **The ancient “idea of India”**. The course will be co-taught by some ten scholars, six or seven of whom will visit us from various partsof India.

**Prof. Srinivas Reddy's lecture in the IKS course, 9 January 2020:**

This presentation will explore*artha*as a polyvalent concept within Indian cultural history. In additionto a review of the*Arthaśāstra*of Kauṭilya/Cāṇakya, we will explore broaders pan-Indic themes related to governance, political theory, moral instruction and ethics. Perspectives will be drawn from Sanskrit, Tamil and Telugu sources to provide a multidimensional analysisof historical developments, convergences and disparities.
