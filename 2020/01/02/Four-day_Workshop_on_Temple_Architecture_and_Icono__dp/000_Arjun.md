Amrita Darshanam, International Centre for Spiritual Studies (ICSS),  
Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bangalore takes great pleasure to inform  
you that the Centre is organizing a Four-day Workshop on Temple  
Architecture and Iconography (Level 3) between 1 and 4 February 2020.  
  
Prof. Dr. MS Krishna Murthy, Dr. Manoj Gundanna and Ar. Prof.  
Madhusudhanan Kalaichelvan will be adorning the workshop as the  
resource persons.  
  
A detailed study of the Temple Art, Architecture and Iconography of  
the following periods will be carried out:  
  
1. The Buddhist Period  
2. The Chola Period  
3. The Rashtrakuta Period  
4. The Nolamba and the Ganga Period  
  
In addition to this, an overview of Texts on Indian Art and  
Architecture and Iconometry will be given.  
  
Please note that you may register for the Level 3 workshop even if you  
have not attended the levels 1 and 2. The video recordings of all the  
levels will be made available by the Centre.  
  
For more details and for registration please visit our website:
