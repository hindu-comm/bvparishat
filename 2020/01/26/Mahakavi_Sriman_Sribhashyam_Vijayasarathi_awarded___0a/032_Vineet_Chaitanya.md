In ISCII standard many additional symbols were added to Enhance Devanagari script.

For an introduction to ISCII please see:

<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Script_Code_for_Information_Interchange>.

It was aimed that anyone familiar with these additional scripts could read the texts of other Indian languages fairly accurately.
