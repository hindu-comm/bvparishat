\> The letter La is perhaps more used in kannaDa than in Telugu. Some of the La sound is absorbed by Da in Telugu, e.g. chOLa -> chODa. kaLa is an exception as another native word kala, meaning dream, already exists in dESi telugu. As an aside, the TV anchors nowadays pronounce La as la! They pronounce peLli
