Annamayya's lyric has,

  

" If staying in caves makes one a sage, there is no greater save than lion"

  

If growing beard makes one a tapasvi, there is no greater sage than a bear"

  

If living nude makes one a vandaag, there is no greater vandaag than a child"

  

and so on.
