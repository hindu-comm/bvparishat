10.093.06

  

# उ॒त नो॑ दे॒वाव॒श्विना॑ शु॒भस्पती॒ धाम॑भिर्मि॒त्रावरु॑णा उरुष्यतां । म॒हः स रा॒य एष॒तेऽति॒ धन्वे॑व दुरि॒ता ॥ 

Which Geldner translates as ,Und die beiden Götter Asvin, die Herren der Schönheit, Mitra und Varuna sollen in Person uns schützen. Zu grossen Reichtümern kommt er, an den Fährlichkeiten vorüber wie der Reisende an den wüsten Strecken.

Ie,And the two gods Asvin, the lords of beauty, Mitra and Varuna are supposed to protect us in person. He comes to great riches, past the perils like the **traveler on the desert routes.**
