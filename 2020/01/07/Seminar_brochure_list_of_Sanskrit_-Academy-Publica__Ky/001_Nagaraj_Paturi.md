Indic Academy is pleased to extend travel grants for the upcomingTwo-day National Seminar On Publications of Sanskrit Academy, Hyderabad to attend/ present papers. We are happy to support three scholars, by way of a travel grant of upto ₹ 10,000 each or actuals whichever is less.

Sanskrit Academy, Osmania University, Hyderabad is organizing a Two-Day National Seminar on the Publications of Sanskrit Academy, Hyderabad. The seminar will be held in Sanskrit Academy, Osmania University, Hyderabad, tentatively on 6th and 7th March, 2020.

<https://www.indictoday.com/grants/travel-grant-for-national-seminar-on-publications-of-sanskrit-academy-at-hyderabad/>

  

Applications for travel grants should be sent to[nam...@indica.org.in]()by 25th February with profile/CV and a Statement of Purpose. Acceptance of their paper by Sanskrit Academy is mandatory for the paper presenters applying for this grant. Selected candidates are expected to submit an article about experiences and learning at the seminar, which may be published on[www.indictoday.com.](http://www.indictoday.com/)
