Poetry

  

कवि: काव्यं करोतीति केचिदूचुर्विपश्चित: ।

करोति काव्यमात्मानं लेखनी केवलं कवे: ।।

  

Some wise folks say that a poet composes poetry. Actually, the poem makes itself and the poet merely holds the pen.
