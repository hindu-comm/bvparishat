Poetry

  

यत्र शब्दार्थयोरस्ति सौहार्दं रससिद्धये ।

रमन्ते रसिकास्तत्र काव्यास्वादमधुव्रता: ।।

  

The connoisseurs, dedicated to the enjoyment of the sweetness of poetry, delight when words and meanings join in friendship to produce aesthetic pleasure.
