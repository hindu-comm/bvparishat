Poetry

  

न मुखादुद्गतं काव्यं न कर्णं याति कस्यचित् ।

हृदयाद् हृदयं यान्ति काव्यानीति मतिर्मम ।।

  

A poem does not arise from someone’s mouth and reach someone’s ear. I believe that poetry goes from heart to heart.
