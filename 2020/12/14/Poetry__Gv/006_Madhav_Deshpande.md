Poetry

  

पदानि रसपूर्णानि रसिकान् रमयन्ति चेत्

कवेर्द्राक्षाफलानीव स एव सुकविर्भवेत् ।।

  

If the words of a poet filled with juices like grapes delight the connoisseurs, only then can he become a good poet.
