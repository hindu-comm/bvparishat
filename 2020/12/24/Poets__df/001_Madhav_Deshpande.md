ब्रह्मणा निर्मितं विश्वं सुखदु:खमयं सदा ।

करुणोऽपि कवेर्लोके रसत्वेनोपचर्यते ।।

  

The world created by the Creator is always filled with joys and sorrows. But in the world of a poet, even \[the experience of\] pathos can be given a delightful treatment.
