Non-Poetry

  

नीरसा यत्र सर्वेऽर्था: पदानि स्खलितानि च ।

तत्काव्यं शृण्वतां लोके केवलं शीर्षवेदना ।।

  

There can only be a headache for the people listening to poetry where all the meanings are joyless and words faltering.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
