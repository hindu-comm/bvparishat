नमो वः

Attempted an anuloma-pratiloma verse. The first 2 lines of the verse are addressed to Shiva. The same 2 lines read backwards are addressed to Vishnu

नटपावन दान्ताक्ष त्वं सदातन भाव्यसे । (शिव)

सेव्यभा नतदास त्वं क्षन्ता दानवपाटन ॥ (विष्णु)

Shiva - नटपावन - O Bestower of grace on dancers! दान्ताक्ष - O One wth the senses (अक्ष) controlled (दान्त)! सदातन - O Eternal Being त्वं भाव्यसे - I meditate on you

Vishnu -

सेव्यभा - सेव्या भाः यस्य सः - O One whose divine lustre is worthy of worship! नत-दास - O One whose devotees bow down before him! दानव-पाटन - O Vanquisher of asuras! त्वं क्षन्ता - you are a forgiver of our sins

  

Regards

Shankar Rajaraman
