I have seen the Telugu ardhānusvāra previously.

అం - anusvara

అఁ – ardhānusvāra

  

Although Kannada and Telugu scripts are very similar, Kannada does not seem to have (or previously had) this ardhānusvāra.

  

In Hindi, the word for serpent is written as साँप , never as साम्प or सांप.  The pronunciation is different in these three cases - साँप, साम्प and सांप.

  

Is the Telugu అఁ (ardhānusvāra) pronounced like Hindi अँ ?

If I write సాఁప్ will it be pronounced like Hindi साँप ? (I know that there is no such word as సాఁప్ in Telugu.)
