Reminds me that Urdu doesn’t differentiate the anunāsika from the anusvāra. Urdu has only two anunāsika-s, meem and noon (ن,م). The word साँप is written as sānp (سانپ). The nasalized vowel at the end of a word is represented by the noon without its dot. For example, यहाँ is written as یہاں
