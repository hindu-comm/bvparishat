Words, Meanings and Poetry

  

विना शब्दं भवेदर्थ: विनार्थं शब्दसम्भव: ।

शब्दार्थौ सरसौ यत्र तत्र काव्यसमुद्भव: ।।

  

There can be meaning without a word and a word without meaning. However, poetry arises only when the meaning and the word delightfully enhance each other.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
