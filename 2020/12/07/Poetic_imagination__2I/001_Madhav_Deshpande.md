Poetic imagination

  

हृदये कवे: स्फुरन्ती नित्यम् प्रतिभैव कारयति कविताम् ।

हृदये रसिकानां सा प्रवहन्ती भावयति रसनाम् ।।

  

The creative imagination always bursting forth in the heart of the poet causes the appearance of a poem, and that same imagination flowing in the hearts of the connoisseurs causes enjoyment.
