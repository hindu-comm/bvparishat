We have used the Apte's Sanskrit-Hindi dictionary.

It gives the etymological information.

For example, the entry for परागः is

परागःपरागपुं\* \[परा+गम्+ड\]पुष्पम्पुष्पम्

1. पुष्पराज, स्फुटपरागपरागतपंकजम्(शि\* ६/२)(अमरु\* ५४)  
2. धूलि(रघु\* ४/३०)  
3. स्नान के पश्चात् सेवन किया जाने वाला सुगंधित चूर्ण  
4. चन्दन  
5. सूर्य या चन्द्रमा का ग्रहण  
6. यश, प्रसिद्धि  
7. स्वाधीनता

  

So you see that the analysis of परागःis given as \[परा+गम्+ड\]

Now the question is how do you link the nominative form to the praatipadikam.

  

This we have done manually.

For all the nominative word forms, which are the headwords, we have provided the praatipadikam manually.

  

I hope this answers your question.
