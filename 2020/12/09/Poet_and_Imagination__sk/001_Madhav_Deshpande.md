Poet and Imagination

  

प्रेरयति प्रतिभा कविहृदयं रसपूर्णकाव्यनिर्माणे ।

प्रतिभां विना स कुर्यात् गणनां चणकस्य राशीनाम् ।।

  

The creative imagination inspires the heart of a poet to create a delightful poem. Without it, he will just count the heaps of garbanzo beans.
