Poet and Imagination

  

अप्रातिभस्य काव्यस्य ब्रूहि स्याद्रसना कथम् ।

दण्डकाष्ठं न मूढोऽपि रसहीनं जिघत्सति ।।

  

Tell me how can one enjoy a poem produced without any creative imagination. Not even a fool wants to eat a wooden stick bereft of juice.
