My 1000th Krishna verse and continuing

  

हृदयस्थे हरौ नित्यं रमे रामे मनोरमे ।  
निमीलिताभ्यां नेत्राभ्यां तं पश्यामि दिवानिशम् ।।१०००।।  
  
\[My response\]: "I always rejoice with Krishna, the beautiful embodiment of joy, who resides in my heart. Day and night, I see him with my closed eyes."
