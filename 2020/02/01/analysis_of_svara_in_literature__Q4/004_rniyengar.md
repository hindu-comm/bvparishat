The three svaras U, A and S of Rigveda bifurcated to become the five, six and seven svaras of SAma-gAna. These are indeed the musical notes of a particular rAga ( very close to kAfi/Kharaharapriya) even though the SAma Vedic svaras are denoted as Prathama, Dvitiya, etc. At CAHC we are working on the transition between sacred and classical music, by analysing actual recitations, records and texts. It is a fascinating study.

  

RN Iyengar
