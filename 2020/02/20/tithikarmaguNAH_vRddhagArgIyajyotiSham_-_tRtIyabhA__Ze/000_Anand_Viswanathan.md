The pre-print version (to be published in tattvadīpa, Journal of Academy of Sanskrit Research, Melkote) of the fifth chapter of वृद्धगार्गीयज्योतिषम्, an important unpublished vedāṅgajyotiṣa text is now available at [academia](https://www.academia.edu/42023011/Vṛddhagārgīya_Jyotiṣa_3) and [researchgate](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/339376715_Tithi-karma-gunah_Vrddhagargiya_Jyotisa_3).

  

This chapter is titled तिथिकर्मगुणाः. A discussion on the ancient concept of tithi (before the mathematical definition came into vogue) accompanies the edited text.

  

1\) The present publication is also relevant to the discussion on \[[Tithi-devatā & Homa](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!msg/bvparishat/wr5dRZqQMdI/0Tw8KSYqAgAJ)\] that happened previously in bvp.  

  

2) The preceding chapters of this text can be accessed [here](https://www.academia.edu/39541822/T_A_T_T_V_A_D_%C3%88_P_A_H_Journal_of_Academy_of_Sanskrit_Research_Academy_of_Sanskrit_Research) (Chapters 1,2 and 3 - सांवत्सरनिर्देशः , अङ्गसमुद्देशः and परिभाषा) and [here](https://www.academia.edu/40624384/V%E1%B9%9Bddhag%C4%81rg%C4%ABya_Jyoti%E1%B9%A3a_2_Continued_from_previous_issue_) (Chapter 4 - नक्षत्रकर्मगुणाः)
