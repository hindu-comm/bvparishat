Sri Subbuji,  
  
Here are two very good books in Kannada, published in recent years. Both these are available for buying.  
  
1. ಭಾರತೀಯ ತತ್ತ್ವಶಾಸ್ತ್ರ ಪ್ರವೇಶ (bharatiya tattvashaastra pravesha) by Dr. M. Prabhakara Joshi and Prof. M. A. Hegde. 343 pages, published in year 2008 by Akshara Prakashana (<https://www.aksharaprakashana.com/index.php?route=product/product&path=68&product_id=49>)  
  
2. ಷಡ್ದರ್ಶನಸಂಗ್ರಹ (ShaD-darshana-sangraha) by Shataavadhaani Dr R Ganesh, 282 pages, published by Sahitya Prakashana. 2nd Edition published in 2018. This being a very recent publications is available in bookshops and in online sites like sapnaonline and navakarnatakaonline.  
  
Vardhan
