It is really very simple:  
  
  
vaidikaiḥ karmabhiḥ puṇyair niṣekādir dvijanmanām  
kāryaḥ **śarīra**saṃskāraḥ pāvanaḥ pretya ceha ca (Manusmṛti 2.26)  
  
gārbhair homair jātakarmacauḍamauñjīnibandhanaiḥ  
baijikaṃ gārbhikaṃ caino dvijānām apamṛjyate (Manusmṛti 2.27)  
  
svādhyāyena vratair homais traividyenejyayā sutaiḥ  
mahāyajñaiś ca yajñaiś ca brāhm**īyaṃ** kriyate **tanuḥ** (Manusmṛti 2.28)
