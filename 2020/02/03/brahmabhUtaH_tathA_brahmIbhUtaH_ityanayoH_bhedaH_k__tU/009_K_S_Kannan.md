I am puzzled by the gloss of Korada-ji on  

ब्राह्मीयं क्रियते तनुः of मनु-स्मृति.

  

The word *tanu (=*body*)* is feminine.

How can ब्राह्मीयं (= neuter) qualify that?

  

Is it not more straightforward and simple to take it as

इयं तनुः ब्राह्मी क्रियते?
