To whom it may be concern,

Greting from Paris and Kalpana's organisation!  

  

Kalpana is a not profit organisationl multi-disciplinary arts organization based near of Paris, contributing to the vibrant pluralistic Arts and Culture through its dedication to South Asian Classical arts and also others cultures. Acting in the role of presenters, promoters and producers of various artistic events, Kalpana is in favor of exploring the frontiers in aesthetically pleasing ways between South Asian classical arts and comparable art forms elsewhere in the world.  

  

We develop:

\- some exhibitions: photography, painting, ..

\- Exchange and discussion on thinking, art and literatureevents of different cultures: (site[www.kalpana.fr](http://www.kalpana.fr/))

\- Edition paper and on-line :[www.e-kapana.org](http://www.e-kapana.org/)(site under construction)

\- we are opening some drama, arts school in Paris.

  

For Indian Culture, we develop 4 meeting per year in different approach. If you could recommendus some people to participate.

Also,we publish and edit some articles online and papers. The subject of this year is "Modernity and Tradition".

  

Don't hesitate to contact me for more information.

  

Hope to hear from you as soon as possible!

  

Mr Bala - President of Kalpana's Organisation
