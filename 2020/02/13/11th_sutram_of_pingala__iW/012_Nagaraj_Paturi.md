[[Nagaraj Paturi	2020-02-14, 12:52:08 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/bvparishat/c/iWm6sJs8GuE)]]



In taala, on Tabla or Mridangam , ta, tat are one maatraa each. tat + pause + tat makes the first tat into two maatraas. The pause is filling time and that is how it contributes a maatraa. So time is the determining factor.

  

त कि ट is three maatraas, त क्कि ट is four maatra as. त pause कि ट also is four maatraas.
