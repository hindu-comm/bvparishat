Respected scholars,

Is anyone aware of this kind of alankara in Sanskrit or other indian literature? Even in Gujarati literature I am aware about only one work i.e. vasantavilasaphagu.

  

In this alankara, the end of first pada and start of second pada have similar sounds.

  

  

बाहुलता अति कोमल कमलमृणाल समान ।

जीपइ उदरि पंचानन आनन नही उपमान ॥ ६४ ॥

  

See the internal similarities between  

कोमल and कमल,  

पंचानन and आनन.  

  

Also see another example.

  

अलिजन वसइं अनंत रे वसंत तिहां परधान ।

तरूअर वासनिकेतन केतन किसलसंतान ॥ १७ ॥
