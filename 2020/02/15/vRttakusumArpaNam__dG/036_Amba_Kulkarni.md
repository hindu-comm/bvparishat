Thank you Murthy Garu.

  

From the context of Pingala's suutras, I do not think 'misrau ca' refers to the 'Virahanka/Hemachandra/Fibonnaci'numbers.

Here the context is of the akshara v.rttas, and all the 6 pratyayas - na.s.ta, uddi.s.ta etc deal with the arrangement of akshara v.rttas.

  

Here 'Misrau ca' refers to getting 'n' akshar variations(prastaara) from the 'n-1' akshara prastaara. And 'Misrau ca' says, you do it by adding 'G' and 'L' to the previous one.

So I think there is no reference to the 'total number of prastaaras with specific maatraas'.
