It is *sūtra* 8.21 (*miśrau ca*). Halāyudha: *gakāro gakāreṇa saṃśliṣṭo miśra ucyate, lakāraś ca lakāreṇa; miśrāv iti gakāra-lakārābhyām pratyekam abhisaṃbadhyate* ...  
  
There is a comprehensive study of the *pratyayas* by Ludwig Alsdorf (in German): "*Die Pratyayas. Ein Beitrag zur indischen Mathematik*. In: *Zeitschrift für Indologie und Iranistik*. 9 (1933-34), pp. 97-157:  
<http://menadoc.bibliothek.uni-halle.de/dmg/periodical/titleinfo/152294>  
  
  
See also the analysis of the *pratyaya* section of Ratnākaraśānti's *Chandoratnākāra* by Michael Hahn (in English):  
*Ratnākaraśānti's Chandoratnākāra*. Edited by Michael Hahn. Kathmandu 1982 (Nepal Research Centre. Miscellaneous Papers. 34), pp. 3-19 (s. attachment).  
  
Download of the complete file:
