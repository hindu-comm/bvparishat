Ask Heaven (Tian Wen)

  

Qu Yuan, a Chinese philosopher who lived from 340 to 278 BC wrote

“ At the primordial beginning

 Who was the reporter?

 Before the universe took place

 How could one measure it?”

Ref: Fundamental Forces of Nature

The story of Gauge Fields

By Kerson Huang, World Scientific, New Jersey, 2007

  

Rig Veda in my way of understanding avoids these basic questions by assuming existence of a prior universe- Vishnu asking Brahma to reproduce the universe as it used to be.

  

Is there an equivalent set of questions raised by any Indian Rishi?
