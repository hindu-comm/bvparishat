Poet to his wife न मात्यङ्गेषु ते सुभ्रु लावण्यातिशयो ध्रुवम् । अत एव त्वया पक्वे भोजनेऽपि स दृश्यते ॥ Dear! So excessive is your beauty (लावण्य = beauty/saltiness) that it cannot be contained in your limbs and therefore has spilled over to the food cooked by you
