Benefits of Masks

  

शरीरबन्धने य: स्यात् स गुणो मुखबन्धने ।

इति वैद्योत्तमा: प्राहुर्निपुणं तन्निबोधत ।।

  

The benefit that one gets by covering the whole body is available just by covering one’s mouth. This is what the best of the doctors say.
