Benefits of Masks

  

मुखबन्धे हि भूयांसो गुणा: सन्तीति कथ्यते ।

कलहोपशमे शान्ति: श्रूयतेऽद्य गृहे गृहे ।।

  

There are said to be many virtues in covering one’s mouth. One can hear peace in every house when the quarrels cease.
