> > I wanted to ask if anyone knows the answer to this question. Usually > it is said that an avyaya never changes its form. If that is true, > then why is it that in sandhi karya, they change their form. For > example in Bhagavadgita (9/31), shashvat + shantim changes to > shashvac + chantim. >
> 

  

I think when it is said 'an avyaya never changes its form' what is meant is that it does not undergo changes on the basis of vibhakti, number, gender and tense. In a sandhi too the fundamental form of the avyaya word is not lost since we are able to realize it when the sandhi is parsed.
