What to do?

  

“किं कर्म किमकर्मेति कवयोऽप्यत्र मोहिता:” ।

अकर्मणा मया स्थेयं गृहबद्धेन सर्वदा ।।

  

Even the wise are confused about what is action and non-action. As I am homebound, the only thing for me to do is non-action at all times.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics
