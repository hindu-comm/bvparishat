मदिरादुर्गन्धमुखे नव्यकवौ पठति काव्यमत्युच्चैः ।

किं घ्राणं किमु कर्णौ भ्रातः पिदधामि हस्ताभ्याम् ॥

  

Dear brother! Tell me this: When the modern poet, his mouth smelling of alcohol, loudly recites his poem, should I cover my nose or my ears with my hands?

  

Regards

Shankar Rajaraman
