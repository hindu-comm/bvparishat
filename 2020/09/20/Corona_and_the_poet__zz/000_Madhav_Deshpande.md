Corona and the poet

  

सुकवेर्मुखबद्धस्य लेखनी तु प्रवर्तते ।

कथं कुर्यात् करोनेयं लेखन्या मुखबन्धनम् ।।

  

Though the great poet is wearing a mask, his pen can still function. How can Corona put a mask on the pen?

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
