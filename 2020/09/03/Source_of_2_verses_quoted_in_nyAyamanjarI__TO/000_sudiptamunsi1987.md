Dear Scholars,

I will be grateful for any relevant information regarding the source of the following verses quoted by Bhaṭṭa Jayanta in his *Nyāyamañjarī* –

प्रकृतिप्रत्ययौ यद्वदपेक्षेते परस्परम्।  

पदं पदान्तरं तद्वद् वाक्यं वाक्यान्तरं तथा॥

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

वाक्यार्थप्रत्यये तेषां प्रवृत्तौ नान्तरीयकम्।  

पाके ज्वालेव काष्ठानां पदार्थप्रतिपादनम्॥

Both appear on p. 160 of Part 2 of *Nyāyamañjarī* with the commentary *Granthibhaṅga* by Cakradhara, edited by Gaurinath Sastri, and published by Sampurnanand Sanskrit University, 1983.
