नमोऽस्तु कालिदासाय व्यधत्त नियतं विधिः । यन्निर्माणचिरश्रान्तो नान्यं यत्सदृशं पुनः ॥

  

Salutations to Kalidasa. Surely the Creator was so perpetually tired after creating him that he did not venture to create someone similar to him once again

  

Regards

Shankar Rajaraman
