नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

\>The medical analysis below would classify चैतन्य as "sensation",

In this way it can be termed physical, like people losing the

sense of smell through coronavirus. But the inception of

thought in मन may not be confined in the body, since it can

easily be perceived that मन is not "contained" in the body.

Please help if sources exist to explain this dichotomy\<   ---- Vid Bijoy Misra

  

  

Before offering some प्रमाणम् , let me clarify in plain language --

  

If you take it as चैतन्यम् then it is आत्मा -- when it comes to
मनस् it will be ज्ञानम् , i e sense / sensation .

  

What is the problem ?

  

The medical science wants to discuss the issue without आत्मा , i e does not wish to go beyond मनस् and wants चैतन्यम् / sensation is associated with मनस् ।

  

For a clear picture we have to separate आत्मा - इन्द्रिय+ मनस् and
चैतन्यम् - ज्ञानम् -----

  

1\. आत्मा -- चैतन्यम्

2\. मनस्-- ज्ञानम्

  

An entity ' that contains आत्मा only ' can have both मनस् and इन्द्रिय(s) in a working condition .

  

Sometimes although the आत्मा is there the मनस् or इन्द्रिय or both
may not be working ( a mad man / a blind man etc) .

  

It is due to the presence of आत्मा that one is (called) alive in spite of the above condition (s) . आत्मा causes or itself is चैतन्यम् ।

  

ज्ञानम् / sensation / sense - will happen if and only if there is the combination of -- मनस्+ इन्द्रिय+ अर्थ ( अर्थ means the thing that is the subject of perception ) .

  

So one should accept that -- मनस् is there in the body or the body ' contains ' मनस् ।

  

\>can be **termed physical**, like people losing the

sense of smell through coronavirus\<

  

losing the sense of smell or वासनाज्ञानम् - involves मनस् and
इन्द्रिय ( mind and sense-organ ) - so cannot be termed 'purely physical ' .

  

**वैशेषिकदर्शनम् (also न्यायदर्शनम्)** shows the logic that is the proof of the **existence of मनस्** --

  

आत्मेन्द्रियार्थसन्निकर्षे ज्ञानस्य भावो’भावश्च मनसोलिङ्गम् ( **वै सू , 3-2-1** )

  

When the चक्षुस् (eye) coupled with आत्मा comes in contact with a घट
then one gets घटज्ञानम् and if such an आत्मा coupled with another इन्द्रिय ( नासिका / nose) then the

same person is not getting घटज्ञानम् - this suggests that there is a candidate who is helping in getting घटज्ञानम् and the same is मनस् ।

  

Hari explains that the चैतन्यम् itself is शब्द and the same is activating the beings in all activities --

  

सैषा संसारिणां संज्ञा बहिरन्तश्च वर्तते ।

तन्मात्रमनतिक्रान्तं चैतन्यं सर्वजन्तुषु ॥

  

अर्थक्रियासु वाक् सर्वान् समीहयति देहिनः ।

तदुत्क्रान्तौ विसंज्ञो’यं दृश्यते काष्ठकुड्यवत् ॥ **वाक्यपदीयम् , ब्रह्मकाण्डः , 126,127**
