पर्वतो वह्निमान् धूमात् वदन् नैयायिकः स्थितः ।

रोगग्रस्तं स्वदेहं तु धूमपानात् न दृष्टवान् ॥

  

The Naiyayika stood there saying, 'The Mountain has fire because of smoke'. But he did not notice his body getting adisease because of inhaling smoke.
