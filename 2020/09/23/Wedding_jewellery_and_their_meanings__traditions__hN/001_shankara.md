Namaste,

  

You may refer to the books in list give below. Most of these books are available online at [archive.org](http://archive.org).  

  

Besidesthes, P.K. Gode's writings contain some articles on ancient Hindu ornaments, especially the nose ornament. All his writings are available online at [archive.org](http://archive.org), compiled under two titles - Studies in Indian Cultural History vols 1 to 3 and Studies in Indian Literary History vols 1 to 3.

1.  A History of Indian Dress by Charles Louis Fabri 1960 2.  Dress And Ornaments In Ancient India by Mohini Verma 1984 3.  Indian Jewellery by Thomas Holben Hendley 1909 (Offprint From
    Journal of Indian Art) 4.  Indian Jewellery, Dance of the Peacock by Usha R Krishnan, Bharath
    Ramamrutham 2006  
5.  Prachin Bharat Ke Prasadhan by Atridev Vidyalankar 1958 6.  Prachin Bharatiya Vesha Bhusha by Motichandra 1950 alt 7.  Prachin Bharatiya Vesha Bhusha by Roshan Alkaji 2002 (NBT) 8.  Textile Industry in Ancient India by Joges Chandra Ray 1917 (JBORS) 9.  Textile Manufactures & Costumes of the People of India by J Forbes
    Watson 1866 10. The Costume of Hindostan by Balt Solvyns 1804 11. The History of Indian Costume from the 3rd to 7th Century AD by Moti
    Chanda 1956

regards
