नमो विद्वद्भ्यः!  

  

Presenting to you a second set of six assorted Corona verses.

(Here is the first set for reference:<https://groups.google.com/forum/#!msg/bvparishat/jxVmrdxNsvo/Bag62JJnAgAJ>)



1\.

चिक्रोड उवाच  
न स्पृशेद्रघुनाथो मां करोनास्पर्शशङ्कया \|  
तथापि सेतुनिर्माणे भागं वक्ष्यामि सर्वथा \|\|  

  

The squirrelsaid - "Rama may not come and pat me due to the fear of Corona. Nevertheless, I will still do my dutyand participate in the construction of the bridge"

  

--

  

2\.

मयूर उवाच

द्रुतमारुह्य मां स्कन्द कुत्र गन्तुं प्रवर्तसे \|

वायुयानानि सर्वाणि रुद्धानीति श्रुतं न किम् \|\|

  

नारदेन प्रतिज्ञातं फलं प्राप्तुं यदीच्छसि \|

हेरम्बमनुसृत्य त्वं कुरु पित्रोः प्रदक्षिणम् \|\|  

  

The peacock said - " स्कन्द ! Where are you off to, having come and perched on me? Did you not hear that air travel is severely restricted? Now, if what you want is the fruit that Narada promised, then follow what Ganesha is doing, and do a प्रदक्षिणम् of your parents; there is no other way!"

  

--

  

3\.

नैव शक्यं विमानेनप्रयाणमिति दुःखितः \|  
सीतापहरणाकाङ्क्षांतत्याज स दशाननः \|\|

  

Having realized that it was impossible to travel by air, Ravana abandonedhis plan to kidnap Sita with great sadness.

  

--

  

4\.

निषिद्धवायुमार्गोऽपि लङ्कां प्राप्तुं समुत्सुकः \|

महार्णवं स्वबाहुभ्यां ततार कपिकुञ्जरः \|\|

  

Eager to reach Lanka but disallowed from taking the aerialroute, Hanuman ended up swimmingacross the ocean by himself.

  

--

  

5\.

अहल्या तर्कयामास   
त्रस्तमिदंजगद्भाति करोनास्पर्शशङ्कया \|  
तथापि मां शिलाभूतां रामः स्पर्शेन मोक्ष्यति \|\|  

  

"The entire world seems to be afraid of touching anything, due to the Coronavirus situation. However, I am certain that Rama will come and touch me, and liberate me from my curse" - thus argued Ahalya with conviction.

  

--

  

6\.

आचार्य उवाच

विस्मृतं किं त्वया सूत्रं नस्तद्धित इति भ्रमात् \|

मद्रशब्दान्वितो राजन् मद्रराजन् कथं भवेत् \|\|

  

शिष्य उवाच

स्पर्शशब्देन भीतोऽस्मि भाषान्तरेऽपि सर्वथा \|

न प्रयुक्तो गुरो तस्मात् टचाख्यः प्रत्ययो मया \|\|  

  

The teacher asked - "Have you forgotten the *sootram* नस्तद्धिते ?How can मद्र *shabda* when combined withराजन् *shabda* result inमद्रराजन् ? (Shouldn't it be मद्रराजः ?)"

  

The student replied - "Oh Acharya! The very mention of the wordस्पर्श scares me,even if it is represented in another language . Therefore, I did not use the टच् / touch प्रत्ययः, (and thus left the compound word as मद्रराजन् itself)"

  

--

  

धन्यवादाः

जयानन्दः
