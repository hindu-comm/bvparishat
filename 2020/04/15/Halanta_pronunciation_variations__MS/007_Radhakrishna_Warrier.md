I have heard people pronounce the Sanskrit ‘t’ halant (त्) as dŭ where the ŭ represents a samvṛta ukāra as in Malayalam words nāḍŭ (country),
kāḍŭ (forest) etc. The voicing (t to d change) and the addition of the samvṛta ukāra are due to the influence of the mother tongue, I believe.
