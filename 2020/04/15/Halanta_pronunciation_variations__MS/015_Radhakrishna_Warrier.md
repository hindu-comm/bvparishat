Malayalis add the saṃvṛta ukāram (ŭ) to the end of even foreign loan words, just as the Kannada and Telugu people add the full short u.
Malayalis say bassŭ (bus), kāṟŭ (car), laiṯṯŭ (light) etc. Some people retain the saṃvṛta ukāram at word endings even when speaking in English.
