Yes, no PNG (person, number, gender) suffixes for verbs in Malayalam. 
Other Dravidian family languages have PNG suffixes, but like Malayalam, adjectives, prepositions or post positions associated with nouns do not change their forms with the gender of the noun. Inanimate objects (say, a table) doesn't have gender in the way that Indo-Aryan languages have.
