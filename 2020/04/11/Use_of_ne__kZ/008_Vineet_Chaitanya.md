Hindi passive construction would be typically:

  

राम द्वारा बालि मारा गया.

  

No Hindi speaker would consider following as a passive!  

राम ने बालि को मारा.

  

Kamta Prasad Guru therefore distinguishes between "कर्मणि प्रयोग" and "कर्मणि वाच्य".
