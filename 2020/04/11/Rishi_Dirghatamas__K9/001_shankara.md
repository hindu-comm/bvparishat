Namaste,

  

Giving below a short list of resources on Rishi Dirghatamas and his philosophy. Most of these books are available at [archive.org](http://archive.org).  

  

Regarding his name, Swami Harshananda says that "since he was born blind, he was named Dirghatams (one who was in darkness for a long time). However he regained sight by pleasing Agni through his hymsn."  

1.  Poet Philosophers of Rigveda - Kunhan Raja 1963 (Ganesh & Co)  
2.  The Thousand Syllabled Speech (Asya Vamiya Sukta) Part 1 Vision In
    Long Darkness - VS Agarwal 1963  
3.  Life and Vision of Vedic Seers Volume 2 - Dirghatamas - Satya
    Prakash Singh 2006  
4.  The Seers of Rig Veda - VG Rahurkar 1964 (UOP) 5.  Rigved Ke Bananewale Rishi - Babu Surajbhanu 1914  
6.   Bibliographical Dictionary Of Puranic Personages Akshaya Kumari
    Devi  
7.  The Legend of Dirghatamas by Umesh Chandra Sharma in Bharatibhanam
    KV Sharma Fel Vol 8.   Mysticism in the Rgveda by TG Mainkar

  

regards  
shankara
