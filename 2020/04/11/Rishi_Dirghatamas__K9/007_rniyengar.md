Dear Sri Shankar, Perhaps you are referring to the following.

  

ASYA VAMASYA HYMN

(THE RIDDLE OF THE UNIVERSE)

RGVEDA 1-164

SANSKRIT TEXT

With the Bhasyas of Sayana and Atmananda and

With English Translation and Notes

BY

Dr. C. KUNHAN RAJA

Professor of Sanskrit, Andhra University, Waltair

Publishers:

GANESH & CO. (MADRAS) PRIVATE Ltd., MADRAS—17 1956

  

It is a comprehensive scholarly work with text, translation and notes. The preface to the book by the then Chief Minister of UP is also quite impressive. The commentaries are not by Kunhan Raja, They are by Sayana and Atmananda. Atmananda's commentary is very original. He considers all the three: adhiyajna, adhidaiva and adhyatma explanations, following Yaskacharya.

Do you know any biographical details of Atmananda and/or his other works?  

  

Regards

  

RNI
