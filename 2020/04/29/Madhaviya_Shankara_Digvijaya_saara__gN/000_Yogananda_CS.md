Madhaviya Shankara Digvijaya saara is a short biography of Jagadguru Sri Adi Shankaracharya compiled using 214 verses from an ancient work, Sri Madhaviya Shankara Digvijaya (also known as Samkshepa-Sankara-Vijaya) authored by sage Sri Vidyaranya, the 12th Jagadguru of the Śringeri Śarada Pītham from 1380-1386. The text includes an English translation of the verses. The selection of the 214 verses (from the original collection of 1843 verses) has been done by Vidwan G. Mahabaleshwara Bhat, Asthana Vidwan of Sringeri Sri Sharadapeetham.

  

Humbly present an ebook version of this work on this auspicious occasion of Sri Shankara Jayanthi 2020.
