Continuing my Krishna verses

  

हृदि मे ते पदन्यासो वेणोस्ते मधुरो ध्वनि: ।  
यावत् प्रवर्तते कृष्ण तावन्मे स्पन्दनं हृदि ।।१०७३।।  
  
O Krishna, as long as you step into my heart and and the sweet sound of your flute flows in, my heart continues to beat.
