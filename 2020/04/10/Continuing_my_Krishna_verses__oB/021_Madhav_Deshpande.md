Continuing my Krishna verses

  

एहि माधव का चिन्ता स्तुह्यात्मानं निरन्तरम् ।  
अद्वितीये हरौ लोके कोऽन्यस्ते कुरुते स्तवम् ।।१०८७।।  
  
Come, O Krishna, why are you worried? Praise yourself at all times.
With you alone being in the world, who else will praise you?
