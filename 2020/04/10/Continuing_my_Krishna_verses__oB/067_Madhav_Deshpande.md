Continuing my Krishna verses

  

सर्वासु दिक्षु गोविन्द तवैव प्रतिमा हरे ।  
अन्तर्बहिश्च गोविन्द: किमन्यज्जगतीतले ।।१११५।।  
  
In all directions, O Krishna, I see only your reflection. You are inside and outside. What else is there on the surface of this earth?
