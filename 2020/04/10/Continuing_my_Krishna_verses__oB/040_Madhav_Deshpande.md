अद्वितीये हरौ लोके केन क्रीडति क: कदा ।  
माधवो माधवं सृष्ट्वा तेन क्रीडति सर्वदा ।।११०२।।  
  
When Krishna is all that exists in the world, who can play with whom and when? Madhava/Krishna created Madhava and he always plays with him.
