अद्वितीये हरौ लोके क: कं पश्यति दर्पणे ।  
दर्पणात् पुरतश्चापि दर्पणोऽपि स माधव: ।।११०१।।  
  
When Krishna is all that exists in the world, who sees whom in the mirror? Krishna is himself in front of the mirror and he is also the mirror.
