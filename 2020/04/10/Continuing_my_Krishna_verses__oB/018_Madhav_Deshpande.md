उद्गता ध्वनय: सर्वे वेणोस्ते मधुराद् ध्वने: ।  
पदानि ते पदन्यासात् त्वत्त एव सरस्वती ।।१०८५।।  
  
All sounds emerge from the sweet sound of your flute. All words emerge from your foot steps, and Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, arises from you.
