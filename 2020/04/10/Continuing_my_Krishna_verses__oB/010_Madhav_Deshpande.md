Continuing my Krishna verses

  

कृष्णचन्द्रकरस्पर्शादुन्मीलन्ति सतां गिर: ।  
कवीनां मुखपद्मेभ्य: स्वयमायाति भारती ।।१०७९।।  
  
With the touch of the beams of the Moon of Krishna, the words of the poets blossom. The goddess of speech arrives on her own through the lotus-like mouths of the poets.
