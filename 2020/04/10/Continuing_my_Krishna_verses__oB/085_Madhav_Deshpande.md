Continuing my Krishna verses

  

पौर्णमास्यां शशी शुक्लो रौति कुत्र गतो हरि: ।  
कृष्णे पक्षे समायातेऽमायामायाति कृष्णताम् ।।११२५।।  
  
The white moon of the full moon night is crying: "Where did Krishna go?"
When Krishna comes close , the moon of the new moon night becomes dark like Krishna.
