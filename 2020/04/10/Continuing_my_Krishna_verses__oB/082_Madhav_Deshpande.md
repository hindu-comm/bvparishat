Continuing my Krishna verses

  

सरस्वती गिरां देवी गिरन्ती सततं गुणान् ।  
त्वदीयान् न परं पारं याति श्रान्ता युगे युगे ।।११२२।।  
  
Sarasvati, the goddess of speech is constantly expressing your qualities, but cannot reach completion. Age after age, she is exhausted.
