क्रीडतो मृदि कृष्णस्य चन्द्रोऽभूत् शुक्लकन्दुक: ।  
खे क्षिप्तस्तेन हस्ताभ्यां तेन कृष्णकलङ्कित: ।।११२४।।  
  
While playing on the ground, the moon was a white playing ball for Krishna. He hurled it into the sky with his hands, and that is why it has dark spots.
