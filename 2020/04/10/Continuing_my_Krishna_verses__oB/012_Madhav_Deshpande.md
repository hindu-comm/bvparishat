Continuing my Krishna verses

  

न मेद्वैते न वाद्वैते कृष्ण एवरतिर्मम ।  
यत्रासौ रमते तत्र रमेऽहं तस्यलीलया ।।१०८१।।  
  
I have no attraction for the conceptions of duality and non-duality. I am attached only to Krishna. I delight in his sports where he is playing.
