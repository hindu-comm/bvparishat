Continuing my Krishna verses

  

ग्रीष्मो न बाधते चित्तं न शीतं शिशिरे मम ।  
कृष्णस्य साहचर्येण वसन्तो मम सर्वदा ।।१०७०।।  
  
The summer heat does not bother my mind, nor does the cold winter.
Because Krishna is with me, I always have spring.
