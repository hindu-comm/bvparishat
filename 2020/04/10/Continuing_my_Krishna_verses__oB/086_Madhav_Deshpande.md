सर्वतन्त्रस्वतन्त्रस्त्वं जगद् भ्रामयसीश्वर: ।  
भ्राम्यतस्ते जगच्चक्रात् पतनान्नो निवारय ।।११२६।।  
  
O Krishna, you as the Lord, completely independent in your decisions, drive the wheel of this world. Please help us so that we may not fall from the rotation of this wheel.
