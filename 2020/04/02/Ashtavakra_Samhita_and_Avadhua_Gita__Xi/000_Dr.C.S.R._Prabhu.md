Both present top level Advaita experience as sure shot for Moksha.

  

They are supposed to be more ancient than Yoga Vasishta and the works of Adi Shankaracharya on Advaita.

  

I seek confirmation about their authorship and relative time period of the two texts ,if possible, in the sequence after the Veda and Upanishads
