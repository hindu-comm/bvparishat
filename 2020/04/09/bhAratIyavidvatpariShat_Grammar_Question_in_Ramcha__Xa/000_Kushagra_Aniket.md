I have a question about the grammatical (Paninian) accuracy of अतुलितबलधामं in the famous verse "अतुलितबलधामं हेमशैलाभदेहं..." Is it correct to takeअतुलितबलधामं as अकारान्त since it is a compound (समस्तपद)? Or should it be taken as नकारान्त? If taken asनकारान्त because धामन् is नकारान्त, it will becomeअतुलितबलधाम in the accusative case (द्वितीया).
