One needs to watch Vaakyaartha sadas and other such occasions to witness this nuanced beauty of this respectful intimate affection with ruthlessly uncompromising critical observation and response towards each other from senior scholars towards the juniors and vice versa.  

  

My mother used to share with us how my father and his guru used to move and converse like bosom friends totally absorbed in their topic while all the formalities of respectful gestures and adressing of the s'ishya towards guru and vaatsalyaatmaka gestures and addressing of the guru towards sishya were well intact.
