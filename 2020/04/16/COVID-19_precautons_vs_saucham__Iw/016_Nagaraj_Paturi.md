\>One doesnot need a dharam shastra advisory not to go close to a person who has cold or cough or visible sickness as there is a chance of catching

  

One should not need a government advisory to keep social distance in a contagious pandemic condition. But there is an advisory now. Government is struggling to get that executed properly and strictly.

  

This helps the thinking people understand the purpose of certain instructions of Dharma shaastras.

  

It needs subtle thinking to understand the abstruse aspects such as understanding the right spirit of a letter does not mean justification of the wrong understanding and execution of the letter.

  

It needs capacity to nuanced understanding to realise that helping right understanding of the past based on current new understandings does not imply justification of wrong practices if any of the present.
