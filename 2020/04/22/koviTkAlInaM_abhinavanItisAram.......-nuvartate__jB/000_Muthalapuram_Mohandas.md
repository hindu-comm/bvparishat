* ***अभिनवनीतिसारम्** **- अनुवर्तते         
          **- मुत्तलपुरं मोहन्दास्****

**माता पिता वा शत्रुस्स्यात् कोविदेन तु बाधितः।**

**सभामध्ये धरेत्तस्मात् मुखावरणमुत्तमम्।। (14)**

Once one contracts Corona, even parents will be like enemies.

So make it a practice to wear a good face mask when you meet others.

**एकधा च दशधा शतधा च सहस्रधा च**

**रणे पार्थशरोवृष्टिस्तथा कोविदव्यापनम्।। (15)**

The Covid spreads like the arrows from Arjun’s bow.

One initially, multiplies into ten, then into hundred in

flight and thousand while reaching the target.



**हर्ताल् कदा भवति? कारणमस्य किं वा?**

**मृत्तैलमूल्यमधिकं बत! जायते वा?**

**सामूहिकं सपदिकोविदव्यापनञ्च**

**जानाति नैव जगदीश कुतो मनुष्यः?  (16)**

The hike in petrol prices, outset of a *hartal* and its

causes cannot be predicted. Similarly when Corona

turning into pandemic proportions is not known

to the Almighty. What to say then about man’s helplessness!

**शकटं पञ्चहस्तेषु दशहस्तेषु वाजिनं**

**गजं हस्तसहस्रेषु कोरोणां दूरतस्त्यजेत्। (17)**

One should keep oneself away by five palm length from

vehicle, ten lengths from horse, thousand from elephant.

The distance should be even greater from Covid-affected.

**मानवस्य लघुतामुदाहरन्**

**राष्ट्रदुर्मदमपि च संहरन्।**

**कोविदस्य च समीक्ष्य ताण्डवं**

**ज्ञायतेह विधिरेव शक्तिमान्।। (18)**

Observing the way the virus is running riot,

proving that man is insignificant and suppressing

the rudeness of the monopolies of the superpowers,

feel after all destiny is omnipotent.

**रूपयौवनसम्पन्ने विशालकुलसंभवे**

**विद्वज्जने च मूढे वा कोविदस्य नहि भेदः। (19)**

Covid-19 makes no partiality among the wise and foolish,

elegant and ugly, elite, lowly, young and old.

**..... अनुवर्तिष्यते**
