The word वर्षा is used in faminine and plural only when meaning "rainy season" according Amara:

  

स्त्रियां प्रावृट् स्त्रियां भूम्नि वर्षा:  

  

and also शरद् and प्रावृट् are used in feminine gender, but not in plural.

  

Other names of ऋतु are used in masculine gender.
