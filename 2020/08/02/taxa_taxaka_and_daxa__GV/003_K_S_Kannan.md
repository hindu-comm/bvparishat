*dexter* means right (as against *sinister* which means left).

As in dextrose in chemistry.

It is the right one spoken of as भगवत्तर.

दक्ष and दक्षिण have an obvious reference to the "right".

What is to your right when you stand facing the rising Sun

is the Southern Direction, दक्षिणा दिक्.
