Dear Jaidev Ji,

  

  Please note that this book, the Yathārthabodhinī, is not a translation of Vāmana-Paṇḍita's Yathārtha-Dīpikā, but a gist in Marathi based on the work of Vāmana-Paṇḍita. But this is in a somewhat more modern Marathi as compared to Vāmana-Paṇḍita's original work.
