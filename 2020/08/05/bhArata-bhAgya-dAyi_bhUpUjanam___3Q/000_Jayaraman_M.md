**॥भारत-भाग्य-दायि भूपूजनम्॥**

*॥A composition for today's momentous occasion॥*

**

*  
*****

**भोगीन्द्रशायिन् भुवनैकनाथ **

**भूभारभङ्गाय भृतावतार **

**भूयोऽद्य भूमन् भवदालयार्थम् **

**भूपूजनं भारतभाग्यदायि॥१॥**

*Oh! Lord resting on sesha, Lord of universe! Incarnation to remove evil! All pervasive! Again, Today, your temple Bhumipujana (is happening) for Bharata's well being!*



**भग्नेशचाप प्रभया समेत **

**भूम्यात्मजाभार्य बहुप्रताप **

**भूयोऽद्य भूमन् भवदालयार्थम् **

**भूपूजनं भारतभाग्यदायि॥२॥**

*  
Oh! the one who broke Shiva’s bow! Oh! glowing divinity! Consort of Sita! Valorous one! Again, Today, your temple Bhumipujana (is happening) for Bharata's well being!*



**भयापहर्तर्-भरताग्रजन्मन् **

**सभ्रातृक भ्रान्तवनान्तरेश **

**भूयोऽद्य भूमन् भवदालयार्थम् **

**भूपूजनं भारतभाग्यदायि॥३॥**

*Oh Remover of fear! Elder brother of Bharata! One who is accompanied by Brother (Lakshmana)! one who roamed in the forests! Again, Today, your temple Bhumipujana (is happening) for Bharata's well being!*



**भीमारिनाशिन्नभयप्रदायिन् **

**भूपालमौले भजनीयमूर्ते **

**भूयोऽद्य भूमन् भवदालयार्थम् **

**भूपूजनं भारतभाग्यदायि॥४॥**

*Oh the destroyer of terrible foes! Giver of refuge (to Vibhishana)! Best among the kings! Endowed with beautiful form! Today, your temple Bhumipujana (is happening) for Bharata's well being!*



**भग्नं हृतं त्वत्सुर-सेव्यसद्म **

**त्यागेन भक्त्या पुनराप्तवन्तः।**

**भूयोऽद्य भूमन् भवदालयार्थम् **

**भूपूजनं भारतभाग्यदायि॥५॥**

*(We have) again gained the glorious temple that was destroyed and snatched away. Today, your temple Bhumipujana (is happening) for Bharata's well being!*
