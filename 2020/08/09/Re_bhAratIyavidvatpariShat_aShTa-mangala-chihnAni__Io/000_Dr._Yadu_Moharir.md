Namaste Swaroop Sharma-Ji,

  

During Maharashtrian Marriages aShTavinaayaa's are invoked before the couple gets married.

  

Purpose being to get a clearance from Eight Directions [(अष्टदिक्पाल) by invoking अष्टविनायक](http://spokensanskrit.org/index.php?tran_input=अष्टदिक्पाल&direct=se&script=hk&link=yes&mode=3)
for avoiding any unforeseen events to occur during the auspicious ceremony.  

  

स्वस्ति श्री गणनायकं गजमुखं,मोरेश्वरं सिद्धिदं ।

बल्लाळो मुरुडं विनायकमहं,चिन्तामणि स्थेवरं\|\|

लेण्याद्रिं गिरिजात्मकं सुरवरदं,विघ्नेश्वरम् ओज़रम्\|

ग्रामे रांजण संस्थितम् गणपतिः,कुर्यात् सदा मङ्गलं\|\|१\|\|
