Corona Worries

  

क: पस्पर्श मुखं ते चन्द्र कलङ्क: कथं त्वया लब्ध: ।

कोरोनाभयकाले कुत: सखे भ्रमसि रे नभसि ।।

  

O Moon, who touched your face, and how did you get that spot? At the time of the danger of the Coronavirus, why are you wandering in the sky?
