Weddings in Corona times

  

मा स्पृश मा स्पृश मामिति निगदन्ती नववधूर्गता दूरम् ।

प्रणयं दर्शयतु कथं वर एकाकी निषण्णोऽत्र ।।

  

Saying “don’t touch me, don’t touch me,” the new bride went away. How can the poor groom sitting here alone express his love?
