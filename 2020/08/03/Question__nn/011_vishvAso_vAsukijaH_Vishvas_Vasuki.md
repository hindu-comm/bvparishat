Also, regarding "अन्नंभट्ट , कृष्णमाचार्य , भट्टोजिदीक्षित etc are names that follow neither आपस्तम्ब -- द्व्यक्षरं चतुरक्षरं वानाम - पूर्वमाख्यातोत्तरम् (आ गृसू 5-25-1) nor बौधायन --

ऋष्यणूकं देवतानूकं वा ( बौ गृ सू 2-2-28) ", my pretty confident guess is that many of these brAhmaNa-s would have had a compliant ritual name, separate from the mundane name. Even Apastamba says in nAmakaraNa-prakaraNa: तद्रहस्यं भवति ॥ . This practice continues today in many families (especially TN).
