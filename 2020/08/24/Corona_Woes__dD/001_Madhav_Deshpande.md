Corona Woes

  

कोरोने निर्घृणे त्वत्तो भीतो मे प्रतिवेशिक: ।

न मे दृष्टिपथं याति सुखं मेऽपहृतं त्वया ।।

  

The lady next door says: “O cruel Corona, my neighbor is scared of you. I don’t get to see him. You have taken away my happiness.”
