Corona Woes

  

यावद्भयं करोनातस्तावत् स्याद् ग्रहणं कथम् ।

चन्द्रसूर्यौ भयं किं वां राहुर्दूरं पलायित: ।।

  

As long as there is the danger of the Coronavirus, how can an eclipse take place. O Moon and Sun, what is your fear? The demon Rahu \[who swallows the sun and the moon\] has run away.
