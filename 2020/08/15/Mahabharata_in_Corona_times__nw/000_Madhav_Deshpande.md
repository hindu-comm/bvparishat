Mahabharata in Corona times

  

कृष्णाया: पञ्च पतय: प्रासादे परित: स्थिता: ।

न यान्ति निकटे तस्या: कोरोनाभयविह्वला: ।।

  

The five husbands of Draupadi stand around her in the palace, but being scared of Corona, they do not come near her.
