Corona!

  

हरिपदकमलाभ्यां सन्ततं निस्सरन्ती

स्वशिरसि निजशान्त्यै शङ्करो यां बबन्ध ।

जगदखिलमिदं सा निर्मलीकर्तुकामा

विषविकिरकरोनां नाशयत्वाशु गङ्गा ।।

  

May the river Ganges, continuously flowing from the lotus-feet of Lord Vishnu and the one whom Shiva ties on his head to calm him down, desiring to purify this whole world quickly destroy the Coronavirus, that is spreading poison.
