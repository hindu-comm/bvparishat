साहस्र नूपुर नाद विसृष्टा,  

आलुवा सरित् पुनरपि वहति ...



Sāhasra nūpura nāda visṛṣṭā 

Āluvā sarit punarapi vahati  ...

  

This is the Sanskrit rendering of an old Malayalam movie song. The translation is almost word to word, and the song preserves the tune of the old Malayalam song.

This is the old Malayalam film song:

*Āyiram pādasaraṅṅaḷ kiluṅṅi,*

*Āluvāppuẓa piṉṉeyum oẓuki ...*

ആയിരം പാദസരങ്ങൾ കിലുങ്ങി

ആലുവാപ്പുഴ പിന്നെയുമൊഴുകി ...

(Rough and inelegant translation of the Malayalam lines:

A thousand anklets sounded, and River Āluvā flowed again ...)
