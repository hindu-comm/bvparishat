In Odia, uses would beतू for intimacy (third person),  तुमे for intimate with respect (second person), आपण for distant and respectful (third person plural verb)

Hindi has आप (singular) आप लोग, आप सब ( two words, nor suffix, plural), both take third person plural verb. Odia has आपण (singular) and आपणमाने (plural, suffixed). Both take third person plural verb.
