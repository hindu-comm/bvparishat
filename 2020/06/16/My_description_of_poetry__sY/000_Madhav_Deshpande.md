My description of poetry

  

काष्ठा परेयं रमणीयताया:

प्रत्यक्षरं नृत्यति यत्र वाणी ।

येऽपि स्युरेते सहजा विलासा

मनोरमास्ते सकला भवन्ति ।।

  

It is the absolute excellence of beauty when the speech dances with every letter. What may be the most normal gestures, they all become so beautiful.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
