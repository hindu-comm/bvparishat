I agree about the Ramayana instance:

धर्मात्मा सत्यसन्धश्च रामो दाशरथिर् यदि ।

पौरुषे चाप्रतिद्वन्द्वश्शरैनं जहि रावणिम् ॥.

  

Such utterances are called Acts of Truth, and are apparently imprecatory/invocatory  

(present in Buddhism also as सच्चकिरिया, if I remember aright).
