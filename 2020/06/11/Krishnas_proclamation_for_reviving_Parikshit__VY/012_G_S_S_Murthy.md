There is an interesting swearing by Srirama in the context of fighting with Khara in Aranyakanda. Asking Lakshmana to guard Sita in the cave, he says," शापितो मम पादाभ्यां गम्यतां वत्स मा चिरम्" \[३-२४-१३ Kalyan edition\]. What exactly doesthe expression "शापितो मम पादाभ्याम्" convey? Does this swearing occur in Sanskrit texts elsewhere? I do not understand the commentary of Sri Govndarajaeither which says," मम पादाभ्यामित्युक्तिः लक्ष्मणव्यवहारानुसारेण".

Scholars may guide.
