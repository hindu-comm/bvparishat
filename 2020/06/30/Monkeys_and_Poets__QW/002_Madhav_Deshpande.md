Monkeys and Poets

  

ये न: स्वशब्दलीलाभिर्हरन्ति सततं मन: ।

कवयस्ते नमस्तेभ्यस्तदन्ये कपयो मता: ।।

  

I offer my salutations to those poets \[kavi\] who constantly captivate our minds with the display of the beauty of their words. Others are just like monkeys \[kapi\].
