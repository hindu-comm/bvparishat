Monkeys and Poets

  

एकाक्षरविकारेण कपय: कवयोऽभवन् ।

समानं सर्वमन्यच्चेत् शस्यन्ते कवय: कुत: ।।

  

With the change of a single letter, monkeys became poets. \[In Sanskrit, kapi=monkey \> kavi=poet\]. If everything else is the same, why are the poets praised?
