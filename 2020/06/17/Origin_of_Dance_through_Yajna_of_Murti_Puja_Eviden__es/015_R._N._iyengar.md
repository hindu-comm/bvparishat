My primary interest has been to see the links between classical music and the Samagana, which can not be separated from RV and Yajnas. However, music and dance are closely related and incidentally I came across the following in the TaaNDya-mahaabraahmaNa (aka Panchavimsha Br.).There is reference to dancing by the maids of the YajamAna-patnI. The word 'nrtya' is not in the original text but the Sutras which preserve the procedures mention that the maids go round the MArjAlIya singing and dancing.
