There is an article on Prishodaradi words in Samhhashanasandesa 2012 Oct issue, pg 13, wherein Hamsa is derived as follows.

  

हंसः इत्यत्र अनुस्वारागमः दृश्यते । (हन् + अच् + हसागमः, अथवाहस् + अच् + अनुस्वारागमः).
