Jagannatha-ji, one more reference here:

  

श्रियःपत्येनित्यागणितगुणमाणिक्यविशद-

प्रभाजालोल्लासोपहतसकलावद्यतमसे\|

जगज्जन्म**स्थेम**प्रलयरचनाशीलवपुषे

नमोऽशेषाम्नायस्मृतिहृदयदीप्तायहरये\|\|

  

ITRANS:

shriyaH patye nityAgaNitaguNamANikyavishada-

prabhAjAlollAsopahatasakalAvadyatamase \|

jagajjanma**sthema**pralayarachanAshIlavapuShe

namo.asheShAmnAyasmR^itihR^idayadIptAya haraye \|\|
