Thanks, yes लालित should be the word. In Malayalam लाळितम् (ലാളിതം) means caressed, as in तावकाङ्गुली लाळितम् (താവകാംഗുലീ ലാളിതം) caressed by your fingers.

  

श्रीमल्ललितालालितः would mean caressed byश्रीमद् ललिता (Goddess Lalita = Bhagavati).

  

In everyday spoken Malayalam, lāḷana (ലാളന) means treatment with fondness, indulgent treatment, as a mother does to her child.
