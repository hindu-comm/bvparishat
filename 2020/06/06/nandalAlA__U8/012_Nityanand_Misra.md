Also note the use of the word ‘anupalāla’ in the Atharvaveda’s Śaunaka-saṃhitā

  

“palālānupalālau śarkuṃ kokaṃ malimlucaṃ palījakam, āśreṣaṃ vavrivāsasamṛkṣagrīvaṃ pramīlinam” (8.6.2)

  

Monier-Williams: “अनुपलाल m. N. of a demon dangerous to children, AV. viii, 6, 2.”

  

I have not checked the commentaries on the ‘mantra’ but *prima facie* the sense of a child should be there in the word ‘anup-alāla’.
