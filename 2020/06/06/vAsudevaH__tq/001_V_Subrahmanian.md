It is very nice to read the very many citations for the word 'vaasudeva'. Here is a Vishnu Puranam verse that Shankaracharya has cited in the introduction to the Vishnu Sahasra Nama bhashyam: Yama tells his attendants not to go anywhere near those who have the following conviction:

  

सकलमिदमहं च वासुदेवः परमपुमान्परमेश्वरः स एकः ।

इति मतिर(च)मला भवत्यनन्ते हृदयगते व्रज तान्विहाय दूरात् \|\| 3.7.32
