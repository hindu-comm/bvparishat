Joy of poetry

  

कालिदासगिर: श्रोतुं स्वयमायाति भारती ।

कवेरस्तित्वनास्तित्वे न तस्या रमते मन: ।।

  

Sarasvati, the goddess of speech, herself comes to listen to the poetry of Kalidasa. Her mind does not rejoice in the debate over the existence of the poet.
