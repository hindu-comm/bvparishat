Joy of Poetry

  

भोजने षड्रसा: सन्ति, काव्ये नव रसा: स्मृता: ।

भुक्त्वा काव्यं पठन् नित्यमुभयोर्लभते रसम् ।।

  

There are six flavors in food and there are said to be nine in poetry. One who always recites poetry after finishing one’s dinner enjoys flavors of both.
