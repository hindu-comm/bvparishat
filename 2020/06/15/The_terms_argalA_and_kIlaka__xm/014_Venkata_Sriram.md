कवच अर्गळ कीलक in सप्तशती goes as one integrated set and all 3 should be chanted in order as they are complementary to each other.

  

The कवचम् (armour) generally is worn by fastening & integrating all the individual parts (shields) pertaining to different parts of the body; in order to fasten and integrate all the pieces, one requires the nuts / bolts / nails (कील:) ; after fastening and joining all the individual shields into an armour, one should close the armour with a bolt to make it a tight armour so that it doesn't get loosen. This final closure of armour is done with withअर्गळ:

  

Thus, equipped with such armour (कवचम्) which is joined and fastened with कीलक: and closed with अर्गळ:, theउपासकः gets the protection fromचण्डिका.
