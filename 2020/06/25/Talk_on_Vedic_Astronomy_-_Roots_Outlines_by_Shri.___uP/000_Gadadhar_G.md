Shri G.S. Raju garu was born in the Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh. He graduated from Andhra University with a B.Sc in Mathematics. He then received his Engineering degree from Indian Institute of Science (IISC), Bangalore in Communications in 1966. He was selected by UPSC for working in the ministry of Communications for 36 years before he retired as the Deputy Director General in charge of Technology, Approvals etc. for the Southern Region of India. He represented India in UN Development Working Groups for 3 years in IWP '13 on Mobile Communications and Cellular Systems. Subsequently, he represented Mobile Satellite Group of Studies as a ‘Rapporteur’, in Asia Pacific Telecommunity for a group headquartered in Bangkok consisting of 57 countries.  
  
Shri G.S.Raju garu was introduced to Pujya Guruji Dr. Vedavyas in 1966. Since then he has learnt many astronomical links in the Vedic literature.The books he authored include Vedic Sciences in Mahabharata, Our Universe, Spiritual Living and Targets in Life. He has also authored four telugu books - Endukandee meek ee srama?, audio book Chivaraku Migiledi, Bharateeya Khogola Vigyanam (Vedic Astronomy) and Mahatmula Mahaprasthana. He conducted ten Vedic Seminars and published and edited the Vedic Seminal Proceedings. Over the past 25 years he has conducted various summer camps as Managing Trustee of Vedavyasa Bharati Trust under the guidance of Pujya Guruji. He is currently the Vice President of USCEFI and managing Trustee of Vedavyasa Bharati Trust, Bangalore and founder of International Institute of Vedic Sciences, Rishi Vatika, a dream project for Dr. Vedavyas.  
  
International Institute of Vedic Sciences is promoted by Vedavyasa Bharati Trust, USCEFI and Yoga Brotherhood of America with the blessings of Poojya Guruji Dr. Vedavyas.
