\> In Malayalam, there is a word tūlika meaning feather used for writing. It is used as a figurative word for pen also – as in the tūlika is more powerful than the sword. I don’t know whether it is a Sanskrit/Prakrit origin word or a pure Dravidian word  

  

--- Prof. Wakankar's quote from Kalidasa's Kumaarasambhavamshows that it is of Sanskrit origin. Deerghaantaतूलिका
