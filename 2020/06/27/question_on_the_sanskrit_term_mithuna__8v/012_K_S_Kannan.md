*dyaus* (Masculine) and *prithivI* (Feminine).

  

And *yajn"a* is to be taken in a bigger/more extensive sense.

  

And the sUtra is  

पत्युर् **नो** यज्ञसंयोगे \|  

(not **नौ**).
