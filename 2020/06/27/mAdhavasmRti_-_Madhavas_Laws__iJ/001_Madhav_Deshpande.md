माधवस्मृति “Madhava’s Laws”

  

माता रक्षति कौमारे भार्या रक्षति यौवने ।

वार्धके रक्षति सुता न स स्वातन्त्र्यमर्हति ।।

  

“His mother protects him during his childhood. His wife protects him during his youth. His daughter protects him during his old age. He does not need independence.”
