The politician's desire

  

“अलक्षेन्द्रवदेवाहं जगज्जेष्यामि लीलया ।”

इत्येव राज्यकामस्य वाचिवीरस्य कामना ।।

  

The politician, as a warrior of words, only desires this: “Like Alexander the Great, I will easily conquer the world.”

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics
