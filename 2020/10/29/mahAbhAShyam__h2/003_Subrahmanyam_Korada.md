नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

There is another point that requires serious attention -- sarcastic comments by some म्लेच्छ-s --- how can one author so many works ? So there must have been two Vyasas / Patanjalis/Kalidasas / Bhasas and so on .

This is also simply with a दुर्बुद्धि to generate a kind of inferiority complex (आत्मन्यूनताभावः) in the minds of Indians .

  

In the Theses we receive for adjudication , the students unhesitatingly write such things - there were two Bhasas etc. . Why to blame others ? If the Guide’s / Supervisor's ' fundamentals are strong'

how would he allow such remarks ? उच्छिष्टभोजनम् रसवत्तरं भवति खलु । ’ यस्य नास्ति स्वयंप्रज्ञा शास्त्रं तस्य करोति किम् ?  

  

भवतिह्यस्माकम् अप्रत्यक्षमपि चिरन्तनानां प्रत्यक्षम् । तथा च व्यासादयो देवादिभिः प्रत्यक्षं व्यवहरन्तीति स्मर्यते । यस्तु ब्रूयात् इदानीन्तनानामिव पूर्वेषाम् अपि नास्ति देवादिभिः व्यवहर्तुं सामर्थ्यमिति स जगद्वैचित्र्यं प्रतिषेधेत् .... ।

                                   
                    

                                   
                                   
                      **ब्रह्मसूत्रशाङ्करभाष्यम् , 1-3-33**

ऋषीणामपि मन्त्रब्राह्मणदर्शिनां सामर्थ्यं नास्मदीयेन सामर्थ्येन उपमातुं
युक्तम् ! ibid , 1-3-33

  

In order to see that people get rid of the three kinds of दोष-s , Patanjali authored - महाभाष्यम् (वाग्दोषाः ) , योगानुशासनम् (मनोदोषाः ) , चरकसूत्रम् (कायदोषाः ) ।

  

न हिपूर्वजो मूढ आसीदिति आत्मनापि मूढेन भवितव्यमिति किञ्चिदस्ति प्रमाणम् ? ibid , 2-1-11 ( Acarya says - you check the horoscopes of both ) .

  

संशयमात्रेणापि परपक्षः प्रतिषिध्यते ! ibid , 3-3-54

  

Let me offer something in this regard ---

  

The following is not for glory but for justification / vindication --- you = Mr X .

  

While riding a Motorcycle or driving a Motor Car in a bumper to bumper traffic I shall teach / discuss four Vedangas / Darsanas in four
different mediums .

  

You may select discussion on ज्योतिषम् - the compatibility of the horoscopes of bride and groom (I have already exhibitedto one of my Sishyas) or even Tantram from परशुरामकल्पसूत्रम् down to कामकलाविलासः ।

  

66 years is not an age according to Ayurveda .

  

I do not believe in the following -- birth , death , time , fever , infection etc. - and if you do, it is your cup of tea .

  

Such a task is धर्म or अधर्म ?

  

Neither -- ' अशिष्टाप्रतिषिद्धं ..... नैव तद्दोषाय भवति नाभ्युदयाय (हिक्कितहसितकण्डूयितानि ) -- **महाभाष्यम् , ऋलृक् ( the thing / activity that is neither prescribed nor prohibited causes neither Dharma nor Adharma ) .**
