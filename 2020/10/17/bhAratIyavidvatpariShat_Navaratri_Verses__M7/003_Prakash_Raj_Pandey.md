Most families worship Navadurga.s during navaratri-- each having a special day toher. But we worship Mahishasuramardini during the whole of Navaratri. On the seventh day, we take Bilva phala and nine different plant leaves (patrika) into the puja-room and invoke nine goddesses in them, and worship them for three consecutive days. Here is the list of plants. and the Devi.s we invoke on those leaves.

  

कदली--ब्रह्माणी  
दाडिमी -- रक्तदन्तिका

धान्य--लक्ष्मी

हरिद्रा -- दुर्गा

मानक-- चामुण्डा

कर्चूर -- कालिका

बिल्व -- शिवा

अशोक -- शोकहारिणी

जयन्ती -- कार्तिकी  
  
Now it is very difficult tofind मानक and कर्चूर. On the tenth day, we worship जया, विजया and अपराजिता.
