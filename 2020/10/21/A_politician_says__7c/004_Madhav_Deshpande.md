A politician says:

  

त्वदीयाय हितायैव मतमिच्छामि तावकम् ।

मतं दास्यसि मह्यं ते जानीषे स्वं हितं यदि ।।

  

A politician says: “I want your vote only for your own benefit. If you know what is good for you, you will give me your vote.”
