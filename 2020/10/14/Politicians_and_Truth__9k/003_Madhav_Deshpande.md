Politicians and Truth

  

सत्यं च राजनीतिश्च द्वयमेकत्र दुर्लभम् ।

सिद्धिं विन्दन्ति राजान: कौटिल्यपथगामिन: ।।

  

It is difficult to find truth and politics in one person. Kings achieve success by following devious paths. \[Following the path of Kauṭilya\].
