Many of these Subhashitas are actually commented upon by commentators.The commentaries sometimes cite references from various Niti- and Dharma-shastras to justify the subhashitas.

  

For instance, in this shloka, every foot can be independently justified on the basis of the Niti texts:

  

अलसस्य कुतो विद्या अविद्यस्य कुतो धनम्।

अधनस्य कुतो मित्रम् अमित्रस्य कुतस्सुखम् ॥
