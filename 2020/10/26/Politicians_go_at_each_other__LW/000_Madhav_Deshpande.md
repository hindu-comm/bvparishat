Politicians go at each other:

  

मल्लो मल्लं यथा दृष्ट्वा बाहू उद्यम्य गर्जति ।

तथा मतैषिणो लोके गर्जन्त्युच्चैर्दिवानिशम् ।।

  

As a wrestler raises his arms and roars when he sees another wrestler, so do the politicians desirous of votes roar day and night.
