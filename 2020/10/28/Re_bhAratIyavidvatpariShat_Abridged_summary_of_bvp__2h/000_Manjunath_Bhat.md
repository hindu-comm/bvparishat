Alas! it is a great loss for us of sudden demise of Dr. H N Bhat ji, an eminent scholar of Sanskrit and Prakrit as well.  When I got appointed at the Ecole Francaise D extereme Orient, Pondicherry, I was the most junior and he was the most senior fellow among Indians those who were working at the Ecole. We both were from Karnataka.  I got an opportunity to work with Dr. H N Bhat for Balaramayana Project. Even though we were in the same chamber I used to talk through google talk!

I was fortunate to have an association with him and to learn many things from him. My deepest condolences üôèüôè
