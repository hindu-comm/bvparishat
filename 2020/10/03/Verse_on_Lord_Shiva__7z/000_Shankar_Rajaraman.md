स्वस्मात्तिरोहिताय

स्वस्मै स्वेन प्रकाशयित्रे स्वम् । स्वस्मिन्विश्रान्तिजुषे

स्वस्य महिम्ना महेश्वराय नमः ॥

He reveals himself - hidden from himself - to himself by means of himself. He is established in himself due to his own greatness. Him, Maheshwara, I salute.

Regards

Shankar Rajaraman
