Trump says "Trust Me."

  

आप्तो यथार्थवक्तेति प्रोक्तं शास्त्रै: पुरातनै: ।

प्राप्तोऽयथार्थवक्तायमाप्तोऽस्मीति प्रभाषते ।।

  

The ancient philosophies say that a trustworthy person is one who speaks the truth. But here comes this speaker of untruth and he loudly says: “Trust me!”
