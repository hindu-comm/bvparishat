I have composed four simple Samskrita verses on Devis - **Durga-Lakshmi-Sarasvati** intending to introduce some aspects of purpose of this festival to children.  

  

The Text and English translation are given below -  

नवरात्र-सन्दर्भे सरलसंस्कृतेन  

**देवीस्तुतिः -Devīstutiḥ**

  

**सरस्वति नमस्तेऽस्तु**

**चतुर्मुखपतिव्रते।**

**भूयाद् ज्ञानाय मे विद्या**

**विवादाय न जातुचित्॥1 ॥**

sarasvati namaste'stu caturmukhapativrate।

bhūyād jñānāya me vidyā vivādāya na jātucit॥

*Oh Goddess Sarasvati! Consort of the Four-faced Brahman! Salutations. Let my learning lead me to knowledge and not for argumentation!*

***  
***

**नमस्तेऽस्तु महालक्ष्मि!**

**क्षीरोदतनये शुभे।**

**भूयाद् दानाय मे वित्तं  
**

**मदाय न कदाचन॥2॥**

namaste'stu mahālakṣmi! kṣīrodatanaye śubhe।  

bhūyād dānāya me vittaṃ madāya na kadācana॥

*Oh Goddess Mahālakṣmi! Daughter of the Milky ocean! Saluations. Let my wealth be for sharing and not for arrogance.*

  

**दुर्गादेवि! नमस्तुभ्यं  
**

**सिंहारूढे शिवप्रिये।**

**भूयात् शक्तिः सुरक्षायै  
**

**परेषां पीडनाय न॥3॥**

durgādevi namastubhyaṃ siṃhārūḍhe śivapriye।

bhūyāt śaktiḥ surakṣāyai pareṣāṃ pīḍanāya na॥

*Oh Goddess Durgā! Seated on a Lion! Dear to Śiva! Salutations. Let my strength be for protection and not for harming anyone!*

***  
***

**विद्यया सम्पदा शक्त्या  
**

**समेताः सक्षमा वयम्।**

**अक्षय्य-सुखपात्राणि  
**

**भारताः स्याम भारते॥4 ॥**

vidyayā sampadā śaktyā sametāḥ sakṣamā vayam।

akṣayya-sukhapātrāṇi bhāratāḥ syāma bhārate॥

*Let us all be endowed with wisdom, wealth and power and be capable and attain happiness in plenty. (And finally,) Staying in Bhārata let us continue our pursuit towards light.*
