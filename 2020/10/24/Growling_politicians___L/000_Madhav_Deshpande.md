Growling politicians

  

वीथीषु गुर्गुरायन्ते कुक्कुरास्ते यथाऽनिशम् ।

रात्रिन्दिवं तथा लोके निर्वाचनमतैषिण: ।।

As the dogs constantly growl in the streets, so do the politicians desiring votes in the election day and night.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
