On a cup of tea

  

लभ्यते चेच्चहा प्रातर्जीवितं सफलं मम ।

इदमेकं चहापानं रहस्यं जीवितस्य मे ।।

  

If I get a cup of tea in the morning, then life is worth living. A cup of tea is the sole secret of my life.
