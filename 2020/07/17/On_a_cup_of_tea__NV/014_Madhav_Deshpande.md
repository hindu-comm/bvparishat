On a cup of tea

  

“पेया चहा न वा पेया” संशीतिर्मनसो यदि ।

“सर्वस्य चाह”मित्युक्तं स्वयं भगवता स्मर ।।

  

If you have a doubt whether you should or should not have a cup of tea, remember that the Lord himself said: “I am the tea for all.”
