On a cup of tea

  

प्रातरुत्थाय किं कार्यमिति चिन्ता न युज्यते ।

चहास्मरणमात्रेण शय्यां त्यजति पण्डित: ।।

  

It is not appropriate to worry about what to do after getting up in the morning. A wise person gets up from his bed with just the thought of a cup of tea.
