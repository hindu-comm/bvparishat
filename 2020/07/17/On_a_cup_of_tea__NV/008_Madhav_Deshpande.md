On a cup of tea

  

असार: खलु संसारो लभ्यते न चहा यदि ।

विनामृतं यथा स्वर्गस्तथायुर्मे चहां विना ।।

  

The whole world seems worthless, if I don’t get a cup of tea. Life without tea is like heaven without nectar.
