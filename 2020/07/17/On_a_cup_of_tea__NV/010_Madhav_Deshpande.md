On a cup of tea

  

हास्यामि जगतीं कृत्स्नां न हास्यामि चहामहम् ।

हास्यामि चेत् चहां हा हा निरानन्दं भवेज्जगत् ।।

  

I will give up the whole world, but will not give up my cup of tea. If I were to give up my cup of tea, alas, this world would be completely joyless.
