The pleasure of tea (Chai) in the rare meter Mayūrasāriṇī.  
  
चित्ततोषणं चकारतो मे  
मात्रया मनो विलासमेतु।

यक्षवैभवं यकारतः स्यात्  
चाय ते पयःपते नमस्ते।।  
  
May my heart be satiated by "ch."  
May my mind rejoice in "a."  
May I enjoy the wealth of a Yaksha in "i."

I bow to you, O Chai, the lord of all drinks.
