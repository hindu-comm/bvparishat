On a cup of tea

  

चहापानरसास्वादो ब्रह्मानन्दसहोदर: ।

कथं चहाविहीनास्ते सत्यं विद्युरिदं वद ।।

  

The delight of drinking a cup of tea is equivalent to the delight of experiencing the Ultimate Reality. Tell me, how will those who do not drink tea ever know this truth?
