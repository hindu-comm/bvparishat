On a cup of tea

  

चहापानेऽमृतास्वादो यदि जायेत भूतले ।

आटोप: स्वर्गगमने त्यज्यतां सकलो जनै: ।।

  

If one can have the joy of nectar in this very world with a cup of tea, people should abandon their exertion to go to heaven.
