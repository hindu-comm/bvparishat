A poet and a logician

  

कवितार्किकयोर्मध्ये को भेदो वद माधव ।

एकश्चिनोति पुष्पाणि परस्य परशु: करे ।।

  

O Madhava, tell me what is the difference between a poet and a logician. One collects flowers, while the other holds an axe in his hand.

  

\[Ockham's Razor!\]
