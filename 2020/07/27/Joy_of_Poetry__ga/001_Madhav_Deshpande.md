Joy of Poetry

  

वेद्यान्तरं विगलितं ब्रह्मानन्दस्थितात्मनाम् ।

वेद्यान्तरं विगलितं काव्यानन्दस्थितात्मनाम् ।।

  

Those who are resting in the bliss of Brahman are no longer aware of anything else. Those who are resting in the bliss of poetry are no longer aware of anything else.
