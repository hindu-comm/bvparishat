Joy of Poetry

  

आनन्दमयमेतन्मे जगत्काव्येऽवभासते ।

कुत एतत्परित्यज्य निवर्तिष्ये कदाचन ।।

  

In poetry, this world appears to me entirely filled with bliss. Why would I ever leave this world and return?
