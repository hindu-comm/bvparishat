For the first it is yes:

  

  

षस्जँ गतौ(to go)

भ्वादिःपरस्मैपदीसकर्मकःसेट्

सज्जति।

  

For the second, the root is

  

०४.००८९

[सिध्](https://ashtadhyayi.com/dhatu/04.0089)षिधुँ संराद्धौदिवादिः, परस्मैपदी, अकर्मकः, अनिट्(to be thoroughly prepared,to be accomplished,to be achieved,to be ready)  

  

The णिच् will be सेधयति।

There is a separate dhatu



०५.००१९

[साध्](https://ashtadhyayi.com/dhatu/05.0019)साधँ संसिद्धौस्वादिः, परस्मैपदी, सकर्मकः, अनिट्(to accomplish,to attain,to fulfill,to achieve)

declined as साध्नोति of which णिच् will be साधयति।
