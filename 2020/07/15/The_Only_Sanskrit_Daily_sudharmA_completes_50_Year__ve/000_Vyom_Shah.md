नमस्तेभ्यः विद्वाद्भ्यः!

  

On the occasion of सुधर्मा completing 50 years, with God's grace and blessings of Gurus, I tried paying a tribute to 50 Years of dedication by a shloka in अनुष्टुप्

  

बाणव्योममिताब्देभ्यो वार्तापत्राय संस्कृते।

तस्मै वारदराजाय स्वस्ति सदा सुधर्मणे॥

  

(I offer) Salutations forever to the only Sanskrit Newspaper, initiated by Shri Varadaraj Iyengar, published since 50 years.

  

I would be grateful to receive suggestions to make improvements in this verse.

  

Regards,

Vyom A. Shah
