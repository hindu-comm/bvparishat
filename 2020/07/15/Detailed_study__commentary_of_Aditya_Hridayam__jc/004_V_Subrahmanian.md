> > The well known meaning of the word ‘*Aditya*’ is the Sun. *Hridayam* > means the heart. ‘The Heart of Aditya’ means the real significance or > meaning behind the worship of Sun or the meditation on Sun God. >
> *Aditya-hridayam* is seen in the *sarga* 105 of *Yuddha* *kanda* of > Valmiki Ramayana. It contains 31 shlokas. The context of the shloka is > – Sage Agastya initiates Rama through this sanatana-mantra and Rama > attained victory over Ravana after doing this worship. >
> This set of shlokas is viewed as an independent text and millions of > devotees recite it every day. This stotra is usually taught to > children and students for health, success and achievement in life.. > (आरोग्यं भास्कारादिच्छेत्) >
