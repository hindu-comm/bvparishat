Subbuji,

  

There are several versions of Adityahridayastotra of which 2 are most popular - the short version with 31 verses from Valmiki Ramayana and a longer version with 170 verses from Bhavishyottara Purana.  

  

The Arsha version from Valmiki Ramayana, Yuddhakanda, chapter 105 has several commentaries. PFA Adityahridaya with 3 commentaries Tilaka, Bhushana and Siromani.  

  

Link to relevant page of NCC - <https://vmlt.in/ncc/2/77/a>

  

regards  
shankara
