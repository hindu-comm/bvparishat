श्रद्धा, मति, & निष्ठा are linked in Chandogya.

  

श्रद्धा and सत्य on the one hand,

and अश्रद्धा and अनृत on the other  

\- are linked elsewhere in the Vedic.

  

Nirukta links श्रत् and सत्य.
