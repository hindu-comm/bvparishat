Namaskaara,

  

न दृष्टं स्वस्य निर्यातं मलं येन दिनद्वयम्।

दिनचतुष्टयादृष्टां स प्रियां न प्रतीक्षते॥

The one who has not seen his faeces discharged for two days, does not wait (to see) his beloved, whom he has not seen (although) for four days.
