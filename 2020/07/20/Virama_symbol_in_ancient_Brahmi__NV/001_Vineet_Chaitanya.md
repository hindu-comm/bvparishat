One cannot introduce concept of स्वर-मात्रा without having concept of "हलन्त-चिह्न".

One can formally derive:

स्वर-मात्रा = हलन्त-चिह्न + corresponding-स्वर

So the "हलन्त-चिह्न" should be as old as स्वर-मात्रा.
