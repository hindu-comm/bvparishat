नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

1\. Is there a valid practice of reciting theshikShavalli with

"शिक्षांव्याख्यास्यामः । ... "

  

2\. Is there a reason for this difference?

  

I don't seeशीक्षाin any dictionary I could lay my hands on,.... expect for this entry in MW Cologne Dictionary:

(I understand about not all vaidika prayoga-a showing up in dictionaries.... )

  

शीक्षा f. incorrect form of शिक्षा (q.v.), TĀr.; ĀpŚr. &c.

  

                             ------- Vid Prabhu

  

' Incorrect form ' - itself is incorrect .

  

That person did not learn व्याकरणम् or पूर्वमीमांसा ।

  

In the beginning of **महाभाष्यम् (पस्पशाह्निकम्)** , Patamjalisays --

  

अथ शब्दानुशासनम् । केषां शब्दानाम्? लौकिकानां वैदिकानां च ।

  

Earlier to Panini , there were differentव्याकरण-s for वैदिकशब्द-s ( प्रातिशाख्या-s ) and लौकिकशब्द-s

( व्याकरण-s .) .

In fact there is no difference between लौकिकशब्द-s and वैदिकशब्द-s
(or even in meaning ).

  

**Sabaraswamy** in his **भाष्यम् ( पूर्वमीमांसा -- आकृत्यधिकरणम् , 1-3-9-30 सू ) clealy says that there is no difference in terms of form or meaning betwqeen वैदिक and लौकिक शब्द-s -----**

  

**य एवलौकिकाः शब्दाः त एव वैदिकाः त एव तेषामर्थाः .. ।**

  

Then why should Patanjali mention वैदिकशब्दाः separately ?

  

**ब्राह्मणवसिष्ठन्यायेन** -- ब्राह्मणा आयाताः वसिष्ठो’प्यायातः ।

Vasistha is also a ब्राह्मण - but , since he is respected more , his name is taken separately .

  

Another fact is that there is some difference in the form (not meaning ) of certain शब्द-s in लोक and वेद --

  

शिक्षा -शीक्षा

प्रजाः - प्रजासः

देवाः - देवासः

कर्णैः - कर्णेभिः etc

I discussed this aspect in both English translation of Brahmakanda of Vakyapadiyamas well as Theories of

Language : Oriental and Occidental (both online) .

  

Panini ruled - **व्यत्ययो बहुलम् - 3-1-85** -- there willbe difference in terms of वर्ण- लिङ्ग- पुरुष - वचन etc - see महाभाष्यम् ।

He also repeated the सूत्रम् ’ **बहुलं छन्दसि** ’ 2-4-39 for eleven times (total 12 times) - this also means that there will be a difference between लौकिक and वैदिक forms in certain cases only .

  

Some people think thatthere is **स्वर in वेद and not in लोक - this is wrong** -- Panini did not make any अधिकारसूत्रम् like ' छन्दसि ’ before स्वरप्रकरणम् ( see **शब्दकौस्तुभ**) - therefore there is स्वर in संस्क्रुतम् per se . But people do not respect स्वर in लोक ( **लोके स्वरविशये आदरः नास्ति** ) - this aspect is touched by **Mammatacarya also - संयोगो विप्रयोगश्च .... ।**

  

In शीक्षावल्ली only there is - अथाधिज्यौतिषम् । ज्योतिषम् itself is called ज्यौतिषम् ( **ज्योतिषः इदं ज्यौतिषम् -- नागेश** in

**लघुशब्देन्दुशेखर** -- but I think it can be ज्योतिषमेव ज्यौतिषम् - **प्रज्ञाद्यण् स्वार्थे** )
