The world of poetry

  

रस एव परं तत्त्वमितरन्मायिकं जगत् ।

रसे मग्नस्य मनसो जगता मायिकेन किम् ।।

  

Bliss is the highest reality, and everything else is a mere mirage. For the mind immersed in bliss, what is the point of dealing with this world of mirage?
