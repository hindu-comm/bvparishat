The world of poetry

  

नियतेर्नियमानेतज्जगत्काव्येऽतिवर्तते ।

वेद्यान्तरे विगलिते जानेऽहं नापरं जगत् ।।

  

In poetry, the world transcends the laws of nature. As I leave behind my awareness of everything else, I don’t know of any other world.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies  

  

\[Residence: Campbell, California, USA\]
