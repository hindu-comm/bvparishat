A story that my father told me -

  

मला माझ्या वडिलांनी त्यांच्या शाळेतली एक गंमत सांगितलेली आठवली. वर्गातले मास्तर फार मारकुटे होते, व त्यामुळे विद्यार्थ्यांचा थरकाप होत असे. फटकन् कबुलीजबाब देऊन टाकला तर मार कमी मिळत असे. एकदा मास्तरांनी दरडावून विचारले - शाकुंतल नाटक कुणी लिहिले? गोट्या तू सांग. तेव्हा नेहमीप्रमाणे गोट्याची आपणच काहीतरी चूक केली अशी समजूत झाली आणि त्याने चटकन् कबूल करून टाकले - "हो सर, हे नाटक मीच लिहिले. चुकलो. पुन्हा अशी चूक करणार नाही."

  

I just remembered an incident from my father's school days that he once told me. One of the teachers used to punish the students a lot, and hence the students were always worried about being punished. If you quickly admitted your error, you would probably get less punishment. Once the teacher asked the class in his normal raised voice: "Who wrote the drama Shakuntalam? Ok, you Gotu, tell me the answer." Gotu was scared of punishment as usual, and knew that if he admitted his mischief, the punishment might be reduced. So he just went ahead and admitted: "Yes, Sir. I myself wrote that drama. I am so sorry, Sir. I will not do such a thing again."
