A cup of tea

  

विना चहां न जागर्ति निशि सुप्तं कवेर्मन: ।

दधते कवितारूपं चहाया बिन्दवो मुखे ।।

  

Without a cup of tea, the poet’s mind, asleep at night, does not wake up. Then the drops of tea in the mouth turn into words of a poem.
