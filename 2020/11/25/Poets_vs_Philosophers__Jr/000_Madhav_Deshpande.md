Poets vs Philosophers

  

अस्ति नास्तीति वादेषु सक्ता: पण्डितमानिन: ।

प्रतिभाकल्पिते लोके विहरन्ति कवीश्वरा: ।।

  

While the so-called scholars immerse themselves into debates about whether the world exists or not, great poets frolic in the world created by their imagination.
