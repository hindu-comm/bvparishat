Yakṣagāna Padya or Prasanga is a collection of poems written to form a musical drama. The poems are composed in Kannada. The oldest surviving parasanga books are believed to have been composed in the 15th century or even earlier.

Yakṣagāna & Its Sanskrit Sources Hegde G.S. Parimaal Publications
