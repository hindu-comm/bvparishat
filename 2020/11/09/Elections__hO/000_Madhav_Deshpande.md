Elections

स्वयंवरे यथा वीरा: तथा निर्वाचनेऽपि ते ।

मामेव वरयस्वेति याचन्ते सर्वदा भुवि ।।

As in Svayamvaras, so in elections, the heroes always beg in the world: “you have to choose me.”

  

Madhav M. Deshpande
