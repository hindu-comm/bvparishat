Veṅkaṭādhvarin employs this metre twice in his *Viśvaguṇādarśa-campū*

\- verses 224 and 290 - in the descriptions of Rāmānuja and Vedānta-deśika.

The commentary says, the def. of the metre is not available in *Vr̥tta-ratnākara*.
