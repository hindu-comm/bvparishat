Elections come and go

  

निर्वाचनानि लोकेऽस्मिन् आयास्यन्ति पुन: पुन: ।

चिन्तयालं कषायं त्वं पिब माधव मा शुच: ।।

  

In this world, the elections will come again and again. Stop worrying, Madhava, drink your cup of tea and don’t feel sad.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

Senior Fellow, Oxford Center for Hindu Studies
