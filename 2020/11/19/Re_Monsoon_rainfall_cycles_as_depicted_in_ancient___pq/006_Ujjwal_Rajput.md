ब॒हून् अर्था॒न् प्रति॑पादयती॒दम्। तेषां॑ सूर्याय॒णस्य॒ मध्य॒म् इत्य॒यम् एक॑तमः।

Well, it yields various meanings. Of them one is equinox.  
  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

Well, the sanskrit search yields that it means "Equinox," which means  
the day on which day and night durations are equal.
