नमो विद्वद्भ्यः

  

शिष्येभ्यः समुवाच यो हि कृपया तत्त्वं परं मुक्तिदम्।

काश्मीरद्विजमुख्यदुःखकथनं श्रुत्वा कृपालुर्गुरुः॥१

पश्चात्तत्तिलकं ररक्ष खलु तद्यज्ञोपवीतं तथा।

सोऽयं तेगबहादुरस्य महिमा सङ्गीयते सद्गुरोः॥२

  

He who through mercy, told his disciples (about) the fundamental true element (which is) the bestower of Mukti. The merciful Guru, who on hearing the statement about the sufferings of the Pandits of Kashmir, then protected their tilaka (sacred forehead mark) and yajñopavīta (sacred thread). This is the greatness of the Sadguru Teg Bahadur, which is being sung.
