Sarasvati's son, the poet

  

सरस्वती गिरां देवी कविं सूते सुतं मुदा ।

यथादिष्ट: स्वमात्रासौ सरसं तनुते जगत् ।।

  

Sarasvati, the goddes of speech, happily gives birth to a poet. As instructed by his mother, he fills the world with delight.
