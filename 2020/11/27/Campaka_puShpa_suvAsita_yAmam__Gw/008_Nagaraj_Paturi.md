In a fifteenth century classical Telugu narrative poem Manucharitramu/ Svaarochishamanusambhavamu, an extremely exquisitely well-crafted full of relishable embellishment the poet Allasani Peddana the senior court poet of Sri Krishna Deva Raya says , in the introductory part of the work that Sri Krishna Deva Raya praised his style as ***s'ireeshakusuma pes'ala***, as delicate as a ***s'ireesha***flower.
