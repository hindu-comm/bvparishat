Yes, the śirīṣa puṣpam has also a very significant presence in Malayalam literature. It is the beautiful Vāka Pūvŭ, flower of the Vāka tree.

Being a city-bred guy, I never had a chance to see the Vāka Pū.

Many movie songs mention both the flower and the tree.

“Valkalam ūriya vasanta yāmini

Vāka mara cōṭṭil uṟakkam āyi ..”

After removing her clothes of tree-bark (valkalam), the Spring Night is sleeping beneath the Vāka tree …”
